# Soteriology

The doctrine of salvation — how God rescues fallen humanity from sin and death through the person and work of Jesus Christ. Salvation encompasses past (justification), present (sanctification), and future (glorification) dimensions.

## The Ordo Salutis

| Stage | Description | Key Text |
|:---|:---|:---|
| Election | God's sovereign choice | [[bibles/NASB-IL/Ephesians/Ephesians 1#v4\|Eph 1:4]] |
| Calling | Effectual summons through the gospel | [[bibles/NASB-IL/Romans/Romans 8#v30\|Rom 8:30]] |
| Regeneration | New birth by the Spirit | [[bibles/NASB-IL/John/John 3#v5\|John 3:5]] |
| Faith and Repentance | Human response to grace | [[bibles/NASB-IL/Acts/Acts 2#v38\|Acts 2:38]] |
| Justification | Declared righteous by faith | [[bibles/NASB-IL/Romans/Romans 3#v24\|Rom 3:24]] |
| Sanctification | Progressive transformation | [[bibles/NASB-IL/Philippians/Philippians 2#v12\|Phil 2:12-13]] |
| Glorification | Final conformity to Christ's image | [[bibles/NASB-IL/Romans/Romans 8#v29\|Rom 8:29-30]] |

## The Work of Christ

- **Substitution**: [[bibles/NASB-IL/2 Corinthians/2 Corinthians 5#v21|2 Cor 5:21]] — "He made Him who knew no sin to be sin on our behalf"
- **Propitiation**: [[bibles/NASB-IL/Romans/Romans 3#v25|Rom 3:25]] — "A propitiation in His [[concepts/greek/G129 - haima|blood]]"
- **Redemption**: [[bibles/NASB-IL/Ephesians/Ephesians 1#v7|Eph 1:7]] — "Redemption through His blood"
- **Reconciliation**: [[bibles/NASB-IL/2 Corinthians/2 Corinthians 5#v18|2 Cor 5:18-19]] — "God was in Christ reconciling the world"

## The Grace-Faith Nexus

[[bibles/NASB-IL/Ephesians/Ephesians 2#v8|Eph 2:8-9]] — "By [[concepts/greek/G5485 - charis|grace]] you have been saved through [[concepts/greek/G4102 - pistis|faith]]; and that not of yourselves, it is the gift of God."

## Canonical Arc

Salvation's story: promise (Gen 3:15) → prefiguration (Passover, sacrificial system) → prophecy (Isa 53) → accomplishment (the Cross) → proclamation (Acts-Epistles) → consummation (Revelation).
