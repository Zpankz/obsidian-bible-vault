## 2 John 1:1-4

<sup>3</sup> Grace, mercy [and] peace will be with us, from God the Father and from Jesus Christ, the Son of the Father, in truth and love.

<sup>4</sup> I was very glad to find [some] of your children walking in truth, just as we have received commandment [to do] from the Father.[^1]
