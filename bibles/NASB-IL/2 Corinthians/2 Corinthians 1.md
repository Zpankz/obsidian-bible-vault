## 2 Corinthians 1:7-11

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> and our hope for you is firmly grounded, knowing that as you are sharers of our sufferings, so also you are [sharers] of our comfort.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> For we do not want you to be unaware, brethren, of our affliction which came [to us] in Asia, that we were burdened excessively, beyond our strength, so that we despaired even of life;

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> indeed, we had the sentence of death within ourselves so that we would not trust in ourselves, but in God who raises the dead;

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> who delivered us from so great a [peril of] death, and will deliver [us], He on whom we have set our hope. And He will yet deliver us,

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> you also joining in helping us through your prayers, so that thanks may be given by many persons on our behalf for the favor bestowed on us through [the prayers] [of] many.

## 2 Corinthians 1:15-24

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> In this confidence I intended at first to come to you, so that you might twice receive a blessing;

###### v16
<sup>16</sup> that is, to pass your way into Macedonia, and again from Macedonia to come to you, and by you to be helped on my journey to Judea.

###### v17
<sup>17</sup> Therefore, I was not vacillating when I intended to do this, was I? Or what I purpose, do I purpose according to the flesh, so that with me there will be yes, yes and no, no [at the same time]?

###### v18
<sup>18</sup> But as God is faithful, our word to you is not yes and no.

###### v19
<sup>19</sup> For the Son of God, Christ Jesus, who was preached among you by us-- by me and Silvanus and Timothy-- was not yes and no, but is yes in Him.

###### v20
<sup>20</sup> For as many as are the promises of God, in Him they are yes; therefore also through Him is our Amen to the glory of God through us.

###### v21
<sup>21</sup> Now He who establishes us with you in Christ and anointed us is God,

###### v22
<sup>22</sup> who also sealed us and gave [us] the Spirit in our hearts as a pledge.

###### v23
<sup>23</sup> But I call God as witness to my soul, that to spare you I did not come again to Corinth.

###### v24
<sup>24</sup> Not that we lord it over your faith, but are workers with you for your joy; for in your faith you are standing firm.
