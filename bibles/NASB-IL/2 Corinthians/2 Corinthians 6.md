# 2 Corinthians 6

## 2 Corinthians 6:1-10

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> And working together [with Him], we also urge you not to receive the grace of God in vain--

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> for He says, 'AT THE ACCEPTABLE TIME I LISTENED TO YOU, AND ON THE DAY OF SALVATION I HELPED YOU.' Behold, now is 'THE ACCEPTABLE TIME,' behold, now is 'THE DAY OF SALVATION '--

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> giving no cause for offense in anything, so that the ministry will not be discredited,

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> but in everything commending ourselves as servants of God, in much endurance, in afflictions, in hardships, in distresses,

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> in beatings, in imprisonments, in tumults, in labors, in sleeplessness, in hunger,

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> in purity, in knowledge, in patience, in kindness, in the Holy Spirit, in genuine love,

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> in the word of truth, in the power of God; by the weapons of righteousness for the right hand and the left,

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> by glory and dishonor, by evil report and good report; [regarded] as deceivers and yet true;

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> as unknown yet well-known, as dying yet behold, we live; as punished yet not put to death,

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> as sorrowful yet always rejoicing, as poor yet making many rich, as having nothing yet possessing all things.

## 2 Corinthians 6:11-18

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> Our mouth has spoken freely to you, O Corinthians, our heart is opened wide.

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> You are not restrained by us, but you are restrained in your own affections.

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> Now in a like exchange-- I speak as to children-- open wide [to us] also.

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> Do not be bound together with unbelievers; for what partnership have righteousness and lawlessness, or what fellowship has light with darkness?

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> Or what harmony has Christ with Belial, or what has a believer in common with an unbeliever?

###### v16
<sup>16</sup> Or what agreement has the temple of God with idols? For we are the temple of the living God; just as God said, 'I WILL DWELL IN THEM AND WALK AMONG THEM; AND I WILL BE THEIR GOD, AND THEY SHALL BE MY PEOPLE.

###### v17
<sup>17</sup> 'Therefore, COME OUT FROM THEIR MIDST AND BE SEPARATE,' says the Lord. 'AND DO NOT TOUCH WHAT IS UNCLEAN; And I will welcome you.

###### v18
<sup>18</sup> 'And I will be a father to you, And you shall be sons and daughters to Me,' Says the Lord Almighty.
