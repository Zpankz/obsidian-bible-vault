# Exodus 37

## Exodus 37:1-9

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> Now Bezalel made the ark of acacia wood; its length was two and a half cubits, and its width one and a half cubits, and its height one and a half cubits;

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> and he overlaid it with pure gold inside and out, and made a gold molding for it all around.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> He cast four rings of gold for it on its four feet; even two rings on one side of it, and two rings on the other side of it.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> He made poles of acacia wood and overlaid them with gold.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> He put the poles into the rings on the sides of the ark, to carry it.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> He made a mercy seat of pure gold, two and a half cubits long and one and a half cubits wide.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> He made two cherubim of gold; he made them of hammered work at the two ends of the mercy seat;

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> one cherub at the one end and one cherub at the other end; he made the cherubim [of one piece] with the mercy seat at the two ends.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> The cherubim had [their] wings spread upward, covering the mercy seat with their wings, with their faces toward each other; the faces of the cherubim were toward the mercy seat.

## Exodus 37:10-24

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> Then he made the table of acacia wood, two cubits long and a cubit wide and one and a half cubits high.

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> He overlaid it with pure gold, and made a gold molding for it all around.

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> He made a rim for it of a handbreadth all around, and made a gold molding for its rim all around.

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> He cast four gold rings for it and put the rings on the four corners that were on its four feet.

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> Close by the rim were the rings, the holders for the poles to carry the table.

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> He made the poles of acacia wood and overlaid them with gold, to carry the table.

###### v16
<sup>16</sup> He made the utensils which were on the table, its dishes and its pans and its bowls and its jars, with which to pour out drink offerings, of pure gold.

###### v17
<sup>17</sup> Then he made the lampstand of pure gold. He made the lampstand of hammered work, its base and its shaft; its cups, its bulbs and its flowers were [of one piece] with it.

###### v18
<sup>18</sup> There were six branches going out of its sides; three branches of the lampstand from the one side of it and three branches of the lampstand from the other side of it;

###### v19
<sup>19</sup> three cups shaped like almond [blossoms], a bulb and a flower in one branch, and three cups shaped like almond [blossoms], a bulb and a flower in the other branch-- so for the six branches going out of the lampstand.

###### v20
<sup>20</sup> In the lampstand [there were] four cups shaped like almond [blossoms], its bulbs and its flowers;

###### v21
<sup>21</sup> and a bulb was under the [first] pair of branches [coming] out of it, and a bulb under the [second] pair of branches [coming] out of it, and a bulb under the [third] pair of branches [coming] out of it, for the six branches coming out of the lampstand.

###### v22
<sup>22</sup> Their bulbs and their branches were [of one piece] with it; the whole of it [was] a single hammered work of pure gold.

###### v23
<sup>23</sup> He made its seven lamps with its snuffers and its trays of pure gold.

###### v24
<sup>24</sup> He made it and all its utensils from a talent of pure gold.

## Exodus 37:25-29

###### v25
<sup>25</sup> Then he made the altar of incense of acacia wood: a cubit long and a cubit wide, square, and two cubits high; its horns were [of one piece] with it.

###### v26
<sup>26</sup> He overlaid it with pure gold, its top and its sides all around, and its horns; and he made a gold molding for it all around.

###### v27
<sup>27</sup> He made two golden rings for it under its molding, on its two sides-- on opposite sides-- as holders for poles with which to carry it.

###### v28
<sup>28</sup> He made the poles of acacia wood and overlaid them with gold.

###### v29
<sup>29</sup> And he made the holy anointing oil and the pure, fragrant incense of spices, the work of a perfumer.
