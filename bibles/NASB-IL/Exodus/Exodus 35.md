# Exodus 35

## Exodus 35:1-19

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> Then Moses assembled all the congregation of the sons of Israel, and said to them, 'These are the things that the LORD has commanded [you] to do:

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> 'For six days work may be done, but on the seventh day you shall have a holy [day], a sabbath of complete rest to the LORD; whoever does any work on it shall be put to death.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> 'You shall not kindle a fire in any of your dwellings on the sabbath day.'

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> Moses spoke to all the congregation of the sons of Israel, saying, 'This is the thing which the LORD has commanded, saying,

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> 'Take from among you a contribution to the LORD; whoever is of a willing heart, let him bring it as the LORD'S contribution: gold, silver, and bronze,

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> and blue, purple and scarlet [material], fine linen, goats' [hair],

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> and rams' skins dyed red, and porpoise skins, and acacia wood,

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> and oil for lighting, and spices for the anointing oil, and for the fragrant incense,

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> and onyx stones and setting stones for the ephod and for the breastpiece.

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> 'Let every skillful man among you come, and make all that the LORD has commanded:

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> the tabernacle, its tent and its covering, its hooks and its boards, its bars, its pillars, and its sockets;

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> the ark and its poles, the mercy seat, and the curtain of the screen;

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> the table and its poles, and all its utensils, and the bread of the Presence;

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> the lampstand also for the light and its utensils and its lamps and the oil for the light;

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> and the altar of incense and its poles, and the anointing oil and the fragrant incense, and the screen for the doorway at the entrance of the tabernacle;

###### v16
<sup>16</sup> the altar of burnt offering with its bronze grating, its poles, and all its utensils, the basin and its stand;

###### v17
<sup>17</sup> the hangings of the court, its pillars and its sockets, and the screen for the gate of the court;

###### v18
<sup>18</sup> the pegs of the tabernacle and the pegs of the court and their cords;

###### v19
<sup>19</sup> the woven garments for ministering in the holy place, the holy garments for Aaron the priest and the garments of his sons, to minister as priests.''

## Exodus 35:20-29

###### v20
<sup>20</sup> Then all the congregation of the sons of Israel departed from Moses' presence.

###### v21
<sup>21</sup> Everyone whose heart stirred him and everyone whose spirit moved him came [and] brought the LORD'S contribution for the work of the tent of meeting and for all its service and for the holy garments.

###### v22
<sup>22</sup> Then all whose hearts moved them, both men and women, came [and] brought brooches and earrings and signet rings and bracelets, all articles of gold; so [did] every man who presented an offering of gold to the LORD.

###### v23
<sup>23</sup> Every man, who had in his possession blue and purple and scarlet [material] and fine linen and goats' [hair] and rams' skins dyed red and porpoise skins, brought them.

###### v24
<sup>24</sup> Everyone who could make a contribution of silver and bronze brought the LORD'S contribution; and every man who had in his possession acacia wood for any work of the service brought it.

###### v25
<sup>25</sup> All the skilled women spun with their hands, and brought what they had spun, [in] blue and purple [and] scarlet [material] and [in] fine linen.

###### v26
<sup>26</sup> All the women whose heart stirred with a skill spun the goats' [hair].

###### v27
<sup>27</sup> The rulers brought the onyx stones and the stones for setting for the ephod and for the breastpiece;

###### v28
<sup>28</sup> and the spice and the oil for the light and for the anointing oil and for the fragrant incense.

###### v29
<sup>29</sup> The Israelites, all the men and women, whose heart moved them to bring [material] for all the work, which the LORD had commanded through Moses to be done, brought a freewill offering to the LORD.

## Exodus 35:30-35

###### v30
<sup>30</sup> Then Moses said to the sons of Israel, 'See, the LORD has called by name Bezalel the son of Uri, the son of Hur, of the tribe of Judah.

###### v31
<sup>31</sup> 'And He has filled him with the Spirit of God, in wisdom, in understanding and in knowledge and in all craftsmanship;

###### v32
<sup>32</sup> to make designs for working in gold and in silver and in bronze,

###### v33
<sup>33</sup> and in the cutting of stones for settings and in the carving of wood, so as to perform in every inventive work.

###### v34
<sup>34</sup> 'He also has put in his heart to teach, both he and Oholiab, the son of Ahisamach, of the tribe of Dan.

###### v35
<sup>35</sup> 'He has filled them with skill to perform every work of an engraver and of a designer and of an embroiderer, in blue and in purple [and] in scarlet [material], and in fine linen, and of a weaver, as performers of every work and makers of designs.
