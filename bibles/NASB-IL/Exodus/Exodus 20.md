## Exodus 20:1-11

<sup>1</sup> Then God spoke all these words, saying,[^1]

<sup>2</sup> 'I am the LORD your God, who brought you out of the land of Egypt, out of the house of slavery.[^2][^3][^4][^5][^6][^7][^8][^9]

<sup>3</sup> 'You shall have no other gods before Me.[^10][^11][^12][^13][^14][^15][^16][^17]

<sup>4</sup> 'You shall not make for yourself an idol, or any likeness of what is in heaven above or on the earth beneath or in the water under the earth.[^18][^19][^20][^21][^22][^23][^24][^25]

<sup>5</sup> 'You shall not worship them or serve them; for I, the LORD your God, am a jealous God, visiting the iniquity of the fathers on the children, on the third and the fourth generations of those who hate Me,[^26][^27][^28][^29][^30][^31][^32][^33]

<sup>6</sup> but showing lovingkindness to thousands, to those who love Me and keep My commandments.[^34][^35][^36][^37][^38][^39]

<sup>7</sup> 'You shall not take the name of the LORD your God in vain, for the LORD will not leave him unpunished who takes His name in vain.[^40][^41][^42][^43]

<sup>8</sup> 'Remember the sabbath day, to keep it holy.[^44][^45][^46][^47][^48][^49][^50][^51]

<sup>9</sup> 'Six days you shall labor and do all your work,

<sup>10</sup> but the seventh day is a sabbath of the LORD your God; [in it] you shall not do any work, you or your son or your daughter, your male or your female servant or your cattle or your sojourner who stays with you.[^52]

<sup>11</sup> 'For in six days the LORD made the heavens and the earth, the sea and all that is in them, and rested on the seventh day; therefore the LORD blessed the sabbath day and made it holy.[^53][^54][^55]

## Exodus 20:22-26

<sup>22</sup> Then the LORD said to Moses, 'Thus you shall say to the sons of Israel, 'You yourselves have seen that I have spoken to you from heaven.

<sup>23</sup> 'You shall not make [other gods] besides Me; gods of silver or gods of gold, you shall not make for yourselves.

<sup>24</sup> 'You shall make an altar of earth for Me, and you shall sacrifice on it your burnt offerings and your peace offerings, your sheep and your oxen; in every place where I cause My name to be remembered, I will come to you and bless you.[^90][^91][^92][^93][^94][^95][^96][^97]

<sup>25</sup> 'If you make an altar of stone for Me, you shall not build it of cut stones, for if you wield your tool on it, you will profane it.

<sup>26</sup> 'And you shall not go up by steps to My altar, so that your nakedness will not be exposed on it.'
