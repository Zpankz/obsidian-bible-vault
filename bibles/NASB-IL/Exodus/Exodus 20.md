# Exodus 20

## Exodus 20:1-11

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> Then God spoke all these words, saying,

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> 'I am the LORD your God, who brought you out of the land of Egypt, out of the house of slavery.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> 'You shall have no other gods before Me.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> 'You shall not make for yourself an idol, or any likeness of what is in heaven above or on the earth beneath or in the water under the earth.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> 'You shall not worship them or serve them; for I, the LORD your God, am a jealous God, visiting the iniquity of the fathers on the children, on the third and the fourth generations of those who hate Me,

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> but showing lovingkindness to thousands, to those who love Me and keep My commandments.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> 'You shall not take the name of the LORD your God in vain, for the LORD will not leave him unpunished who takes His name in vain.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> 'Remember the sabbath day, to keep it holy.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> 'Six days you shall labor and do all your work,

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> but the seventh day is a sabbath of the LORD your God; [in it] you shall not do any work, you or your son or your daughter, your male or your female servant or your cattle or your sojourner who stays with you.

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> 'For in six days the LORD made the heavens and the earth, the sea and all that is in them, and rested on the seventh day; therefore the LORD blessed the sabbath day and made it holy.

## Exodus 20:22-26

###### v22
<sup>22</sup> Then the LORD said to Moses, 'Thus you shall say to the sons of Israel, 'You yourselves have seen that I have spoken to you from heaven.

###### v23
<sup>23</sup> 'You shall not make [other gods] besides Me; gods of silver or gods of gold, you shall not make for yourselves.

###### v24
<sup>24</sup> 'You shall make an altar of earth for Me, and you shall sacrifice on it your burnt offerings and your peace offerings, your sheep and your oxen; in every place where I cause My name to be remembered, I will come to you and bless you.

###### v25
<sup>25</sup> 'If you make an altar of stone for Me, you shall not build it of cut stones, for if you wield your tool on it, you will profane it.

###### v26
<sup>26</sup> 'And you shall not go up by steps to My altar, so that your nakedness will not be exposed on it.'
