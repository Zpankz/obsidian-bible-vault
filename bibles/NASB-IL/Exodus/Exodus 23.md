# Exodus 23

## Exodus 23:1-9

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> 'You shall not bear a false report; do not join your hand with a wicked man to be a malicious witness.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> 'You shall not follow the masses in doing evil, nor shall you testify in a dispute so as to turn aside after a multitude in order to pervert [justice];

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> nor shall you be partial to a poor man in his dispute.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> 'If you meet your enemy's ox or his donkey wandering away, you shall surely return it to him.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> 'If you see the donkey of one who hates you lying [helpless] under its load, you shall refrain from leaving it to him, you shall surely release [it] with him.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> 'You shall not pervert the justice [due] to your needy [brother] in his dispute.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> 'Keep far from a false charge, and do not kill the innocent or the righteous, for I will not acquit the guilty.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> 'You shall not take a bribe, for a bribe blinds the clear-sighted and subverts the cause of the just.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> 'You shall not oppress a stranger, since you yourselves know the feelings of a stranger, for you [also] were strangers in the land of Egypt.

## Exodus 23:10-19

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> 'You shall sow your land for six years and gather in its yield,

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> but [on] the seventh year you shall let it rest and lie fallow, so that the needy of your people may eat; and whatever they leave the beast of the field may eat. You are to do the same with your vineyard [and] your olive grove.

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> 'Six days you are to do your work, but on the seventh day you shall cease [from labor] so that your ox and your donkey may rest, and the son of your female slave, as well as your stranger, may refresh themselves.

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> 'Now concerning everything which I have said to you, be on your guard; and do not mention the name of other gods, nor let [them] be heard from your mouth.

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> 'Three times a year you shall celebrate a feast to Me.

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> 'You shall observe the Feast of Unleavened Bread; for seven days you are to eat unleavened bread, as I commanded you, at the appointed time in the month Abib, for in it you came out of Egypt. And none shall appear before Me empty-handed.

###### v16
<sup>16</sup> 'Also [you shall observe] the Feast of the Harvest [of] the first fruits of your labors [from] what you sow in the field; also the Feast of the Ingathering at the end of the year when you gather in [the fruit of] your labors from the field.

###### v17
<sup>17</sup> 'Three times a year all your males shall appear before the Lord GOD.

###### v18
<sup>18</sup> 'You shall not offer the blood of My sacrifice with leavened bread; nor is the fat of My feast to remain overnight until morning.

###### v19
<sup>19</sup> 'You shall bring the choice first fruits of your soil into the house of the LORD your God. 'You are not to boil a young goat in the milk of its mother.

## Exodus 23:20-33

###### v20
<sup>20</sup> 'Behold, I am going to send an angel before you to guard you along the way and to bring you into the place which I have prepared.

###### v21
<sup>21</sup> 'Be on your guard before him and obey his voice; do not be rebellious toward him, for he will not pardon your transgression, since My name is in him.

###### v22
<sup>22</sup> 'But if you truly obey his voice and do all that I say, then I will be an enemy to your enemies and an adversary to your adversaries.

###### v23
<sup>23</sup> 'For My angel will go before you and bring you in to [the land of] the Amorites, the Hittites, the Perizzites, the Canaanites, the Hivites and the Jebusites; and I will completely destroy them.

###### v24
<sup>24</sup> 'You shall not worship their gods, nor serve them, nor do according to their deeds; but you shall utterly overthrow them and break their [sacred] pillars in pieces.

###### v25
<sup>25</sup> 'But you shall serve the LORD your God, and He will bless your bread and your water; and I will remove sickness from your midst.

###### v26
<sup>26</sup> 'There shall be no one miscarrying or barren in your land; I will fulfill the number of your days.

###### v27
<sup>27</sup> 'I will send My terror ahead of you, and throw into confusion all the people among whom you come, and I will make all your enemies turn [their] backs to you.

###### v28
<sup>28</sup> 'I will send hornets ahead of you so that they will drive out the Hivites, the Canaanites, and the Hittites before you.

###### v29
<sup>29</sup> 'I will not drive them out before you in a single year, that the land may not become desolate and the beasts of the field become too numerous for you.

###### v30
<sup>30</sup> 'I will drive them out before you little by little, until you become fruitful and take possession of the land.

###### v31
<sup>31</sup> 'I will fix your boundary from the Red Sea to the sea of the Philistines, and from the wilderness to the River [Euphrates]; for I will deliver the inhabitants of the land into your hand, and you will drive them out before you.

###### v32
<sup>32</sup> 'You shall make no covenant with them or with their gods.

###### v33
<sup>33</sup> 'They shall not live in your land, because they will make you sin against Me; for [if] you serve their gods, it will surely be a snare to you.'
