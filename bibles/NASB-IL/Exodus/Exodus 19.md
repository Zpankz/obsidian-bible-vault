# Exodus 19

## Exodus 19:1-8

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> In the third month after the sons of Israel had gone out of the land of Egypt, on that very day they came into the wilderness of Sinai.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> When they set out from Rephidim, they came to the wilderness of Sinai and camped in the wilderness; and there Israel camped in front of the mountain.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> Moses went up to God, and the LORD called to him from the mountain, saying, 'Thus you shall say to the house of Jacob and tell the sons of Israel:

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> 'You yourselves have seen what I did to the Egyptians, and [how] I bore you on eagles' wings, and brought you to Myself.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> 'Now then, if you will indeed obey My voice and keep My covenant, then you shall be My own possession among all the peoples, for all the earth is Mine;

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> and you shall be to Me a kingdom of priests and a holy nation.' These are the words that you shall speak to the sons of Israel.'

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> So Moses came and called the elders of the people, and set before them all these words which the LORD had commanded him.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> All the people answered together and said, 'All that the LORD has spoken we will do!' And Moses brought back the words of the people to the LORD.

## Exodus 19:9-15

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> The LORD said to Moses, 'Behold, I will come to you in a thick cloud, so that the people may hear when I speak with you and may also believe in you forever.' Then Moses told the words of the people to the LORD.

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> The LORD also said to Moses, 'Go to the people and consecrate them today and tomorrow, and let them wash their garments;

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> and let them be ready for the third day, for on the third day the LORD will come down on Mount Sinai in the sight of all the people.

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> 'You shall set bounds for the people all around, saying, 'Beware that you do not go up on the mountain or touch the border of it; whoever touches the mountain shall surely be put to death.

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> 'No hand shall touch him, but he shall surely be stoned or shot through; whether beast or man, he shall not live.' When the ram's horn sounds a long blast, they shall come up to the mountain.'

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> So Moses went down from the mountain to the people and consecrated the people, and they washed their garments.

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> He said to the people, 'Be ready for the third day; do not go near a woman.'

## Exodus 19:16-25

###### v16
<sup>16</sup> So it came about on the third day, when it was morning, that there were thunder and lightning flashes and a thick cloud upon the mountain and a very loud trumpet sound, so that all the people who [were] in the camp trembled.

###### v17
<sup>17</sup> And Moses brought the people out of the camp to meet God, and they stood at the foot of the mountain.

###### v18
<sup>18</sup> Now Mount Sinai [was] all in smoke because the LORD descended upon it in fire; and its smoke ascended like the smoke of a furnace, and the whole mountain quaked violently.

###### v19
<sup>19</sup> When the sound of the trumpet grew louder and louder, Moses spoke and God answered him with thunder.

###### v20
<sup>20</sup> The LORD came down on Mount Sinai, to the top of the mountain; and the LORD called Moses to the top of the mountain, and Moses went up.

###### v21
<sup>21</sup> Then the LORD spoke to Moses, 'Go down, warn the people, so that they do not break through to the LORD to gaze, and many of them perish.

###### v22
<sup>22</sup> 'Also let the priests who come near to the LORD consecrate themselves, or else the LORD will break out against them.'

###### v23
<sup>23</sup> Moses said to the LORD, 'The people cannot come up to Mount Sinai, for You warned us, saying, 'Set bounds about the mountain and consecrate it.''

###### v24
<sup>24</sup> Then the LORD said to him, 'Go down and come up [again], you and Aaron with you; but do not let the priests and the people break through to come up to the LORD, or He will break forth upon them.'

###### v25
<sup>25</sup> So Moses went down to the people and told them.
