# 1 Chronicles 3

## 1 Chronicles 3:1-9

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> Now these were the sons of David who were born to him in Hebron: the firstborn [was] Amnon, by Ahinoam the Jezreelitess; the second [was] Daniel, by Abigail the Carmelitess;

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> the third [was] Absalom the son of Maacah, the daughter of Talmai king of Geshur; the fourth [was] Adonijah the son of Haggith;

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> the fifth [was] Shephatiah, by Abital; the sixth [was] Ithream, by his wife Eglah.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> Six were born to him in Hebron, and there he reigned seven years and six months. And in Jerusalem he reigned thirty-three years.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> These were born to him in Jerusalem: Shimea, Shobab, Nathan and Solomon, four, by Bath-shua the daughter of Ammiel;

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> and Ibhar, Elishama, Eliphelet,

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> Nogah, Nepheg and Japhia,

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> Elishama, Eliada and Eliphelet, nine.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> All [these were] the sons of David, besides the sons of the concubines; and Tamar [was] their sister.

## 1 Chronicles 3:10-24

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> Now Solomon's son [was] Rehoboam, Abijah [was] his son, Asa his son, Jehoshaphat his son,

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> Joram his son, Ahaziah his son, Joash his son,

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> Amaziah his son, Azariah his son, Jotham his son,

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> Ahaz his son, Hezekiah his son, Manasseh his son,

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> Amon his son, Josiah his son.

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> The sons of Josiah [were] Johanan the firstborn, and the second [was] Jehoiakim, the third Zedekiah, the fourth Shallum.

###### v16
<sup>16</sup> The sons of Jehoiakim [were] Jeconiah his son, Zedekiah his son.

###### v17
<sup>17</sup> The sons of Jeconiah, the prisoner, [were] Shealtiel his son,

###### v18
<sup>18</sup> and Malchiram, Pedaiah, Shenazzar, Jekamiah, Hoshama and Nedabiah.

###### v19
<sup>19</sup> The sons of Pedaiah [were] Zerubbabel and Shimei. And the sons of Zerubbabel [were] Meshullam and Hananiah, and Shelomith [was] their sister;

###### v20
<sup>20</sup> and Hashubah, Ohel, Berechiah, Hasadiah and Jushab-hesed, five.

###### v21
<sup>21</sup> The sons of Hananiah [were] Pelatiah and Jeshaiah, the sons of Rephaiah, the sons of Arnan, the sons of Obadiah, the sons of Shecaniah.

###### v22
<sup>22</sup> The descendants of Shecaniah [were] Shemaiah, and the sons of Shemaiah: Hattush, Igal, Bariah, Neariah and Shaphat, six.

###### v23
<sup>23</sup> The sons of Neariah [were] Elioenai, Hizkiah and Azrikam, three.

###### v24
<sup>24</sup> The sons of Elioenai [were] Hodaviah, Eliashib, Pelaiah, Akkub, Johanan, Delaiah and Anani, seven.
