# 1 Chronicles 2

## 1 Chronicles 2:1-17

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> These are the sons of Israel: Reuben, Simeon, Levi, Judah, Issachar, Zebulun,

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> Dan, Joseph, Benjamin, Naphtali, Gad and Asher.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> The sons of Judah [were] Er, Onan and Shelah; [these] three were born to him by Bath-shua the Canaanitess. And Er, Judah's firstborn, was wicked in the sight of the LORD, so He put him to death.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> Tamar his daughter-in-law bore him Perez and Zerah. Judah had five sons in all.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> The sons of Perez [were] Hezron and Hamul.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> The sons of Zerah [were] Zimri, Ethan, Heman, Calcol and Dara; five of them in all.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> The son of Carmi [was] Achar, the troubler of Israel, who violated the ban.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> The son of Ethan [was] Azariah.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> Now the sons of Hezron, who were born to him [were] Jerahmeel, Ram and Chelubai.

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> Ram became the father of Amminadab, and Amminadab became the father of Nahshon, leader of the sons of Judah;

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> Nahshon became the father of Salma, Salma became the father of Boaz,

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> Boaz became the father of Obed, and Obed became the father of Jesse;

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> and Jesse became the father of Eliab his firstborn, then Abinadab the second, Shimea the third,

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> Nethanel the fourth, Raddai the fifth,

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> Ozem the sixth, David the seventh;

###### v16
<sup>16</sup> and their sisters [were] Zeruiah and Abigail. And the three sons of Zeruiah [were] Abshai, Joab and Asahel.

###### v17
<sup>17</sup> Abigail bore Amasa, and the father of Amasa was Jether the Ishmaelite.

## 1 Chronicles 2:18-55

###### v18
<sup>18</sup> Now Caleb the son of Hezron had sons by Azubah [his] wife, and by Jerioth; and these were her sons: Jesher, Shobab, and Ardon.

###### v19
<sup>19</sup> When Azubah died, Caleb married Ephrath, who bore him Hur.

###### v20
<sup>20</sup> Hur became the father of Uri, and Uri became the father of Bezalel.

###### v21
<sup>21</sup> Afterward Hezron went in to the daughter of Machir the father of Gilead, whom he married when he was sixty years old; and she bore him Segub.

###### v22
<sup>22</sup> Segub became the father of Jair, who had twenty-three cities in the land of Gilead.

###### v23
<sup>23</sup> But Geshur and Aram took the towns of Jair from them, with Kenath and its villages, [even] sixty cities. All these were the sons of Machir, the father of Gilead.

###### v24
<sup>24</sup> After the death of Hezron in Caleb-ephrathah, Abijah, Hezron's wife, bore him Ashhur the father of Tekoa.

###### v25
<sup>25</sup> Now the sons of Jerahmeel the firstborn of Hezron [were] Ram the firstborn, then Bunah, Oren, Ozem [and] Ahijah.

###### v26
<sup>26</sup> Jerahmeel had another wife, whose name was Atarah; she was the mother of Onam.

###### v27
<sup>27</sup> The sons of Ram, the firstborn of Jerahmeel, were Maaz, Jamin and Eker.

###### v28
<sup>28</sup> The sons of Onam were Shammai and Jada. And the sons of Shammai [were] Nadab and Abishur.

###### v29
<sup>29</sup> The name of Abishur's wife [was] Abihail, and she bore him Ahban and Molid.

###### v30
<sup>30</sup> The sons of Nadab [were] Seled and Appaim, and Seled died without sons.

###### v31
<sup>31</sup> The son of Appaim [was] Ishi. And the son of Ishi [was] Sheshan. And the son of Sheshan [was] Ahlai.

###### v32
<sup>32</sup> The sons of Jada the brother of Shammai [were] Jether and Jonathan, and Jether died without sons.

###### v33
<sup>33</sup> The sons of Jonathan [were] Peleth and Zaza. These were the sons of Jerahmeel.

###### v34
<sup>34</sup> Now Sheshan had no sons, only daughters. And Sheshan had an Egyptian servant whose name was Jarha.

###### v35
<sup>35</sup> Sheshan gave his daughter to Jarha his servant in marriage, and she bore him Attai.

###### v36
<sup>36</sup> Attai became the father of Nathan, and Nathan became the father of Zabad,

###### v37
<sup>37</sup> and Zabad became the father of Ephlal, and Ephlal became the father of Obed,

###### v38
<sup>38</sup> and Obed became the father of Jehu, and Jehu became the father of Azariah,

###### v39
<sup>39</sup> and Azariah became the father of Helez, and Helez became the father of Eleasah,

###### v40
<sup>40</sup> and Eleasah became the father of Sismai, and Sismai became the father of Shallum,

###### v41
<sup>41</sup> and Shallum became the father of Jekamiah, and Jekamiah became the father of Elishama.

###### v42
<sup>42</sup> Now the sons of Caleb, the brother of Jerahmeel, [were] Mesha his firstborn, who was the father of Ziph; and his son was Mareshah, the father of Hebron.

###### v43
<sup>43</sup> The sons of Hebron [were] Korah and Tappuah and Rekem and Shema.

###### v44
<sup>44</sup> Shema became the father of Raham, the father of Jorkeam; and Rekem became the father of Shammai.

###### v45
<sup>45</sup> The son of Shammai was Maon, and Maon [was] the father of Bethzur.

###### v46
<sup>46</sup> Ephah, Caleb's concubine, bore Haran, Moza and Gazez; and Haran became the father of Gazez.

###### v47
<sup>47</sup> The sons of Jahdai [were] Regem, Jotham, Geshan, Pelet, Ephah and Shaaph.

###### v48
<sup>48</sup> Maacah, Caleb's concubine, bore Sheber and Tirhanah.

###### v49
<sup>49</sup> She also bore Shaaph the father of Madmannah, Sheva the father of Machbena and the father of Gibea; and the daughter of Caleb [was] Achsah.

###### v50
<sup>50</sup> These were the sons of Caleb. The sons of Hur, the firstborn of Ephrathah, [were] Shobal the father of Kiriath-jearim,

###### v51
<sup>51</sup> Salma the father of Bethlehem [and] Hareph the father of Beth-gader.

###### v52
<sup>52</sup> Shobal the father of Kiriath-jearim had sons: Haroeh, half of the Manahathites,

###### v53
<sup>53</sup> and the families of Kiriath-jearim: the Ithrites, the Puthites, the Shumathites and the Mishraites; from these came the Zorathites and the Eshtaolites.

###### v54
<sup>54</sup> The sons of Salma [were] Bethlehem and the Netophathites, Atroth-beth-joab and half of the Manahathites, the Zorites.

###### v55
<sup>55</sup> The families of scribes who lived at Jabez [were] the Tirathites, the Shimeathites [and] the Sucathites. Those are the Kenites who came from Hammath, the father of the house of Rechab.
