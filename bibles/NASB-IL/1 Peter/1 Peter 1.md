## 1 Peter 1:13-23

<sup>13</sup> Therefore, prepare your minds for action, keep sober [in spirit], fix your hope completely on the grace to be brought to you at the revelation of Jesus Christ.[^69][^70][^71][^72][^73][^74][^75][^76]

<sup>14</sup> As obedient children, do not be conformed to the former lusts [which were yours] in your ignorance,[^77][^78][^79][^80][^81][^82][^83]

<sup>15</sup> but like the Holy One who called you, be holy yourselves also in all [your] behavior;[^84][^85][^86][^87][^88][^89][^90][^91]

<sup>16</sup> because it is written, 'YOU SHALL BE HOLY, FOR I AM HOLY.'[^92][^93][^94]

<sup>17</sup> If you address as Father the One who impartially judges according to each one's work, conduct yourselves in fear during the time of your stay [on earth];[^95][^96][^97]

<sup>18</sup> knowing that you were not redeemed with perishable things like silver or gold from your futile way of life inherited from your forefathers,[^98][^99][^100][^101][^102][^103][^104][^105]

<sup>19</sup> but with precious blood, as of a lamb unblemished and spotless, [the blood] of Christ.[^106][^107][^108][^109][^110][^111][^112][^113]

<sup>20</sup> For He was foreknown before the foundation of the world, but has appeared in these last times for the sake of you[^114][^115][^116][^117]

<sup>21</sup> who through Him are believers in God, who raised Him from the dead and gave Him glory, so that your faith and hope are in God.

<sup>22</sup> Since you have in obedience to the truth purified your souls for a sincere love of the brethren, fervently love one another from the heart,[^118][^119][^120][^121][^122][^123][^124][^125]

<sup>23</sup> for you have been born again not of seed which is perishable but imperishable, [that is], through the living and enduring word of God.[^126][^127][^128][^129][^130][^131][^132][^133]
