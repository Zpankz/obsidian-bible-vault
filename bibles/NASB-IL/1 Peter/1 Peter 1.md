## 1 Peter 1:13-23

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> Therefore, prepare your minds for action, keep sober [in spirit], fix your hope completely on the grace to be brought to you at the revelation of Jesus Christ.

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> As obedient children, do not be conformed to the former lusts [which were yours] in your ignorance,

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> but like the Holy One who called you, be holy yourselves also in all [your] behavior;

###### v16
<sup>16</sup> because it is written, 'YOU SHALL BE HOLY, FOR I AM HOLY.'

###### v17
<sup>17</sup> If you address as Father the One who impartially judges according to each one's work, conduct yourselves in fear during the time of your stay [on earth];

###### v18
<sup>18</sup> knowing that you were not redeemed with perishable things like silver or gold from your futile way of life inherited from your forefathers,

###### v19
<sup>19</sup> but with precious blood, as of a lamb unblemished and spotless, [the blood] of Christ.

###### v20
<sup>20</sup> For He was foreknown before the foundation of the world, but has appeared in these last times for the sake of you

###### v21
<sup>21</sup> who through Him are believers in God, who raised Him from the dead and gave Him glory, so that your faith and hope are in God.

###### v22
<sup>22</sup> Since you have in obedience to the truth purified your souls for a sincere love of the brethren, fervently love one another from the heart,

###### v23
<sup>23</sup> for you have been born again not of seed which is perishable but imperishable, [that is], through the living and enduring word of God.
