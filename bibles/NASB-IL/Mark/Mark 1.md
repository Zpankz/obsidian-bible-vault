## Mark 1:1-8

<sup>1</sup> The beginning of the gospel of Jesus Christ, the Son of God.[^1]

<sup>2</sup> As it is written in Isaiah the prophet: 'BEHOLD, I SEND MY MESSENGER AHEAD OF YOU, WHO WILL PREPARE YOUR WAY;[^2][^3][^4]

<sup>3</sup> THE VOICE OF ONE CRYING IN THE WILDERNESS, 'MAKE READY THE WAY OF THE LORD, MAKE HIS PATHS STRAIGHT.''[^5][^6]

<sup>4</sup> John the Baptist appeared in the wilderness preaching a baptism of repentance for the forgiveness of sins.

<sup>5</sup> And all the country of Judea was going out to him, and all the people of Jerusalem; and they were being baptized by him in the Jordan River, confessing their sins.

<sup>6</sup> John was clothed with camel's hair and [wore] a leather belt around his waist, and his diet was locusts and wild honey.[^7]

<sup>7</sup> And he was preaching, and saying, 'After me One is coming who is mightier than I, and I am not fit to stoop down and untie the thong of His sandals.[^8]

<sup>8</sup> 'I baptized you with water; but He will baptize you with the Holy Spirit.'[^9]

<sup>12</sup> Immediately the Spirit impelled Him [to] [go] out into the wilderness.[^12]

<sup>13</sup> And He was in the wilderness forty days being tempted by Satan; and He was with the wild beasts, and the angels were ministering to Him.

## Mark 1:14-22

<sup>14</sup> Now after John had been taken into custody, Jesus came into Galilee, preaching the gospel of God,[^13]

<sup>15</sup> and saying, 'The time is fulfilled, and the kingdom of God is at hand; repent and believe in the gospel.'[^14][^15][^16][^17][^18][^19][^20][^21]

<sup>16</sup> As He was going along by the Sea of Galilee, He saw Simon and Andrew, the brother of Simon, casting a net in the sea; for they were fishermen.[^22]

<sup>17</sup> And Jesus said to them, 'Follow Me, and I will make you become fishers of men.'

<sup>18</sup> Immediately they left their nets and followed Him.

<sup>19</sup> Going on a little farther, He saw James the son of Zebedee, and John his brother, who were also in the boat mending the nets.

<sup>20</sup> Immediately He called them; and they left their father Zebedee in the boat with the hired servants, and went away to follow Him.

<sup>21</sup> They went into Capernaum; and immediately on the Sabbath He entered the synagogue and [began] to teach.

<sup>22</sup> They were amazed at His teaching; for He was teaching them as [one] having authority, and not as the scribes.
