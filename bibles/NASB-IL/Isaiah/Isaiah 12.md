# Isaiah 12

## Isaiah 12:1-3

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> Then you will say on that day, 'I will give thanks to You, O LORD; For although You were angry with me, Your anger is turned away, And You comfort me.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> 'Behold, God is my salvation, I will trust and not be afraid; For the LORD GOD is my strength and song, And He has become my salvation.'

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> Therefore you will joyously draw water From the springs of salvation.

## Isaiah 12:4-6

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> And in that day you will say, 'Give thanks to the LORD, call on His name. Make known His deeds among the peoples; Make [them] remember that His name is exalted.'

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> Praise the LORD in song, for He has done excellent things; Let this be known throughout the earth.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> Cry aloud and shout for joy, O inhabitant of Zion, For great in your midst is the Holy One of Israel.
