## Isaiah 52:1-6

<sup>1</sup> Awake, awake, Clothe yourself in your strength, O Zion; Clothe yourself in your beautiful garments, O Jerusalem, the holy city; For the uncircumcised and the unclean Will no longer come into you.[^1][^2][^3][^4][^5]

<sup>2</sup> Shake yourself from the dust, rise up, O captive Jerusalem; Loose yourself from the chains around your neck, O captive daughter of Zion.

<sup>3</sup> For thus says the LORD, 'You were sold for nothing and you will be redeemed without money.'

<sup>4</sup> For thus says the Lord GOD, 'My people went down at the first into Egypt to reside there; then the Assyrian oppressed them without cause.

<sup>5</sup> 'Now therefore, what do I have here,' declares the LORD, 'seeing that My people have been taken away without cause?' [Again] the LORD declares, 'Those who rule over them howl, and My name is continually blasphemed all day long.[^6]

<sup>6</sup> 'Therefore My people shall know My name; therefore in that day I am the one who is speaking, 'Here I am.''[^7]

## Isaiah 52:7-12

<sup>7</sup> How lovely on the mountains Are the feet of him who brings good news, Who announces peace And brings good news of happiness, Who announces salvation, [And] says to Zion, 'Your God reigns!'[^8][^9][^10][^11][^12][^13][^14][^15]

<sup>8</sup> Listen! Your watchmen lift up [their] voices, They shout joyfully together; For they will see with their own eyes When the LORD restores Zion.

<sup>9</sup> Break forth, shout joyfully together, You waste places of Jerusalem; For the LORD has comforted His people, He has redeemed Jerusalem.

<sup>10</sup> The LORD has bared His holy arm In the sight of all the nations, That all the ends of the earth may see The salvation of our God.[^16]

<sup>11</sup> Depart, depart, go out from there, Touch nothing unclean; Go out of the midst of her, purify yourselves, You who carry the vessels of the LORD.[^17]

<sup>12</sup> But you will not go out in haste, Nor will you go as fugitives; For the LORD will go before you, And the God of Israel [will be] your rear guard.[^18][^19][^20]

## Isaiah 52:13-15

<sup>13</sup> Behold, My servant will prosper, He will be high and lifted up and greatly exalted.[^21][^22]

<sup>14</sup> Just as many were astonished at you, [My people], So His appearance was marred more than any man And His form more than the sons of men.[^23][^24][^25]

<sup>15</sup> Thus He will sprinkle many nations, Kings will shut their mouths on account of Him; For what had not been told them they will see, And what they had not heard they will understand.
