# Isaiah 62

## Isaiah 62:1-5

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> For Zion's sake I will not keep silent, And for Jerusalem's sake I will not keep quiet, Until her righteousness goes forth like brightness, And her salvation like a torch that is burning.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> The nations will see your righteousness, And all kings your glory; And you will be called by a new name Which the mouth of the LORD will designate.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> You will also be a crown of beauty in the hand of the LORD, And a royal diadem in the hand of your God.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> It will no longer be said to you, 'Forsaken,' Nor to your land will it any longer be said, 'Desolate'; But you will be called, 'My delight is in her,' And your land, 'Married'; For the LORD delights in you, And [to Him] your land will be married.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> For [as] a young man marries a virgin, [So] your sons will marry you; And [as] the bridegroom rejoices over the bride, [So] your God will rejoice over you.

## Isaiah 62:6-9

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> On your walls, O Jerusalem, I have appointed watchmen; All day and all night they will never keep silent. You who remind the LORD, take no rest for yourselves;

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> And give Him no rest until He establishes And makes Jerusalem a praise in the earth.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> The LORD has sworn by His right hand and by His strong arm, 'I will never again give your grain [as] food for your enemies; Nor will foreigners drink your new wine for which you have labored.'

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> But those who garner it will eat it and praise the LORD; And those who gather it will drink it in the courts of My sanctuary.

## Isaiah 62:10-12

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> Go through, go through the gates, Clear the way for the people; Build up, build up the highway, Remove the stones, lift up a standard over the peoples.

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> Behold, the LORD has proclaimed to the end of the earth, Say to the daughter of Zion, 'Lo, your salvation comes; Behold His reward is with Him, and His recompense before Him.'

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> And they will call them, 'The holy people, The redeemed of the LORD'; And you will be called, 'Sought out, a city not forsaken.'
