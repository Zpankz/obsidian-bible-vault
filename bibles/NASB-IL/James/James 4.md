## James 4:1-10

<sup>1</sup> What is the source of quarrels and conflicts among you? Is not the source your pleasures that wage war in your members?[^1][^2][^3][^4][^5][^6][^7][^8]

<sup>2</sup> You lust and do not have; [so] you commit murder. You are envious and cannot obtain; [so] you fight and quarrel. You do not have because you do not ask.[^9][^10][^11][^12][^13][^14]

<sup>3</sup> You ask and do not receive, because you ask with wrong motives, so that you may spend [it] on your pleasures.[^15][^16][^17][^18][^19][^20]

<sup>4</sup> You adulteresses, do you not know that friendship with the world is hostility toward God? Therefore whoever wishes to be a friend of the world makes himself an enemy of God.[^21][^22][^23][^24][^25][^26][^27][^28]

<sup>5</sup> Or do you think that the Scripture speaks to no purpose: 'He jealously desires the Spirit which He has made to dwell in us'?[^29][^30][^31]

<sup>6</sup> But He gives a greater grace. Therefore [it] says, 'GOD IS OPPOSED TO THE PROUD, BUT GIVES GRACE TO THE HUMBLE.'[^32][^33][^34][^35][^36][^37][^38][^39]

<sup>7</sup> Submit therefore to God. Resist the devil and he will flee from you.[^40][^41][^42][^43][^44][^45][^46][^47]

<sup>8</sup> Draw near to God and He will draw near to you. Cleanse your hands, you sinners; and purify your hearts, you double-minded.[^48][^49][^50][^51][^52][^53][^54][^55]

<sup>9</sup> Be miserable and mourn and weep; let your laughter be turned into mourning and your joy to gloom.[^56][^57][^58][^59][^60]

<sup>10</sup> Humble yourselves in the presence of the Lord, and He will exalt you.[^61][^62][^63][^64][^65][^66][^67][^68]

## James 4:11-17

<sup>11</sup> Do not speak against one another, brethren. He who speaks against a brother or judges his brother, speaks against the law and judges the law; but if you judge the law, you are not a doer of the law but a judge [of it].[^69][^70][^71][^72][^73][^74]

<sup>12</sup> There is [only] one Lawgiver and Judge, the One who is able to save and to destroy; but who are you who judge your neighbor?[^75][^76]

<sup>13</sup> Come now, you who say, 'Today or tomorrow we will go to such and such a city, and spend a year there and engage in business and make a profit.'[^77][^78]

<sup>14</sup> Yet you do not know what your life will be like tomorrow. You are [just] a vapor that appears for a little while and then vanishes away.[^79][^80][^81][^82][^83][^84][^85][^86]

<sup>15</sup> Instead, [you ought] to say, 'If the Lord wills, we will live and also do this or that.'[^87][^88][^89][^90][^91]

<sup>16</sup> But as it is, you boast in your arrogance; all such boasting is evil.[^92][^93]

<sup>17</sup> Therefore, to one who knows [the] right thing to do and does not do it, to him it is sin.[^94][^95][^96][^97][^98][^99][^100][^101]
