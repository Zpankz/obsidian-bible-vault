## Hosea 6:1-3

<sup>1</sup> 'Come, let us return to the LORD. For He has torn [us], but He will heal us; He has wounded [us], but He will bandage us.[^1][^2][^3][^4][^5][^6][^7][^8]

<sup>2</sup> 'He will revive us after two days; He will raise us up on the third day, That we may live before Him.[^9][^10][^11][^12]

<sup>3</sup> 'So let us know, let us press on to know the LORD. His going forth is as certain as the dawn; And He will come to us like the rain, Like the spring rain watering the earth.'[^13][^14][^15][^16][^17][^18]

## Hosea 6:4-11

<sup>4</sup> What shall I do with you, O Ephraim? What shall I do with you, O Judah? For your loyalty is like a morning cloud And like the dew which goes away early.

<sup>5</sup> Therefore I have hewn [them] in pieces by the prophets; I have slain them by the words of My mouth; And the judgments on you are [like] the light that goes forth.

<sup>6</sup> For I delight in loyalty rather than sacrifice, And in the knowledge of God rather than burnt offerings.[^19][^20][^21][^22][^23][^24][^25][^26]

<sup>7</sup> But like Adam they have transgressed the covenant; There they have dealt treacherously against Me.

<sup>8</sup> Gilead is a city of wrongdoers, Tracked with bloody [footprints].

<sup>9</sup> And as raiders wait for a man, [So] a band of priests murder on the way to Shechem; Surely they have committed crime.

<sup>10</sup> In the house of Israel I have seen a horrible thing; Ephraim's harlotry is there, Israel has defiled itself.

<sup>11</sup> Also, O Judah, there is a harvest appointed for you, When I restore the fortunes of My people.
