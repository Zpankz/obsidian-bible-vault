# Hosea 8

## Hosea 8:1-7

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> [Put] the trumpet to your lips! Like an eagle [the enemy comes] against the house of the LORD, Because they have transgressed My covenant And rebelled against My law.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> They cry out to Me, 'My God, we of Israel know You!'

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> Israel has rejected the good; The enemy will pursue him.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> They have set up kings, but not by Me; They have appointed princes, but I did not know [it]. With their silver and gold they have made idols for themselves, That they might be cut off.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> He has rejected your calf, O Samaria, [saying], 'My anger burns against them!' How long will they be incapable of innocence?

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> For from Israel is even this! A craftsman made it, so it is not God; Surely the calf of Samaria will be broken to pieces.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> For they sow the wind And they reap the whirlwind. The standing grain has no heads; It yields no grain. Should it yield, strangers would swallow it up.

## Hosea 8:8-14

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> Israel is swallowed up; They are now among the nations Like a vessel in which no one delights.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> For they have gone up to Assyria, [Like] a wild donkey all alone; Ephraim has hired lovers.

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> Even though they hire [allies] among the nations, Now I will gather them up; And they will begin to diminish Because of the burden of the king of princes.

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> Since Ephraim has multiplied altars for sin, They have become altars of sinning for him.

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> Though I wrote for him ten thousand [precepts] of My law, They are regarded as a strange thing.

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> As for My sacrificial gifts, They sacrifice the flesh and eat [it], [But] the LORD has taken no delight in them. Now He will remember their iniquity, And punish [them] for their sins; They will return to Egypt.

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> For Israel has forgotten his Maker and built palaces; And Judah has multiplied fortified cities, But I will send a fire on its cities that it may consume its palatial dwellings.
