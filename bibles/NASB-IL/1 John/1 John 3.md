## 1 John 3:4-10

<sup>4</sup> Everyone who practices sin also practices lawlessness; and sin is lawlessness.[^22][^23][^24][^25][^26][^27][^28][^29]

<sup>5</sup> You know that He appeared in order to take away sins; and in Him there is no sin.[^30][^31][^32][^33]

<sup>6</sup> No one who abides in Him sins; no one who sins has seen Him or knows Him.[^34][^35][^36][^37]

<sup>7</sup> Little children, make sure no one deceives you; the one who practices righteousness is righteous, just as He is righteous;[^38][^39][^40]

<sup>8</sup> the one who practices sin is of the devil; for the devil has sinned from the beginning. The Son of God appeared for this purpose, to destroy the works of the devil.[^41][^42][^43][^44][^45][^46][^47][^48]

<sup>9</sup> No one who is born of God practices sin, because His seed abides in him; and he cannot sin, because he is born of God.[^49][^50][^51][^52][^53][^54][^55][^56]

<sup>10</sup> By this the children of God and the children of the devil are obvious: anyone who does not practice righteousness is not of God, nor the one who does not love his brother.[^57][^58]
