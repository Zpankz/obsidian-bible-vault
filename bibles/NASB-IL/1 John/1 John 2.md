<sup>7</sup> Beloved, I am not writing a new commandment to you, but an old commandment which you have had from the beginning; the old commandment is the word which you have heard.[^46][^47][^48][^49][^50][^51]

<sup>8</sup> On the other hand, I am writing a new commandment to you, which is true in Him and in you, because the darkness is passing away and the true Light is already shining.[^52][^53][^54][^55][^56][^57][^58][^59]

<sup>9</sup> The one who says he is in the Light and [yet] hates his brother is in the darkness until now.[^60][^61][^62]

<sup>10</sup> The one who loves his brother abides in the Light and there is no cause for stumbling in him.[^63][^64]

<sup>11</sup> But the one who hates his brother is in the darkness and walks in the darkness, and does not know where he is going because the darkness has blinded his eyes.[^65]

## 1 John 2:12-17

<sup>12</sup> I am writing to you, little children, because your sins have been forgiven you for His name's sake.[^66][^67][^68]

<sup>14</sup> I have written to you, fathers, because you know Him who has been from the beginning. I have written to you, young men, because you are strong, and the word of God abides in you, and you have overcome the evil one.[^71][^72][^73][^74][^75][^76][^77]

<sup>15</sup> Do not love the world nor the things in the world. If anyone loves the world, the love of the Father is not in him.[^78][^79][^80][^81][^82][^83][^84][^85]

<sup>16</sup> For all that is in the world, the lust of the flesh and the lust of the eyes and the boastful pride of life, is not from the Father, but is from the world.[^86][^87][^88][^89][^90][^91][^92][^93]

<sup>17</sup> The world is passing away, and [also] its lusts; but the one who does the will of God lives forever.[^94][^95][^96][^97][^98][^99][^100][^101]
