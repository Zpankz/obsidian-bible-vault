## 1 John 4:7-13

<sup>7</sup> Beloved, let us love one another, for love is from God; and everyone who loves is born of God and knows God.[^32][^33][^34][^35][^36][^37][^38][^39]

<sup>8</sup> The one who does not love does not know God, for God is love.[^40][^41][^42][^43][^44][^45][^46][^47]

<sup>9</sup> By this the love of God was manifested in us, that God has sent His only begotten Son into the world so that we might live through Him.[^48][^49][^50][^51][^52][^53][^54][^55]

<sup>10</sup> In this is love, not that we loved God, but that He loved us and sent His Son [to be] the propitiation for our sins.[^56][^57][^58][^59][^60][^61][^62][^63]

<sup>11</sup> Beloved, if God so loved us, we also ought to love one another.[^64][^65][^66][^67][^68][^69][^70][^71]

<sup>12</sup> No one has seen God at any time; if we love one another, God abides in us, and His love is perfected in us.[^72][^73][^74][^75]

<sup>13</sup> By this we know that we abide in Him and He in us, because He has given us of His Spirit.[^76][^77][^78][^79][^80]

## 1 John 4:17-21

<sup>17</sup> By this, love is perfected with us, so that we may have confidence in the day of judgment; because as He is, so also are we in this world.[^101][^102][^103][^104][^105][^106][^107]

<sup>18</sup> There is no fear in love; but perfect love casts out fear, because fear involves punishment, and the one who fears is not perfected in love.[^108][^109][^110][^111][^112][^113][^114]

<sup>19</sup> We love, because He first loved us.[^115][^116][^117][^118][^119][^120][^121][^122]

<sup>20</sup> If someone says, 'I love God,' and hates his brother, he is a liar; for the one who does not love his brother whom he has seen, cannot love God whom he has not seen.[^123][^124][^125][^126][^127][^128]

<sup>21</sup> And this commandment we have from Him, that the one who loves God should love his brother also.[^129][^130][^131][^132][^133][^134]
