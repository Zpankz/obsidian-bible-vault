# Nahum 2

## Nahum 2:1-10

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> The one who scatters has come up against you. Man the fortress, watch the road; Strengthen your back, summon all [your] strength.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> For the LORD will restore the splendor of Jacob Like the splendor of Israel, Even though devastators have devastated them And destroyed their vine branches.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> The shields of his mighty men are [colored] red, The warriors are dressed in scarlet, The chariots are [enveloped] in flashing steel When he is prepared [to march], And the cypress [spears] are brandished.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> The chariots race madly in the streets, They rush wildly in the squares, Their appearance is like torches, They dash to and fro like lightning flashes.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> He remembers his nobles; They stumble in their march, They hurry to her wall, And the mantelet is set up.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> The gates of the rivers are opened And the palace is dissolved.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> It is fixed: She is stripped, she is carried away, And her handmaids are moaning like the sound of doves, Beating on their breasts.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> Though Nineveh [was] like a pool of water throughout her days, Now they are fleeing; 'Stop, stop,' But no one turns back.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> Plunder the silver! Plunder the gold! For there is no limit to the treasure-- Wealth from every kind of desirable object.

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> She is emptied! Yes, she is desolate and waste! Hearts are melting and knees knocking! Also anguish is in the whole body And all their faces are grown pale!

## Nahum 2:11-13

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> Where is the den of the lions And the feeding place of the young lions, Where the lion, lioness and lion's cub prowled, With nothing to disturb [them]?

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> The lion tore enough for his cubs, Killed [enough] for his lionesses, And filled his lairs with prey And his dens with torn flesh.

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> 'Behold, I am against you,' declares the LORD of hosts. 'I will burn up her chariots in smoke, a sword will devour your young lions; I will cut off your prey from the land, and no longer will the voice of your messengers be heard.'
