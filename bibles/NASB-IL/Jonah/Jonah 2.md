## Jonah 2:1-9

<sup>1</sup> Then Jonah prayed to the LORD his God from the stomach of the fish,[^1][^2][^3][^4]

<sup>2</sup> and he said, 'I called out of my distress to the LORD, And He answered me. I cried for help from the depth of Sheol; You heard my voice.[^5][^6][^7][^8]

<sup>3</sup> 'For You had cast me into the deep, Into the heart of the seas, And the current engulfed me. All Your breakers and billows passed over me.[^9][^10]

<sup>4</sup> 'So I said, 'I have been expelled from Your sight. Nevertheless I will look again toward Your holy temple.'[^11]

<sup>5</sup> 'Water encompassed me to the point of death. The great deep engulfed me, Weeds were wrapped around my head.[^12]

<sup>6</sup> 'I descended to the roots of the mountains. The earth with its bars [was] around me forever, But You have brought up my life from the pit, O LORD my God.

<sup>7</sup> 'While I was fainting away, I remembered the LORD, And my prayer came to You, Into Your holy temple.[^13]

<sup>8</sup> 'Those who regard vain idols Forsake their faithfulness,[^14][^15][^16]

<sup>9</sup> But I will sacrifice to You With the voice of thanksgiving. That which I have vowed I will pay. Salvation is from the LORD.'[^17][^18][^19][^20][^21]
