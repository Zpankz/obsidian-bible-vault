## 2 Thessalonians 2:3-12

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> Let no one in any way deceive you, for [it will not come] unless the apostasy comes first, and the man of lawlessness is revealed, the son of destruction,

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> who opposes and exalts himself above every so-called god or object of worship, so that he takes his seat in the temple of God, displaying himself as being God.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> Do you not remember that while I was still with you, I was telling you these things?

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> And you know what restrains him now, so that in his time he will be revealed.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> For the mystery of lawlessness is already at work; only he who now restrains [will do so] until he is taken out of the way.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> Then that lawless one will be revealed whom the Lord will slay with the breath of His mouth and bring to an end by the appearance of His coming;

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> [that is], the one whose coming is in accord with the activity of Satan, with all power and signs and false wonders,

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> and with all the deception of wickedness for those who perish, because they did not receive the love of the truth so as to be saved.

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> For this reason God will send upon them a deluding influence so that they will believe what is false,

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> in order that they all may be judged who did not believe the truth, but took pleasure in wickedness.
