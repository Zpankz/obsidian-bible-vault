# Jeremiah 45

## Jeremiah 45:1-5

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> [This] [is] the message which Jeremiah the prophet spoke to Baruch the son of Neriah, when he had written down these words in a book at Jeremiah's dictation, in the fourth year of Jehoiakim the son of Josiah, king of Judah, saying:

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> 'Thus says the LORD the God of Israel to you, O Baruch:

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> 'You said, 'Ah, woe is me! For the LORD has added sorrow to my pain; I am weary with my groaning and have found no rest.''

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> 'Thus you are to say to him, 'Thus says the LORD, 'Behold, what I have built I am about to tear down, and what I have planted I am about to uproot, that is, the whole land.'

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> 'But you, are you seeking great things for yourself? Do not seek [them]; for behold, I am going to bring disaster on all flesh,' declares the LORD, 'but I will give your life to you as booty in all the places where you may go.''
