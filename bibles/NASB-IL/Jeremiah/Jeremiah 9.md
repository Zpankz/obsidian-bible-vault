# Jeremiah 9

## Jeremiah 9:1-11

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> Oh that my head were waeyrs And my eyes a fountain of tears, That I might weep day and night For the slain of the daughter of my people!

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> Oh that I had in the desert A wayfarers' lodging place; That I might leave my people And go from them! For all of them are adulterers, An assembly of treacherous men.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> 'They bend their tongue [like] their bow; Lies and not truth prevail in the land; For they proceed from evil to evil, And they do not know Me,' declares the LORD.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> 'Let everyone be on guard against his neighbor, And do not trust any brother; Because every brother deals craftily, And every neighbor goes about as a slanderer.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> 'Everyone deceives his neighbor And does not speak the truth, They have taught their tongue to speak lies; They weary themselves committing iniquity.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> 'Your dwelling is in the midst of deceit; Through deceit they refuse to know Me,' declares the LORD.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> Therefore thus says the LORD of hosts, 'Behold, I will refine them and assay them; For what [else] can I do, because of the daughter of My people?

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> 'Their tongue is a deadly arrow; It speaks deceit; With his mouth one speaks peace to his neighbor, But inwardly he sets an ambush for him.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> 'Shall I not punish them for these things?' declares the LORD. 'On a nation such as this Shall I not avenge Myself?

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> 'For the mountains I will take up a weeping and wailing, And for the pastures of the wilderness a dirge, Because they are laid waste so that no one passes through, And the lowing of the cattle is not heard; Both the birds of the sky and the beasts have fled; they are gone.

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> 'I will make Jerusalem a heap of ruins, A haunt of jackals; And I will make the cities of Judah a desolation, without inhabitant.'

## Jeremiah 9:12-22

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> Who is the wise man that may understand this? And [who is] he to whom the mouth of the LORD has spoken, that he may declare it? Why is the land ruined, laid waste like a desert, so that no one passes through?

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> The LORD said, 'Because they have forsaken My law which I set before them, and have not obeyed My voice nor walked according to it,

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> but have walked after the stubbornness of their heart and after the Baals, as their fathers taught them,'

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> therefore thus says the LORD of hosts, the God of Israel, 'behold, I will feed them, this people, with wormwood and give them poisoned water to drink.

###### v16
<sup>16</sup> 'I will scatter them among the nations, whom neither they nor their fathers have known; and I will send the sword after them until I have annihilated them.'

###### v17
<sup>17</sup> Thus says the LORD of hosts, 'Consider and call for the mourning women, that they may come; And send for the wailing women, that they may come!

###### v18
<sup>18</sup> 'Let them make haste and take up a wailing for us, That our eyes may shed tears And our eyelids flow with water.

###### v19
<sup>19</sup> 'For a voice of wailing is heard from Zion, 'How are we ruined! We are put to great shame, For we have left the land, Because they have cast down our dwellings.''

###### v20
<sup>20</sup> Now hear the word of the LORD, O you women, And let your ear receive the word of His mouth; Teach your daughters wailing, And everyone her neighbor a dirge.

###### v21
<sup>21</sup> For death has come up through our windows; It has entered our palaces To cut off the children from the streets, The young men from the town squares.

###### v22
<sup>22</sup> Speak, 'Thus says the LORD, 'The corpses of men will fall like dung on the open field, And like the sheaf after the reaper, But no one will gather [them].''

## Jeremiah 9:23-26

###### v23
<sup>23</sup> Thus says the LORD, 'Let not a wise man boast of his wisdom, and let not the mighty man boast of his might, let not a rich man boast of his riches;

###### v24
<sup>24</sup> but let him who boasts boast of this, that he understands and knows Me, that I am the LORD who exercises lovingkindness, justice and righteousness on earth; for I delight in these things,' declares the LORD.

###### v25
<sup>25</sup> 'Behold, the days are coming,' declares the LORD, 'that I will punish all who are circumcised and yet uncircumcised--

###### v26
<sup>26</sup> Egypt and Judah, and Edom and the sons of Ammon, and Moab and all those inhabiting the desert who clip the hair on their temples; for all the nations are uncircumcised, and all the house of Israel are uncircumcised of heart.'
