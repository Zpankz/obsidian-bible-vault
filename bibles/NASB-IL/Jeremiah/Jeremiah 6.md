# Jeremiah 6

## Jeremiah 6:1-8

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> 'Flee for safety, O sons of Benjamin, From the midst of Jerusalem! Now blow a trumpet in Tekoa And raise a signal over Beth-haccerem; For evil looks down from the north, And a great destruction.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> 'The comely and dainty one, the daughter of Zion, I will cut off.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> 'Shepherds and their flocks will come to her, They will pitch [their] tents around her, They will pasture each in his place.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> 'Prepare war against her; Arise, and let us attack at noon. Woe to us, for the day declines, For the shadows of the evening lengthen!

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> 'Arise, and let us attack by night And destroy her palaces!'

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> For thus says the LORD of hosts, 'Cut down her trees And cast up a siege against Jerusalem. This is the city to be punished, In whose midst there is only oppression.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> 'As a well keeps its waters fresh, So she keeps fresh her wickedness. Violence and destruction are heard in her; Sickness and wounds are ever before Me.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> 'Be warned, O Jerusalem, Or I shall be alienated from you, And make you a desolation, A land not inhabited.'

## Jeremiah 6:9-17

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> Thus says the LORD of hosts, 'They will thoroughly glean as the vine the remnant of Israel; Pass your hand again like a grape gatherer Over the branches.'

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> To whom shall I speak and give warning That they may hear? Behold, their ears are closed And they cannot listen. Behold, the word of the LORD has become a reproach to them; They have no delight in it.

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> But I am full of the wrath of the LORD; I am weary with holding [it] in. 'Pour [it] out on the children in the street And on the gathering of young men together; For both husband and wife shall be taken, The aged and the very old.

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> 'Their houses shall be turned over to others, Their fields and their wives together; For I will stretch out My hand Against the inhabitants of the land,' declares the LORD.

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> 'For from the least of them even to the greatest of them, Everyone is greedy for gain, And from the prophet even to the priest Everyone deals falsely.

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> 'They have healed the brokenness of My people superficially, Saying, 'Peace, peace,' But there is no peace.

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> 'Were they ashamed because of the abomination they have done? They were not even ashamed at all; They did not even know how to blush. Therefore they shall fall among those who fall; At the time that I punish them, They shall be cast down,' says the LORD.

###### v16
<sup>16</sup> Thus says the LORD, 'Stand by the ways and see and ask for the ancient paths, Where the good way is, and walk in it; And you will find rest for your souls. But they said, 'We will not walk [in it].'

###### v17
<sup>17</sup> 'And I set watchmen over you, [saying], 'Listen to the sound of the trumpet!' But they said, 'We will not listen.'

## Jeremiah 6:18-30

###### v18
<sup>18</sup> 'Therefore hear, O nations, And know, O congregation, what is among them.

###### v19
<sup>19</sup> 'Hear, O earth: behold, I am bringing disaster on this people, The fruit of their plans, Because they have not listened to My words, And as for My law, they have rejected it also.

###### v20
<sup>20</sup> 'For what purpose does frankincense come to Me from Sheba And the sweet cane from a distant land? Your burnt offerings are not acceptable And your sacrifices are not pleasing to Me.'

###### v21
<sup>21</sup> Therefore, thus says the LORD, 'Behold, I am laying stumbling blocks before this people. And they will stumble against them, Fathers and sons together; Neighbor and friend will perish.'

###### v22
<sup>22</sup> Thus says the LORD, 'Behold, a people is coming from the north land, And a great nation will be aroused from the remote parts of the earth.

###### v23
<sup>23</sup> 'They seize bow and spear; They are cruel and have no mercy; Their voice roars like the sea, And they ride on horses, Arrayed as a man for the battle Against you, O daughter of Zion!'

###### v24
<sup>24</sup> We have heard the report of it; Our hands are limp. Anguish has seized us, Pain as of a woman in childbirth.

###### v25
<sup>25</sup> Do not go out into the field And do not walk on the road, For the enemy has a sword, Terror is on every side.

###### v26
<sup>26</sup> O daughter of my people, put on sackcloth And roll in ashes; Mourn as for an only son, A lamentation most bitter. For suddenly the destroyer Will come upon us.

###### v27
<sup>27</sup> 'I have made you an assayer [and] a tester among My people, That you may know and assay their way.'

###### v28
<sup>28</sup> All of them are stubbornly rebellious, Going about as a talebearer. [They are] bronze and iron; They, all of them, are corrupt.

###### v29
<sup>29</sup> The bellows blow fiercely, The lead is consumed by the fire; In vain the refining goes on, But the wicked are not separated.

###### v30
<sup>30</sup> They call them rejected silver, Because the LORD has rejected them.
