# Numbers 7

## Numbers 7:1-9

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> Now on the day that Moses had finished setting up the tabernacle, he anointed it and consecrated it with all its furnishings and the altar and all its utensils; he anointed them and consecrated them also.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> Then the leaders of Israel, the heads of their fathers' households, made an offering (they were the leaders of the tribes; they were the ones who were over the numbered men).

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> When they brought their offering before the LORD, six covered carts and twelve oxen, a cart for [every] two of the leaders and an ox for each one, then they presented them before the tabernacle.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> Then the LORD spoke to Moses, saying,

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> 'Accept [these things] from them, that they may be used in the service of the tent of meeting, and you shall give them to the Levites, [to] each man according to his service.'

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> So Moses took the carts and the oxen and gave them to the Levites.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> Two carts and four oxen he gave to the sons of Gershon, according to their service,

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> and four carts and eight oxen he gave to the sons of Merari, according to their service, under the direction of Ithamar the son of Aaron the priest.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> But he did not give [any] to the sons of Kohath because theirs [was] the service of the holy [objects, which] they carried on the shoulder.
