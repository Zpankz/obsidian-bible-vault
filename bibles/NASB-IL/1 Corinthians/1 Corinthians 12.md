# 1 Corinthians 12

## 1 Corinthians 12:1-11

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> Now concerning spiritual [gifts], brethren, I do not want you to be unaware.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> You know that when you were pagans, [you were] led astray to the mute idols, however you were led.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> Therefore I make known to you that no one speaking by the Spirit of God says, 'Jesus is accursed'; and no one can say, 'Jesus is Lord,' except by the Holy Spirit.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> Now there are varieties of gifts, but the same Spirit.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> And there are varieties of ministries, and the same Lord.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> There are varieties of effects, but the same God who works all things in all [persons].

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> But to each one is given the manifestation of the Spirit for the common good.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> For to one is given the word of wisdom through the Spirit, and to another the word of knowledge according to the same Spirit;

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> to another faith by the same Spirit, and to another gifts of healing by the one Spirit,

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> and to another the effecting of miracles, and to another prophecy, and to another the distinguishing of spirits, to another [various] kinds of tongues, and to another the interpretation of tongues.

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> But one and the same Spirit works all these things, distributing to each one individually just as He wills.

## 1 Corinthians 12:12-26

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> For even as the body is one and [yet] has many members, and all the members of the body, though they are many, are one body, so also is Christ.

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> For by one Spirit we were all baptized into one body, whether Jews or Greeks, whether slaves or free, and we were all made to drink of one Spirit.

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> For the body is not one member, but many.

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> If the foot says, 'Because I am not a hand, I am not [a part] of the body,' it is not for this reason any the less [a part] of the body.

###### v16
<sup>16</sup> And if the ear says, 'Because I am not an eye, I am not [a part] of the body,' it is not for this reason any the less [a part] of the body.

###### v17
<sup>17</sup> If the whole body were an eye, where would the hearing be? If the whole were hearing, where would the sense of smell be?

###### v18
<sup>18</sup> But now God has placed the members, each one of them, in the body, just as He desired.

###### v19
<sup>19</sup> If they were all one member, where would the body be?

###### v20
<sup>20</sup> But now there are many members, but one body.

###### v21
<sup>21</sup> And the eye cannot say to the hand, 'I have no need of you'; or again the head to the feet, 'I have no need of you.'

###### v22
<sup>22</sup> On the contrary, it is much truer that the members of the body which seem to be weaker are necessary;

###### v23
<sup>23</sup> and those [members] of the body which we deem less honorable, on these we bestow more abundant honor, and our less presentable members become much more presentable,

###### v24
<sup>24</sup> whereas our more presentable members have no need [of it]. But God has [so] composed the body, giving more abundant honor to that [member] which lacked,

###### v25
<sup>25</sup> so that there may be no division in the body, but [that] the members may have the same care for one another.

###### v26
<sup>26</sup> And if one member suffers, all the members suffer with it; if [one] member is honored, all the members rejoice with it.

## 1 Corinthians 12:27-31

###### v27
<sup>27</sup> Now you are Christ's body, and individually members of it.

###### v28
<sup>28</sup> And God has appointed in the church, first apostles, second prophets, third teachers, then miracles, then gifts of healings, helps, administrations, [various] kinds of tongues.

###### v29
<sup>29</sup> All are not apostles, are they? All are not prophets, are they? All are not teachers, are they? All are not [workers of] miracles, are they?

###### v30
<sup>30</sup> All do not have gifts of healings, do they? All do not speak with tongues, do they? All do not interpret, do they?

###### v31
<sup>31</sup> But earnestly desire the greater gifts. And I show you a still more excellent way.
