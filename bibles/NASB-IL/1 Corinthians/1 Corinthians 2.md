# 1 Corinthians 2

## 1 Corinthians 2:1-5

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> And when I came to you, brethren, I did not come with superiority of speech or of wisdom, proclaiming to you the testimony of God.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> For I determined to know nothing among you except Jesus Christ, and Him crucified.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> I was with you in weakness and in fear and in much trembling,

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> and my message and my preaching were not in persuasive words of wisdom, but in demonstration of the Spirit and of power,

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> so that your faith would not rest on the wisdom of men, but on the power of God.

## 1 Corinthians 2:6-16

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> Yet we do speak wisdom among those who are mature; a wisdom, however, not of this age nor of the rulers of this age, who are passing away;

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> but we speak God's wisdom in a mystery, the hidden [wisdom] which God predestined before the ages to our glory;

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> [the wisdom] which none of the rulers of this age has understood; for if they had understood it they would not have crucified the Lord of glory;

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> but just as it is written, 'THINGS WHICH EYE HAS NOT SEEN AND EAR HAS NOT HEARD, AND [which] HAVE NOT ENTERED THE HEART OF MAN, ALL THAT GOD HAS PREPARED FOR THOSE WHO LOVE HIM.'

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> For to us God revealed [them] through the Spirit; for the Spirit searches all things, even the depths of God.

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> For who among men knows the [thoughts] of a man except the spirit of the man which is in him? Even so the [thoughts] of God no one knows except the Spirit of God.

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> Now we have received, not the spirit of the world, but the Spirit who is from God, so that we may know the things freely given to us by God,

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> which things we also speak, not in words taught by human wisdom, but in those taught by the Spirit, combining spiritual [thoughts] with spiritual [words].

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> But a natural man does not accept the things of the Spirit of God, for they are foolishness to him; and he cannot understand them, because they are spiritually appraised.

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> But he who is spiritual appraises all things, yet he himself is appraised by no one.

###### v16
<sup>16</sup> For WHO HAS KNOWN THE MIND OF THE LORD, THAT HE WILL INSTRUCT HIM? But we have the mind of Christ.
