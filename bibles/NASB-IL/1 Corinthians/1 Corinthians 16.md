# 1 Corinthians 16

## 1 Corinthians 16:1-4

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> Now concerning the collection for the saints, as I directed the churches of Galatia, so do you also.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> On the first day of every week each one of you is to put aside and save, as he may prosper, so that no collections be made when I come.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> When I arrive, whomever you may approve, I will send them with letters to carry your gift to Jerusalem;

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> and if it is fitting for me to go also, they will go with me.

## 1 Corinthians 16:13-18

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> Be on the alert, stand firm in the faith, act like men, be strong.

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> Let all that you do be done in love.

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> Now I urge you, brethren (you know the household of Stephanas, that they were the first fruits of Achaia, and that they have devoted themselves for ministry to the saints),

###### v16
<sup>16</sup> that you also be in subjection to such men and to everyone who helps in the work and labors.

###### v17
<sup>17</sup> I rejoice over the coming of Stephanas and Fortunatus and Achaicus, because they have supplied what was lacking on your part.

###### v18
<sup>18</sup> For they have refreshed my spirit and yours. Therefore acknowledge such men.
