## 1 Corinthians 13:1-3

<sup>1</sup> If I speak with the tongues of men and of angels, but do not have love, I have become a noisy gong or a clanging cymbal.[^1][^2][^3][^4][^5][^6][^7][^8]

<sup>2</sup> If I have [the gift of] prophecy, and know all mysteries and all knowledge; and if I have all faith, so as to remove mountains, but do not have love, I am nothing.[^9][^10][^11][^12]

<sup>3</sup> And if I give all my possessions to feed [the poor], and if I surrender my body to be burned, but do not have love, it profits me nothing.[^13][^14]

## 1 Corinthians 13:4-7

<sup>4</sup> Love is patient, love is kind [and] is not jealous; love does not brag [and] is not arrogant,[^15][^16][^17][^18][^19][^20][^21][^22]

<sup>5</sup> does not act unbecomingly; it does not seek its own, is not provoked, does not take into account a wrong [suffered],[^23][^24][^25][^26][^27][^28][^29][^30]

<sup>6</sup> does not rejoice in unrighteousness, but rejoices with the truth;[^31][^32][^33][^34][^35][^36][^37][^38]

<sup>7</sup> bears all things, believes all things, hopes all things, endures all things.[^39][^40][^41][^42][^43][^44][^45][^46]

## 1 Corinthians 13:8-13

<sup>8</sup> Love never fails; but if [there are gifts of] prophecy, they will be done away; if [there are] tongues, they will cease; if [there is] knowledge, it will be done away.[^47][^48][^49]

<sup>9</sup> For we know in part and we prophesy in part;[^50][^51][^52][^53][^54][^55]

<sup>10</sup> but when the perfect comes, the partial will be done away.[^56][^57]

<sup>11</sup> When I was a child, I used to speak like a child, think like a child, reason like a child; when I became a man, I did away with childish things.[^58][^59][^60][^61]

<sup>12</sup> For now we see in a mirror dimly, but then face to face; now I know in part, but then I will know fully just as I also have been fully known.[^62][^63][^64][^65][^66][^67][^68][^69]

<sup>13</sup> But now faith, hope, love, abide these three; but the greatest of these is love.[^70][^71][^72][^73][^74][^75][^76][^77]
