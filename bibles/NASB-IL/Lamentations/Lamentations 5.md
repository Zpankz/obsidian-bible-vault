# Lamentations 5

## Lamentations 5:1-16

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> Remember, O LORD, what has befallen us; Look, and see our reproach!

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> Our inheritance has been turned over to strangers, Our houses to aliens.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> We have become orphans without a father, Our mothers are like widows.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> We have to pay for our drinking water, Our wood comes [to us] at a price.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> Our pursuers are at our necks; We are worn out, there is no rest for us.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> We have submitted to Egypt [and] Assyria to get enough bread.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> Our fathers sinned, [and] are no more; It is we who have borne their iniquities.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> Slaves rule over us; There is no one to deliver us from their hand.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> We get our bread at the risk of our lives Because of the sword in the wilderness.

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> Our skin has become as hot as an oven, Because of the burning heat of famine.

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> They ravished the women in Zion, The virgins in the cities of Judah.

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> Princes were hung by their hands; Elders were not respected.

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> Young men worked at the grinding mill, And youths stumbled under [loads] of wood.

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> Elders are gone from the gate, Young men from their music.

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> The joy of our hearts has ceased; Our dancing has been turned into mourning.

###### v16
<sup>16</sup> The crown has fallen from our head; Woe to us, for we have sinned!

## Lamentations 5:17-22

###### v17
<sup>17</sup> Because of this our heart is faint, Because of these things our eyes are dim;

###### v18
<sup>18</sup> Because of Mount Zion which lies desolate, Foxes prowl in it.

###### v19
<sup>19</sup> You, O LORD, rule forever; Your throne is from generation to generation.

###### v20
<sup>20</sup> Why do You forget us forever? Why do You forsake us so long?

###### v21
<sup>21</sup> Restore us to You, O LORD, that we may be restored; Renew our days as of old,

###### v22
<sup>22</sup> Unless You have utterly rejected us [And] are exceedingly angry with us.
