# Romans 15

## Romans 15:1-4

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> Now we who are strong ought to bear the weaknesses of those without strength and not [just] please ourselves.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> Each of us is to please his neighbor for his good, to his edification.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> For even Christ did not please Himself; but as it is written, 'THE REPROACHES OF THOSE WHO REPROACHED YOU FELL ON ME.'

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> For whatever was written in earlier times was written for our instruction, so that through perseverance and the encouragement of the Scriptures we might have hope.

###### v7

###### v8

<sup>10</sup> Again he says, 'REJOICE, O GENTILES, WITH HIS PEOPLE.'

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> And again, 'PRAISE THE LORD ALL YOU GENTILES, AND LET ALL THE PEOPLES PRAISE HIM.'

###### v20
<sup>20</sup> And thus I aspired to preach the gospel, not where Christ was [already] named, so that I would not build on another man's foundation;

###### v21
<sup>21</sup> but as it is written, 'THEY WHO HAD NO NEWS OF HIM SHALL SEE, AND THEY WHO HAVE NOT HEARD SHALL UNDERSTAND.'

## Romans 15:22-29

###### v22
<sup>22</sup> For this reason I have often been prevented from coming to you;

###### v23
<sup>23</sup> but now, with no further place for me in these regions, and since I have had for many years a longing to come to you

###### v24
<sup>24</sup> whenever I go to Spain-- for I hope to see you in passing, and to be helped on my way there by you, when I have first enjoyed your company for a while--

###### v25
<sup>25</sup> but now, I am going to Jerusalem serving the saints.

###### v26
<sup>26</sup> For Macedonia and Achaia have been pleased to make a contribution for the poor among the saints in Jerusalem.

###### v27
<sup>27</sup> Yes, they were pleased [to do so], and they are indebted to them. For if the Gentiles have shared in their spiritual things, they are indebted to minister to them also in material things.

###### v28
<sup>28</sup> Therefore, when I have finished this, and have put my seal on this fruit of theirs, I will go on by way of you to Spain.

###### v29
<sup>29</sup> I know that when I come to you, I will come in the fullness of the blessing of Christ.
