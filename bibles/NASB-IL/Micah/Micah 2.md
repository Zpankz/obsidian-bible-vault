# Micah 2

## Micah 2:1-5

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> Woe to those who scheme iniquity, Who work out evil on their beds! When morning comes, they do it, For it is in the power of their hands.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> They covet fields and then seize [them], And houses, and take [them] away. They rob a man and his house, A man and his inheritance.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> Therefore thus says the LORD, 'Behold, I am planning against this family a calamity From which you cannot remove your necks; And you will not walk haughtily, For it will be an evil time.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> 'On that day they will take up against you a taunt And utter a bitter lamentation [and] say, 'We are completely destroyed! He exchanges the portion of my people; How He removes it from me! To the apostate He apportions our fields.'

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> 'Therefore you will have no one stretching a measuring line For you by lot in the assembly of the LORD.

## Micah 2:6-11

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> 'Do not speak out,' [so] they speak out. [But if] they do not speak out concerning these things, Reproaches will not be turned back.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> 'Is it being said, O house of Jacob: 'Is the Spirit of the LORD impatient? Are these His doings?' Do not My words do good To the one walking uprightly?

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> 'Recently My people have arisen as an enemy-- You strip the robe off the garment From unsuspecting passers-by, [From] those returned from war.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> 'The women of My people you evict, Each [one] from her pleasant house. From her children you take My splendor forever.

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> 'Arise and go, For this is no place of rest Because of the uncleanness that brings on destruction, A painful destruction.

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> 'If a man walking after wind and falsehood Had told lies [and said], 'I will speak out to you concerning wine and liquor,' He would be spokesman to this people.

## Micah 2:12-13

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> 'I will surely assemble all of you, Jacob, I will surely gather the remnant of Israel. I will put them together like sheep in the fold; Like a flock in the midst of its pasture They will be noisy with men.

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> 'The breaker goes up before them; They break out, pass through the gate and go out by it. So their king goes on before them, And the LORD at their head.'
