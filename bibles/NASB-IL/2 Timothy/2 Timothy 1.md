# 2 Timothy 1

## 2 Timothy 1:1-5

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> Paul, an apostle of Christ Jesus by the will of God, according to the promise of life in Christ Jesus,

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> To Timothy, my beloved son: Grace, mercy [and] peace from God the Father and Christ Jesus our Lord.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> I thank God, whom I serve with a clear conscience the way my forefathers did, as I constantly remember you in my prayers night and day,

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> longing to see you, even as I recall your tears, so that I may be filled with joy.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> For I am mindful of the sincere faith within you, which first dwelt in your grandmother Lois and your mother Eunice, and I am sure that [it is] in you as well.

## 2 Timothy 1:6-14

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> For this reason I remind you to kindle afresh the gift of God which is in you through the laying on of my hands.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> For God has not given us a spirit of timidity, but of power and love and discipline.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> Therefore do not be ashamed of the testimony of our Lord or of me His prisoner, but join with [me] in suffering for the gospel according to the power of God,

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> who has saved us and called us with a holy calling, not according to our works, but according to His own purpose and grace which was granted us in Christ Jesus from all eternity,

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> but now has been revealed by the appearing of our Savior Christ Jesus, who abolished death and brought life and immortality to light through the gospel,

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> for which I was appointed a preacher and an apostle and a teacher.

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> For this reason I also suffer these things, but I am not ashamed; for I know whom I have believed and I am convinced that He is able to guard what I have entrusted to Him until that day.

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> Retain the standard of sound words which you have heard from me, in the faith and love which are in Christ Jesus.

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> Guard, through the Holy Spirit who dwells in us, the treasure which has been entrusted to [you].

###### v17
<sup>17</sup> but when he was in Rome, he eagerly searched for me and found me--

###### v18
<sup>18</sup> the Lord grant to him to find mercy from the Lord on that day-- and you know very well what services he rendered at Ephesus.
