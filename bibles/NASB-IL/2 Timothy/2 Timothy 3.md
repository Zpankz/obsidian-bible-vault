## 2 Timothy 3:1-9

<sup>1</sup> But realize this, that in the last days difficult times will come.[^1][^2][^3][^4][^5][^6][^7]

<sup>2</sup> For men will be lovers of self, lovers of money, boastful, arrogant, revilers, disobedient to parents, ungrateful, unholy,[^8][^9][^10][^11][^12]

<sup>3</sup> unloving, irreconcilable, malicious gossips, without self-control, brutal, haters of good,[^13]

<sup>4</sup> treacherous, reckless, conceited, lovers of pleasure rather than lovers of God,

<sup>5</sup> holding to a form of godliness, although they have denied its power; Avoid such men as these.[^14][^15][^16][^17][^18][^19][^20][^21]

<sup>6</sup> For among them are those who enter into households and captivate weak women weighed down with sins, led on by various impulses,[^22]

<sup>7</sup> always learning and never able to come to the knowledge of the truth.[^23][^24][^25]

<sup>8</sup> Just as Jannes and Jambres opposed Moses, so these [men] also oppose the truth, men of depraved mind, rejected in regard to the faith.[^26][^27]

<sup>9</sup> But they will not make further progress; for their folly will be obvious to all, just as Jannes's and Jambres's folly was also.

## 2 Timothy 3:10-17

<sup>10</sup> Now you followed my teaching, conduct, purpose, faith, patience, love, perseverance,[^28]

<sup>11</sup> persecutions, [and] sufferings, such as happened to me at Antioch, at Iconium [and] at Lystra; what persecutions I endured, and out of them all the Lord rescued me![^29]

<sup>12</sup> Indeed, all who desire to live godly in Christ Jesus will be persecuted.[^30][^31][^32][^33][^34][^35][^36][^37]

<sup>13</sup> But evil men and impostors will proceed [from bad] to worse, deceiving and being deceived.[^38]

<sup>14</sup> You, however, continue in the things you have learned and become convinced of, knowing from whom you have learned [them],[^39][^40][^41][^42][^43][^44]

<sup>15</sup> and that from childhood you have known the sacred writings which are able to give you the wisdom that leads to salvation through faith which is in Christ Jesus.[^45][^46][^47][^48][^49][^50][^51]

<sup>16</sup> All Scripture is inspired by God and profitable for teaching, for reproof, for correction, for training in righteousness;[^52][^53][^54][^55][^56][^57][^58][^59]

<sup>17</sup> so that the man of God may be adequate, equipped for every good work.[^60][^61][^62][^63][^64][^65][^66]
