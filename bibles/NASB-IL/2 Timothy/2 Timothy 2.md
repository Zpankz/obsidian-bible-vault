# 2 Timothy 2

## 2 Timothy 2:1-7

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> You therefore, my son, be strong in the grace that is in Christ Jesus.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> The things which you have heard from me in the presence of many witnesses, entrust these to faithful men who will be able to teach others also.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> Suffer hardship with [me], as a good soldier of Christ Jesus.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> No soldier in active service entangles himself in the affairs of everyday life, so that he may please the one who enlisted him as a soldier.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> Also if anyone competes as an athlete, he does not win the prize unless he competes according to the rules.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> The hard-working farmer ought to be the first to receive his share of the crops.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> Consider what I say, for the Lord will give you understanding in everything.

## 2 Timothy 2:8-13

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> Remember Jesus Christ, risen from the dead, descendant of David, according to my gospel,

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> for which I suffer hardship even to imprisonment as a criminal; but the word of God is not imprisoned.

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> For this reason I endure all things for the sake of those who are chosen, so that they also may obtain the salvation which is in Christ Jesus [and] with [it] eternal glory.

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> It is a trustworthy statement: For if we died with Him, we will also live with Him;

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> If we endure, we will also reign with Him; If we deny Him, He also will deny us;

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> If we are faithless, He remains faithful, for He cannot deny Himself.

## 2 Timothy 2:22-26

###### v22
<sup>22</sup> Now flee from youthful lusts and pursue righteousness, faith, love [and] peace, with those who call on the Lord from a pure heart.

###### v23
<sup>23</sup> But refuse foolish and ignorant speculations, knowing that they produce quarrels.

###### v24
<sup>24</sup> The Lord's bond-servant must not be quarrelsome, but be kind to all, able to teach, patient when wronged,

###### v25
<sup>25</sup> with gentleness correcting those who are in opposition, if perhaps God may grant them repentance leading to the knowledge of the truth,

###### v26
<sup>26</sup> and they may come to their senses [and escape] from the snare of the devil, having been held captive by him to do his will.
