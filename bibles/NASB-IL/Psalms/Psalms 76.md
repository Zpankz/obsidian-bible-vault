# Psalms 76

## Psalms 76:1-6

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> For the choir director; on stringed instruments. A Psalm of Asaph, a Song. God is known in Judah; His name is great in Israel.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> His tabernacle is in Salem; His dwelling place also is in Zion.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> There He broke the flaming arrows, The shield and the sword and the weapons of war. Selah.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> You are resplendent, More majestic than the mountains of prey.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> The stouthearted were plundered, They sank into sleep; And none of the warriors could use his hands.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> At Your rebuke, O God of Jacob, Both rider and horse were cast into a dead sleep.

## Psalms 76:7-12

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> You, even You, are to be feared; And who may stand in Your presence when once You are angry?

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> You caused judgment to be heard from heaven; The earth feared and was still

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> When God arose to judgment, To save all the humble of the earth. Selah.

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> For the wrath of man shall praise You; With a remnant of wrath You will gird Yourself.

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> Make vows to the LORD your God and fulfill [them]; Let all who are around Him bring gifts to Him who is to be feared.

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> He will cut off the spirit of princes; He is feared by the kings of the earth.
