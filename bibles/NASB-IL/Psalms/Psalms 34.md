## Psalms 34:1-10

<sup>1</sup> [A Psalm] of David when he feigned madness before Abimelech, who drove him away and he departed. I will bless the LORD at all times; His praise shall continually be in my mouth.[^1][^2][^3][^4][^5][^6][^7][^8]

<sup>2</sup> My soul will make its boast in the LORD; The humble will hear it and rejoice.[^9][^10][^11][^12]

<sup>3</sup> O magnify the LORD with me, And let us exalt His name together.[^13][^14][^15][^16][^17][^18][^19][^20]

<sup>4</sup> I sought the LORD, and He answered me, And delivered me from all my fears.[^21][^22][^23][^24][^25][^26][^27][^28]

<sup>5</sup> They looked to Him and were radiant, And their faces will never be ashamed.[^29][^30][^31][^32][^33][^34][^35][^36]

<sup>6</sup> This poor man cried, and the LORD heard him And saved him out of all his troubles.[^37][^38][^39][^40][^41][^42][^43]

<sup>7</sup> The angel of the LORD encamps around those who fear Him, And rescues them.[^44][^45][^46][^47][^48][^49][^50][^51]

<sup>8</sup> O taste and see that the LORD is good; How blessed is the man who takes refuge in Him![^52][^53][^54][^55][^56][^57][^58][^59]

<sup>9</sup> O fear the LORD, you His saints; For to those who fear Him there is no want.[^60][^61][^62][^63][^64][^65][^66][^67]

<sup>10</sup> The young lions do lack and suffer hunger; But they who seek the LORD shall not be in want of any good thing.[^68][^69][^70][^71][^72]

## Psalms 34:11-22

<sup>11</sup> Come, you children, listen to me; I will teach you the fear of the LORD.[^73][^74][^75][^76]

<sup>12</sup> Who is the man who desires life And loves [length of] days that he may see good?[^77][^78]

<sup>13</sup> Keep your tongue from evil And your lips from speaking deceit.[^79][^80][^81]

<sup>14</sup> Depart from evil and do good; Seek peace and pursue it.[^82][^83][^84][^85][^86][^87][^88][^89]

<sup>15</sup> The eyes of the LORD are toward the righteous And His ears are [open] to their cry.[^90][^91][^92][^93][^94][^95][^96][^97]

<sup>16</sup> The face of the LORD is against evildoers, To cut off the memory of them from the earth.

<sup>17</sup> [The righteous] cry, and the LORD hears And delivers them out of all their troubles.[^98][^99][^100][^101][^102][^103][^104][^105]

<sup>18</sup> The LORD is near to the brokenhearted And saves those who are crushed in spirit.[^106][^107][^108][^109][^110][^111][^112][^113]

<sup>19</sup> Many are the afflictions of the righteous, But the LORD delivers him out of them all.[^114][^115][^116][^117][^118][^119][^120][^121]

<sup>20</sup> He keeps all his bones, Not one of them is broken.[^122]

<sup>21</sup> Evil shall slay the wicked, And those who hate the righteous will be condemned.

<sup>22</sup> The LORD redeems the soul of His servants, And none of those who take refuge in Him will be condemned.[^123][^124][^125][^126][^127][^128][^129][^130]
