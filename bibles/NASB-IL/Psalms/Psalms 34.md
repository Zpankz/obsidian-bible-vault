# Psalms 34

## Psalms 34:1-10

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> [A Psalm] of David when he feigned madness before Abimelech, who drove him away and he departed. I will bless the LORD at all times; His praise shall continually be in my mouth.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> My soul will make its boast in the LORD; The humble will hear it and rejoice.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> O magnify the LORD with me, And let us exalt His name together.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> I sought the LORD, and He answered me, And delivered me from all my fears.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> They looked to Him and were radiant, And their faces will never be ashamed.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> This poor man cried, and the LORD heard him And saved him out of all his troubles.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> The angel of the LORD encamps around those who fear Him, And rescues them.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> O taste and see that the LORD is good; How blessed is the man who takes refuge in Him!

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> O fear the LORD, you His saints; For to those who fear Him there is no want.

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> The young lions do lack and suffer hunger; But they who seek the LORD shall not be in want of any good thing.

## Psalms 34:11-22

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> Come, you children, listen to me; I will teach you the fear of the LORD.

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> Who is the man who desires life And loves [length of] days that he may see good?

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> Keep your tongue from evil And your lips from speaking deceit.

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> Depart from evil and do good; Seek peace and pursue it.

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> The eyes of the LORD are toward the righteous And His ears are [open] to their cry.

###### v16
<sup>16</sup> The face of the LORD is against evildoers, To cut off the memory of them from the earth.

###### v17
<sup>17</sup> [The righteous] cry, and the LORD hears And delivers them out of all their troubles.

###### v18
<sup>18</sup> The LORD is near to the brokenhearted And saves those who are crushed in spirit.

###### v19
<sup>19</sup> Many are the afflictions of the righteous, But the LORD delivers him out of them all.

###### v20
<sup>20</sup> He keeps all his bones, Not one of them is broken.

###### v21
<sup>21</sup> Evil shall slay the wicked, And those who hate the righteous will be condemned.

###### v22
<sup>22</sup> The LORD redeems the soul of His servants, And none of those who take refuge in Him will be condemned.
