# Psalms 82

## Psalms 82:1-5

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> A Psalm of Asaph. God takes His stand in His own congregation; He judges in the midst of the rulers.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> How long will you judge unjustly And show partiality to the wicked? Selah.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> Vindicate the weak and fatherless; Do justice to the afflicted and destitute.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> Rescue the weak and needy; Deliver [them] out of the hand of the wicked.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> They do not know nor do they understand; They walk about in darkness; All the foundations of the earth are shaken.

## Psalms 82:6-8

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> I said, 'You are gods, And all of you are sons of the Most High.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> 'Nevertheless you will die like men And fall like [any] one of the princes.'

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> Arise, O God, judge the earth! For it is You who possesses all the nations.
