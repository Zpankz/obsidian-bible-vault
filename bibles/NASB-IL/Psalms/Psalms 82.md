## Psalms 82:1-5

<sup>1</sup> A Psalm of Asaph. God takes His stand in His own congregation; He judges in the midst of the rulers.[^1][^2][^3]

<sup>2</sup> How long will you judge unjustly And show partiality to the wicked? Selah.

<sup>3</sup> Vindicate the weak and fatherless; Do justice to the afflicted and destitute.

<sup>4</sup> Rescue the weak and needy; Deliver [them] out of the hand of the wicked.[^4]

<sup>5</sup> They do not know nor do they understand; They walk about in darkness; All the foundations of the earth are shaken.[^5]

## Psalms 82:6-8

<sup>6</sup> I said, 'You are gods, And all of you are sons of the Most High.[^6][^7]

<sup>7</sup> 'Nevertheless you will die like men And fall like [any] one of the princes.'

<sup>8</sup> Arise, O God, judge the earth! For it is You who possesses all the nations.[^8][^9]
