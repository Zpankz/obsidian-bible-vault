# Psalms 92

## Psalms 92:1-6

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> A Psalm, a Song for the Sabbath day. It is good to give thanks to the LORD And to sing praises to Your name, O Most High;

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> To declare Your lovingkindness in the morning And Your faithfulness by night,

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> With the ten-stringed lute and with the harp, With resounding music upon the lyre.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> For You, O LORD, have made me glad by what You have done, I will sing for joy at the works of Your hands.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> How great are Your works, O LORD! Your thoughts are very deep.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> A senseless man has no knowledge, Nor does a stupid man understand this:

## Psalms 92:7-15

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> That when the wicked sprouted up like grass And all who did iniquity flourished, It [was only] that they might be destroyed forevermore.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> But You, O LORD, are on high forever.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> For, behold, Your enemies, O LORD, For, behold, Your enemies will perish; All who do iniquity will be scattered.

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> But You have exalted my horn like [that of] the wild ox; I have been anointed with fresh oil.

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> And my eye has looked [exultantly] upon my foes, My ears hear of the evildoers who rise up against me.

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> The righteous man will flourish like the palm tree, He will grow like a cedar in Lebanon.

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> Planted in the house of the LORD, They will flourish in the courts of our God.

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> They will still yield fruit in old age; They shall be full of sap and very green,

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> To declare that the LORD is upright; [He is] my rock, and there is no unrighteousness in Him.
