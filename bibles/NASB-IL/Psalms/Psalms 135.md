## Psalms 135:1-4

<sup>1</sup> Praise the LORD! Praise the name of the LORD; Praise [Him], O servants of the LORD,[^1]

<sup>2</sup> You who stand in the house of the LORD, In the courts of the house of our God!

<sup>3</sup> Praise the LORD, for the LORD is good; Sing praises to His name, for it is lovely.

<sup>4</sup> For the LORD has chosen Jacob for Himself, Israel for His own possession.[^2]

## Psalms 135:5-14

<sup>5</sup> For I know that the LORD is great And that our Lord is above all gods.

<sup>6</sup> Whatever the LORD pleases, He does, In heaven and in earth, in the seas and in all deeps.[^3][^4][^5]

<sup>7</sup> He causes the vapors to ascend from the ends of the earth; Who makes lightnings for the rain, Who brings forth the wind from His treasuries.

<sup>8</sup> He smote the firstborn of Egypt, Both of man and beast.

<sup>9</sup> He sent signs and wonders into your midst, O Egypt, Upon Pharaoh and all his servants.

<sup>10</sup> He smote many nations And slew mighty kings,

<sup>11</sup> Sihon, king of the Amorites, And Og, king of Bashan, And all the kingdoms of Canaan;

<sup>12</sup> And He gave their land as a heritage, A heritage to Israel His people.

<sup>13</sup> Your name, O LORD, is everlasting, Your remembrance, O LORD, throughout all generations.

<sup>14</sup> For the LORD will judge His people And will have compassion on His servants.[^6]

## Psalms 135:15-21

<sup>15</sup> The idols of the nations are [but] silver and gold, The work of man's hands.

<sup>16</sup> They have mouths, but they do not speak; They have eyes, but they do not see;

<sup>17</sup> They have ears, but they do not hear, Nor is there any breath at all in their mouths.

<sup>18</sup> Those who make them will be like them, [Yes], everyone who trusts in them.

<sup>19</sup> O house of Israel, bless the LORD; O house of Aaron, bless the LORD;

<sup>20</sup> O house of Levi, bless the LORD; You who revere the LORD, bless the LORD.

<sup>21</sup> Blessed be the LORD from Zion, Who dwells in Jerusalem. Praise the LORD!
