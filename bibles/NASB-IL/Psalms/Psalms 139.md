# Psalms 139

## Psalms 139:1-6

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> For the choir director. A Psalm of David. O LORD, You have searched me and known [me].

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> You know when I sit down and when I rise up; You understand my thought from afar.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> You scrutinize my path and my lying down, And are intimately acquainted with all my ways.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> Even before there is a word on my tongue, Behold, O LORD, You know it all.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> You have enclosed me behind and before, And laid Your hand upon me.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> [Such] knowledge is too wonderful for me; It is [too] high, I cannot attain to it.

## Psalms 139:7-16

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> Where can I go from Your Spirit? Or where can I flee from Your presence?

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> If I ascend to heaven, You are there; If I make my bed in Sheol, behold, You are there.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> If I take the wings of the dawn, If I dwell in the remotest part of the sea,

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> Even there Your hand will lead me, And Your right hand will lay hold of me.

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> If I say, 'Surely the darkness will overwhelm me, And the light around me will be night,'

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> Even the darkness is not dark to You, And the night is as bright as the day. Darkness and light are alike [to You].

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> For You formed my inward parts; You wove me in my mother's womb.

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> I will give thanks to You, for I am fearfully and wonderfully made; Wonderful are Your works, And my soul knows it very well.

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> My frame was not hidden from You, When I was made in secret, [And] skillfully wrought in the depths of the earth;

###### v16
<sup>16</sup> Your eyes have seen my unformed substance; And in Your book were all written The days that were ordained [for me], When as yet there was not one of them.

## Psalms 139:17-24

###### v17
<sup>17</sup> How precious also are Your thoughts to me, O God! How vast is the sum of them!

###### v18
<sup>18</sup> If I should count them, they would outnumber the sand. When I awake, I am still with You.

###### v19
<sup>19</sup> O that You would slay the wicked, O God; Depart from me, therefore, men of bloodshed.

###### v20
<sup>20</sup> For they speak against You wickedly, And Your enemies take [Your name] in vain.

###### v21
<sup>21</sup> Do I not hate those who hate You, O LORD? And do I not loathe those who rise up against You?

###### v22
<sup>22</sup> I hate them with the utmost hatred; They have become my enemies.

###### v23
<sup>23</sup> Search me, O God, and know my heart; Try me and know my anxious thoughts;

###### v24
<sup>24</sup> And see if there be any hurtful way in me, And lead me in the everlasting way.
