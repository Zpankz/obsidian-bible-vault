# Psalms 116

## Psalms 116:1-9

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> I love the LORD, because He hears My voice [and] my supplications.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> Because He has inclined His ear to me, Therefore I shall call [upon Him] as long as I live.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> The cords of death encompassed me And the terrors of Sheol came upon me; I found distress and sorrow.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> Then I called upon the name of the LORD: 'O LORD, I beseech You, save my life!'

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> Gracious is the LORD, and righteous; Yes, our God is compassionate.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> The LORD preserves the simple; I was brought low, and He saved me.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> Return to your rest, O my soul, For the LORD has dealt bountifully with you.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> For You have rescued my soul from death, My eyes from tears, My feet from stumbling.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> I shall walk before the LORD In the land of the living.

## Psalms 116:10-19

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> I believed when I said, 'I am greatly afflicted.'

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> I said in my alarm, 'All men are liars.'

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> What shall I render to the LORD For all His benefits toward me?

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> I shall lift up the cup of salvation And call upon the name of the LORD.

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> I shall pay my vows to the LORD, Oh [may it be] in the presence of all His people.

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> Precious in the sight of the LORD Is the death of His godly ones.

###### v16
<sup>16</sup> O LORD, surely I am Your servant, I am Your servant, the son of Your handmaid, You have loosed my bonds.

###### v17
<sup>17</sup> To You I shall offer a sacrifice of thanksgiving, And call upon the name of the LORD.

###### v18
<sup>18</sup> I shall pay my vows to the LORD, Oh [may it be] in the presence of all His people,

###### v19
<sup>19</sup> In the courts of the LORD'S house, In the midst of you, O Jerusalem. Praise the LORD!
