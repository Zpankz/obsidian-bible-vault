## Psalms 147:1-11

<sup>1</sup> Praise the LORD! For it is good to sing praises to our God; For it is pleasant [and] praise is becoming.[^1]

<sup>2</sup> The LORD builds up Jerusalem; He gathers the outcasts of Israel.

<sup>3</sup> He heals the brokenhearted And binds up their wounds.[^2][^3][^4][^5][^6][^7][^8][^9]

<sup>4</sup> He counts the number of the stars; He gives names to all of them.[^10]

<sup>5</sup> Great is our Lord and abundant in strength; His understanding is infinite.[^11][^12][^13]

<sup>6</sup> The LORD supports the afflicted; He brings down the wicked to the ground.

<sup>7</sup> Sing to the LORD with thanksgiving; Sing praises to our God on the lyre,[^14]

<sup>8</sup> Who covers the heavens with clouds, Who provides rain for the earth, Who makes grass to grow on the mountains.

<sup>9</sup> He gives to the beast its food, [And] to the young ravens which cry.[^15][^16][^17]

<sup>10</sup> He does not delight in the strength of the horse; He does not take pleasure in the legs of a man.

<sup>11</sup> The LORD favors those who fear Him, Those who wait for His lovingkindness.[^18][^19][^20][^21][^22][^23][^24]

## Psalms 147:12-20

<sup>12</sup> Praise the LORD, O Jerusalem! Praise your God, O Zion!

<sup>13</sup> For He has strengthened the bars of your gates; He has blessed your sons within you.[^25][^26][^27][^28][^29][^30]

<sup>14</sup> He makes peace in your borders; He satisfies you with the finest of the wheat.[^31][^32][^33][^34]

<sup>15</sup> He sends forth His command to the earth; His word runs very swiftly.[^35]

<sup>16</sup> He gives snow like wool; He scatters the frost like ashes.

<sup>17</sup> He casts forth His ice as fragments; Who can stand before His cold?

<sup>18</sup> He sends forth His word and melts them; He causes His wind to blow and the waters to flow.

<sup>19</sup> He declares His words to Jacob, His statutes and His ordinances to Israel.[^36]

<sup>20</sup> He has not dealt thus with any nation; And as for His ordinances, they have not known them. Praise the LORD!
