# Psalms 107

## Psalms 107:1-9

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> Oh give thanks to the LORD, for He is good, For His lovingkindness is everlasting.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> Let the redeemed of the LORD say [so], Whom He has redeemed from the hand of the adversary

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> And gathered from the lands, From the east and from the west, From the north and from the south.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> They wandered in the wilderness in a desert region; They did not find a way to an inhabited city.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> [They were] hungry and thirsty; Their soul fainted within them.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> Then they cried out to the LORD in their trouble; He delivered them out of their distresses.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> He led them also by a straight way, To go to an inhabited city.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> Let them give thanks to the LORD for His lovingkindness, And for His wonders to the sons of men!

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> For He has satisfied the thirsty soul, And the hungry soul He has filled with what is good.

## Psalms 107:10-16

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> There were those who dwelt in darkness and in the shadow of death, Prisoners in misery and chains,

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> Because they had rebelled against the words of God And spurned the counsel of the Most High.

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> Therefore He humbled their heart with labor; They stumbled and there was none to help.

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> Then they cried out to the LORD in their trouble; He saved them out of their distresses.

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> He brought them out of darkness and the shadow of death And broke their bands apart.

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> Let them give thanks to the LORD for His lovingkindness, And for His wonders to the sons of men!

###### v16
<sup>16</sup> For He has shattered gates of bronze And cut bars of iron asunder.

## Psalms 107:17-22

###### v17
<sup>17</sup> Fools, because of their rebellious way, And because of their iniquities, were afflicted.

###### v18
<sup>18</sup> Their soul abhorred all kinds of food, And they drew near to the gates of death.

###### v19
<sup>19</sup> Then they cried out to the LORD in their trouble; He saved them out of their distresses.

###### v20
<sup>20</sup> He sent His word and healed them, And delivered [them] from their destructions.

###### v21
<sup>21</sup> Let them give thanks to the LORD for His lovingkindness, And for His wonders to the sons of men!

###### v22
<sup>22</sup> Let them also offer sacrifices of thanksgiving, And tell of His works with joyful singing.

## Psalms 107:23-32

###### v23
<sup>23</sup> Those who go down to the sea in ships, Who do business on great waters;

###### v24
<sup>24</sup> They have seen the works of the LORD, And His wonders in the deep.

###### v25
<sup>25</sup> For He spoke and raised up a stormy wind, Which lifted up the waves of the sea.

###### v26
<sup>26</sup> They rose up to the heavens, they went down to the depths; Their soul melted away in [their] misery.

###### v27
<sup>27</sup> They reeled and staggered like a drunken man, And were at their wits' end.

###### v28
<sup>28</sup> Then they cried to the LORD in their trouble, And He brought them out of their distresses.

###### v29
<sup>29</sup> He caused the storm to be still, So that the waves of the sea were hushed.

###### v30
<sup>30</sup> Then they were glad because they were quiet, So He guided them to their desired haven.

###### v31
<sup>31</sup> Let them give thanks to the LORD for His lovingkindness, And for His wonders to the sons of men!

###### v32
<sup>32</sup> Let them extol Him also in the congregation of the people, And praise Him at the seat of the elders.

## Psalms 107:33-43

###### v33
<sup>33</sup> He changes rivers into a wilderness And springs of water into a thirsty ground;

###### v34
<sup>34</sup> A fruitful land into a salt waste, Because of the wickedness of those who dwell in it.

###### v35
<sup>35</sup> He changes a wilderness into a pool of water And a dry land into springs of water;

###### v36
<sup>36</sup> And there He makes the hungry to dwell, So that they may establish an inhabited city,

###### v37
<sup>37</sup> And sow fields and plant vineyards, And gather a fruitful harvest.

###### v38
<sup>38</sup> Also He blesses them and they multiply greatly, And He does not let their cattle decrease.

###### v39
<sup>39</sup> When they are diminished and bowed down Through oppression, misery and sorrow,

###### v40
<sup>40</sup> He pours contempt upon princes And makes them wander in a pathless waste.

###### v41
<sup>41</sup> But He sets the needy securely on high away from affliction, And makes [his] families like a flock.

###### v42
<sup>42</sup> The upright see it and are glad; But all unrighteousness shuts its mouth.

###### v43
<sup>43</sup> Who is wise? Let him give heed to these things, And consider the lovingkindnesses of the LORD.
