# Psalms 94

## Psalms 94:1-11

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> O LORD, God of vengeance, God of vengeance, shine forth!

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> Rise up, O Judge of the earth, Render recompense to the proud.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> How long shall the wicked, O LORD, How long shall the wicked exult?

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> They pour forth [words], they speak arrogantly; All who do wickedness vaunt themselves.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> They crush Your people, O LORD, And afflict Your heritage.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> They slay the widow and the stranger And murder the orphans.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> They have said, 'The LORD does not see, Nor does the God of Jacob pay heed.'

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> Pay heed, you senseless among the people; And when will you understand, stupid ones?

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> He who planted the ear, does He not hear? He who formed the eye, does He not see?

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> He who chastens the nations, will He not rebuke, [Even] He who teaches man knowledge?

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> The LORD knows the thoughts of man, That they are a [mere] breath.

## Psalms 94:12-23

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> Blessed is the man whom You chasten, O LORD, And whom You teach out of Your law;

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> That You may grant him relief from the days of adversity, Until a pit is dug for the wicked.

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> For the LORD will not abandon His people, Nor will He forsake His inheritance.

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> For judgment will again be righteous, And all the upright in heart will follow it.

###### v16
<sup>16</sup> Who will stand up for me against evildoers? Who will take his stand for me against those who do wickedness?

###### v17
<sup>17</sup> If the LORD had not been my help, My soul would soon have dwelt in [the abode of] silence.

###### v18
<sup>18</sup> If I should say, 'My foot has slipped,' Your lovingkindness, O LORD, will hold me up.

###### v19
<sup>19</sup> When my anxious thoughts multiply within me, Your consolations delight my soul.

###### v20
<sup>20</sup> Can a throne of destruction be allied with You, One which devises mischief by decree?

###### v21
<sup>21</sup> They band themselves together against the life of the righteous And condemn the innocent to death.

###### v22
<sup>22</sup> But the LORD has been my stronghold, And my God the rock of my refuge.

###### v23
<sup>23</sup> He has brought back their wickedness upon them And will destroy them in their evil; The LORD our God will destroy them.
