# Psalms 148

## Psalms 148:1-6

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> Praise the LORD! Praise the LORD from the heavens; Praise Him in the heights!

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> Praise Him, all His angels; Praise Him, all His hosts!

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> Praise Him, sun and moon; Praise Him, all stars of light!

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> Praise Him, highest heavens, And the waters that are above the heavens!

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> Let them praise the name of the LORD, For He commanded and they were created.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> He has also established them forever and ever; He has made a decree which will not pass away.

## Psalms 148:7-14

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> Praise the LORD from the earth, Sea monsters and all deeps;

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> Fire and hail, snow and clouds; Stormy wind, fulfilling His word;

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> Mountains and all hills; Fruit trees and all cedars;

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> Beasts and all cattle; Creeping things and winged fowl;

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> Kings of the earth and all peoples; Princes and all judges of the earth;

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> Both young men and virgins; Old men and children.

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> Let them praise the name of the LORD, For His name alone is exalted; His glory is above earth and heaven.

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> And He has lifted up a horn for His people, Praise for all His godly ones; [Even] for the sons of Israel, a people near to Him. Praise the LORD!
