# Psalms 2

## Psalms 2:1-6

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> Why are the nations in an uproar And the peoples devising a vain thing?

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> The kings of the earth take their stand And the rulers take counsel together Against the LORD and against His Anointed, saying,

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> 'Let us tear their fetters apart And cast away their cords from us!'

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> He who sits in the heavens laughs, The Lord scoffs at them.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> Then He will speak to them in His anger And terrify them in His fury, saying,

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> 'But as for Me, I have installed My King Upon Zion, My holy mountain.'

## Psalms 2:10-12

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> Now therefore, O kings, show discernment; Take warning, O judges of the earth.

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> Worship the LORD with reverence And rejoice with trembling.

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> Do homage to the Son, that He not become angry, and you perish [in] the way, For His wrath may soon be kindled. How blessed are all who take refuge in Him!
