## Psalms 2:1-6

<sup>1</sup> Why are the nations in an uproar And the peoples devising a vain thing?[^1][^2][^3][^4][^5]

<sup>2</sup> The kings of the earth take their stand And the rulers take counsel together Against the LORD and against His Anointed, saying,[^6][^7][^8]

<sup>3</sup> 'Let us tear their fetters apart And cast away their cords from us!'[^9]

<sup>4</sup> He who sits in the heavens laughs, The Lord scoffs at them.[^10][^11][^12][^13][^14]

<sup>5</sup> Then He will speak to them in His anger And terrify them in His fury, saying,[^15]

<sup>6</sup> 'But as for Me, I have installed My King Upon Zion, My holy mountain.'[^16]

<sup>11</sup> Worship the LORD with reverence And rejoice with trembling.[^31][^32][^33][^34][^35][^36]

<sup>12</sup> Do homage to the Son, that He not become angry, and you perish [in] the way, For His wrath may soon be kindled. How blessed are all who take refuge in Him![^37][^38][^39][^40][^41][^42][^43][^44]
