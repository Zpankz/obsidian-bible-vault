# Psalms 131

## Psalms 131:1-3

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> A Song of Ascents, of David. O LORD, my heart is not proud, nor my eyes haughty; Nor do I involve myself in great matters, Or in things too difficult for me.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> Surely I have composed and quieted my soul; Like a weaned child [rests] against his mother, My soul is like a weaned child within me.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> O Israel, hope in the LORD From this time forth and forever.
