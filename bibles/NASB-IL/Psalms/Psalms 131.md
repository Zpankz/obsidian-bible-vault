## Psalms 131:1-3

<sup>1</sup> A Song of Ascents, of David. O LORD, my heart is not proud, nor my eyes haughty; Nor do I involve myself in great matters, Or in things too difficult for me.[^1][^2][^3]

<sup>2</sup> Surely I have composed and quieted my soul; Like a weaned child [rests] against his mother, My soul is like a weaned child within me.[^4][^5][^6][^7][^8][^9][^10]

<sup>3</sup> O Israel, hope in the LORD From this time forth and forever.[^11]
