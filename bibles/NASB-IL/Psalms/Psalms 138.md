## Psalms 138:1-5

<sup>1</sup> [A Psalm] of David. I will give You thanks with all my heart; I will sing praises to You before the gods.[^1]

<sup>2</sup> I will bow down toward Your holy temple And give thanks to Your name for Your lovingkindness and Your truth; For You have magnified Your word according to all Your name.[^2][^3][^4][^5][^6][^7][^8][^9]

<sup>3</sup> On the day I called, You answered me; You made me bold with strength in my soul.[^10][^11][^12][^13][^14][^15][^16][^17]

<sup>4</sup> All the kings of the earth will give thanks to You, O LORD, When they have heard the words of Your mouth.

<sup>5</sup> And they will sing of the ways of the LORD, For great is the glory of the LORD.

## Psalms 138:6-8

<sup>6</sup> For though the LORD is exalted, Yet He regards the lowly, But the haughty He knows from afar.[^18][^19][^20][^21][^22][^23][^24][^25]

<sup>7</sup> Though I walk in the midst of trouble, You will revive me; You will stretch forth Your hand against the wrath of my enemies, And Your right hand will save me.[^26][^27][^28][^29][^30][^31][^32][^33]

<sup>8</sup> The LORD will accomplish what concerns me; Your lovingkindness, O LORD, is everlasting; Do not forsake the works of Your hands.[^34][^35][^36][^37][^38][^39][^40][^41]
