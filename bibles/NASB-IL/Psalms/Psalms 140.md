# Psalms 140

## Psalms 140:1-7

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> For the choir director. A Psalm of David. Rescue me, O LORD, from evil men; Preserve me from violent men

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> Who devise evil things in [their] hearts; They continually stir up wars.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> They sharpen their tongues as a serpent; Poison of a viper is under their lips. Selah.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> Keep me, O LORD, from the hands of the wicked; Preserve me from violent men Who have purposed to trip up my feet.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> The proud have hidden a trap for me, and cords; They have spread a net by the wayside; They have set snares for me. Selah.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> I said to the LORD, 'You are my God; Give ear, O LORD, to the voice of my supplications.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> 'O GOD the Lord, the strength of my salvation, You have covered my head in the day of battle.

## Psalms 140:8-13

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> 'Do not grant, O LORD, the desires of the wicked; Do not promote his [evil] device, [that] they [not] be exalted. Selah.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> 'As for the head of those who surround me, May the mischief of their lips cover them.

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> 'May burning coals fall upon them; May they be cast into the fire, Into deep pits from which they cannot rise.

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> 'May a slanderer not be established in the earth; May evil hunt the violent man speedily.'

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> I know that the LORD will maintain the cause of the afflicted And justice for the poor.

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> Surely the righteous will give thanks to Your name; The upright will dwell in Your presence.
