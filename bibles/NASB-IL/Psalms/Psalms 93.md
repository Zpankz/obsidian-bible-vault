# Psalms 93

## Psalms 93:1-5

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> The LORD reigns, He is clothed with majesty; The LORD has clothed and girded Himself with strength; Indeed, the world is firmly established, it will not be moved.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> Your throne is established from of old; You are from everlasting.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> The floods have lifted up, O LORD, The floods have lifted up their voice, The floods lift up their pounding waves.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> More than the sounds of many waters, [Than] the mighty breakers of the sea, The LORD on high is mighty.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> Your testimonies are fully confirmed; Holiness befits Your house, O LORD, forevermore.
