# Psalms 106

## Psalms 106:1-5

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> Praise the LORD! Oh give thanks to the LORD, for He is good; For His lovingkindness is everlasting.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> Who can speak of the mighty deeds of the LORD, Or can show forth all His praise?

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> How blessed are those who keep justice, Who practice righteousness at all times!

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> Remember me, O LORD, in [Your] favor toward Your people; Visit me with Your salvation,

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> That I may see the prosperity of Your chosen ones, That I may rejoice in the gladness of Your nation, That I may glory with Your inheritance.

## Psalms 106:6-12

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> We have sinned like our fathers, We have committed iniquity, we have behaved wickedly.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> Our fathers in Egypt did not understand Your wonders; They did not remember Your abundant kindnesses, But rebelled by the sea, at the Red Sea.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> Nevertheless He saved them for the sake of His name, That He might make His power known.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> Thus He rebuked the Red Sea and it dried up, And He led them through the deeps, as through the wilderness.

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> So He saved them from the hand of the one who hated [them], And redeemed them from the hand of the enemy.

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> The waters covered their adversaries; Not one of them was left.

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> Then they believed His words; They sang His praise.

## Psalms 106:13-33

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> They quickly forgot His works; They did not wait for His counsel,

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> But craved intensely in the wilderness, And tempted God in the desert.

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> So He gave them their request, But sent a wasting disease among them.

###### v16
<sup>16</sup> When they became envious of Moses in the camp, And of Aaron, the holy one of the LORD,

###### v17
<sup>17</sup> The earth opened and swallowed up Dathan, And engulfed the company of Abiram.

###### v18
<sup>18</sup> And a fire blazed up in their company; The flame consumed the wicked.

###### v19
<sup>19</sup> They made a calf in Horeb And worshiped a molten image.

###### v20
<sup>20</sup> Thus they exchanged their glory For the image of an ox that eats grass.

###### v21
<sup>21</sup> They forgot God their Savior, Who had done great things in Egypt,

###### v22
<sup>22</sup> Wonders in the land of Ham [And] awesome things by the Red Sea.

###### v23
<sup>23</sup> Therefore He said that He would destroy them, Had not Moses His chosen one stood in the breach before Him, To turn away His wrath from destroying [them].

###### v24
<sup>24</sup> Then they despised the pleasant land; They did not believe in His word,

###### v25
<sup>25</sup> But grumbled in their tents; They did not listen to the voice of the LORD.

###### v26
<sup>26</sup> Therefore He swore to them That He would cast them down in the wilderness,

###### v27
<sup>27</sup> And that He would cast their seed among the nations And scatter them in the lands.

###### v28
<sup>28</sup> They joined themselves also to Baal-peor, And ate sacrifices offered to the dead.

###### v29
<sup>29</sup> Thus they provoked [Him] to anger with their deeds, And the plague broke out among them.

###### v30
<sup>30</sup> Then Phinehas stood up and interposed, And so the plague was stayed.

###### v31
<sup>31</sup> And it was reckoned to him for righteousness, To all generations forever.

###### v32
<sup>32</sup> They also provoked [Him] to wrath at the waters of Meribah, So that it went hard with Moses on their account;

###### v33
<sup>33</sup> Because they were rebellious against His Spirit, He spoke rashly with his lips.

## Psalms 106:34-48

###### v34
<sup>34</sup> They did not destroy the peoples, As the LORD commanded them,

###### v35
<sup>35</sup> But they mingled with the nations And learned their practices,

###### v36
<sup>36</sup> And served their idols, Which became a snare to them.

###### v37
<sup>37</sup> They even sacrificed their sons and their daughters to the demons,

###### v38
<sup>38</sup> And shed innocent blood, The blood of their sons and their daughters, Whom they sacrificed to the idols of Canaan; And the land was polluted with the blood.

###### v39
<sup>39</sup> Thus they became unclean in their practices, And played the harlot in their deeds.

###### v40
<sup>40</sup> Therefore the anger of the LORD was kindled against His people And He abhorred His inheritance.

###### v41
<sup>41</sup> Then He gave them into the hand of the nations, And those who hated them ruled over them.

###### v42
<sup>42</sup> Their enemies also oppressed them, And they were subdued under their power.

###### v43
<sup>43</sup> Many times He would deliver them; They, however, were rebellious in their counsel, And [so] sank down in their iniquity.

###### v44
<sup>44</sup> Nevertheless He looked upon their distress When He heard their cry;

###### v45
<sup>45</sup> And He remembered His covenant for their sake, And relented according to the greatness of His lovingkindness.

###### v46
<sup>46</sup> He also made them [objects] of compassion In the presence of all their captors.

###### v47
<sup>47</sup> Save us, O LORD our God, And gather us from among the nations, To give thanks to Your holy name And glory in Your praise.

###### v48
<sup>48</sup> Blessed be the LORD, the God of Israel, From everlasting even to everlasting. And let all the people say, 'Amen.' Praise the LORD!
