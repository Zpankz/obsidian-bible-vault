# Psalms 105

## Psalms 105:1-7

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> Oh give thanks to the LORD, call upon His name; Make known His deeds among the peoples.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> Sing to Him, sing praises to Him; Speak of all His wonders.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> Glory in His holy name; Let the heart of those who seek the LORD be glad.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> Seek the LORD and His strength; Seek His face continually.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> Remember His wonders which He has done, His marvels and the judgments uttered by His mouth,

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> O seed of Abraham, His servant, O sons of Jacob, His chosen ones!

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> He is the LORD our God; His judgments are in all the earth.

## Psalms 105:8-24

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> He has remembered His covenant forever, The word which He commanded to a thousand generations,

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> [The] [covenant] which He made with Abraham, And His oath to Isaac.

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> Then He confirmed it to Jacob for a statute, To Israel as an everlasting covenant,

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> Saying, 'To you I will give the land of Canaan As the portion of your inheritance,'

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> When they were only a few men in number, Very few, and strangers in it.

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> And they wandered about from nation to nation, From [one] kingdom to another people.

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> He permitted no man to oppress them, And He reproved kings for their sakes:

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> 'Do not touch My anointed ones, And do My prophets no harm.'

###### v16
<sup>16</sup> And He called for a famine upon the land; He broke the whole staff of bread.

###### v17
<sup>17</sup> He sent a man before them, Joseph, [who] was sold as a slave.

###### v18
<sup>18</sup> They afflicted his feet with fetters, He himself was laid in irons;

###### v19
<sup>19</sup> Until the time that his word came to pass, The word of the LORD tested him.

###### v20
<sup>20</sup> The king sent and released him, The ruler of peoples, and set him free.

###### v21
<sup>21</sup> He made him lord of his house And ruler over all his possessions,

###### v22
<sup>22</sup> To imprison his princes at will, That he might teach his elders wisdom.

###### v23
<sup>23</sup> Israel also came into Egypt; Thus Jacob sojourned in the land of Ham.

###### v24
<sup>24</sup> And He caused His people to be very fruitful, And made them stronger than their adversaries.

## Psalms 105:25-45

###### v25
<sup>25</sup> He turned their heart to hate His people, To deal craftily with His servants.

###### v26
<sup>26</sup> He sent Moses His servant, [And] Aaron, whom He had chosen.

###### v27
<sup>27</sup> They performed His wondrous acts among them, And miracles in the land of Ham.

###### v28
<sup>28</sup> He sent darkness and made [it] dark; And they did not rebel against His words.

###### v29
<sup>29</sup> He turned their waters into blood And caused their fish to die.

###### v30
<sup>30</sup> Their land swarmed with frogs [Even] in the chambers of their kings.

###### v31
<sup>31</sup> He spoke, and there came a swarm of flies [And] gnats in all their territory.

###### v32
<sup>32</sup> He gave them hail for rain, [And] flaming fire in their land.

###### v33
<sup>33</sup> He struck down their vines also and their fig trees, And shattered the trees of their territory.

###### v34
<sup>34</sup> He spoke, and locusts came, And young locusts, even without number,

###### v35
<sup>35</sup> And ate up all vegetation in their land, And ate up the fruit of their ground.

###### v36
<sup>36</sup> He also struck down all the firstborn in their land, The first fruits of all their vigor.

###### v37
<sup>37</sup> Then He brought them out with silver and gold, And among His tribes there was not one who stumbled.

###### v38
<sup>38</sup> Egypt was glad when they departed, For the dread of them had fallen upon them.

###### v39
<sup>39</sup> He spread a cloud for a covering, And fire to illumine by night.

###### v40
<sup>40</sup> They asked, and He brought quail, And satisfied them with the bread of heaven.

###### v41
<sup>41</sup> He opened the rock and water flowed out; It ran in the dry places [like] a river.

###### v42
<sup>42</sup> For He remembered His holy word [With] Abraham His servant;

###### v43
<sup>43</sup> And He brought forth His people with joy, His chosen ones with a joyful shout.

###### v44
<sup>44</sup> He gave them also the lands of the nations, That they might take possession of [the fruit of] the peoples' labor,

###### v45
<sup>45</sup> So that they might keep His statutes And observe His laws, Praise the LORD!
