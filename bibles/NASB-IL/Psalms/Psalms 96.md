# Psalms 96

## Psalms 96:1-9

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> Sing to the LORD a new song; Sing to the LORD, all the earth.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> Sing to the LORD, bless His name; Proclaim good tidings of His salvation from day to day.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> Tell of His glory among the nations, His wonderful deeds among all the peoples.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> For great is the LORD and greatly to be praised; He is to be feared above all gods.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> For all the gods of the peoples are idols, But the LORD made the heavens.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> Splendor and majesty are before Him, Strength and beauty are in His sanctuary.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> Ascribe to the LORD, O families of the peoples, Ascribe to the LORD glory and strength.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> Ascribe to the LORD the glory of His name; Bring an offering and come into His courts.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> Worship the LORD in holy attire; Tremble before Him, all the earth.

## Psalms 96:10-13

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> Say among the nations, 'The LORD reigns; Indeed, the world is firmly established, it will not be moved; He will judge the peoples with equity.'

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> Let the heavens be glad, and let the earth rejoice; Let the sea roar, and all it contains;

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> Let the field exult, and all that is in it. Then all the trees of the forest will sing for joy

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> Before the LORD, for He is coming, For He is coming to judge the earth. He will judge the world in righteousness And the peoples in His faithfulness.
