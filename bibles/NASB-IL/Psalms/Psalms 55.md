# Psalms 55

## Psalms 55:1-8

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> For the choir director; on stringed instruments. A Maskil of David. Give ear to my prayer, O God; And do not hide Yourself from my supplication.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> Give heed to me and answer me; I am restless in my complaint and am surely distracted,

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> Because of the voice of the enemy, Because of the pressure of the wicked; For they bring down trouble upon me And in anger they bear a grudge against me.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> My heart is in anguish within me, And the terrors of death have fallen upon me.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> Fear and trembling come upon me, And horror has overwhelmed me.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> I said, 'Oh, that I had wings like a dove! I would fly away and be at rest.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> 'Behold, I would wander far away, I would lodge in the wilderness. Selah.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> 'I would hasten to my place of refuge From the stormy wind [and] tempest.'

## Psalms 55:9-15

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> Confuse, O Lord, divide their tongues, For I have seen violence and strife in the city.

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> Day and night they go around her upon her walls, And iniquity and mischief are in her midst.

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> Destruction is in her midst; Oppression and deceit do not depart from her streets.

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> For it is not an enemy who reproaches me, Then I could bear [it]; Nor is it one who hates me who has exalted himself against me, Then I could hide myself from him.

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> But it is you, a man my equal, My companion and my familiar friend;

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> We who had sweet fellowship together Walked in the house of God in the throng.

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> Let death come deceitfully upon them; Let them go down alive to Sheol, For evil is in their dwelling, in their midst.

## Psalms 55:16-23

###### v16
<sup>16</sup> As for me, I shall call upon God, And the LORD will save me.

###### v17
<sup>17</sup> Evening and morning and at noon, I will complain and murmur, And He will hear my voice.

###### v18
<sup>18</sup> He will redeem my soul in peace from the battle [which is] against me, For they are many [who strive] with me.

###### v19
<sup>19</sup> God will hear and answer them-- Even the one who sits enthroned from of old-- Selah. With whom there is no change, And who do not fear God.

###### v20
<sup>20</sup> He has put forth his hands against those who were at peace with him; He has violated his covenant.

###### v21
<sup>21</sup> His speech was smoother than butter, But his heart was war; His words were softer than oil, Yet they were drawn swords.

###### v22
<sup>22</sup> Cast your burden upon the LORD and He will sustain you; He will never allow the righteous to be shaken.

###### v23
<sup>23</sup> But You, O God, will bring them down to the pit of destruction; Men of bloodshed and deceit will not live out half their days. But I will trust in You.
