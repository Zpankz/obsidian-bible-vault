## Psalms 23:1-6

<sup>1</sup> A Psalm of David. The LORD is my shepherd, I shall not want.[^1][^2][^3][^4][^5][^6][^7][^8]

<sup>2</sup> He makes me lie down in green pastures; He leads me beside quiet waters.[^9][^10][^11][^12][^13][^14][^15][^16]

<sup>3</sup> He restores my soul; He guides me in the paths of righteousness For His name's sake.[^17][^18][^19][^20][^21][^22][^23][^24]

<sup>4</sup> Even though I walk through the valley of the shadow of death, I fear no evil, for You are with me; Your rod and Your staff, they comfort me.[^25][^26][^27][^28][^29][^30][^31][^32]

<sup>5</sup> You prepare a table before me in the presence of my enemies; You have anointed my head with oil; My cup overflows.[^33][^34][^35][^36][^37][^38][^39][^40]

<sup>6</sup> Surely goodness and lovingkindness will follow me all the days of my life, And I will dwell in the house of the LORD forever.[^41][^42][^43][^44][^45][^46][^47][^48]
