# Psalms 56

## Psalms 56:1-7

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> For the choir director; according to Jonath elem rehokim. A Mikhtam of David, when the Philistines seized him in Gath. Be gracious to me, O God, for man has trampled upon me; Fighting all day long he oppresses me.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> My foes have trampled upon me all day long, For they are many who fight proudly against me.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> When I am afraid, I will put my trust in You.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> In God, whose word I praise, In God I have put my trust; I shall not be afraid. What can [mere] man do to me?

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> All day long they distort my words; All their thoughts are against me for evil.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> They attack, they lurk, They watch my steps, As they have waited [to take] my life.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> Because of wickedness, cast them forth, In anger put down the peoples, O God!

## Psalms 56:8-13

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> You have taken account of my wanderings; Put my tears in Your bottle. Are [they] not in Your book?

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> Then my enemies will turn back in the day when I call; This I know, that God is for me.

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> In God, [whose] word I praise, In the LORD, [whose] word I praise,

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> In God I have put my trust, I shall not be afraid. What can man do to me?

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> Your vows are [binding] upon me, O God; I will render thank offerings to You.

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> For You have delivered my soul from death, Indeed my feet from stumbling, So that I may walk before God In the light of the living.
