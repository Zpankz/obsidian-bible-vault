# Psalms 59

## Psalms 59:1-7

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> For the choir director; [set to] Al-tashheth. A Mikhtam of David, when Saul sent [men] and they watched the house in order to kill him. Deliver me from my enemies, O my God; Set me [securely] on high away from those who rise up against me.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> Deliver me from those who do iniquity And save me from men of bloodshed.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> For behold, they have set an ambush for my life; Fierce men launch an attack against me, Not for my transgression nor for my sin, O LORD,

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> For no guilt of [mine], they run and set themselves against me. Arouse Yourself to help me, and see!

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> You, O LORD God of hosts, the God of Israel, Awake to punish all the nations; Do not be gracious to any [who are] treacherous in iniquity. Selah.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> They return at evening, they howl like a dog, And go around the city.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> Behold, they belch forth with their mouth; Swords are in their lips, For, [they say], 'Who hears?'

## Psalms 59:8-17

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> But You, O LORD, laugh at them; You scoff at all the nations.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> [Because of] his strength I will watch for You, For God is my stronghold.

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> My God in His lovingkindness will meet me; God will let me look [triumphantly] upon my foes.

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> Do not slay them, or my people will forget; Scatter them by Your power, and bring them down, O Lord, our shield.

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> [On account of] the sin of their mouth [and] the words of their lips, Let them even be caught in their pride, And on account of curses and lies which they utter.

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> Destroy [them] in wrath, destroy [them] that they may be no more; That [men] may know that God rules in Jacob To the ends of the earth. Selah.

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> They return at evening, they howl like a dog, And go around the city.

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> They wander about for food And growl if they are not satisfied.

###### v16
<sup>16</sup> But as for me, I shall sing of Your strength; Yes, I shall joyfully sing of Your lovingkindness in the morning, For You have been my stronghold And a refuge in the day of my distress.

###### v17
<sup>17</sup> O my strength, I will sing praises to You; For God is my stronghold, the God who shows me lovingkindness.
