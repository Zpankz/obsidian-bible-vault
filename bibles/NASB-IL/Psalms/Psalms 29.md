# Psalms 29

## Psalms 29:1-11

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> A Psalm of David. Ascribe to the LORD, O sons of the mighty, Ascribe to the LORD glory and strength.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> Ascribe to the LORD the glory due to His name; Worship the LORD in holy array.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> The voice of the LORD is upon the waters; The God of glory thunders, The LORD is over many waters.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> The voice of the LORD is powerful, The voice of the LORD is majestic.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> The voice of the LORD breaks the cedars; Yes, the LORD breaks in pieces the cedars of Lebanon.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> He makes Lebanon skip like a calf, And Sirion like a young wild ox.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> The voice of the LORD hews out flames of fire.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> The voice of the LORD shakes the wilderness; The LORD shakes the wilderness of Kadesh.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> The voice of the LORD makes the deer to calve And strips the forests bare; And in His temple everything says, 'Glory!'

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> The LORD sat [as King] at the flood; Yes, the LORD sits as King forever.

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> The LORD will give strength to His people; The LORD will bless His people with peace.
