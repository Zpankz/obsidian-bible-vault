# Psalms 50

## Psalms 50:1-6

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> A Psalm of Asaph. The Mighty One, God, the LORD, has spoken, And summoned the earth from the rising of the sun to its setting.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> Out of Zion, the perfection of beauty, God has shone forth.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> May our God come and not keep silence; Fire devours before Him, And it is very tempestuous around Him.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> He summons the heavens above, And the earth, to judge His people:

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> 'Gather My godly ones to Me, Those who have made a covenant with Me by sacrifice.'

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> And the heavens declare His righteousness, For God Himself is judge. Selah.

## Psalms 50:7-15

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> 'Hear, O My people, and I will speak; O Israel, I will testify against you; I am God, your God.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> 'I do not reprove you for your sacrifices, And your burnt offerings are continually before Me.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> 'I shall take no young bull out of your house Nor male goats out of your folds.

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> 'For every beast of the forest is Mine, The cattle on a thousand hills.

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> 'I know every bird of the mountains, And everything that moves in the field is Mine.

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> 'If I were hungry I would not tell you, For the world is Mine, and all it contains.

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> 'Shall I eat the flesh of bulls Or drink the blood of male goats?

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> 'Offer to God a sacrifice of thanksgiving And pay your vows to the Most High;

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> Call upon Me in the day of trouble; I shall rescue you, and you will honor Me.'

## Psalms 50:16-23

###### v16
<sup>16</sup> But to the wicked God says, 'What right have you to tell of My statutes And to take My covenant in your mouth?

###### v17
<sup>17</sup> 'For you hate discipline, And you cast My words behind you.

###### v18
<sup>18</sup> 'When you see a thief, you are pleased with him, And you associate with adulterers.

###### v19
<sup>19</sup> 'You let your mouth loose in evil And your tongue frames deceit.

###### v20
<sup>20</sup> 'You sit and speak against your brother; You slander your own mother's son.

###### v21
<sup>21</sup> 'These things you have done and I kept silence; You thought that I was just like you; I will reprove you and state [the case] in order before your eyes.

###### v22
<sup>22</sup> 'Now consider this, you who forget God, Or I will tear [you] in pieces, and there will be none to deliver.

###### v23
<sup>23</sup> 'He who offers a sacrifice of thanksgiving honors Me; And to him who orders [his] way [aright] I shall show the salvation of God.'
