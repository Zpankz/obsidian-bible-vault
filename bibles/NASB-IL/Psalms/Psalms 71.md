# Psalms 71

## Psalms 71:1-13

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> In You, O LORD, I have taken refuge; Let me never be ashamed.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> In Your righteousness deliver me and rescue me; Incline Your ear to me and save me.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> Be to me a rock of habitation to which I may continually come; You have given commandment to save me, For You are my rock and my fortress.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> Rescue me, O my God, out of the hand of the wicked, Out of the grasp of the wrongdoer and ruthless man,

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> For You are my hope; O Lord GOD, [You are] my confidence from my youth.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> By You I have been sustained from [my] birth; You are He who took me from my mother's womb; My praise is continually of You.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> I have become a marvel to many, For You are my strong refuge.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> My mouth is filled with Your praise And with Your glory all day long.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> Do not cast me off in the time of old age; Do not forsake me when my strength fails.

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> For my enemies have spoken against me; And those who watch for my life have consulted together,

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> Saying, 'God has forsaken him; Pursue and seize him, for there is no one to deliver.'

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> O God, do not be far from me; O my God, hasten to my help!

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> Let those who are adversaries of my soul be ashamed [and] consumed; Let them be covered with reproach and dishonor, who seek to injure me.

## Psalms 71:14-24

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> But as for me, I will hope continually, And will praise You yet more and more.

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> My mouth shall tell of Your righteousness [And] of Your salvation all day long; For I do not know the sum [of them].

###### v16
<sup>16</sup> I will come with the mighty deeds of the Lord GOD; I will make mention of Your righteousness, Yours alone.

###### v17
<sup>17</sup> O God, You have taught me from my youth, And I still declare Your wondrous deeds.

###### v18
<sup>18</sup> And even when [I am] old and gray, O God, do not forsake me, Until I declare Your strength to [this] generation, Your power to all who are to come.

###### v19
<sup>19</sup> For Your righteousness, O God, [reaches] to the heavens, You who have done great things; O God, who is like You?

###### v20
<sup>20</sup> You who have shown me many troubles and distresses Will revive me again, And will bring me up again from the depths of the earth.

###### v21
<sup>21</sup> May You increase my greatness And turn [to] comfort me.

###### v22
<sup>22</sup> I will also praise You with a harp, [Even] Your truth, O my God; To You I will sing praises with the lyre, O Holy One of Israel.

###### v23
<sup>23</sup> My lips will shout for joy when I sing praises to You; And my soul, which You have redeemed.

###### v24
<sup>24</sup> My tongue also will utter Your righteousness all day long; For they are ashamed, for they are humiliated who seek my hurt.
