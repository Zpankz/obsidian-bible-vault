# Psalms 101

## Psalms 101:1-8

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> A Psalm of David. I will sing of lovingkindness and justice, To You, O LORD, I will sing praises.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> I will give heed to the blameless way. When will You come to me? I will walk within my house in the integrity of my heart.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> I will set no worthless thing before my eyes; I hate the work of those who fall away; It shall not fasten its grip on me.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> A perverse heart shall depart from me; I will know no evil.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> Whoever secretly slanders his neighbor, him I will destroy; No one who has a haughty look and an arrogant heart will I endure.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> My eyes shall be upon the faithful of the land, that they may dwell with me; He who walks in a blameless way is the one who will minister to me.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> He who practices deceit shall not dwell within my house; He who speaks falsehood shall not maintain his position before me.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> Every morning I will destroy all the wicked of the land, So as to cut off from the city of the LORD all those who do iniquity.
