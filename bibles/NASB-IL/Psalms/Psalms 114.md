## Psalms 114:1-8

<sup>1</sup> When Israel went forth from Egypt, The house of Jacob from a people of strange language,

<sup>2</sup> Judah became His sanctuary, Israel, His dominion.

<sup>3</sup> The sea looked and fled; The Jordan turned back.[^1]

<sup>4</sup> The mountains skipped like rams, The hills, like lambs.[^2][^3]

<sup>5</sup> What ails you, O sea, that you flee? O Jordan, that you turn back?

<sup>6</sup> O mountains, that you skip like rams? O hills, like lambs?

<sup>7</sup> Tremble, O earth, before the Lord, Before the God of Jacob,[^4]

<sup>8</sup> Who turned the rock into a pool of water, The flint into a fountain of water.[^5][^6][^7]
