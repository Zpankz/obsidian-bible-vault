# Psalms 114

## Psalms 114:1-8

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> When Israel went forth from Egypt, The house of Jacob from a people of strange language,

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> Judah became His sanctuary, Israel, His dominion.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> The sea looked and fled; The Jordan turned back.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> The mountains skipped like rams, The hills, like lambs.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> What ails you, O sea, that you flee? O Jordan, that you turn back?

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> O mountains, that you skip like rams? O hills, like lambs?

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> Tremble, O earth, before the Lord, Before the God of Jacob,

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> Who turned the rock into a pool of water, The flint into a fountain of water.
