# Psalms 15

## Psalms 15:1-5

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> A Psalm of David. O LORD, who may abide in Your tent? Who may dwell on Your holy hill?

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> He who walks with integrity, and works righteousness, And speaks truth in his heart.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> He does not slander with his tongue, Nor does evil to his neighbor, Nor takes up a reproach against his friend;

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> In whose eyes a reprobate is despised, But who honors those who fear the LORD; He swears to his own hurt and does not change;

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> He does not put out his money at interest, Nor does he take a bribe against the innocent. He who does these things will never be shaken.
