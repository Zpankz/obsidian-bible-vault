## Psalms 15:1-5

<sup>1</sup> A Psalm of David. O LORD, who may abide in Your tent? Who may dwell on Your holy hill?[^1][^2][^3][^4][^5][^6][^7]

<sup>2</sup> He who walks with integrity, and works righteousness, And speaks truth in his heart.[^8][^9][^10][^11][^12][^13]

<sup>3</sup> He does not slander with his tongue, Nor does evil to his neighbor, Nor takes up a reproach against his friend;[^14][^15]

<sup>4</sup> In whose eyes a reprobate is despised, But who honors those who fear the LORD; He swears to his own hurt and does not change;[^16]

<sup>5</sup> He does not put out his money at interest, Nor does he take a bribe against the innocent. He who does these things will never be shaken.[^17][^18][^19]
