# Psalms 149

## Psalms 149:1-5

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> Praise the LORD! Sing to the LORD a new song, [And] His praise in the congregation of the godly ones.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> Let Israel be glad in his Maker; Let the sons of Zion rejoice in their King.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> Let them praise His name with dancing; Let them sing praises to Him with timbrel and lyre.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> For the LORD takes pleasure in His people; He will beautify the afflicted ones with salvation.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> Let the godly ones exult in glory; Let them sing for joy on their beds.

## Psalms 149:6-9

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> [Let] the high praises of God [be] in their mouth, And a two-edged sword in their hand,

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> To execute vengeance on the nations And punishment on the peoples,

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> To bind their kings with chains And their nobles with fetters of iron,

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> To execute on them the judgment written; This is an honor for all His godly ones. Praise the LORD!
