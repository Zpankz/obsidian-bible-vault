## Psalms 103:1-5

<sup>1</sup> [A Psalm] of David. Bless the LORD, O my soul, And all that is within me, [bless] His holy name.[^1][^2][^3][^4][^5][^6][^7][^8]

<sup>2</sup> Bless the LORD, O my soul, And forget none of His benefits;[^9][^10][^11][^12][^13][^14][^15][^16]

<sup>3</sup> Who pardons all your iniquities, Who heals all your diseases;[^17][^18][^19][^20][^21][^22][^23][^24]

<sup>4</sup> Who redeems your life from the pit, Who crowns you with lovingkindness and compassion;[^25][^26][^27][^28][^29][^30][^31][^32]

<sup>5</sup> Who satisfies your years with good things, [So that] your youth is renewed like the eagle.[^33][^34][^35][^36][^37][^38][^39][^40]

## Psalms 103:6-18

<sup>6</sup> The LORD performs righteous deeds And judgments for all who are oppressed.[^41][^42][^43][^44]

<sup>7</sup> He made known His ways to Moses, His acts to the sons of Israel.[^45][^46]

<sup>8</sup> The LORD is compassionate and gracious, Slow to anger and abounding in lovingkindness.[^47][^48][^49][^50][^51][^52][^53][^54]

<sup>9</sup> He will not always strive [with us], Nor will He keep [His anger] forever.[^55][^56][^57][^58]

<sup>10</sup> He has not dealt with us according to our sins, Nor rewarded us according to our iniquities.[^59][^60][^61][^62][^63][^64]

<sup>11</sup> For as high as the heavens are above the earth, So great is His lovingkindness toward those who fear Him.[^65][^66][^67][^68][^69]

<sup>12</sup> As far as the east is from the west, So far has He removed our transgressions from us.[^70][^71][^72][^73][^74][^75][^76]

<sup>13</sup> Just as a father has compassion on [his] children, So the LORD has compassion on those who fear Him.[^77][^78][^79][^80][^81][^82][^83][^84]

<sup>14</sup> For He Himself knows our frame; He is mindful that we are [but] dust.[^85][^86][^87][^88]

<sup>15</sup> As for man, his days are like grass; As a flower of the field, so he flourishes.[^89][^90]

<sup>16</sup> When the wind has passed over it, it is no more, And its place acknowledges it no longer.[^91]

<sup>17</sup> But the lovingkindness of the LORD is from everlasting to everlasting on those who fear Him, And His righteousness to children's children,[^92][^93]

<sup>18</sup> To those who keep His covenant And remember His precepts to do them.

## Psalms 103:19-22

<sup>19</sup> The LORD has established His throne in the heavens, And His sovereignty rules over all.[^94][^95][^96][^97][^98][^99][^100][^101]

<sup>20</sup> Bless the LORD, you His angels, Mighty in strength, who perform His word, Obeying the voice of His word![^102][^103][^104]

<sup>21</sup> Bless the LORD, all you His hosts, You who serve Him, doing His will.

<sup>22</sup> Bless the LORD, all you works of His, In all places of His dominion; Bless the LORD, O my soul!
