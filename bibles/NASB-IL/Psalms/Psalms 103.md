# Psalms 103

## Psalms 103:1-5

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> [A Psalm] of David. Bless the LORD, O my soul, And all that is within me, [bless] His holy name.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> Bless the LORD, O my soul, And forget none of His benefits;

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> Who pardons all your iniquities, Who heals all your diseases;

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> Who redeems your life from the pit, Who crowns you with lovingkindness and compassion;

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> Who satisfies your years with good things, [So that] your youth is renewed like the eagle.

## Psalms 103:6-18

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> The LORD performs righteous deeds And judgments for all who are oppressed.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> He made known His ways to Moses, His acts to the sons of Israel.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> The LORD is compassionate and gracious, Slow to anger and abounding in lovingkindness.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> He will not always strive [with us], Nor will He keep [His anger] forever.

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> He has not dealt with us according to our sins, Nor rewarded us according to our iniquities.

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> For as high as the heavens are above the earth, So great is His lovingkindness toward those who fear Him.

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> As far as the east is from the west, So far has He removed our transgressions from us.

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> Just as a father has compassion on [his] children, So the LORD has compassion on those who fear Him.

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> For He Himself knows our frame; He is mindful that we are [but] dust.

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> As for man, his days are like grass; As a flower of the field, so he flourishes.

###### v16
<sup>16</sup> When the wind has passed over it, it is no more, And its place acknowledges it no longer.

###### v17
<sup>17</sup> But the lovingkindness of the LORD is from everlasting to everlasting on those who fear Him, And His righteousness to children's children,

###### v18
<sup>18</sup> To those who keep His covenant And remember His precepts to do them.

## Psalms 103:19-22

###### v19
<sup>19</sup> The LORD has established His throne in the heavens, And His sovereignty rules over all.

###### v20
<sup>20</sup> Bless the LORD, you His angels, Mighty in strength, who perform His word, Obeying the voice of His word!

###### v21
<sup>21</sup> Bless the LORD, all you His hosts, You who serve Him, doing His will.

###### v22
<sup>22</sup> Bless the LORD, all you works of His, In all places of His dominion; Bless the LORD, O my soul!
