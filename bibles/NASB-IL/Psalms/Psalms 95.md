## Psalms 95:1-6

<sup>1</sup> O come, let us sing for joy to the LORD, Let us shout joyfully to the rock of our salvation.[^1][^2][^3][^4][^5][^6][^7][^8]

<sup>2</sup> Let us come before His presence with thanksgiving, Let us shout joyfully to Him with psalms.[^9][^10]

<sup>3</sup> For the LORD is a great God And a great King above all gods,[^11][^12][^13]

<sup>4</sup> In whose hand are the depths of the earth, The peaks of the mountains are His also.

<sup>5</sup> The sea is His, for it was He who made it, And His hands formed the dry land.

<sup>6</sup> Come, let us worship and bow down, Let us kneel before the LORD our Maker.[^14][^15][^16][^17][^18][^19][^20][^21]

## Psalms 95:7-11

<sup>7</sup> For He is our God, And we are the people of His pasture and the sheep of His hand. Today, if you would hear His voice,[^22][^23][^24]

<sup>8</sup> Do not harden your hearts, as at Meribah, As in the day of Massah in the wilderness,[^25][^26][^27][^28][^29][^30]

<sup>9</sup> 'When your fathers tested Me, They tried Me, though they had seen My work.

<sup>10</sup> 'For forty years I loathed [that] generation, And said they are a people who err in their heart, And they do not know My ways.

<sup>11</sup> 'Therefore I swore in My anger, Truly they shall not enter into My rest.'[^31][^32][^33][^34][^35]
