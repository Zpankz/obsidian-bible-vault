# Psalms 95

## Psalms 95:1-6

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> O come, let us sing for joy to the LORD, Let us shout joyfully to the rock of our salvation.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> Let us come before His presence with thanksgiving, Let us shout joyfully to Him with psalms.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> For the LORD is a great God And a great King above all gods,

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> In whose hand are the depths of the earth, The peaks of the mountains are His also.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> The sea is His, for it was He who made it, And His hands formed the dry land.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> Come, let us worship and bow down, Let us kneel before the LORD our Maker.

## Psalms 95:7-11

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> For He is our God, And we are the people of His pasture and the sheep of His hand. Today, if you would hear His voice,

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> Do not harden your hearts, as at Meribah, As in the day of Massah in the wilderness,

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> 'When your fathers tested Me, They tried Me, though they had seen My work.

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> 'For forty years I loathed [that] generation, And said they are a people who err in their heart, And they do not know My ways.

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> 'Therefore I swore in My anger, Truly they shall not enter into My rest.'
