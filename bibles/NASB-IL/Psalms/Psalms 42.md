# Psalms 42

## Psalms 42:1-5

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> For the choir director. A Maskil of the sons of Korah. As the deer pants for the water brooks, So my soul pants for You, O God.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> My soul thirsts for God, for the living God; When shall I come and appear before God?

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> My tears have been my food day and night, While [they] say to me all day long, 'Where is your God?'

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> These things I remember and I pour out my soul within me. For I used to go along with the throng [and] lead them in procession to the house of God, With the voice of joy and thanksgiving, a multitude keeping festival.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> Why are you in despair, O my soul? And [why] have you become disturbed within me? Hope in God, for I shall again praise Him [For] the help of His presence.

## Psalms 42:6-11

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> O my God, my soul is in despair within me; Therefore I remember You from the land of the Jordan And the peaks of Hermon, from Mount Mizar.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> Deep calls to deep at the sound of Your waterfalls; All Your breakers and Your waves have rolled over me.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> The LORD will command His lovingkindness in the daytime; And His song will be with me in the night, A prayer to the God of my life.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> I will say to God my rock, 'Why have You forgotten me? Why do I go mourning because of the oppression of the enemy?'

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> As a shattering of my bones, my adversaries revile me, While they say to me all day long, 'Where is your God?'

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> Why are you in despair, O my soul? And why have you become disturbed within me? Hope in God, for I shall yet praise Him, The help of my countenance and my God.
