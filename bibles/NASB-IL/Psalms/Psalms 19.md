## Psalms 19:1-6

<sup>1</sup> For the choir director. A Psalm of David. The heavens are telling of the glory of God; And their expanse is declaring the work of His hands.[^1][^2][^3][^4][^5][^6][^7][^8]

<sup>2</sup> Day to day pours forth speech, And night to night reveals knowledge.[^9][^10]

<sup>3</sup> There is no speech, nor are there words; Their voice is not heard.

<sup>4</sup> Their line has gone out through all the earth, And their utterances to the end of the world. In them He has placed a tent for the sun,[^11]

<sup>5</sup> Which is as a bridegroom coming out of his chamber; It rejoices as a strong man to run his course.

<sup>6</sup> Its rising is from one end of the heavens, And its circuit to the other end of them; And there is nothing hidden from its heat.

## Psalms 19:7-14

<sup>7</sup> The law of the LORD is perfect, restoring the soul; The testimony of the LORD is sure, making wise the simple.[^12][^13][^14][^15][^16][^17][^18][^19]

<sup>8</sup> The precepts of the LORD are right, rejoicing the heart; The commandment of the LORD is pure, enlightening the eyes.[^20][^21][^22][^23][^24][^25][^26][^27]

<sup>9</sup> The fear of the LORD is clean, enduring forever; The judgments of the LORD are true; they are righteous altogether.[^28][^29]

<sup>10</sup> They are more desirable than gold, yes, than much fine gold; Sweeter also than honey and the drippings of the honeycomb.

<sup>11</sup> Moreover, by them Your servant is warned; In keeping them there is great reward.[^30][^31]

<sup>12</sup> Who can discern [his] errors? Acquit me of hidden [faults].[^32][^33][^34][^35]

<sup>13</sup> Also keep back Your servant from presumptuous [sins]; Let them not rule over me; Then I will be blameless, And I shall be acquitted of great transgression.[^36][^37]

<sup>14</sup> Let the words of my mouth and the meditation of my heart Be acceptable in Your sight, O LORD, my rock and my Redeemer.[^38][^39][^40][^41][^42][^43][^44][^45]
