# Psalms 19

## Psalms 19:1-6

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> For the choir director. A Psalm of David. The heavens are telling of the glory of God; And their expanse is declaring the work of His hands.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> Day to day pours forth speech, And night to night reveals knowledge.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> There is no speech, nor are there words; Their voice is not heard.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> Their line has gone out through all the earth, And their utterances to the end of the world. In them He has placed a tent for the sun,

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> Which is as a bridegroom coming out of his chamber; It rejoices as a strong man to run his course.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> Its rising is from one end of the heavens, And its circuit to the other end of them; And there is nothing hidden from its heat.

## Psalms 19:7-14

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> The law of the LORD is perfect, restoring the soul; The testimony of the LORD is sure, making wise the simple.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> The precepts of the LORD are right, rejoicing the heart; The commandment of the LORD is pure, enlightening the eyes.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> The fear of the LORD is clean, enduring forever; The judgments of the LORD are true; they are righteous altogether.

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> They are more desirable than gold, yes, than much fine gold; Sweeter also than honey and the drippings of the honeycomb.

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> Moreover, by them Your servant is warned; In keeping them there is great reward.

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> Who can discern [his] errors? Acquit me of hidden [faults].

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> Also keep back Your servant from presumptuous [sins]; Let them not rule over me; Then I will be blameless, And I shall be acquitted of great transgression.

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> Let the words of my mouth and the meditation of my heart Be acceptable in Your sight, O LORD, my rock and my Redeemer.
