# Psalms 69

## Psalms 69:1-12

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> For the choir director; according to Shoshannim. [A Psalm] of David. Save me, O God, For the waters have threatened my life.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> I have sunk in deep mire, and there is no foothold; I have come into deep waters, and a flood overflows me.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> I am weary with my crying; my throat is parched; My eyes fail while I wait for my God.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> Those who hate me without a cause are more than the hairs of my head; Those who would destroy me are powerful, being wrongfully my enemies; What I did not steal, I then have to restore.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> O God, it is You who knows my folly, And my wrongs are not hidden from You.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> May those who wait for You not be ashamed through me, O Lord GOD of hosts; May those who seek You not be dishonored through me, O God of Israel,

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> Because for Your sake I have borne reproach; Dishonor has covered my face.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> I have become estranged from my brothers And an alien to my mother's sons.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> For zeal for Your house has consumed me, And the reproaches of those who reproach You have fallen on me.

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> When I wept in my soul with fasting, It became my reproach.

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> When I made sackcloth my clothing, I became a byword to them.

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> Those who sit in the gate talk about me, And I [am] the song of the drunkards.

## Psalms 69:13-21

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> But as for me, my prayer is to You, O LORD, at an acceptable time; O God, in the greatness of Your lovingkindness, Answer me with Your saving truth.

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> Deliver me from the mire and do not let me sink; May I be delivered from my foes and from the deep waters.

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> May the flood of water not overflow me Nor the deep swallow me up, Nor the pit shut its mouth on me.

###### v16
<sup>16</sup> Answer me, O LORD, for Your lovingkindness is good; According to the greatness of Your compassion, turn to me,

###### v17
<sup>17</sup> And do not hide Your face from Your servant, For I am in distress; answer me quickly.

###### v18
<sup>18</sup> Oh draw near to my soul [and] redeem it; Ransom me because of my enemies!

###### v19
<sup>19</sup> You know my reproach and my shame and my dishonor; All my adversaries are before You.

###### v20
<sup>20</sup> Reproach has broken my heart and I am so sick. And I looked for sympathy, but there was none, And for comforters, but I found none.

###### v21
<sup>21</sup> They also gave me gall for my food And for my thirst they gave me vinegar to drink.

## Psalms 69:22-29

###### v22
<sup>22</sup> May their table before them become a snare; And when they are in peace, [may it become] a trap.

###### v23
<sup>23</sup> May their eyes grow dim so that they cannot see, And make their loins shake continually.

###### v24
<sup>24</sup> Pour out Your indignation on them, And may Your burning anger overtake them.

###### v25
<sup>25</sup> May their camp be desolate; May none dwell in their tents.

###### v26
<sup>26</sup> For they have persecuted him whom You Yourself have smitten, And they tell of the pain of those whom You have wounded.

###### v27
<sup>27</sup> Add iniquity to their iniquity, And may they not come into Your righteousness.

###### v28
<sup>28</sup> May they be blotted out of the book of life And may they not be recorded with the righteous.

###### v29
<sup>29</sup> But I am afflicted and in pain; May Your salvation, O God, set me [securely] on high.

## Psalms 69:30-36

###### v30
<sup>30</sup> I will praise the name of God with song And magnify Him with thanksgiving.

###### v31
<sup>31</sup> And it will please the LORD better than an ox [Or] a young bull with horns and hoofs.

###### v32
<sup>32</sup> The humble have seen [it and] are glad; You who seek God, let your heart revive.

###### v33
<sup>33</sup> For the LORD hears the needy And does not despise His [who are] prisoners.

###### v34
<sup>34</sup> Let heaven and earth praise Him, The seas and everything that moves in them.

###### v35
<sup>35</sup> For God will save Zion and build the cities of Judah, That they may dwell there and possess it.

###### v36
<sup>36</sup> The descendants of His servants will inherit it, And those who love His name will dwell in it.
