## Psalms 33:1-11

<sup>1</sup> Sing for joy in the LORD, O you righteous ones; Praise is becoming to the upright.[^1]

<sup>2</sup> Give thanks to the LORD with the lyre; Sing praises to Him with a harp of ten strings.

<sup>3</sup> Sing to Him a new song; Play skillfully with a shout of joy.

<sup>4</sup> For the word of the LORD is upright, And all His work is [done] in faithfulness.[^2][^3][^4]

<sup>5</sup> He loves righteousness and justice; The earth is full of the lovingkindness of the LORD.[^5][^6]

<sup>6</sup> By the word of the LORD the heavens were made, And by the breath of His mouth all their host.[^7][^8][^9][^10][^11][^12][^13]

<sup>7</sup> He gathers the waters of the sea together as a heap; He lays up the deeps in storehouses.

<sup>8</sup> Let all the earth fear the LORD; Let all the inhabitants of the world stand in awe of Him.[^14][^15]

<sup>9</sup> For He spoke, and it was done; He commanded, and it stood fast.[^16][^17][^18][^19][^20][^21]

<sup>10</sup> The LORD nullifies the counsel of the nations; He frustrates the plans of the peoples.[^22][^23][^24][^25][^26][^27][^28][^29]

<sup>11</sup> The counsel of the LORD stands forever, The plans of His heart from generation to generation.[^30][^31][^32][^33][^34][^35][^36][^37]

## Psalms 33:12-22

<sup>12</sup> Blessed is the nation whose God is the LORD, The people whom He has chosen for His own inheritance.[^38][^39][^40]

<sup>13</sup> The LORD looks from heaven; He sees all the sons of men;[^41][^42]

<sup>14</sup> From His dwelling place He looks out On all the inhabitants of the earth,

<sup>15</sup> He who fashions the hearts of them all, He who understands all their works.

<sup>16</sup> The king is not saved by a mighty army; A warrior is not delivered by great strength.

<sup>17</sup> A horse is a false hope for victory; Nor does it deliver anyone by its great strength.[^43]

<sup>18</sup> Behold, the eye of the LORD is on those who fear Him, On those who hope for His lovingkindness,[^44][^45][^46][^47][^48][^49][^50]

<sup>19</sup> To deliver their soul from death And to keep them alive in famine.[^51][^52][^53]

<sup>20</sup> Our soul waits for the LORD; He is our help and our shield.[^54][^55][^56]

<sup>21</sup> For our heart rejoices in Him, Because we trust in His holy name.[^57]

<sup>22</sup> Let Your lovingkindness, O LORD, be upon us, According as we have hoped in You.[^58][^59]
