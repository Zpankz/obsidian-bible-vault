# Psalms 33

## Psalms 33:1-11

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> Sing for joy in the LORD, O you righteous ones; Praise is becoming to the upright.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> Give thanks to the LORD with the lyre; Sing praises to Him with a harp of ten strings.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> Sing to Him a new song; Play skillfully with a shout of joy.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> For the word of the LORD is upright, And all His work is [done] in faithfulness.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> He loves righteousness and justice; The earth is full of the lovingkindness of the LORD.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> By the word of the LORD the heavens were made, And by the breath of His mouth all their host.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> He gathers the waters of the sea together as a heap; He lays up the deeps in storehouses.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> Let all the earth fear the LORD; Let all the inhabitants of the world stand in awe of Him.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> For He spoke, and it was done; He commanded, and it stood fast.

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> The LORD nullifies the counsel of the nations; He frustrates the plans of the peoples.

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> The counsel of the LORD stands forever, The plans of His heart from generation to generation.

## Psalms 33:12-22

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> Blessed is the nation whose God is the LORD, The people whom He has chosen for His own inheritance.

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> The LORD looks from heaven; He sees all the sons of men;

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> From His dwelling place He looks out On all the inhabitants of the earth,

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> He who fashions the hearts of them all, He who understands all their works.

###### v16
<sup>16</sup> The king is not saved by a mighty army; A warrior is not delivered by great strength.

###### v17
<sup>17</sup> A horse is a false hope for victory; Nor does it deliver anyone by its great strength.

###### v18
<sup>18</sup> Behold, the eye of the LORD is on those who fear Him, On those who hope for His lovingkindness,

###### v19
<sup>19</sup> To deliver their soul from death And to keep them alive in famine.

###### v20
<sup>20</sup> Our soul waits for the LORD; He is our help and our shield.

###### v21
<sup>21</sup> For our heart rejoices in Him, Because we trust in His holy name.

###### v22
<sup>22</sup> Let Your lovingkindness, O LORD, be upon us, According as we have hoped in You.
