## Psalms 32:1-6

<sup>1</sup> [A Psalm] of David. A Maskil. How blessed is he whose transgression is forgiven, Whose sin is covered![^1][^2][^3][^4][^5][^6][^7][^8]

<sup>2</sup> How blessed is the man to whom the LORD does not impute iniquity, And in whose spirit there is no deceit![^9][^10][^11]

<sup>3</sup> When I kept silent [about my sin], my body wasted away Through my groaning all day long.[^12]

<sup>4</sup> For day and night Your hand was heavy upon me; My vitality was drained away [as] with the fever heat of summer. Selah.

<sup>5</sup> I acknowledged my sin to You, And my iniquity I did not hide; I said, 'I will confess my transgressions to the LORD'; And You forgave the guilt of my sin. Selah.[^13][^14][^15][^16][^17][^18][^19][^20]

<sup>6</sup> Therefore, let everyone who is godly pray to You in a time when You may be found; Surely in a flood of great waters they will not reach him.[^21][^22][^23]

## Psalms 32:7-11

<sup>7</sup> You are my hiding place; You preserve me from trouble; You surround me with songs of deliverance. Selah.[^24][^25][^26][^27][^28][^29][^30][^31]

<sup>8</sup> I will instruct you and teach you in the way which you should go; I will counsel you with My eye upon you.[^32][^33][^34][^35][^36][^37][^38][^39]

<sup>9</sup> Do not be as the horse or as the mule which have no understanding, Whose trappings include bit and bridle to hold them in check, [Otherwise] they will not come near to you.[^40]

<sup>10</sup> Many are the sorrows of the wicked, But he who trusts in the LORD, lovingkindness shall surround him.[^41][^42]

<sup>11</sup> Be glad in the LORD and rejoice, you righteous ones; And shout for joy, all you who are upright in heart.[^43]
