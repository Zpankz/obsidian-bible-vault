# Psalms 32

## Psalms 32:1-6

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> [A Psalm] of David. A Maskil. How blessed is he whose transgression is forgiven, Whose sin is covered!

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> How blessed is the man to whom the LORD does not impute iniquity, And in whose spirit there is no deceit!

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> When I kept silent [about my sin], my body wasted away Through my groaning all day long.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> For day and night Your hand was heavy upon me; My vitality was drained away [as] with the fever heat of summer. Selah.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> I acknowledged my sin to You, And my iniquity I did not hide; I said, 'I will confess my transgressions to the LORD'; And You forgave the guilt of my sin. Selah.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> Therefore, let everyone who is godly pray to You in a time when You may be found; Surely in a flood of great waters they will not reach him.

## Psalms 32:7-11

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> You are my hiding place; You preserve me from trouble; You surround me with songs of deliverance. Selah.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> I will instruct you and teach you in the way which you should go; I will counsel you with My eye upon you.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> Do not be as the horse or as the mule which have no understanding, Whose trappings include bit and bridle to hold them in check, [Otherwise] they will not come near to you.

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> Many are the sorrows of the wicked, But he who trusts in the LORD, lovingkindness shall surround him.

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> Be glad in the LORD and rejoice, you righteous ones; And shout for joy, all you who are upright in heart.
