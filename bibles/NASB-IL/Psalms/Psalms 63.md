## Psalms 63:1-2

<sup>1</sup> A Psalm of David, when he was in the wilderness of Judah. O God, You are my God; I shall seek You earnestly; My soul thirsts for You, my flesh yearns for You, In a dry and weary land where there is no water.[^1][^2][^3][^4][^5][^6][^7][^8]

<sup>2</sup> Thus I have seen You in the sanctuary, To see Your power and Your glory.

## Psalms 63:3-6

<sup>3</sup> Because Your lovingkindness is better than life, My lips will praise You.[^9][^10][^11][^12][^13][^14]

<sup>4</sup> So I will bless You as long as I live; I will lift up my hands in Your name.[^15][^16]

<sup>5</sup> My soul is satisfied as with marrow and fatness, And my mouth offers praises with joyful lips.[^17]

<sup>6</sup> When I remember You on my bed, I meditate on You in the night watches,[^18][^19][^20][^21][^22][^23]

## Psalms 63:7-11

<sup>7</sup> For You have been my help, And in the shadow of Your wings I sing for joy.[^24][^25][^26]

<sup>8</sup> My soul clings to You; Your right hand upholds me.[^27][^28][^29][^30]

<sup>9</sup> But those who seek my life to destroy it, Will go into the depths of the earth.

<sup>10</sup> They will be delivered over to the power of the sword; They will be a prey for foxes.

<sup>11</sup> But the king will rejoice in God; Everyone who swears by Him will glory, For the mouths of those who speak lies will be stopped.[^31]
