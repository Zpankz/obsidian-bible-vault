# Psalms 63

## Psalms 63:1-2

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> A Psalm of David, when he was in the wilderness of Judah. O God, You are my God; I shall seek You earnestly; My soul thirsts for You, my flesh yearns for You, In a dry and weary land where there is no water.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> Thus I have seen You in the sanctuary, To see Your power and Your glory.

## Psalms 63:3-6

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> Because Your lovingkindness is better than life, My lips will praise You.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> So I will bless You as long as I live; I will lift up my hands in Your name.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> My soul is satisfied as with marrow and fatness, And my mouth offers praises with joyful lips.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> When I remember You on my bed, I meditate on You in the night watches,

## Psalms 63:7-11

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> For You have been my help, And in the shadow of Your wings I sing for joy.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> My soul clings to You; Your right hand upholds me.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> But those who seek my life to destroy it, Will go into the depths of the earth.

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> They will be delivered over to the power of the sword; They will be a prey for foxes.

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> But the king will rejoice in God; Everyone who swears by Him will glory, For the mouths of those who speak lies will be stopped.
