# Psalms 110

## Psalms 110:1-4

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> A Psalm of David. The LORD says to my Lord: 'Sit at My right hand Until I make Your enemies a footstool for Your feet.'

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> The LORD will stretch forth Your strong scepter from Zion, [saying], 'Rule in the midst of Your enemies.'

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> Your people will volunteer freely in the day of Your power; In holy array, from the womb of the dawn, Your youth are to You [as] the dew.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> The LORD has sworn and will not change His mind, 'You are a priest forever According to the order of Melchizedek.'

## Psalms 110:5-7

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> The Lord is at Your right hand; He will shatter kings in the day of His wrath.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> He will judge among the nations, He will fill [them] with corpses, He will shatter the chief men over a broad country.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> He will drink from the brook by the wayside; Therefore He will lift up [His] head.
