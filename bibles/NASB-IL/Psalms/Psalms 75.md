# Psalms 75

## Psalms 75:1-5

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> For the choir director; [set to] Al-tashheth. A Psalm of Asaph, a Song. We give thanks to You, O God, we give thanks, For Your name is near; Men declare Your wondrous works.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> 'When I select an appointed time, It is I who judge with equity.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> 'The earth and all who dwell in it melt; It is I who have firmly set its pillars. Selah.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> 'I said to the boastful, 'Do not boast,' And to the wicked, 'Do not lift up the horn;

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> Do not lift up your horn on high, Do not speak with insolent pride.''

## Psalms 75:6-10

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> For not from the east, nor from the west, Nor from the desert [comes] exaltation;

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> But God is the Judge; He puts down one and exalts another.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> For a cup is in the hand of the LORD, and the wine foams; It is well mixed, and He pours out of this; Surely all the wicked of the earth must drain [and] drink down its dregs.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> But as for me, I will declare [it] forever; I will sing praises to the God of Jacob.

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> And all the horns of the wicked He will cut off, [But] the horns of the righteous will be lifted up.
