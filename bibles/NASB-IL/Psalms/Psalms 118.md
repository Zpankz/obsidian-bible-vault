## Psalms 118:1-18

<sup>1</sup> Give thanks to the LORD, for He is good; For His lovingkindness is everlasting.[^1][^2][^3][^4][^5][^6]

<sup>2</sup> Oh let Israel say, 'His lovingkindness is everlasting.'

<sup>3</sup> Oh let the house of Aaron say, 'His lovingkindness is everlasting.'

<sup>4</sup> Oh let those who fear the LORD say, 'His lovingkindness is everlasting.'

<sup>5</sup> From [my] distress I called upon the LORD; The LORD answered me [and] [set me] in a large place.[^7][^8][^9][^10][^11][^12][^13][^14]

<sup>6</sup> The LORD is for me; I will not fear; What can man do to me?[^15][^16][^17][^18][^19][^20][^21][^22]

<sup>7</sup> The LORD is for me among those who help me; Therefore I will look [with satisfaction] on those who hate me.[^23][^24]

<sup>8</sup> It is better to take refuge in the LORD Than to trust in man.[^25][^26][^27][^28]

<sup>9</sup> It is better to take refuge in the LORD Than to trust in princes.

<sup>10</sup> All nations surrounded me; In the name of the LORD I will surely cut them off.

<sup>11</sup> They surrounded me, yes, they surrounded me; In the name of the LORD I will surely cut them off.

<sup>12</sup> They surrounded me like bees; They were extinguished as a fire of thorns; In the name of the LORD I will surely cut them off.

<sup>13</sup> You pushed me violently so that I was falling, But the LORD helped me.[^29][^30]

<sup>14</sup> The LORD is my strength and song, And He has become my salvation.[^31][^32]

<sup>15</sup> The sound of joyful shouting and salvation is in the tents of the righteous; The right hand of the LORD does valiantly.[^33][^34]

<sup>16</sup> The right hand of the LORD is exalted; The right hand of the LORD does valiantly.

<sup>17</sup> I will not die, but live, And tell of the works of the LORD.[^35][^36][^37][^38][^39][^40][^41][^42]

<sup>18</sup> The LORD has disciplined me severely, But He has not given me over to death.[^43][^44][^45]

## Psalms 118:19-29

<sup>19</sup> Open to me the gates of righteousness; I shall enter through them, I shall give thanks to the LORD.[^46]

<sup>20</sup> This is the gate of the LORD; The righteous will enter through it.

<sup>21</sup> I shall give thanks to You, for You have answered me, And You have become my salvation.

<sup>22</sup> The stone which the builders rejected Has become the chief corner [stone].[^47][^48][^49][^50][^51][^52][^53]

<sup>23</sup> This is the LORD'S doing; It is marvelous in our eyes.

<sup>24</sup> This is the day which the LORD has made; Let us rejoice and be glad in it.[^54][^55][^56][^57][^58][^59][^60]

<sup>25</sup> O LORD, do save, we beseech You; O LORD, we beseech You, do send prosperity![^61]

<sup>26</sup> Blessed is the one who comes in the name of the LORD; We have blessed you from the house of the LORD.[^62][^63][^64][^65][^66][^67]

<sup>27</sup> The LORD is God, and He has given us light; Bind the festival sacrifice with cords to the horns of the altar.[^68][^69]

<sup>28</sup> You are my God, and I give thanks to You; [You are] my God, I extol You.[^70][^71]

<sup>29</sup> Give thanks to the LORD, for He is good; For His lovingkindness is everlasting.
