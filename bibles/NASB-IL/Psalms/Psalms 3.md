## Psalms 3:1-3

<sup>1</sup> A Psalm of David, when he fled from Absalom his son. O LORD, how my adversaries have increased! Many are rising up against me.[^1]

<sup>2</sup> Many are saying of my soul, 'There is no deliverance for him in God.' Selah.[^2]

<sup>3</sup> But You, O LORD, are a shield about me, My glory, and the One who lifts my head.[^3][^4][^5][^6][^7][^8][^9][^10]

## Psalms 3:4-8

<sup>4</sup> I was crying to the LORD with my voice, And He answered me from His holy mountain. Selah.[^11][^12][^13]

<sup>5</sup> I lay down and slept; I awoke, for the LORD sustains me.[^14][^15][^16][^17][^18]

<sup>6</sup> I will not be afraid of ten thousands of people Who have set themselves against me round about.[^19][^20][^21][^22][^23][^24]

<sup>7</sup> Arise, O LORD; save me, O my God! For You have smitten all my enemies on the cheek; You have shattered the teeth of the wicked.[^25][^26][^27]

<sup>8</sup> Salvation belongs to the LORD; Your blessing [be] upon Your people! Selah.[^28][^29][^30][^31][^32]
