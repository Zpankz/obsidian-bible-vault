# Psalms 3

## Psalms 3:1-3

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> A Psalm of David, when he fled from Absalom his son. O LORD, how my adversaries have increased! Many are rising up against me.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> Many are saying of my soul, 'There is no deliverance for him in God.' Selah.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> But You, O LORD, are a shield about me, My glory, and the One who lifts my head.

## Psalms 3:4-8

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> I was crying to the LORD with my voice, And He answered me from His holy mountain. Selah.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> I lay down and slept; I awoke, for the LORD sustains me.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> I will not be afraid of ten thousands of people Who have set themselves against me round about.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> Arise, O LORD; save me, O my God! For You have smitten all my enemies on the cheek; You have shattered the teeth of the wicked.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> Salvation belongs to the LORD; Your blessing [be] upon Your people! Selah.
