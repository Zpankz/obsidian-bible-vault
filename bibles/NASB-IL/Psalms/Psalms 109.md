# Psalms 109

## Psalms 109:1-5

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> For the choir director. A Psalm of David. O God of my praise, Do not be silent!

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> For they have opened the wicked and deceitful mouth against me; They have spoken against me with a lying tongue.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> They have also surrounded me with words of hatred, And fought against me without cause.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> In return for my love they act as my accusers; But I am [in] prayer.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> Thus they have repaid me evil for good And hatred for my love.

## Psalms 109:6-20

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> Appoint a wicked man over him, And let an accuser stand at his right hand.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> When he is judged, let him come forth guilty, And let his prayer become sin.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> Let his days be few; Let another take his office.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> Let his children be fatherless And his wife a widow.

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> Let his children wander about and beg; And let them seek [sustenance] far from their ruined homes.

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> Let the creditor seize all that he has, And let strangers plunder the product of his labor.

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> Let there be none to extend lovingkindness to him, Nor any to be gracious to his fatherless children.

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> Let his posterity be cut off; In a following generation let their name be blotted out.

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> Let the iniquity of his fathers be remembered before the LORD, And do not let the sin of his mother be blotted out.

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> Let them be before the LORD continually, That He may cut off their memory from the earth;

###### v16
<sup>16</sup> Because he did not remember to show lovingkindness, But persecuted the afflicted and needy man, And the despondent in heart, to put [them] to death.

###### v17
<sup>17</sup> He also loved cursing, so it came to him; And he did not delight in blessing, so it was far from him.

###### v18
<sup>18</sup> But he clothed himself with cursing as with his garment, And it entered into his body like water And like oil into his bones.

###### v19
<sup>19</sup> Let it be to him as a garment with which he covers himself, And for a belt with which he constantly girds himself.

###### v20
<sup>20</sup> Let this be the reward of my accusers from the LORD, And of those who speak evil against my soul.

## Psalms 109:21-31

###### v21
<sup>21</sup> But You, O GOD, the Lord, deal [kindly] with me for Your name's sake; Because Your lovingkindness is good, deliver me;

###### v22
<sup>22</sup> For I am afflicted and needy, And my heart is wounded within me.

###### v23
<sup>23</sup> I am passing like a shadow when it lengthens; I am shaken off like the locust.

###### v24
<sup>24</sup> My knees are weak from fasting, And my flesh has grown lean, without fatness.

###### v25
<sup>25</sup> I also have become a reproach to them; When they see me, they wag their head.

###### v26
<sup>26</sup> Help me, O LORD my God; Save me according to Your lovingkindness.

###### v27
<sup>27</sup> And let them know that this is Your hand; You, LORD, have done it.

###### v28
<sup>28</sup> Let them curse, but You bless; When they arise, they shall be ashamed, But Your servant shall be glad.

###### v29
<sup>29</sup> Let my accusers be clothed with dishonor, And let them cover themselves with their own shame as with a robe.

###### v30
<sup>30</sup> With my mouth I will give thanks abundantly to the LORD; And in the midst of many I will praise Him.

###### v31
<sup>31</sup> For He stands at the right hand of the needy, To save him from those who judge his soul.
