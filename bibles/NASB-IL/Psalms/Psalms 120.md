# Psalms 120

## Psalms 120:1-4

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> A Song of Ascents. In my trouble I cried to the LORD, And He answered me.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> Deliver my soul, O LORD, from lying lips, From a deceitful tongue.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> What shall be given to you, and what more shall be done to you, You deceitful tongue?

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> Sharp arrows of the warrior, With the [burning] coals of the broom tree.

## Psalms 120:5-7

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> Woe is me, for I sojourn in Meshech, For I dwell among the tents of Kedar!

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> Too long has my soul had its dwelling With those who hate peace.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> I am [for] peace, but when I speak, They are for war.
