# Psalms 91

## Psalms 91:1-8

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> He who dwells in the shelter of the Most High Will abide in the shadow of the Almighty.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> I will say to the LORD, 'My refuge and my fortress, My God, in whom I trust!'

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> For it is He who delivers you from the snare of the trapper And from the deadly pestilence.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> He will cover you with His pinions, And under His wings you may seek refuge; His faithfulness is a shield and bulwark.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> You will not be afraid of the terror by night, Or of the arrow that flies by day;

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> Of the pestilence that stalks in darkness, Or of the destruction that lays waste at noon.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> A thousand may fall at your side And ten thousand at your right hand, [But] it shall not approach you.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> You will only look on with your eyes And see the recompense of the wicked.

## Psalms 91:9-16

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> For you have made the LORD, my refuge, [Even] the Most High, your dwelling place.

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> No evil will befall you, Nor will any plague come near your tent.

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> For He will give His angels charge concerning you, To guard you in all your ways.

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> They will bear you up in their hands, That you do not strike your foot against a stone.

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> You will tread upon the lion and cobra, The young lion and the serpent you will trample down.

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> 'Because he has loved Me, therefore I will deliver him; I will set him [securely] on high, because he has known My name.

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> 'He will call upon Me, and I will answer him; I will be with him in trouble; I will rescue him and honor him.

###### v16
<sup>16</sup> 'With a long life I will satisfy him And let him see My salvation.'
