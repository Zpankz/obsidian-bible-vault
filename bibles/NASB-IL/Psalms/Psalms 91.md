## Psalms 91:1-8

<sup>1</sup> He who dwells in the shelter of the Most High Will abide in the shadow of the Almighty.[^1][^2][^3][^4][^5][^6][^7][^8]

<sup>2</sup> I will say to the LORD, 'My refuge and my fortress, My God, in whom I trust!'[^9][^10][^11][^12][^13][^14][^15][^16]

<sup>3</sup> For it is He who delivers you from the snare of the trapper And from the deadly pestilence.[^17][^18][^19][^20][^21][^22][^23][^24]

<sup>4</sup> He will cover you with His pinions, And under His wings you may seek refuge; His faithfulness is a shield and bulwark.[^25][^26][^27][^28][^29][^30][^31][^32]

<sup>5</sup> You will not be afraid of the terror by night, Or of the arrow that flies by day;[^33][^34][^35][^36][^37][^38]

<sup>6</sup> Of the pestilence that stalks in darkness, Or of the destruction that lays waste at noon.[^39][^40]

<sup>7</sup> A thousand may fall at your side And ten thousand at your right hand, [But] it shall not approach you.[^41][^42][^43][^44]

<sup>8</sup> You will only look on with your eyes And see the recompense of the wicked.[^45][^46][^47][^48][^49][^50]

## Psalms 91:9-16

<sup>9</sup> For you have made the LORD, my refuge, [Even] the Most High, your dwelling place.[^51][^52][^53][^54][^55]

<sup>10</sup> No evil will befall you, Nor will any plague come near your tent.[^56][^57][^58][^59][^60]

<sup>11</sup> For He will give His angels charge concerning you, To guard you in all your ways.[^61][^62][^63][^64][^65][^66][^67][^68]

<sup>12</sup> They will bear you up in their hands, That you do not strike your foot against a stone.[^69][^70][^71]

<sup>13</sup> You will tread upon the lion and cobra, The young lion and the serpent you will trample down.[^72][^73][^74][^75][^76][^77][^78][^79]

<sup>14</sup> 'Because he has loved Me, therefore I will deliver him; I will set him [securely] on high, because he has known My name.[^80][^81][^82][^83][^84][^85][^86][^87]

<sup>15</sup> 'He will call upon Me, and I will answer him; I will be with him in trouble; I will rescue him and honor him.[^88][^89][^90][^91][^92][^93][^94][^95]

<sup>16</sup> 'With a long life I will satisfy him And let him see My salvation.'[^96][^97][^98][^99][^100][^101][^102][^103]
