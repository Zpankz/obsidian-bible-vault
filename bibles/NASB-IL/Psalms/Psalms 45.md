# Psalms 45

## Psalms 45:1-5

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> For the choir director; according to the Shoshannim. A Maskil of the sons of Korah. A Song of Love. My heart overflows with a good theme; I address my verses to the King; My tongue is the pen of a ready writer.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> You are fairer than the sons of men; Grace is poured upon Your lips; Therefore God has blessed You forever.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> Gird Your sword on [Your] thigh, O Mighty One, [In] Your splendor and Your majesty!

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> And in Your majesty ride on victoriously, For the cause of truth and meekness [and] righteousness; Let Your right hand teach You awesome things.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> Your arrows are sharp; The peoples fall under You; [Your arrows are] in the heart of the King's enemies.

## Psalms 45:6-9

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> Your throne, O God, is forever and ever; A scepter of uprightness is the scepter of Your kingdom.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> You have loved righteousness and hated wickedness; Therefore God, Your God, has anointed You With the oil of joy above Your fellows.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> All Your garments are [fragrant with] myrrh and aloes [and] cassia; Out of ivory palaces stringed instruments have made You glad.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> Kings' daughters are among Your noble ladies; At Your right hand stands the queen in gold from Ophir.

## Psalms 45:10-17

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> Listen, O daughter, give attention and incline your ear: Forget your people and your father's house;

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> Then the King will desire your beauty. Because He is your Lord, bow down to Him.

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> The daughter of Tyre [will come] with a gift; The rich among the people will seek your favor.

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> The King's daughter is all glorious within; Her clothing is interwoven with gold.

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> She will be led to the King in embroidered work; The virgins, her companions who follow her, Will be brought to You.

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> They will be led forth with gladness and rejoicing; They will enter into the King's palace.

###### v16
<sup>16</sup> In place of your fathers will be your sons; You shall make them princes in all the earth.

###### v17
<sup>17</sup> I will cause Your name to be remembered in all generations; Therefore the peoples will give You thanks forever and ever.
