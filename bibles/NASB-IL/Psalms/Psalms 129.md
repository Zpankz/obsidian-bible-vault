# Psalms 129

## Psalms 129:1-4

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> A Song of Ascents. 'Many times they have persecuted me from my youth up,' Let Israel now say,

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> 'Many times they have persecuted me from my youth up; Yet they have not prevailed against me.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> 'The plowers plowed upon my back; They lengthened their furrows.'

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> The LORD is righteous; He has cut in two the cords of the wicked.

## Psalms 129:5-8

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> May all who hate Zion Be put to shame and turned backward;

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> Let them be like grass upon the housetops, Which withers before it grows up;

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> With which the reaper does not fill his hand, Or the binder of sheaves his bosom;

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> Nor do those who pass by say, 'The blessing of the LORD be upon you; We bless you in the name of the LORD.'
