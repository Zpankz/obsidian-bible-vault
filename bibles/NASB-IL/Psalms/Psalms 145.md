# Psalms 145

## Psalms 145:1-9

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> [A Psalm] of Praise, of David. I will extol You, my God, O King, And I will bless Your name forever and ever.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> Every day I will bless You, And I will praise Your name forever and ever.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> Great is the LORD, and highly to be praised, And His greatness is unsearchable.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> One generation shall praise Your works to another, And shall declare Your mighty acts.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> On the glorious splendor of Your majesty And on Your wonderful works, I will meditate.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> Men shall speak of the power of Your awesome acts, And I will tell of Your greatness.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> They shall eagerly utter the memory of Your abundant goodness And will shout joyfully of Your righteousness.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> The LORD is gracious and merciful; Slow to anger and great in lovingkindness.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> The LORD is good to all, And His mercies are over all His works.

## Psalms 145:10-21

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> All Your works shall give thanks to You, O LORD, And Your godly ones shall bless You.

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> They shall speak of the glory of Your kingdom And talk of Your power;

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> To make known to the sons of men Your mighty acts And the glory of the majesty of Your kingdom.

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> Your kingdom is an everlasting kingdom, And Your dominion [endures] throughout all generations.

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> The LORD sustains all who fall And raises up all who are bowed down.

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> The eyes of all look to You, And You give them their food in due time.

###### v16
<sup>16</sup> You open Your hand And satisfy the desire of every living thing.

###### v17
<sup>17</sup> The LORD is righteous in all His ways And kind in all His deeds.

###### v18
<sup>18</sup> The LORD is near to all who call upon Him, To all who call upon Him in truth.

###### v19
<sup>19</sup> He will fulfill the desire of those who fear Him; He will also hear their cry and will save them.

###### v20
<sup>20</sup> The LORD keeps all who love Him, But all the wicked He will destroy.

###### v21
<sup>21</sup> My mouth will speak the praise of the LORD, And all flesh will bless His holy name forever and ever.
