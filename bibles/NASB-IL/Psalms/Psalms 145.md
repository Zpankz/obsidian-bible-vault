## Psalms 145:1-9

<sup>1</sup> [A Psalm] of Praise, of David. I will extol You, my God, O King, And I will bless Your name forever and ever.[^1][^2][^3]

<sup>2</sup> Every day I will bless You, And I will praise Your name forever and ever.

<sup>3</sup> Great is the LORD, and highly to be praised, And His greatness is unsearchable.[^4][^5][^6][^7][^8][^9][^10]

<sup>4</sup> One generation shall praise Your works to another, And shall declare Your mighty acts.[^11][^12]

<sup>5</sup> On the glorious splendor of Your majesty And on Your wonderful works, I will meditate.

<sup>6</sup> Men shall speak of the power of Your awesome acts, And I will tell of Your greatness.

<sup>7</sup> They shall eagerly utter the memory of Your abundant goodness And will shout joyfully of Your righteousness.

<sup>8</sup> The LORD is gracious and merciful; Slow to anger and great in lovingkindness.[^13][^14][^15][^16][^17]

<sup>9</sup> The LORD is good to all, And His mercies are over all His works.[^18][^19]

## Psalms 145:10-21

<sup>10</sup> All Your works shall give thanks to You, O LORD, And Your godly ones shall bless You.[^20]

<sup>11</sup> They shall speak of the glory of Your kingdom And talk of Your power;

<sup>12</sup> To make known to the sons of men Your mighty acts And the glory of the majesty of Your kingdom.

<sup>13</sup> Your kingdom is an everlasting kingdom, And Your dominion [endures] throughout all generations.[^21]

<sup>14</sup> The LORD sustains all who fall And raises up all who are bowed down.[^22][^23][^24][^25][^26][^27][^28]

<sup>15</sup> The eyes of all look to You, And You give them their food in due time.[^29][^30][^31][^32]

<sup>16</sup> You open Your hand And satisfy the desire of every living thing.[^33][^34]

<sup>17</sup> The LORD is righteous in all His ways And kind in all His deeds.[^35]

<sup>18</sup> The LORD is near to all who call upon Him, To all who call upon Him in truth.[^36][^37][^38][^39][^40][^41][^42][^43]

<sup>19</sup> He will fulfill the desire of those who fear Him; He will also hear their cry and will save them.[^44][^45][^46][^47][^48][^49][^50][^51]

<sup>20</sup> The LORD keeps all who love Him, But all the wicked He will destroy.[^52][^53][^54][^55][^56]

<sup>21</sup> My mouth will speak the praise of the LORD, And all flesh will bless His holy name forever and ever.
