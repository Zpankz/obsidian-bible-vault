# Psalms 80

## Psalms 80:1-7

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> For the choir director; [set to] El Shoshannim; Eduth. A Psalm of Asaph. Oh, give ear, Shepherd of Israel, You who lead Joseph like a flock; You who are enthroned [above] the cherubim, shine forth!

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> Before Ephraim and Benjamin and Manasseh, stir up Your power And come to save us!

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> O God, restore us And cause Your face to shine [upon us], and we will be saved.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> O LORD God [of] hosts, How long will You be angry with the prayer of Your people?

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> You have fed them with the bread of tears, And You have made them to drink tears in large measure.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> You make us an object of contention to our neighbors, And our enemies laugh among themselves.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> O God [of] hosts, restore us And cause Your face to shine [upon us], and we will be saved.

## Psalms 80:8-19

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> You removed a vine from Egypt; You drove out the nations and planted it.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> You cleared [the ground] before it, And it took deep root and filled the land.

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> The mountains were covered with its shadow, And the cedars of God with its boughs.

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> It was sending out its branches to the sea And its shoots to the River.

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> Why have You broken down its hedges, So that all who pass [that] way pick its [fruit]?

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> A boar from the forest eats it away And whatever moves in the field feeds on it.

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> O God [of] hosts, turn again now, we beseech You; Look down from heaven and see, and take care of this vine,

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> Even the shoot which Your right hand has planted, And on the son whom You have strengthened for Yourself.

###### v16
<sup>16</sup> It is burned with fire, it is cut down; They perish at the rebuke of Your countenance.

###### v17
<sup>17</sup> Let Your hand be upon the man of Your right hand, Upon the son of man whom You made strong for Yourself.

###### v18
<sup>18</sup> Then we shall not turn back from You; Revive us, and we will call upon Your name.

###### v19
<sup>19</sup> O LORD God of hosts, restore us; Cause Your face to shine [upon us], and we will be saved.
