## Psalms 115:1-8

<sup>1</sup> Not to us, O LORD, not to us, But to Your name give glory Because of Your lovingkindness, because of Your truth.[^1][^2][^3]

<sup>2</sup> Why should the nations say, 'Where, now, is their God?'

<sup>3</sup> But our God is in the heavens; He does whatever He pleases.[^4][^5][^6][^7][^8][^9]

<sup>4</sup> Their idols are silver and gold, The work of man's hands.[^10]

<sup>5</sup> They have mouths, but they cannot speak; They have eyes, but they cannot see;

<sup>6</sup> They have ears, but they cannot hear; They have noses, but they cannot smell;

<sup>7</sup> They have hands, but they cannot feel; They have feet, but they cannot walk; They cannot make a sound with their throat.

<sup>8</sup> Those who make them will become like them, Everyone who trusts in them.[^11]

## Psalms 115:9-18

<sup>9</sup> O Israel, trust in the LORD; He is their help and their shield.

<sup>10</sup> O house of Aaron, trust in the LORD; He is their help and their shield.

<sup>11</sup> You who fear the LORD, trust in the LORD; He is their help and their shield.

<sup>12</sup> The LORD has been mindful of us; He will bless [us]; He will bless the house of Israel; He will bless the house of Aaron.[^12][^13][^14][^15][^16]

<sup>13</sup> He will bless those who fear the LORD, The small together with the great.[^17][^18][^19]

<sup>14</sup> May the LORD give you increase, You and your children.[^20][^21][^22]

<sup>15</sup> May you be blessed of the LORD, Maker of heaven and earth.[^23]

<sup>16</sup> The heavens are the heavens of the LORD, But the earth He has given to the sons of men.[^24][^25][^26][^27][^28][^29]

<sup>17</sup> The dead do not praise the LORD, Nor [do] any who go down into silence;[^30][^31]

<sup>18</sup> But as for us, we will bless the LORD From this time forth and forever. Praise the LORD!
