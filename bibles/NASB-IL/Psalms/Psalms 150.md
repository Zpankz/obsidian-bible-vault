## Psalms 150:1-6

<sup>1</sup> Praise the LORD! Praise God in His sanctuary; Praise Him in His mighty expanse.[^1][^2]

<sup>2</sup> Praise Him for His mighty deeds; Praise Him according to His excellent greatness.[^3]

<sup>3</sup> Praise Him with trumpet sound; Praise Him with harp and lyre.

<sup>4</sup> Praise Him with timbrel and dancing; Praise Him with stringed instruments and pipe.[^4][^5]

<sup>5</sup> Praise Him with loud cymbals; Praise Him with resounding cymbals.

<sup>6</sup> Let everything that has breath praise the LORD. Praise the LORD![^6][^7][^8][^9][^10]
