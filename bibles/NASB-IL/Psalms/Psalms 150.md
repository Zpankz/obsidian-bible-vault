# Psalms 150

## Psalms 150:1-6

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> Praise the LORD! Praise God in His sanctuary; Praise Him in His mighty expanse.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> Praise Him for His mighty deeds; Praise Him according to His excellent greatness.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> Praise Him with trumpet sound; Praise Him with harp and lyre.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> Praise Him with timbrel and dancing; Praise Him with stringed instruments and pipe.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> Praise Him with loud cymbals; Praise Him with resounding cymbals.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> Let everything that has breath praise the LORD. Praise the LORD!
