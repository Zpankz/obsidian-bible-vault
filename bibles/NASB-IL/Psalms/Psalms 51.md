# Psalms 51

## Psalms 51:1-6

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> For the choir director. A Psalm of David, when Nathan the prophet came to him, after he had gone in to Bathsheba. Be gracious to me, O God, according to Your lovingkindness; According to the greatness of Your compassion blot out my transgressions.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> Wash me thoroughly from my iniquity And cleanse me from my sin.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> For I know my transgressions, And my sin is ever before me.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> Against You, You only, I have sinned And done what is evil in Your sight, So that You are justified when You speak And blameless when You judge.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> Behold, I was brought forth in iniquity, And in sin my mother conceived me.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> Behold, You desire truth in the innermost being, And in the hidden part You will make me know wisdom.

## Psalms 51:7-13

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> Purify me with hyssop, and I shall be clean; Wash me, and I shall be whiter than snow.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> Make me to hear joy and gladness, Let the bones which You have broken rejoice.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> Hide Your face from my sins And blot out all my iniquities.

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> Create in me a clean heart, O God, And renew a steadfast spirit within me.

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> Do not cast me away from Your presence And do not take Your Holy Spirit from me.

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> Restore to me the joy of Your salvation And sustain me with a willing spirit.

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> [Then] I will teach transgressors Your ways, And sinners will be converted to You.

## Psalms 51:14-19

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> Deliver me from bloodguiltiness, O God, the God of my salvation; [Then] my tongue will joyfully sing of Your righteousness.

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> O Lord, open my lips, That my mouth may declare Your praise.

###### v16
<sup>16</sup> For You do not delight in sacrifice, otherwise I would give it; You are not pleased with burnt offering.

###### v17
<sup>17</sup> The sacrifices of God are a broken spirit; A broken and a contrite heart, O God, You will not despise.

###### v18
<sup>18</sup> By Your favor do good to Zion; Build the walls of Jerusalem.

###### v19
<sup>19</sup> Then You will delight in righteous sacrifices, In burnt offering and whole burnt offering; Then young bulls will be offered on Your altar.
