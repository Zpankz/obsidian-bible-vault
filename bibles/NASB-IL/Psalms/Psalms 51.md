## Psalms 51:1-6

<sup>1</sup> For the choir director. A Psalm of David, when Nathan the prophet came to him, after he had gone in to Bathsheba. Be gracious to me, O God, according to Your lovingkindness; According to the greatness of Your compassion blot out my transgressions.[^1][^2][^3][^4][^5][^6][^7][^8]

<sup>2</sup> Wash me thoroughly from my iniquity And cleanse me from my sin.[^9][^10][^11][^12][^13][^14][^15][^16]

<sup>3</sup> For I know my transgressions, And my sin is ever before me.[^17][^18][^19][^20]

<sup>4</sup> Against You, You only, I have sinned And done what is evil in Your sight, So that You are justified when You speak And blameless when You judge.[^21][^22][^23][^24]

<sup>5</sup> Behold, I was brought forth in iniquity, And in sin my mother conceived me.[^25][^26][^27][^28][^29][^30][^31][^32]

<sup>6</sup> Behold, You desire truth in the innermost being, And in the hidden part You will make me know wisdom.[^33][^34][^35][^36][^37][^38][^39][^40]

## Psalms 51:7-13

<sup>7</sup> Purify me with hyssop, and I shall be clean; Wash me, and I shall be whiter than snow.[^41][^42][^43][^44][^45][^46][^47][^48]

<sup>8</sup> Make me to hear joy and gladness, Let the bones which You have broken rejoice.[^49][^50][^51][^52][^53]

<sup>9</sup> Hide Your face from my sins And blot out all my iniquities.[^54]

<sup>10</sup> Create in me a clean heart, O God, And renew a steadfast spirit within me.[^55][^56][^57][^58][^59][^60][^61][^62]

<sup>11</sup> Do not cast me away from Your presence And do not take Your Holy Spirit from me.[^63][^64][^65][^66][^67][^68]

<sup>12</sup> Restore to me the joy of Your salvation And sustain me with a willing spirit.[^69][^70][^71][^72][^73][^74][^75][^76]

<sup>13</sup> [Then] I will teach transgressors Your ways, And sinners will be converted to You.[^77]

## Psalms 51:14-19

<sup>14</sup> Deliver me from bloodguiltiness, O God, the God of my salvation; [Then] my tongue will joyfully sing of Your righteousness.

<sup>15</sup> O Lord, open my lips, That my mouth may declare Your praise.[^78][^79]

<sup>16</sup> For You do not delight in sacrifice, otherwise I would give it; You are not pleased with burnt offering.[^80][^81][^82][^83]

<sup>17</sup> The sacrifices of God are a broken spirit; A broken and a contrite heart, O God, You will not despise.[^84][^85][^86][^87][^88][^89][^90][^91]

<sup>18</sup> By Your favor do good to Zion; Build the walls of Jerusalem.

<sup>19</sup> Then You will delight in righteous sacrifices, In burnt offering and whole burnt offering; Then young bulls will be offered on Your altar.
