## Psalms 16:1-7

<sup>1</sup> A Mikhtam of David. Preserve me, O God, for I take refuge in You.[^1][^2][^3]

<sup>2</sup> I said to the LORD, 'You are my Lord; I have no good besides You.'[^4]

<sup>3</sup> As for the saints who are in the earth, They are the majestic ones in whom is all my delight.

<sup>4</sup> The sorrows of those who have bartered for another [god] will be multiplied; I shall not pour out their drink offerings of blood, Nor will I take their names upon my lips.[^5][^6][^7][^8][^9]

<sup>5</sup> The LORD is the portion of my inheritance and my cup; You support my lot.[^10][^11][^12][^13][^14][^15]

<sup>6</sup> The lines have fallen to me in pleasant places; Indeed, my heritage is beautiful to me.[^16][^17][^18][^19][^20][^21][^22][^23]

<sup>7</sup> I will bless the LORD who has counseled me; Indeed, my mind instructs me in the night.[^24][^25][^26][^27][^28][^29]

## Psalms 16:8-11

<sup>8</sup> I have set the LORD continually before me; Because He is at my right hand, I will not be shaken.[^30][^31][^32][^33][^34][^35][^36][^37]

<sup>9</sup> Therefore my heart is glad and my glory rejoices; My flesh also will dwell securely.[^38][^39][^40]

<sup>10</sup> For You will not abandon my soul to Sheol; Nor will You allow Your Holy One to undergo decay.[^41][^42][^43][^44]

<sup>11</sup> You will make known to me the path of life; In Your presence is fullness of joy; In Your right hand there are pleasures forever.[^45][^46][^47][^48][^49][^50][^51][^52]
