# Psalms 16

## Psalms 16:1-7

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> A Mikhtam of David. Preserve me, O God, for I take refuge in You.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> I said to the LORD, 'You are my Lord; I have no good besides You.'

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> As for the saints who are in the earth, They are the majestic ones in whom is all my delight.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> The sorrows of those who have bartered for another [god] will be multiplied; I shall not pour out their drink offerings of blood, Nor will I take their names upon my lips.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> The LORD is the portion of my inheritance and my cup; You support my lot.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> The lines have fallen to me in pleasant places; Indeed, my heritage is beautiful to me.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> I will bless the LORD who has counseled me; Indeed, my mind instructs me in the night.

## Psalms 16:8-11

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> I have set the LORD continually before me; Because He is at my right hand, I will not be shaken.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> Therefore my heart is glad and my glory rejoices; My flesh also will dwell securely.

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> For You will not abandon my soul to Sheol; Nor will You allow Your Holy One to undergo decay.

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> You will make known to me the path of life; In Your presence is fullness of joy; In Your right hand there are pleasures forever.
