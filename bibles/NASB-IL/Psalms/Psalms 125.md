# Psalms 125

## Psalms 125:1-3

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> A Song of Ascents. Those who trust in the LORD Are as Mount Zion, which cannot be moved but abides forever.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> As the mountains surround Jerusalem, So the LORD surrounds His people From this time forth and forever.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> For the scepter of wickedness shall not rest upon the land of the righteous, So that the righteous will not put forth their hands to do wrong.

## Psalms 125:4-5

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> Do good, O LORD, to those who are good And to those who are upright in their hearts.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> But as for those who turn aside to their crooked ways, The LORD will lead them away with the doers of iniquity. Peace be upon Israel.
