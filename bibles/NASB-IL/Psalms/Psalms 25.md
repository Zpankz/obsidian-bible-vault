## Psalms 25:1-7

<sup>1</sup> [A Psalm] of David. To You, O LORD, I lift up my soul.[^1][^2][^3][^4][^5]

<sup>2</sup> O my God, in You I trust, Do not let me be ashamed; Do not let my enemies exult over me.[^6][^7][^8][^9][^10][^11]

<sup>3</sup> Indeed, none of those who wait for You will be ashamed; Those who deal treacherously without cause will be ashamed.[^12][^13][^14][^15][^16][^17][^18][^19]

<sup>4</sup> Make me know Your ways, O LORD; Teach me Your paths.[^20][^21][^22][^23][^24][^25][^26][^27]

<sup>5</sup> Lead me in Your truth and teach me, For You are the God of my salvation; For You I wait all the day.[^28][^29][^30][^31][^32][^33][^34][^35]

<sup>6</sup> Remember, O LORD, Your compassion and Your lovingkindnesses, For they have been from of old.

<sup>7</sup> Do not remember the sins of my youth or my transgressions; According to Your lovingkindness remember me, For Your goodness' sake, O LORD.[^36][^37]

## Psalms 25:8-14

<sup>8</sup> Good and upright is the LORD; Therefore He instructs sinners in the way.[^38][^39][^40]

<sup>9</sup> He leads the humble in justice, And He teaches the humble His way.[^41][^42][^43][^44][^45][^46]

<sup>10</sup> All the paths of the LORD are lovingkindness and truth To those who keep His covenant and His testimonies.[^47][^48][^49][^50]

<sup>11</sup> For Your name's sake, O LORD, Pardon my iniquity, for it is great.[^51][^52]

<sup>12</sup> Who is the man who fears the LORD? He will instruct him in the way he should choose.[^53][^54][^55][^56][^57]

<sup>13</sup> His soul will abide in prosperity, And his descendants will inherit the land.[^58]

<sup>14</sup> The secret of the LORD is for those who fear Him, And He will make them know His covenant.[^59][^60][^61][^62][^63][^64][^65]

## Psalms 25:15-22

<sup>15</sup> My eyes are continually toward the LORD, For He will pluck my feet out of the net.[^66][^67][^68][^69][^70][^71][^72]

<sup>16</sup> Turn to me and be gracious to me, For I am lonely and afflicted.

<sup>17</sup> The troubles of my heart are enlarged; Bring me out of my distresses.

<sup>18</sup> Look upon my affliction and my trouble, And forgive all my sins.

<sup>19</sup> Look upon my enemies, for they are many, And they hate me with violent hatred.

<sup>20</sup> Guard my soul and deliver me; Do not let me be ashamed, for I take refuge in You.

<sup>21</sup> Let integrity and uprightness preserve me, For I wait for You.[^73][^74][^75][^76][^77][^78]

<sup>22</sup> Redeem Israel, O God, Out of all his troubles.
