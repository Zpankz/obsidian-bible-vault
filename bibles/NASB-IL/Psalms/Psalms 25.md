# Psalms 25

## Psalms 25:1-7

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> [A Psalm] of David. To You, O LORD, I lift up my soul.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> O my God, in You I trust, Do not let me be ashamed; Do not let my enemies exult over me.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> Indeed, none of those who wait for You will be ashamed; Those who deal treacherously without cause will be ashamed.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> Make me know Your ways, O LORD; Teach me Your paths.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> Lead me in Your truth and teach me, For You are the God of my salvation; For You I wait all the day.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> Remember, O LORD, Your compassion and Your lovingkindnesses, For they have been from of old.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> Do not remember the sins of my youth or my transgressions; According to Your lovingkindness remember me, For Your goodness' sake, O LORD.

## Psalms 25:8-14

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> Good and upright is the LORD; Therefore He instructs sinners in the way.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> He leads the humble in justice, And He teaches the humble His way.

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> All the paths of the LORD are lovingkindness and truth To those who keep His covenant and His testimonies.

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> For Your name's sake, O LORD, Pardon my iniquity, for it is great.

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> Who is the man who fears the LORD? He will instruct him in the way he should choose.

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> His soul will abide in prosperity, And his descendants will inherit the land.

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> The secret of the LORD is for those who fear Him, And He will make them know His covenant.

## Psalms 25:15-22

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> My eyes are continually toward the LORD, For He will pluck my feet out of the net.

###### v16
<sup>16</sup> Turn to me and be gracious to me, For I am lonely and afflicted.

###### v17
<sup>17</sup> The troubles of my heart are enlarged; Bring me out of my distresses.

###### v18
<sup>18</sup> Look upon my affliction and my trouble, And forgive all my sins.

###### v19
<sup>19</sup> Look upon my enemies, for they are many, And they hate me with violent hatred.

###### v20
<sup>20</sup> Guard my soul and deliver me; Do not let me be ashamed, for I take refuge in You.

###### v21
<sup>21</sup> Let integrity and uprightness preserve me, For I wait for You.

###### v22
<sup>22</sup> Redeem Israel, O God, Out of all his troubles.
