# Psalms 136

## Psalms 136:1-9

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> Give thanks to the LORD, for He is good, For His lovingkindness is everlasting.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> Give thanks to the God of gods, For His lovingkindness is everlasting.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> Give thanks to the Lord of lords, For His lovingkindness is everlasting.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> To Him who alone does great wonders, For His lovingkindness is everlasting;

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> To Him who made the heavens with skill, For His lovingkindness is everlasting;

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> To Him who spread out the earth above the waters, For His lovingkindness is everlasting;

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> To Him who made [the] great lights, For His lovingkindness is everlasting:

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> The sun to rule by day, For His lovingkindness is everlasting,

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> The moon and stars to rule by night, For His lovingkindness is everlasting.

## Psalms 136:10-22

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> To Him who smote the Egyptians in their firstborn, For His lovingkindness is everlasting,

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> And brought Israel out from their midst, For His lovingkindness is everlasting,

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> With a strong hand and an outstretched arm, For His lovingkindness is everlasting.

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> To Him who divided the Red Sea asunder, For His lovingkindness is everlasting,

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> And made Israel pass through the midst of it, For His lovingkindness is everlasting;

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> But He overthrew Pharaoh and his army in the Red Sea, For His lovingkindness is everlasting.

###### v16
<sup>16</sup> To Him who led His people through the wilderness, For His lovingkindness is everlasting;

###### v17
<sup>17</sup> To Him who smote great kings, For His lovingkindness is everlasting,

###### v18
<sup>18</sup> And slew mighty kings, For His lovingkindness is everlasting:

###### v19
<sup>19</sup> Sihon, king of the Amorites, For His lovingkindness is everlasting,

###### v20
<sup>20</sup> And Og, king of Bashan, For His lovingkindness is everlasting,

###### v21
<sup>21</sup> And gave their land as a heritage, For His lovingkindness is everlasting,

###### v22
<sup>22</sup> Even a heritage to Israel His servant, For His lovingkindness is everlasting.
