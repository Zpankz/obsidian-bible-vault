# Psalms 57

## Psalms 57:1-6

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> For the choir director; [set to] Al-tashheth. A Mikhtam of David, when he fled from Saul in the cave. Be gracious to me, O God, be gracious to me, For my soul takes refuge in You; And in the shadow of Your wings I will take refuge Until destruction passes by.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> I will cry to God Most High, To God who accomplishes [all things] for me.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> He will send from heaven and save me; He reproaches him who tramples upon me. Selah. God will send forth His lovingkindness and His truth.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> My soul is among lions; I must lie among those who breathe forth fire, [Even] the sons of men, whose teeth are spears and arrows And their tongue a sharp sword.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> Be exalted above the heavens, O God; [Let] Your glory [be] above all the earth.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> They have prepared a net for my steps; My soul is bowed down; They dug a pit before me; They [themselves] have fallen into the midst of it. Selah.

## Psalms 57:7-11

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> My heart is steadfast, O God, my heart is steadfast; I will sing, yes, I will sing praises!

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> Awake, my glory! Awake, harp and lyre! I will awaken the dawn.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> I will give thanks to You, O Lord, among the peoples; I will sing praises to You among the nations.

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> For Your lovingkindness is great to the heavens And Your truth to the clouds.

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> Be exalted above the heavens, O God; [Let] Your glory [be] above all the earth.
