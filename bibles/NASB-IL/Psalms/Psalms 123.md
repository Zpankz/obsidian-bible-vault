## Psalms 123:1-4

<sup>1</sup> A Song of Ascents. To You I lift up my eyes, O You who are enthroned in the heavens![^1][^2][^3][^4][^5][^6][^7]

<sup>2</sup> Behold, as the eyes of servants [look] to the hand of their master, As the eyes of a maid to the hand of her mistress, So our eyes [look] to the LORD our God, Until He is gracious to us.[^8][^9][^10][^11][^12]

<sup>3</sup> Be gracious to us, O LORD, be gracious to us, For we are greatly filled with contempt.

<sup>4</sup> Our soul is greatly filled With the scoffing of those who are at ease, [And] with the contempt of the proud.
