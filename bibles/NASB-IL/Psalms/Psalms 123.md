# Psalms 123

## Psalms 123:1-4

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> A Song of Ascents. To You I lift up my eyes, O You who are enthroned in the heavens!

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> Behold, as the eyes of servants [look] to the hand of their master, As the eyes of a maid to the hand of her mistress, So our eyes [look] to the LORD our God, Until He is gracious to us.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> Be gracious to us, O LORD, be gracious to us, For we are greatly filled with contempt.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> Our soul is greatly filled With the scoffing of those who are at ease, [And] with the contempt of the proud.
