# Psalms 143

## Psalms 143:1-6

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> A Psalm of David. Hear my prayer, O LORD, Give ear to my supplications! Answer me in Your faithfulness, in Your righteousness!

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> And do not enter into judgment with Your servant, For in Your sight no man living is righteous.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> For the enemy has persecuted my soul; He has crushed my life to the ground; He has made me dwell in dark places, like those who have long been dead.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> Therefore my spirit is overwhelmed within me; My heart is appalled within me.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> I remember the days of old; I meditate on all Your doings; I muse on the work of Your hands.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> I stretch out my hands to You; My soul [longs] for You, as a parched land. Selah.

## Psalms 143:7-12

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> Answer me quickly, O LORD, my spirit fails; Do not hide Your face from me, Or I will become like those who go down to the pit.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> Let me hear Your lovingkindness in the morning; For I trust in You; Teach me the way in which I should walk; For to You I lift up my soul.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> Deliver me, O LORD, from my enemies; I take refuge in You.

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> Teach me to do Your will, For You are my God; Let Your good Spirit lead me on level ground.

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> For the sake of Your name, O LORD, revive me. In Your righteousness bring my soul out of trouble.

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> And in Your lovingkindness, cut off my enemies And destroy all those who afflict my soul, For I am Your servant.
