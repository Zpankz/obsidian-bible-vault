## Psalms 97:1-7

<sup>1</sup> The LORD reigns, let the earth rejoice; Let the many islands be glad.[^1]

<sup>2</sup> Clouds and thick darkness surround Him; Righteousness and justice are the foundation of His throne.[^2]

<sup>3</sup> Fire goes before Him And burns up His adversaries round about.[^3]

<sup>4</sup> His lightnings lit up the world; The earth saw and trembled.

<sup>5</sup> The mountains melted like wax at the presence of the LORD, At the presence of the Lord of the whole earth.[^4][^5][^6][^7]

<sup>6</sup> The heavens declare His righteousness, And all the peoples have seen His glory.

<sup>7</sup> Let all those be ashamed who serve graven images, Who boast themselves of idols; Worship Him, all you gods.

## Psalms 97:8-12

<sup>8</sup> Zion heard [this] and was glad, And the daughters of Judah have rejoiced Because of Your judgments, O LORD.

<sup>9</sup> For You are the LORD Most High over all the earth; You are exalted far above all gods.

<sup>10</sup> Hate evil, you who love the LORD, Who preserves the souls of His godly ones; He delivers them from the hand of the wicked.[^8][^9][^10][^11][^12][^13][^14][^15]

<sup>11</sup> Light is sown [like seed] for the righteous And gladness for the upright in heart.[^16][^17][^18][^19][^20][^21][^22][^23]

<sup>12</sup> Be glad in the LORD, you righteous ones, And give thanks to His holy name.[^24][^25][^26]
