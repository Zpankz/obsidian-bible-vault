# Psalms 97

## Psalms 97:1-7

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> The LORD reigns, let the earth rejoice; Let the many islands be glad.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> Clouds and thick darkness surround Him; Righteousness and justice are the foundation of His throne.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> Fire goes before Him And burns up His adversaries round about.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> His lightnings lit up the world; The earth saw and trembled.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> The mountains melted like wax at the presence of the LORD, At the presence of the Lord of the whole earth.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> The heavens declare His righteousness, And all the peoples have seen His glory.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> Let all those be ashamed who serve graven images, Who boast themselves of idols; Worship Him, all you gods.

## Psalms 97:8-12

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> Zion heard [this] and was glad, And the daughters of Judah have rejoiced Because of Your judgments, O LORD.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> For You are the LORD Most High over all the earth; You are exalted far above all gods.

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> Hate evil, you who love the LORD, Who preserves the souls of His godly ones; He delivers them from the hand of the wicked.

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> Light is sown [like seed] for the righteous And gladness for the upright in heart.

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> Be glad in the LORD, you righteous ones, And give thanks to His holy name.
