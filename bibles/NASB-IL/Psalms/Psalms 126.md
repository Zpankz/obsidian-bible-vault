## Psalms 126:1-3

<sup>1</sup> A Song of Ascents. When the LORD brought back the captive ones of Zion, We were like those who dream.[^1][^2][^3][^4][^5][^6][^7][^8]

<sup>2</sup> Then our mouth was filled with laughter And our tongue with joyful shouting; Then they said among the nations, 'The LORD has done great things for them.'[^9]

<sup>3</sup> The LORD has done great things for us; We are glad.[^10]

## Psalms 126:4-6

<sup>4</sup> Restore our captivity, O LORD, As the streams in the South.

<sup>5</sup> Those who sow in tears shall reap with joyful shouting.[^11][^12][^13][^14][^15][^16][^17][^18]

<sup>6</sup> He who goes to and fro weeping, carrying [his] bag of seed, Shall indeed come again with a shout of joy, bringing his sheaves [with him].[^19][^20][^21][^22]
