# Psalms 126

## Psalms 126:1-3

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> A Song of Ascents. When the LORD brought back the captive ones of Zion, We were like those who dream.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> Then our mouth was filled with laughter And our tongue with joyful shouting; Then they said among the nations, 'The LORD has done great things for them.'

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> The LORD has done great things for us; We are glad.

## Psalms 126:4-6

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> Restore our captivity, O LORD, As the streams in the South.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> Those who sow in tears shall reap with joyful shouting.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> He who goes to and fro weeping, carrying [his] bag of seed, Shall indeed come again with a shout of joy, bringing his sheaves [with him].
