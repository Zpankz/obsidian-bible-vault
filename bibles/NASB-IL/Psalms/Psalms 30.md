# Psalms 30

## Psalms 30:1-5

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> A Psalm; a Song at the Dedication of the House. [A Psalm] of David. I will extol You, O LORD, for You have lifted me up, And have not let my enemies rejoice over me.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> O LORD my God, I cried to You for help, and You healed me.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> O LORD, You have brought up my soul from Sheol; You have kept me alive, that I would not go down to the pit.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> Sing praise to the LORD, you His godly ones, And give thanks to His holy name.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> For His anger is but for a moment, His favor is for a lifetime; Weeping may last for the night, But a shout of joy [comes] in the morning.

## Psalms 30:6-12

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> Now as for me, I said in my prosperity, 'I will never be moved.'

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> O LORD, by Your favor You have made my mountain to stand strong; You hid Your face, I was dismayed.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> To You, O LORD, I called, And to the Lord I made supplication:

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> 'What profit is there in my blood, if I go down to the pit? Will the dust praise You? Will it declare Your faithfulness?

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> 'Hear, O LORD, and be gracious to me; O LORD, be my helper.'

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> You have turned for me my mourning into dancing; You have loosed my sackcloth and girded me with gladness,

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> That [my] soul may sing praise to You and not be silent. O LORD my God, I will give thanks to You forever.
