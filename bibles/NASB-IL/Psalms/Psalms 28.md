# Psalms 28

## Psalms 28:1-5

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> [A Psalm] of David. To You, O LORD, I call; My rock, do not be deaf to me, For if You are silent to me, I will become like those who go down to the pit.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> Hear the voice of my supplications when I cry to You for help, When I lift up my hands toward Your holy sanctuary.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> Do not drag me away with the wicked And with those who work iniquity, Who speak peace with their neighbors, While evil is in their hearts.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> Requite them according to their work and according to the evil of their practices; Requite them according to the deeds of their hands; Repay them their recompense.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> Because they do not regard the works of the LORD Nor the deeds of His hands, He will tear them down and not build them up.

## Psalms 28:6-9

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> Blessed be the LORD, Because He has heard the voice of my supplication.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> The LORD is my strength and my shield; My heart trusts in Him, and I am helped; Therefore my heart exults, And with my song I shall thank Him.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> The LORD is their strength, And He is a saving defense to His anointed.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> Save Your people and bless Your inheritance; Be their shepherd also, and carry them forever.
