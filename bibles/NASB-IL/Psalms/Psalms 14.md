# Psalms 14

## Psalms 14:1-3

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> For the choir director. [A Psalm] of David. The fool has said in his heart, 'There is no God.' They are corrupt, they have committed abominable deeds; There is no one who does good.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> The LORD has looked down from heaven upon the sons of men To see if there are any who understand, Who seek after God.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> They have all turned aside, together they have become corrupt; There is no one who does good, not even one.

## Psalms 14:4-7

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> Do all the workers of wickedness not know, Who eat up my people [as] they eat bread, [And] do not call upon the Lord?

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> There they are in great dread, For God is with the righteous generation.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> You would put to shame the counsel of the afflicted, But the LORD is his refuge.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> Oh, that the salvation of Israel would come out of Zion! When the LORD restores His captive people, Jacob will rejoice, Israel will be glad.
