# Psalms 21

## Psalms 21:1-6

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> For the choir director. A Psalm of David. O LORD, in Your strength the king will be glad, And in Your salvation how greatly he will rejoice!

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> You have given him his heart's desire, And You have not withheld the request of his lips. Selah.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> For You meet him with the blessings of good things; You set a crown of fine gold on his head.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> He asked life of You, You gave it to him, Length of days forever and ever.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> His glory is great through Your salvation, Splendor and majesty You place upon him.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> For You make him most blessed forever; You make him joyful with gladness in Your presence.

## Psalms 21:7-13

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> For the king trusts in the LORD, And through the lovingkindness of the Most High he will not be shaken.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> Your hand will find out all your enemies; Your right hand will find out those who hate you.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> You will make them as a fiery oven in the time of your anger; The LORD will swallow them up in His wrath, And fire will devour them.

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> Their offspring You will destroy from the earth, And their descendants from among the sons of men.

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> Though they intended evil against You [And] devised a plot, They will not succeed.

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> For You will make them turn their back; You will aim with Your bowstrings at their faces.

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> Be exalted, O LORD, in Your strength; We will sing and praise Your power.
