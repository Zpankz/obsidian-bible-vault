## Psalms 100:1-5

<sup>1</sup> A Psalm for Thanksgiving. Shout joyfully to the LORD, all the earth.[^1][^2][^3][^4][^5][^6][^7][^8]

<sup>2</sup> Serve the LORD with gladness; Come before Him with joyful singing.[^9][^10][^11][^12][^13][^14][^15][^16]

<sup>3</sup> Know that the LORD Himself is God; It is He who has made us, and not we ourselves; [We are] His people and the sheep of His pasture.[^17][^18][^19][^20][^21][^22][^23][^24]

<sup>4</sup> Enter His gates with thanksgiving [And] His courts with praise. Give thanks to Him, bless His name.[^25][^26][^27][^28][^29][^30][^31][^32]

<sup>5</sup> For the LORD is good; His lovingkindness is everlasting And His faithfulness to all generations.[^33][^34][^35][^36][^37][^38][^39][^40]
