# Psalms 100

## Psalms 100:1-5

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> A Psalm for Thanksgiving. Shout joyfully to the LORD, all the earth.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> Serve the LORD with gladness; Come before Him with joyful singing.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> Know that the LORD Himself is God; It is He who has made us, and not we ourselves; [We are] His people and the sheep of His pasture.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> Enter His gates with thanksgiving [And] His courts with praise. Give thanks to Him, bless His name.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> For the LORD is good; His lovingkindness is everlasting And His faithfulness to all generations.
