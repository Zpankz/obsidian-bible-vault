## Psalms 127:1-5

<sup>1</sup> A Song of Ascents, of Solomon. Unless the LORD builds the house, They labor in vain who build it; Unless the LORD guards the city, The watchman keeps awake in vain.[^1][^2][^3][^4][^5][^6][^7][^8]

<sup>2</sup> It is vain for you to rise up early, To retire late, To eat the bread of painful labors; For He gives to His beloved [even in his] sleep.[^9][^10][^11][^12]

<sup>3</sup> Behold, children are a gift of the LORD, The fruit of the womb is a reward.[^13][^14][^15][^16][^17][^18][^19][^20]

<sup>4</sup> Like arrows in the hand of a warrior, So are the children of one's youth.[^21]

<sup>5</sup> How blessed is the man whose quiver is full of them; They will not be ashamed When they speak with their enemies in the gate.
