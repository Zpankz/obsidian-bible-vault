# Psalms 38

## Psalms 38:1-11

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> A Psalm of David, for a memorial. O LORD, rebuke me not in Your wrath, And chasten me not in Your burning anger.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> For Your arrows have sunk deep into me, And Your hand has pressed down on me.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> There is no soundness in my flesh because of Your indignation; There is no health in my bones because of my sin.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> For my iniquities are gone over my head; As a heavy burden they weigh too much for me.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> My wounds grow foul [and] fester Because of my folly.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> I am bent over and greatly bowed down; I go mourning all day long.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> For my loins are filled with burning, And there is no soundness in my flesh.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> I am benumbed and badly crushed; I groan because of the agitation of my heart.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> Lord, all my desire is before You; And my sighing is not hidden from You.

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> My heart throbs, my strength fails me; And the light of my eyes, even that has gone from me.

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> My loved ones and my friends stand aloof from my plague; And my kinsmen stand afar off.

## Psalms 38:12-22

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> Those who seek my life lay snares [for me]; And those who seek to injure me have threatened destruction, And they devise treachery all day long.

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> But I, like a deaf man, do not hear; And [I am] like a mute man who does not open his mouth.

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> Yes, I am like a man who does not hear, And in whose mouth are no arguments.

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> For I hope in You, O LORD; You will answer, O Lord my God.

###### v16
<sup>16</sup> For I said, 'May they not rejoice over me, [Who], when my foot slips, would magnify themselves against me.'

###### v17
<sup>17</sup> For I am ready to fall, And my sorrow is continually before me.

###### v18
<sup>18</sup> For I confess my iniquity; I am full of anxiety because of my sin.

###### v19
<sup>19</sup> But my enemies are vigorous [and] strong, And many are those who hate me wrongfully.

###### v20
<sup>20</sup> And those who repay evil for good, They oppose me, because I follow what is good.

###### v21
<sup>21</sup> Do not forsake me, O LORD; O my God, do not be far from me!

###### v22
<sup>22</sup> Make haste to help me, O Lord, my salvation!
