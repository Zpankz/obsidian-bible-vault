## Psalms 17:1-7

<sup>1</sup> A Prayer of David. Hear a just cause, O LORD, give heed to my cry; Give ear to my prayer, which is not from deceitful lips.[^1]

<sup>2</sup> Let my judgment come forth from Your presence; Let Your eyes look with equity.

<sup>3</sup> You have tried my heart; You have visited [me] by night; You have tested me and You find nothing; I have purposed that my mouth will not transgress.[^2][^3][^4][^5][^6]

<sup>4</sup> As for the deeds of men, by the word of Your lips I have kept from the paths of the violent.

<sup>5</sup> My steps have held fast to Your paths. My feet have not slipped.[^7]

<sup>6</sup> I have called upon You, for You will answer me, O God; Incline Your ear to me, hear my speech.[^8][^9]

<sup>7</sup> Wondrously show Your lovingkindness, O Savior of those who take refuge at Your right hand From those who rise up [against them].[^10][^11][^12][^13][^14]

## Psalms 17:8-15

<sup>8</sup> Keep me as the apple of the eye; Hide me in the shadow of Your wings[^15][^16][^17][^18][^19][^20][^21][^22]

<sup>9</sup> From the wicked who despoil me, My deadly enemies who surround me.

<sup>10</sup> They have closed their unfeeling [heart], With their mouth they speak proudly.

<sup>11</sup> They have now surrounded us in our steps; They set their eyes to cast [us] down to the ground.

<sup>12</sup> He is like a lion that is eager to tear, And as a young lion lurking in hiding places.

<sup>13</sup> Arise, O LORD, confront him, bring him low; Deliver my soul from the wicked with Your sword,

<sup>14</sup> From men with Your hand, O LORD, From men of the world, whose portion is in [this] life, And whose belly You fill with Your treasure; They are satisfied with children, And leave their abundance to their babes.

<sup>15</sup> As for me, I shall behold Your face in righteousness; I will be satisfied with Your likeness when I awake.[^23][^24][^25][^26][^27][^28][^29][^30]
