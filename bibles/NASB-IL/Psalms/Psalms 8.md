## Psalms 8:3-9

<sup>3</sup> When I consider Your heavens, the work of Your fingers, The moon and the stars, which You have ordained;[^16][^17][^18][^19][^20][^21][^22][^23]

<sup>4</sup> What is man that You take thought of him, And the son of man that You care for him?[^24][^25][^26][^27][^28][^29][^30][^31]

<sup>5</sup> Yet You have made him a little lower than God, And You crown him with glory and majesty![^32][^33][^34]

<sup>6</sup> You make him to rule over the works of Your hands; You have put all things under his feet,[^35][^36][^37][^38][^39]

<sup>7</sup> All sheep and oxen, And also the beasts of the field,

<sup>8</sup> The birds of the heavens and the fish of the sea, Whatever passes through the paths of the seas.

<sup>9</sup> O LORD, our Lord, How majestic is Your name in all the earth![^40]
