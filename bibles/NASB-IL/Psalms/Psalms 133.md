# Psalms 133

## Psalms 133:1-3

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> A Song of Ascents, of David. Behold, how good and how pleasant it is For brothers to dwell together in unity!

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> It is like the precious oil upon the head, Coming down upon the beard, [Even] Aaron's beard, Coming down upon the edge of his robes.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> It is like the dew of Hermon Coming down upon the mountains of Zion; For there the LORD commanded the blessing-- life forever.
