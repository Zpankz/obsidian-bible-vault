## Psalms 133:1-3

<sup>1</sup> A Song of Ascents, of David. Behold, how good and how pleasant it is For brothers to dwell together in unity![^1][^2][^3][^4][^5][^6][^7][^8]

<sup>2</sup> It is like the precious oil upon the head, Coming down upon the beard, [Even] Aaron's beard, Coming down upon the edge of his robes.[^9][^10]

<sup>3</sup> It is like the dew of Hermon Coming down upon the mountains of Zion; For there the LORD commanded the blessing-- life forever.[^11][^12][^13][^14][^15][^16]
