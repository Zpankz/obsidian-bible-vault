# Psalms 146

## Psalms 146:1-4

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> Praise the LORD! Praise the LORD, O my soul!

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> I will praise the LORD while I live; I will sing praises to my God while I have my being.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> Do not trust in princes, In mortal man, in whom there is no salvation.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> His spirit departs, he returns to the earth; In that very day his thoughts perish.

## Psalms 146:5-10

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> How blessed is he whose help is the God of Jacob, Whose hope is in the LORD his God,

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> Who made heaven and earth, The sea and all that is in them; Who keeps faith forever;

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> Who executes justice for the oppressed; Who gives food to the hungry. The LORD sets the prisoners free.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> The LORD opens [the eyes of] the blind; The LORD raises up those who are bowed down; The LORD loves the righteous;

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> The LORD protects the strangers; He supports the fatherless and the widow, But He thwarts the way of the wicked.

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> The LORD will reign forever, Your God, O Zion, to all generations. Praise the LORD!
