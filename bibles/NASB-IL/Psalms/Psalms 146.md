## Psalms 146:1-4

<sup>1</sup> Praise the LORD! Praise the LORD, O my soul![^1]

<sup>2</sup> I will praise the LORD while I live; I will sing praises to my God while I have my being.[^2][^3][^4][^5]

<sup>3</sup> Do not trust in princes, In mortal man, in whom there is no salvation.[^6][^7][^8][^9][^10]

<sup>4</sup> His spirit departs, he returns to the earth; In that very day his thoughts perish.[^11][^12]

## Psalms 146:5-10

<sup>5</sup> How blessed is he whose help is the God of Jacob, Whose hope is in the LORD his God,[^13][^14]

<sup>6</sup> Who made heaven and earth, The sea and all that is in them; Who keeps faith forever;

<sup>7</sup> Who executes justice for the oppressed; Who gives food to the hungry. The LORD sets the prisoners free.[^15][^16]

<sup>8</sup> The LORD opens [the eyes of] the blind; The LORD raises up those who are bowed down; The LORD loves the righteous;[^17][^18][^19][^20]

<sup>9</sup> The LORD protects the strangers; He supports the fatherless and the widow, But He thwarts the way of the wicked.

<sup>10</sup> The LORD will reign forever, Your God, O Zion, to all generations. Praise the LORD![^21]
