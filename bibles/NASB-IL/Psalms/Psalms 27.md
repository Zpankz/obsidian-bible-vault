## Psalms 27:1-6

<sup>1</sup> [A Psalm] of David. The LORD is my light and my salvation; Whom shall I fear? The LORD is the defense of my life; Whom shall I dread?[^1][^2][^3][^4][^5][^6][^7][^8]

<sup>2</sup> When evildoers came upon me to devour my flesh, My adversaries and my enemies, they stumbled and fell.

<sup>3</sup> Though a host encamp against me, My heart will not fear; Though war arise against me, In [spite of] this I shall be confident.[^9][^10][^11][^12][^13]

<sup>4</sup> One thing I have asked from the LORD, that I shall seek: That I may dwell in the house of the LORD all the days of my life, To behold the beauty of the LORD And to meditate in His temple.[^14][^15][^16][^17][^18][^19][^20][^21]

<sup>5</sup> For in the day of trouble He will conceal me in His tabernacle; In the secret place of His tent He will hide me; He will lift me up on a rock.[^22][^23][^24][^25][^26][^27][^28][^29]

<sup>6</sup> And now my head will be lifted up above my enemies around me, And I will offer in His tent sacrifices with shouts of joy; I will sing, yes, I will sing praises to the LORD.[^30][^31][^32][^33][^34]

## Psalms 27:7-14

<sup>7</sup> Hear, O LORD, when I cry with my voice, And be gracious to me and answer me.

<sup>8</sup> [When You said], 'Seek My face,' my heart said to You, 'Your face, O LORD, I shall seek.'[^35][^36][^37][^38][^39][^40][^41][^42]

<sup>9</sup> Do not hide Your face from me, Do not turn Your servant away in anger; You have been my help; Do not abandon me nor forsake me, O God of my salvation!

<sup>10</sup> For my father and my mother have forsaken me, But the LORD will take me up.[^43][^44][^45][^46][^47]

<sup>11</sup> Teach me Your way, O LORD, And lead me in a level path Because of my foes.[^48][^49][^50][^51][^52][^53][^54]

<sup>12</sup> Do not deliver me over to the desire of my adversaries, For false witnesses have risen against me, And such as breathe out violence.

<sup>13</sup> [I would have despaired] unless I had believed that I would see the goodness of the LORD In the land of the living.[^55][^56][^57][^58][^59][^60][^61][^62]

<sup>14</sup> Wait for the LORD; Be strong and let your heart take courage; Yes, wait for the LORD.[^63][^64][^65][^66][^67][^68][^69][^70]
