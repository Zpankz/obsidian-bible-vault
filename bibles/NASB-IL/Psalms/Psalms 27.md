# Psalms 27

## Psalms 27:1-6

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> [A Psalm] of David. The LORD is my light and my salvation; Whom shall I fear? The LORD is the defense of my life; Whom shall I dread?

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> When evildoers came upon me to devour my flesh, My adversaries and my enemies, they stumbled and fell.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> Though a host encamp against me, My heart will not fear; Though war arise against me, In [spite of] this I shall be confident.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> One thing I have asked from the LORD, that I shall seek: That I may dwell in the house of the LORD all the days of my life, To behold the beauty of the LORD And to meditate in His temple.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> For in the day of trouble He will conceal me in His tabernacle; In the secret place of His tent He will hide me; He will lift me up on a rock.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> And now my head will be lifted up above my enemies around me, And I will offer in His tent sacrifices with shouts of joy; I will sing, yes, I will sing praises to the LORD.

## Psalms 27:7-14

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> Hear, O LORD, when I cry with my voice, And be gracious to me and answer me.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> [When You said], 'Seek My face,' my heart said to You, 'Your face, O LORD, I shall seek.'

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> Do not hide Your face from me, Do not turn Your servant away in anger; You have been my help; Do not abandon me nor forsake me, O God of my salvation!

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> For my father and my mother have forsaken me, But the LORD will take me up.

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> Teach me Your way, O LORD, And lead me in a level path Because of my foes.

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> Do not deliver me over to the desire of my adversaries, For false witnesses have risen against me, And such as breathe out violence.

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> [I would have despaired] unless I had believed that I would see the goodness of the LORD In the land of the living.

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> Wait for the LORD; Be strong and let your heart take courage; Yes, wait for the LORD.
