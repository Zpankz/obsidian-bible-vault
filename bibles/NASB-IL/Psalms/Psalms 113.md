# Psalms 113

## Psalms 113:1-9

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> Praise the LORD! Praise, O servants of the LORD, Praise the name of the LORD.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> Blessed be the name of the LORD From this time forth and forever.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> From the rising of the sun to its setting The name of the LORD is to be praised.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> The LORD is high above all nations; His glory is above the heavens.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> Who is like the LORD our God, Who is enthroned on high,

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> Who humbles Himself to behold [The things that are] in heaven and in the earth?

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> He raises the poor from the dust And lifts the needy from the ash heap,

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> To make [them] sit with princes, With the princes of His people.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> He makes the barren woman abide in the house [As] a joyful mother of children. Praise the LORD!
