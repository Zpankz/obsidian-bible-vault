## Psalms 46:1-5

<sup>1</sup> For the choir director. [A Psalm] of the sons of Korah, set to Alamoth. A Song. God is our refuge and strength, A very present help in trouble.[^1][^2][^3][^4][^5][^6][^7][^8]

<sup>2</sup> Therefore we will not fear, though the earth should change And though the mountains slip into the heart of the sea;[^9][^10][^11][^12][^13][^14]

<sup>3</sup> Though its waters roar [and] foam, Though the mountains quake at its swelling pride. Selah.[^15][^16]

<sup>4</sup> There is a river whose streams make glad the city of God, The holy dwelling places of the Most High.[^17][^18][^19]

<sup>5</sup> God is in the midst of her, she will not be moved; God will help her when morning dawns.[^20][^21][^22][^23][^24][^25][^26][^27]

## Psalms 46:6-11

<sup>6</sup> The nations made an uproar, the kingdoms tottered; He raised His voice, the earth melted.

<sup>7</sup> The LORD of hosts is with us; The God of Jacob is our stronghold. Selah.[^28][^29][^30][^31][^32][^33]

<sup>8</sup> Come, behold the works of the LORD, Who has wrought desolations in the earth.[^34][^35]

<sup>9</sup> He makes wars to cease to the end of the earth; He breaks the bow and cuts the spear in two; He burns the chariots with fire.[^36][^37][^38][^39][^40]

<sup>10</sup> 'Cease [striving] and know that I am God; I will be exalted among the nations, I will be exalted in the earth.'[^41][^42][^43][^44][^45][^46][^47][^48]

<sup>11</sup> The LORD of hosts is with us; The God of Jacob is our stronghold. Selah.[^49]
