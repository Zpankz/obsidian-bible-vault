# Psalms 46

## Psalms 46:1-5

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> For the choir director. [A Psalm] of the sons of Korah, set to Alamoth. A Song. God is our refuge and strength, A very present help in trouble.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> Therefore we will not fear, though the earth should change And though the mountains slip into the heart of the sea;

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> Though its waters roar [and] foam, Though the mountains quake at its swelling pride. Selah.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> There is a river whose streams make glad the city of God, The holy dwelling places of the Most High.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> God is in the midst of her, she will not be moved; God will help her when morning dawns.

## Psalms 46:6-11

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> The nations made an uproar, the kingdoms tottered; He raised His voice, the earth melted.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> The LORD of hosts is with us; The God of Jacob is our stronghold. Selah.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> Come, behold the works of the LORD, Who has wrought desolations in the earth.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> He makes wars to cease to the end of the earth; He breaks the bow and cuts the spear in two; He burns the chariots with fire.

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> 'Cease [striving] and know that I am God; I will be exalted among the nations, I will be exalted in the earth.'

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> The LORD of hosts is with us; The God of Jacob is our stronghold. Selah.
