# Psalms 144

## Psalms 144:1-8

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> [A Psalm] of David. Blessed be the LORD, my rock, Who trains my hands for war, [And] my fingers for battle;

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> My lovingkindness and my fortress, My stronghold and my deliverer, My shield and He in whom I take refuge, Who subdues my people under me.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> O LORD, what is man, that You take knowledge of him? Or the son of man, that You think of him?

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> Man is like a mere breath; His days are like a passing shadow.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> Bow Your heavens, O LORD, and come down; Touch the mountains, that they may smoke.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> Flash forth lightning and scatter them; Send out Your arrows and confuse them.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> Stretch forth Your hand from on high; Rescue me and deliver me out of great waters, Out of the hand of aliens

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> Whose mouths speak deceit, And whose right hand is a right hand of falsehood.

## Psalms 144:9-15

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> I will sing a new song to You, O God; Upon a harp of ten strings I will sing praises to You,

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> Who gives salvation to kings, Who rescues David His servant from the evil sword.

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> Rescue me and deliver me out of the hand of aliens, Whose mouth speaks deceit And whose right hand is a right hand of falsehood.

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> Let our sons in their youth be as grown-up plants, And our daughters as corner pillars fashioned as for a palace;

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> Let our garners be full, furnishing every kind of produce, [And] our flocks bring forth thousands and ten thousands in our fields;

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> Let our cattle bear Without mishap and without loss, [Let there be] no outcry in our streets!

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> How blessed are the people who are so situated; How blessed are the people whose God is the LORD!
