# Psalms 122

## Psalms 122:1-5

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> A Song of Ascents, of David. I was glad when they said to me, 'Let us go to the house of the LORD.'

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> Our feet are standing Within your gates, O Jerusalem,

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> Jerusalem, that is built As a city that is compact together;

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> To which the tribes go up, even the tribes of the LORD-- An ordinance for Israel-- To give thanks to the name of the LORD.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> For there thrones were set for judgment, The thrones of the house of David.

## Psalms 122:6-9

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> Pray for the peace of Jerusalem: 'May they prosper who love you.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> 'May peace be within your walls, And prosperity within your palaces.'

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> For the sake of my brothers and my friends, I will now say, 'May peace be within you.'

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> For the sake of the house of the LORD our God, I will seek your good.
