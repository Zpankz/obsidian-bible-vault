# Psalms 104

## Psalms 104:1-9

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> Bless the LORD, O my soul! O LORD my God, You are very great; You are clothed with splendor and majesty,

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> Covering Yourself with light as with a cloak, Stretching out heaven like a [tent] curtain.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> He lays the beams of His upper chambers in the waters; He makes the clouds His chariot; He walks upon the wings of the wind;

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> He makes the winds His messengers, Flaming fire His ministers.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> He established the earth upon its foundations, So that it will not totter forever and ever.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> You covered it with the deep as with a garment; The waters were standing above the mountains.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> At Your rebuke they fled, At the sound of Your thunder they hurried away.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> The mountains rose; the valleys sank down To the place which You established for them.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> You set a boundary that they may not pass over, So that they will not return to cover the earth.

## Psalms 104:10-18

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> He sends forth springs in the valleys; They flow between the mountains;

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> They give drink to every beast of the field; The wild donkeys quench their thirst.

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> Beside them the birds of the heavens dwell; They lift up [their] voices among the branches.

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> He waters the mountains from His upper chambers; The earth is satisfied with the fruit of His works.

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> He causes the grass to grow for the cattle, And vegetation for the labor of man, So that he may bring forth food from the earth,

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> And wine which makes man's heart glad, So that he may make [his] face glisten with oil, And food which sustains man's heart.

###### v16
<sup>16</sup> The trees of the LORD drink their fill, The cedars of Lebanon which He planted,

###### v17
<sup>17</sup> Where the birds build their nests, [And] the stork, whose home is the fir trees.

###### v18
<sup>18</sup> The high mountains are for the wild goats; The cliffs are a refuge for the shephanim.

## Psalms 104:19-30

###### v19
<sup>19</sup> He made the moon for the seasons; The sun knows the place of its setting.

###### v20
<sup>20</sup> You appoint darkness and it becomes night, In which all the beasts of the forest prowl about.

###### v21
<sup>21</sup> The young lions roar after their prey And seek their food from God.

###### v22
<sup>22</sup> [When] the sun rises they withdraw And lie down in their dens.

###### v23
<sup>23</sup> Man goes forth to his work And to his labor until evening.

###### v24
<sup>24</sup> O LORD, how many are Your works! In wisdom You have made them all; The earth is full of Your possessions.

###### v25
<sup>25</sup> There is the sea, great and broad, In which are swarms without number, Animals both small and great.

###### v26
<sup>26</sup> There the ships move along, [And] Leviathan, which You have formed to sport in it.

###### v27
<sup>27</sup> They all wait for You To give them their food in due season.

###### v28
<sup>28</sup> You give to them, they gather [it] up; You open Your hand, they are satisfied with good.

###### v29
<sup>29</sup> You hide Your face, they are dismayed; You take away their spirit, they expire And return to their dust.

###### v30
<sup>30</sup> You send forth Your Spirit, they are created; And You renew the face of the ground.

## Psalms 104:31-35

###### v31
<sup>31</sup> Let the glory of the LORD endure forever; Let the LORD be glad in His works;

###### v32
<sup>32</sup> He looks at the earth, and it trembles; He touches the mountains, and they smoke.

###### v33
<sup>33</sup> I will sing to the LORD as long as I live; I will sing praise to my God while I have my being.

###### v34
<sup>34</sup> Let my meditation be pleasing to Him; As for me, I shall be glad in the LORD.

###### v35
<sup>35</sup> Let sinners be consumed from the earth And let the wicked be no more. Bless the LORD, O my soul. Praise the LORD!
