# Psalms 86

## Psalms 86:1-7

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> A Prayer of David. Incline Your ear, O LORD, [and] answer me; For I am afflicted and needy.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> Preserve my soul, for I am a godly man; O You my God, save Your servant who trusts in You.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> Be gracious to me, O Lord, For to You I cry all day long.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> Make glad the soul of Your servant, For to You, O Lord, I lift up my soul.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> For You, Lord, are good, and ready to forgive, And abundant in lovingkindness to all who call upon You.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> Give ear, O LORD, to my prayer; And give heed to the voice of my supplications!

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> In the day of my trouble I shall call upon You, For You will answer me.

## Psalms 86:8-17

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> There is no one like You among the gods, O Lord, Nor are there any works like Yours.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> All nations whom You have made shall come and worship before You, O Lord, And they shall glorify Your name.

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> For You are great and do wondrous deeds; You alone are God.

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> Teach me Your way, O LORD; I will walk in Your truth; Unite my heart to fear Your name.

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> I will give thanks to You, O Lord my God, with all my heart, And will glorify Your name forever.

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> For Your lovingkindness toward me is great, And You have delivered my soul from the depths of Sheol.

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> O God, arrogant men have risen up against me, And a band of violent men have sought my life, And they have not set You before them.

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> But You, O Lord, are a God merciful and gracious, Slow to anger and abundant in lovingkindness and truth.

###### v16
<sup>16</sup> Turn to me, and be gracious to me; Oh grant Your strength to Your servant, And save the son of Your handmaid.

###### v17
<sup>17</sup> Show me a sign for good, That those who hate me may see [it] and be ashamed, Because You, O LORD, have helped me and comforted me.
