## Psalms 128:1-6

<sup>1</sup> A Song of Ascents. How blessed is everyone who fears the LORD, Who walks in His ways.[^1][^2][^3][^4][^5][^6][^7][^8]

<sup>2</sup> When you shall eat of the fruit of your hands, You will be happy and it will be well with you.[^9][^10][^11][^12][^13][^14][^15][^16]

<sup>3</sup> Your wife shall be like a fruitful vine Within your house, Your children like olive plants Around your table.[^17][^18][^19][^20][^21][^22][^23][^24]

<sup>4</sup> Behold, for thus shall the man be blessed Who fears the LORD.

<sup>5</sup> The LORD bless you from Zion, And may you see the prosperity of Jerusalem all the days of your life.[^25][^26]

<sup>6</sup> Indeed, may you see your children's children. Peace be upon Israel![^27][^28][^29][^30][^31][^32]
