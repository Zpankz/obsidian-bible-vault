# Psalms 128

## Psalms 128:1-6

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> A Song of Ascents. How blessed is everyone who fears the LORD, Who walks in His ways.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> When you shall eat of the fruit of your hands, You will be happy and it will be well with you.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> Your wife shall be like a fruitful vine Within your house, Your children like olive plants Around your table.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> Behold, for thus shall the man be blessed Who fears the LORD.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> The LORD bless you from Zion, And may you see the prosperity of Jerusalem all the days of your life.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> Indeed, may you see your children's children. Peace be upon Israel!
