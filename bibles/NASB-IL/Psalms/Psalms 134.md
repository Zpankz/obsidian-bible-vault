# Psalms 134

## Psalms 134:1-3

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> A Song of Ascents. Behold, bless the LORD, all servants of the LORD, Who serve by night in the house of the LORD!

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> Lift up your hands to the sanctuary And bless the LORD.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> May the LORD bless you from Zion, He who made heaven and earth.
