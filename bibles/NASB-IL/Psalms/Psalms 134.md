## Psalms 134:1-3

<sup>1</sup> A Song of Ascents. Behold, bless the LORD, all servants of the LORD, Who serve by night in the house of the LORD!

<sup>2</sup> Lift up your hands to the sanctuary And bless the LORD.[^1][^2]

<sup>3</sup> May the LORD bless you from Zion, He who made heaven and earth.[^3]
