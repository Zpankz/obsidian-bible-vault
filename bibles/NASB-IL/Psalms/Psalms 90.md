# Psalms 90

## Psalms 90:1-6

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> A Prayer of Moses, the man of God. Lord, You have been our dwelling place in all generations.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> Before the mountains were born Or You gave birth to the earth and the world, Even from everlasting to everlasting, You are God.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> You turn man back into dust And say, 'Return, O children of men.'

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> For a thousand years in Your sight Are like yesterday when it passes by, Or [as] a watch in the night.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> You have swept them away like a flood, they fall asleep; In the morning they are like grass which sprouts anew.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> In the morning it flourishes and sprouts anew; Toward evening it fades and withers away.

## Psalms 90:7-11

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> For we have been consumed by Your anger And by Your wrath we have been dismayed.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> You have placed our iniquities before You, Our secret [sins] in the light of Your presence.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> For all our days have declined in Your fury; We have finished our years like a sigh.

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> As for the days of our life, they contain seventy years, Or if due to strength, eighty years, Yet their pride is [but] labor and sorrow; For soon it is gone and we fly away.

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> Who understands the power of Your anger And Your fury, according to the fear that is due You?

## Psalms 90:12-17

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> So teach us to number our days, That we may present to You a heart of wisdom.

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> Do return, O LORD; how long [will it be]? And be sorry for Your servants.

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> O satisfy us in the morning with Your lovingkindness, That we may sing for joy and be glad all our days.

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> Make us glad according to the days You have afflicted us, [And] the years we have seen evil.

###### v16
<sup>16</sup> Let Your work appear to Your servants And Your majesty to their children.

###### v17
<sup>17</sup> Let the favor of the Lord our God be upon us; And confirm for us the work of our hands; Yes, confirm the work of our hands.
