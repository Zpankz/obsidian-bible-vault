## Psalms 5:1-6

<sup>1</sup> For the choir director; for flute accompaniment. A Psalm of David. Give ear to my words, O LORD, Consider my groaning.[^1][^2][^3][^4][^5]

<sup>2</sup> Heed the sound of my cry for help, my King and my God, For to You I pray.

<sup>3</sup> In the morning, O LORD, You will hear my voice; In the morning I will order [my] [prayer] to You and [eagerly] watch.[^6][^7][^8][^9][^10][^11][^12]

<sup>4</sup> For You are not a God who takes pleasure in wickedness; No evil dwells with You.[^13][^14]

<sup>5</sup> The boastful shall not stand before Your eyes; You hate all who do iniquity.[^15][^16][^17][^18]

<sup>6</sup> You destroy those who speak falsehood; The LORD abhors the man of bloodshed and deceit.

## Psalms 5:7-12

<sup>7</sup> But as for me, by Your abundant lovingkindness I will enter Your house, At Your holy temple I will bow in reverence for You.[^19][^20][^21]

<sup>8</sup> O LORD, lead me in Your righteousness because of my foes; Make Your way straight before me.[^22][^23][^24][^25][^26][^27]

<sup>9</sup> There is nothing reliable in what they say; Their inward part is destruction [itself]. Their throat is an open grave; They flatter with their tongue.[^28][^29]

<sup>10</sup> Hold them guilty, O God; By their own devices let them fall! In the multitude of their transgressions thrust them out, For they are rebellious against You.

<sup>11</sup> But let all who take refuge in You be glad, Let them ever sing for joy; And may You shelter them, That those who love Your name may exult in You.[^30][^31][^32][^33][^34][^35]

<sup>12</sup> For it is You who blesses the righteous man, O LORD, You surround him with favor as with a shield.[^36][^37][^38][^39][^40][^41][^42][^43]
