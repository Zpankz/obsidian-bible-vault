# Psalms 5

## Psalms 5:1-6

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> For the choir director; for flute accompaniment. A Psalm of David. Give ear to my words, O LORD, Consider my groaning.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> Heed the sound of my cry for help, my King and my God, For to You I pray.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> In the morning, O LORD, You will hear my voice; In the morning I will order [my] [prayer] to You and [eagerly] watch.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> For You are not a God who takes pleasure in wickedness; No evil dwells with You.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> The boastful shall not stand before Your eyes; You hate all who do iniquity.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> You destroy those who speak falsehood; The LORD abhors the man of bloodshed and deceit.

## Psalms 5:7-12

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> But as for me, by Your abundant lovingkindness I will enter Your house, At Your holy temple I will bow in reverence for You.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> O LORD, lead me in Your righteousness because of my foes; Make Your way straight before me.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> There is nothing reliable in what they say; Their inward part is destruction [itself]. Their throat is an open grave; They flatter with their tongue.

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> Hold them guilty, O God; By their own devices let them fall! In the multitude of their transgressions thrust them out, For they are rebellious against You.

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> But let all who take refuge in You be glad, Let them ever sing for joy; And may You shelter them, That those who love Your name may exult in You.

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> For it is You who blesses the righteous man, O LORD, You surround him with favor as with a shield.
