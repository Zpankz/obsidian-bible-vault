# Psalms 85

## Psalms 85:1-7

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> For the choir director. A Psalm of the sons of Korah. O LORD, You showed favor to Your land; You restored the captivity of Jacob.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> You forgave the iniquity of Your people; You covered all their sin. Selah.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> You withdrew all Your fury; You turned away from Your burning anger.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> Restore us, O God of our salvation, And cause Your indignation toward us to cease.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> Will You be angry with us forever? Will You prolong Your anger to all generations?

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> Will You not Yourself revive us again, That Your people may rejoice in You?

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> Show us Your lovingkindness, O LORD, And grant us Your salvation.

## Psalms 85:8-13

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> I will hear what God the LORD will say; For He will speak peace to His people, to His godly ones; But let them not turn back to folly.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> Surely His salvation is near to those who fear Him, That glory may dwell in our land.

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> Lovingkindness and truth have met together; Righteousness and peace have kissed each other.

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> Truth springs from the earth, And righteousness looks down from heaven.

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> Indeed, the LORD will give what is good, And our land will yield its produce.

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> Righteousness will go before Him And will make His footsteps into a way.
