## Psalms 141:1-10

<sup>1</sup> A Psalm of David. O LORD, I call upon You; hasten to me! Give ear to my voice when I call to You![^1]

<sup>2</sup> May my prayer be counted as incense before You; The lifting up of my hands as the evening offering.[^2][^3][^4][^5][^6][^7][^8][^9]

<sup>3</sup> Set a guard, O LORD, over my mouth; Keep watch over the door of my lips.[^10][^11][^12][^13][^14][^15][^16]

<sup>4</sup> Do not incline my heart to any evil thing, To practice deeds of wickedness With men who do iniquity; And do not let me eat of their delicacies.[^17][^18][^19][^20][^21][^22]

<sup>5</sup> Let the righteous smite me in kindness and reprove me; It is oil upon the head; Do not let my head refuse it, For still my prayer is against their wicked deeds.[^23][^24][^25][^26]

<sup>6</sup> Their judges are thrown down by the sides of the rock, And they hear my words, for they are pleasant.

<sup>7</sup> As when one plows and breaks open the earth, Our bones have been scattered at the mouth of Sheol.

<sup>8</sup> For my eyes are toward You, O GOD, the Lord; In You I take refuge; do not leave me defenseless.[^27]

<sup>9</sup> Keep me from the jaws of the trap which they have set for me, And from the snares of those who do iniquity.

<sup>10</sup> Let the wicked fall into their own nets, While I pass by safely.
