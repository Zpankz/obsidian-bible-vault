# Psalms 141

## Psalms 141:1-10

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> A Psalm of David. O LORD, I call upon You; hasten to me! Give ear to my voice when I call to You!

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> May my prayer be counted as incense before You; The lifting up of my hands as the evening offering.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> Set a guard, O LORD, over my mouth; Keep watch over the door of my lips.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> Do not incline my heart to any evil thing, To practice deeds of wickedness With men who do iniquity; And do not let me eat of their delicacies.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> Let the righteous smite me in kindness and reprove me; It is oil upon the head; Do not let my head refuse it, For still my prayer is against their wicked deeds.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> Their judges are thrown down by the sides of the rock, And they hear my words, for they are pleasant.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> As when one plows and breaks open the earth, Our bones have been scattered at the mouth of Sheol.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> For my eyes are toward You, O GOD, the Lord; In You I take refuge; do not leave me defenseless.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> Keep me from the jaws of the trap which they have set for me, And from the snares of those who do iniquity.

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> Let the wicked fall into their own nets, While I pass by safely.
