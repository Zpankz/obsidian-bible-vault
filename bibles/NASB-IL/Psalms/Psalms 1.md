## Psalms 1:1-3

<sup>1</sup> How blessed is the man who does not walk in the counsel of the wicked, Nor stand in the path of sinners, Nor sit in the seat of scoffers![^1][^2][^3][^4][^5][^6][^7][^8]

<sup>2</sup> But his delight is in the law of the LORD, And in His law he meditates day and night.[^9][^10][^11][^12][^13][^14][^15][^16]

<sup>3</sup> He will be like a tree [firmly] planted by streams of water, Which yields its fruit in its season And its leaf does not wither; And in whatever he does, he prospers.[^17][^18][^19][^20][^21][^22][^23][^24]

## Psalms 1:4-6

<sup>4</sup> The wicked are not so, But they are like chaff which the wind drives away.[^25][^26][^27][^28][^29][^30]

<sup>5</sup> Therefore the wicked will not stand in the judgment, Nor sinners in the assembly of the righteous.[^31][^32][^33][^34][^35][^36][^37]

<sup>6</sup> For the LORD knows the way of the righteous, But the way of the wicked will perish.[^38][^39][^40][^41][^42][^43][^44][^45]
