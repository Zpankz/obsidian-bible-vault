## Psalms 24:3-6

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> Who may ascend into the hill of the LORD? And who may stand in His holy place?

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> He who has clean hands and a pure heart, Who has not lifted up his soul to falsehood And has not sworn deceitfully.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> He shall receive a blessing from the LORD And righteousness from the God of his salvation.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> This is the generation of those who seek Him, Who seek Your face-- [even] Jacob. Selah.

## Psalms 24:7-10

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> Lift up your heads, O gates, And be lifted up, O ancient doors, That the King of glory may come in!

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> Who is the King of glory? The LORD strong and mighty, The LORD mighty in battle.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> Lift up your heads, O gates, And lift [them] up, O ancient doors, That the King of glory may come in!

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> Who is this King of glory? The LORD of hosts, He is the King of glory. Selah.
