# Psalms 7

## Psalms 7:1-9

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> A Shiggaion of David, which he sang to the LORD concerning Cush, a Benjamite. O LORD my God, in You I have taken refuge; Save me from all those who pursue me, and deliver me,

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> Or he will tear my soul like a lion, Dragging me away, while there is none to deliver.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> O LORD my God, if I have done this, If there is injustice in my hands,

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> If I have rewarded evil to my friend, Or have plundered him who without cause was my adversary,

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> Let the enemy pursue my soul and overtake [it]; And let him trample my life down to the ground And lay my glory in the dust. Selah.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> Arise, O LORD, in Your anger; Lift up Yourself against the rage of my adversaries, And arouse Yourself for me; You have appointed judgment.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> Let the assembly of the peoples encompass You, And over them return on high.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> The LORD judges the peoples; Vindicate me, O LORD, according to my righteousness and my integrity that is in me.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> O let the evil of the wicked come to an end, but establish the righteous; For the righteous God tries the hearts and minds.

## Psalms 7:10-17

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> My shield is with God, Who saves the upright in heart.

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> God is a righteous judge, And a God who has indignation every day.

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> If a man does not repent, He will sharpen His sword; He has bent His bow and made it ready.

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> He has also prepared for Himself deadly weapons; He makes His arrows fiery shafts.

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> Behold, he travails with wickedness, And he conceives mischief and brings forth falsehood.

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> He has dug a pit and hollowed it out, And has fallen into the hole which he made.

###### v16
<sup>16</sup> His mischief will return upon his own head, And his violence will descend upon his own pate.

###### v17
<sup>17</sup> I will give thanks to the LORD according to His righteousness And will sing praise to the name of the LORD Most High.
