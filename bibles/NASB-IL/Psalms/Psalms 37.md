# Psalms 37

## Psalms 37:1-6

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> [A Psalm] of David. Do not fret because of evildoers, Be not envious toward wrongdoers.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> For they will wither quickly like the grass And fade like the green herb.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> Trust in the LORD and do good; Dwell in the land and cultivate faithfulness.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> Delight yourself in the LORD; And He will give you the desires of your heart.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> Commit your way to the LORD, Trust also in Him, and He will do it.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> He will bring forth your righteousness as the light And your judgment as the noonday.

## Psalms 37:7-20

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> Rest in the LORD and wait patiently for Him; Do not fret because of him who prospers in his way, Because of the man who carries out wicked schemes.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> Cease from anger and forsake wrath; Do not fret; [it leads] only to evildoing.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> For evildoers will be cut off, But those who wait for the LORD, they will inherit the land.

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> Yet a little while and the wicked man will be no more; And you will look carefully for his place and he will not be [there].

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> But the humble will inherit the land And will delight themselves in abundant prosperity.

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> The wicked plots against the righteous And gnashes at him with his teeth.

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> The Lord laughs at him, For He sees his day is coming.

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> The wicked have drawn the sword and bent their bow To cast down the afflicted and the needy, To slay those who are upright in conduct.

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> Their sword will enter their own heart, And their bows will be broken.

###### v16
<sup>16</sup> Better is the little of the righteous Than the abundance of many wicked.

###### v17
<sup>17</sup> For the arms of the wicked will be broken, But the LORD sustains the righteous.

###### v18
<sup>18</sup> The LORD knows the days of the blameless, And their inheritance will be forever.

###### v19
<sup>19</sup> They will not be ashamed in the time of evil, And in the days of famine they will have abundance.

###### v20
<sup>20</sup> But the wicked will perish; And the enemies of the LORD will be like the glory of the pastures, They vanish-- like smoke they vanish away.

## Psalms 37:21-33

###### v21
<sup>21</sup> The wicked borrows and does not pay back, But the righteous is gracious and gives.

###### v22
<sup>22</sup> For those blessed by Him will inherit the land, But those cursed by Him will be cut off.

###### v23
<sup>23</sup> The steps of a man are established by the LORD, And He delights in his way.

###### v24
<sup>24</sup> When he falls, he will not be hurled headlong, Because the LORD is the One who holds his hand.

###### v25
<sup>25</sup> I have been young and now I am old, Yet I have not seen the righteous forsaken Or his descendants begging bread.

###### v26
<sup>26</sup> All day long he is gracious and lends, And his descendants are a blessing.

###### v27
<sup>27</sup> Depart from evil and do good, So you will abide forever.

###### v28
<sup>28</sup> For the LORD loves justice And does not forsake His godly ones; They are preserved forever, But the descendants of the wicked will be cut off.

###### v29
<sup>29</sup> The righteous will inherit the land And dwell in it forever.

###### v30
<sup>30</sup> The mouth of the righteous utters wisdom, And his tongue speaks justice.

###### v31
<sup>31</sup> The law of his God is in his heart; His steps do not slip.

###### v32
<sup>32</sup> The wicked spies upon the righteous And seeks to kill him.

###### v33
<sup>33</sup> The LORD will not leave him in his hand Or let him be condemned when he is judged.

## Psalms 37:34-40

###### v34
<sup>34</sup> Wait for the LORD and keep His way, And He will exalt you to inherit the land; When the wicked are cut off, you will see it.

###### v35
<sup>35</sup> I have seen a wicked, violent man Spreading himself like a luxuriant tree in its native soil.

###### v36
<sup>36</sup> Then he passed away, and lo, he was no more; I sought for him, but he could not be found.

###### v37
<sup>37</sup> Mark the blameless man, and behold the upright; For the man of peace will have a posterity.

###### v38
<sup>38</sup> But transgressors will be altogether destroyed; The posterity of the wicked will be cut off.

###### v39
<sup>39</sup> But the salvation of the righteous is from the LORD; He is their strength in time of trouble.

###### v40
<sup>40</sup> The LORD helps them and delivers them; He delivers them from the wicked and saves them, Because they take refuge in Him.
