# Psalms 61

## Psalms 61:1-4

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> For the choir director; on a stringed instrument. [A Psalm] of David. Hear my cry, O God; Give heed to my prayer.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> From the end of the earth I call to You when my heart is faint; Lead me to the rock that is higher than I.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> For You have been a refuge for me, A tower of strength against the enemy.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> Let me dwell in Your tent forever; Let me take refuge in the shelter of Your wings. Selah.

## Psalms 61:5-8

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> For You have heard my vows, O God; You have given [me] the inheritance of those who fear Your name.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> You will prolong the king's life; His years will be as many generations.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> He will abide before God forever; Appoint lovingkindness and truth that they may preserve him.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> So I will sing praise to Your name forever, That I may pay my vows day by day.
