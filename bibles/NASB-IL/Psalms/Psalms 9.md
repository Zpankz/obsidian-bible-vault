# Psalms 9

## Psalms 9:1-10

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> For the choir director; on Muth-labben. A Psalm of David. I will give thanks to the LORD with all my heart; I will tell of all Your wonders.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> I will be glad and exult in You; I will sing praise to Your name, O Most High.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> When my enemies turn back, They stumble and perish before You.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> For You have maintained my just cause; You have sat on the throne judging righteously.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> You have rebuked the nations, You have destroyed the wicked; You have blotted out their name forever and ever.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> The enemy has come to an end in perpetual ruins, And You have uprooted the cities; The very memory of them has perished.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> But the LORD abides forever; He has established His throne for judgment,

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> And He will judge the world in righteousness; He will execute judgment for the peoples with equity.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> The LORD also will be a stronghold for the oppressed, A stronghold in times of trouble;

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> And those who know Your name will put their trust in You, For You, O LORD, have not forsaken those who seek You.

## Psalms 9:11-20

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> Sing praises to the LORD, who dwells in Zion; Declare among the peoples His deeds.

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> For He who requires blood remembers them; He does not forget the cry of the afflicted.

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> Be gracious to me, O LORD; See my affliction from those who hate me, You who lift me up from the gates of death,

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> That I may tell of all Your praises, That in the gates of the daughter of Zion I may rejoice in Your salvation.

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> The nations have sunk down in the pit which they have made; In the net which they hid, their own foot has been caught.

###### v16
<sup>16</sup> The LORD has made Himself known; He has executed judgment. In the work of his own hands the wicked is snared. Higgaion Selah.

###### v17
<sup>17</sup> The wicked will return to Sheol, [Even] all the nations who forget God.

###### v18
<sup>18</sup> For the needy will not always be forgotten, Nor the hope of the afflicted perish forever.

###### v19
<sup>19</sup> Arise, O LORD, do not let man prevail; Let the nations be judged before You.

###### v20
<sup>20</sup> Put them in fear, O LORD; Let the nations know that they are but men. Selah.
