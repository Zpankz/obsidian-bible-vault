# Psalms 130

## Psalms 130:1-4

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> A Song of Ascents. Out of the depths I have cried to You, O LORD.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> Lord, hear my voice! Let Your ears be attentive To the voice of my supplications.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> If You, LORD, should mark iniquities, O Lord, who could stand?

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> But there is forgiveness with You, That You may be feared.

## Psalms 130:5-8

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> I wait for the LORD, my soul does wait, And in His word do I hope.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> My soul [waits] for the Lord More than the watchmen for the morning; [Indeed, more than] the watchmen for the morning.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> O Israel, hope in the LORD; For with the LORD there is lovingkindness, And with Him is abundant redemption.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> And He will redeem Israel From all his iniquities.
