## Psalms 112:1-5

<sup>1</sup> Praise the LORD! How blessed is the man who fears the LORD, Who greatly delights in His commandments.[^1][^2][^3][^4][^5][^6][^7][^8]

<sup>2</sup> His descendants will be mighty on earth; The generation of the upright will be blessed.[^9][^10][^11][^12][^13][^14][^15][^16]

<sup>3</sup> Wealth and riches are in his house, And his righteousness endures forever.[^17][^18][^19][^20][^21][^22]

<sup>4</sup> Light arises in the darkness for the upright; [He is] gracious and compassionate and righteous.[^23][^24][^25]

<sup>5</sup> It is well with the man who is gracious and lends; He will maintain his cause in judgment.[^26]

## Psalms 112:6-10

<sup>6</sup> For he will never be shaken; The righteous will be remembered forever.[^27]

<sup>7</sup> He will not fear evil tidings; His heart is steadfast, trusting in the LORD.[^28][^29][^30][^31][^32][^33][^34]

<sup>8</sup> His heart is upheld, he will not fear, Until he looks [with satisfaction] on his adversaries.

<sup>9</sup> He has given freely to the poor, His righteousness endures forever; His horn will be exalted in honor.[^35]

<sup>10</sup> The wicked will see it and be vexed, He will gnash his teeth and melt away; The desire of the wicked will perish.
