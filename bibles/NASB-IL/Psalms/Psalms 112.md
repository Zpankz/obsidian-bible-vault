# Psalms 112

## Psalms 112:1-5

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> Praise the LORD! How blessed is the man who fears the LORD, Who greatly delights in His commandments.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> His descendants will be mighty on earth; The generation of the upright will be blessed.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> Wealth and riches are in his house, And his righteousness endures forever.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> Light arises in the darkness for the upright; [He is] gracious and compassionate and righteous.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> It is well with the man who is gracious and lends; He will maintain his cause in judgment.

## Psalms 112:6-10

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> For he will never be shaken; The righteous will be remembered forever.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> He will not fear evil tidings; His heart is steadfast, trusting in the LORD.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> His heart is upheld, he will not fear, Until he looks [with satisfaction] on his adversaries.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> He has given freely to the poor, His righteousness endures forever; His horn will be exalted in honor.

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> The wicked will see it and be vexed, He will gnash his teeth and melt away; The desire of the wicked will perish.
