# Psalms 43

## Psalms 43:1-5

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> Vindicate me, O God, and plead my case against an ungodly nation; O deliver me from the deceitful and unjust man!

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> For You are the God of my strength; why have You rejected me? Why do I go mourning because of the oppression of the enemy?

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> O send out Your light and Your truth, let them lead me; Let them bring me to Your holy hill And to Your dwelling places.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> Then I will go to the altar of God, To God my exceeding joy; And upon the lyre I shall praise You, O God, my God.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> Why are you in despair, O my soul? And why are you disturbed within me? Hope in God, for I shall again praise Him, The help of my countenance and my God.
