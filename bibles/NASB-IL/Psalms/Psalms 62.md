## Psalms 62:1-7

<sup>1</sup> For the choir director; according to Jeduthun. A Psalm of David. My soul [waits] in silence for God only; From Him is my salvation.[^1][^2][^3][^4][^5][^6][^7][^8]

<sup>2</sup> He only is my rock and my salvation, My stronghold; I shall not be greatly shaken.[^9][^10][^11][^12]

<sup>3</sup> How long will you assail a man, That you may murder [him], all of you, Like a leaning wall, like a tottering fence?

<sup>4</sup> They have counseled only to thrust him down from his high position; They delight in falsehood; They bless with their mouth, But inwardly they curse. Selah.[^13][^14]

<sup>5</sup> My soul, wait in silence for God only, For my hope is from Him.[^15][^16][^17][^18][^19][^20][^21][^22]

<sup>6</sup> He only is my rock and my salvation, My stronghold; I shall not be shaken.[^23][^24][^25]

<sup>7</sup> On God my salvation and my glory [rest]; The rock of my strength, my refuge is in God.[^26][^27][^28][^29]

## Psalms 62:8-12

<sup>8</sup> Trust in Him at all times, O people; Pour out your heart before Him; God is a refuge for us. Selah.[^30][^31][^32][^33][^34][^35][^36][^37]

<sup>9</sup> Men of low degree are only vanity and men of rank are a lie; In the balances they go up; They are together lighter than breath.[^38][^39][^40]

<sup>10</sup> Do not trust in oppression And do not vainly hope in robbery; If riches increase, do not set [your] heart [upon them].[^41][^42]

<sup>11</sup> Once God has spoken; Twice I have heard this: That power belongs to God;[^43][^44][^45][^46][^47][^48]

<sup>12</sup> And lovingkindness is Yours, O Lord, For You recompense a man according to his work.[^49]
