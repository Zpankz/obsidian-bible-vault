# Psalms 62

## Psalms 62:1-7

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> For the choir director; according to Jeduthun. A Psalm of David. My soul [waits] in silence for God only; From Him is my salvation.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> He only is my rock and my salvation, My stronghold; I shall not be greatly shaken.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> How long will you assail a man, That you may murder [him], all of you, Like a leaning wall, like a tottering fence?

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> They have counseled only to thrust him down from his high position; They delight in falsehood; They bless with their mouth, But inwardly they curse. Selah.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> My soul, wait in silence for God only, For my hope is from Him.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> He only is my rock and my salvation, My stronghold; I shall not be shaken.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> On God my salvation and my glory [rest]; The rock of my strength, my refuge is in God.

## Psalms 62:8-12

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> Trust in Him at all times, O people; Pour out your heart before Him; God is a refuge for us. Selah.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> Men of low degree are only vanity and men of rank are a lie; In the balances they go up; They are together lighter than breath.

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> Do not trust in oppression And do not vainly hope in robbery; If riches increase, do not set [your] heart [upon them].

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> Once God has spoken; Twice I have heard this: That power belongs to God;

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> And lovingkindness is Yours, O Lord, For You recompense a man according to his work.
