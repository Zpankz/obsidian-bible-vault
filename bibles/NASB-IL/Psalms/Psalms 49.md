# Psalms 49

## Psalms 49:1-5

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> For the choir director. A Psalm of the sons of Korah. Hear this, all peoples; Give ear, all inhabitants of the world,

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> Both low and high, Rich and poor together.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> My mouth will speak wisdom, And the meditation of my heart [will be] understanding.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> I will incline my ear to a proverb; I will express my riddle on the harp.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> Why should I fear in days of adversity, When the iniquity of my foes surrounds me,

## Psalms 49:6-14

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> Even those who trust in their wealth And boast in the abundance of their riches?

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> No man can by any means redeem [his] brother Or give to God a ransom for him--

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> For the redemption of his soul is costly, And he should cease [trying] forever--

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> That he should live on eternally, That he should not undergo decay.

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> For he sees [that even] wise men die; The stupid and the senseless alike perish And leave their wealth to others.

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> Their inner thought is [that] their houses are forever [And] their dwelling places to all generations; They have called their lands after their own names.

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> But man in [his] pomp will not endure; He is like the beasts that perish.

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> This is the way of those who are foolish, And of those after them who approve their words. Selah.

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> As sheep they are appointed for Sheol; Death shall be their shepherd; And the upright shall rule over them in the morning, And their form shall be for Sheol to consume So that they have no habitation.

## Psalms 49:15-20

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> But God will redeem my soul from the power of Sheol, For He will receive me. Selah.

###### v16
<sup>16</sup> Do not be afraid when a man becomes rich, When the glory of his house is increased;

###### v17
<sup>17</sup> For when he dies he will carry nothing away; His glory will not descend after him.

###### v18
<sup>18</sup> Though while he lives he congratulates himself-- And though [men] praise you when you do well for yourself--

###### v19
<sup>19</sup> He shall go to the generation of his fathers; They will never see the light.

###### v20
<sup>20</sup> Man in [his] pomp, yet without understanding, Is like the beasts that perish.
