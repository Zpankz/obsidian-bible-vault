# Psalms 88

## Psalms 88:1-9

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> A Song. A Psalm of the sons of Korah. For the choir director; according to Mahalath Leannoth. A Maskil of Heman the Ezrahite. O LORD, the God of my salvation, I have cried out by day and in the night before You.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> Let my prayer come before You; Incline Your ear to my cry!

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> For my soul has had enough troubles, And my life has drawn near to Sheol.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> I am reckoned among those who go down to the pit; I have become like a man without strength,

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> Forsaken among the dead, Like the slain who lie in the grave, Whom You remember no more, And they are cut off from Your hand.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> You have put me in the lowest pit, In dark places, in the depths.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> Your wrath has rested upon me, And You have afflicted me with all Your waves. Selah.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> You have removed my acquaintances far from me; You have made me an object of loathing to them; I am shut up and cannot go out.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> My eye has wasted away because of affliction; I have called upon You every day, O LORD; I have spread out my hands to You.

## Psalms 88:10-18

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> Will You perform wonders for the dead? Will the departed spirits rise [and] praise You? Selah.

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> Will Your lovingkindness be declared in the grave, Your faithfulness in Abaddon?

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> Will Your wonders be made known in the darkness? And Your righteousness in the land of forgetfulness?

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> But I, O LORD, have cried out to You for help, And in the morning my prayer comes before You.

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> O LORD, why do You reject my soul? [Why] do You hide Your face from me?

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> I was afflicted and about to die from my youth on; I suffer Your terrors; I am overcome.

###### v16
<sup>16</sup> Your burning anger has passed over me; Your terrors have destroyed me.

###### v17
<sup>17</sup> They have surrounded me like water all day long; They have encompassed me altogether.

###### v18
<sup>18</sup> You have removed lover and friend far from me; My acquaintances are [in] darkness.
