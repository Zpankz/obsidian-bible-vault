# Psalms 67

## Psalms 67:1-7

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> For the choir director; with stringed instruments. A Psalm. A Song. God be gracious to us and bless us, [And] cause His face to shine upon us-- Selah.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> That Your way may be known on the earth, Your salvation among all nations.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> Let the peoples praise You, O God; Let all the peoples praise You.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> Let the nations be glad and sing for joy; For You will judge the peoples with uprightness And guide the nations on the earth. Selah.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> Let the peoples praise You, O God; Let all the peoples praise You.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> The earth has yielded its produce; God, our God, blesses us.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> God blesses us, That all the ends of the earth may fear Him.
