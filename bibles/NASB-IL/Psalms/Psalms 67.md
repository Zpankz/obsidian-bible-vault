## Psalms 67:1-7

<sup>1</sup> For the choir director; with stringed instruments. A Psalm. A Song. God be gracious to us and bless us, [And] cause His face to shine upon us-- Selah.[^1][^2][^3]

<sup>2</sup> That Your way may be known on the earth, Your salvation among all nations.[^4]

<sup>3</sup> Let the peoples praise You, O God; Let all the peoples praise You.

<sup>4</sup> Let the nations be glad and sing for joy; For You will judge the peoples with uprightness And guide the nations on the earth. Selah.[^5][^6]

<sup>5</sup> Let the peoples praise You, O God; Let all the peoples praise You.

<sup>6</sup> The earth has yielded its produce; God, our God, blesses us.[^7]

<sup>7</sup> God blesses us, That all the ends of the earth may fear Him.
