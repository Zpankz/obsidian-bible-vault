# Psalms 121

## Psalms 121:1-8

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> A Song of Ascents. I will lift up my eyes to the mountains; From where shall my help come?

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> My help [comes] from the LORD, Who made heaven and earth.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> He will not allow your foot to slip; He who keeps you will not slumber.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> Behold, He who keeps Israel Will neither slumber nor sleep.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> The LORD is your keeper; The LORD is your shade on your right hand.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> The sun will not smite you by day, Nor the moon by night.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> The LORD will protect you from all evil; He will keep your soul.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> The LORD will guard your going out and your coming in From this time forth and forever.
