## Psalms 121:1-8

<sup>1</sup> A Song of Ascents. I will lift up my eyes to the mountains; From where shall my help come?[^1][^2][^3][^4][^5][^6][^7]

<sup>2</sup> My help [comes] from the LORD, Who made heaven and earth.[^8][^9][^10][^11][^12][^13][^14][^15]

<sup>3</sup> He will not allow your foot to slip; He who keeps you will not slumber.[^16][^17][^18][^19][^20][^21][^22][^23]

<sup>4</sup> Behold, He who keeps Israel Will neither slumber nor sleep.[^24][^25]

<sup>5</sup> The LORD is your keeper; The LORD is your shade on your right hand.[^26][^27][^28][^29][^30][^31][^32]

<sup>6</sup> The sun will not smite you by day, Nor the moon by night.[^33][^34][^35]

<sup>7</sup> The LORD will protect you from all evil; He will keep your soul.[^36][^37][^38][^39][^40][^41][^42][^43]

<sup>8</sup> The LORD will guard your going out and your coming in From this time forth and forever.[^44][^45][^46][^47][^48][^49][^50][^51]
