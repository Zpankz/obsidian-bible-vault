## Galatians 3:1-5

<sup>1</sup> You foolish Galatians, who has bewitched you, before whose eyes Jesus Christ was publicly portrayed [as] crucified?[^1][^2][^3][^4]

<sup>2</sup> This is the only thing I want to find out from you: did you receive the Spirit by the works of the Law, or by hearing with faith?[^5][^6][^7][^8][^9]

<sup>3</sup> Are you so foolish? Having begun by the Spirit, are you now being perfected by the flesh?[^10][^11]

<sup>4</sup> Did you suffer so many things in vain-- if indeed it was in vain?[^12]

<sup>5</sup> So then, does He who provides you with the Spirit and works miracles among you, do it by the works of the Law, or by hearing with faith?

## Galatians 3:6-18

<sup>6</sup> Even so Abraham BELIEVED GOD, AND IT WAS RECKONED TO HIM AS RIGHTEOUSNESS.[^13][^14][^15][^16][^17]

<sup>7</sup> Therefore, be sure that it is those who are of faith who are sons of Abraham.

<sup>8</sup> The Scripture, foreseeing that God would justify the Gentiles by faith, preached the gospel beforehand to Abraham, [saying], 'ALL THE NATIONS WILL BE BLESSED IN YOU.'[^18][^19][^20][^21]

<sup>9</sup> So then those who are of faith are blessed with Abraham, the believer.[^22][^23]

<sup>10</sup> For as many as are of the works of the Law are under a curse; for it is written, 'CURSED IS EVERYONE WHO DOES NOT ABIDE BY ALL THINGS WRITTEN IN THE BOOK OF THE LAW, TO PERFORM THEM.'[^24][^25][^26][^27][^28][^29][^30][^31]

<sup>11</sup> Now that no one is justified by the Law before God is evident; for, 'THE RIGHTEOUS MAN SHALL LIVE BY FAITH.'[^32][^33][^34][^35]

<sup>12</sup> However, the Law is not of faith; on the contrary, 'HE WHO PRACTICES THEM SHALL LIVE BY THEM.'[^36]

<sup>13</sup> Christ redeemed us from the curse of the Law, having become a curse for us-- for it is written, 'CURSED IS EVERYONE WHO HANGS ON A TREE '--[^37][^38][^39][^40][^41][^42][^43][^44]

<sup>14</sup> in order that in Christ Jesus the blessing of Abraham might come to the Gentiles, so that we would receive the promise of the Spirit through faith.[^45][^46][^47][^48][^49][^50][^51]

<sup>15</sup> Brethren, I speak in terms of human relations: even though it is [only] a man's covenant, yet when it has been ratified, no one sets it aside or adds conditions to it.[^52]

<sup>16</sup> Now the promises were spoken to Abraham and to his seed. He does not say, 'And to seeds,' as [referring] to many, but [rather] to one, 'And to your seed,' that is, Christ.[^53][^54][^55][^56][^57]

<sup>17</sup> What I am saying is this: the Law, which came four hundred and thirty years later, does not invalidate a covenant previously ratified by God, so as to nullify the promise.

<sup>18</sup> For if the inheritance is based on law, it is no longer based on a promise; but God has granted it to Abraham by means of a promise.

## Galatians 3:19-29

<sup>19</sup> Why the Law then? It was added because of transgressions, having been ordained through angels by the agency of a mediator, until the seed would come to whom the promise had been made.[^58][^59][^60][^61]

<sup>20</sup> Now a mediator is not for one [party only]; whereas God is [only] one.[^62]

<sup>21</sup> Is the Law then contrary to the promises of God? May it never be! For if a law had been given which was able to impart life, then righteousness would indeed have been based on law.[^63][^64]

<sup>22</sup> But the Scripture has shut up everyone under sin, so that the promise by faith in Jesus Christ might be given to those who believe.[^65][^66][^67]

<sup>23</sup> But before faith came, we were kept in custody under the law, being shut up to the faith which was later to be revealed.[^68]

<sup>24</sup> Therefore the Law has become our tutor [to lead us] to Christ, so that we may be justified by faith.[^69][^70]

<sup>25</sup> But now that faith has come, we are no longer under a tutor.

<sup>26</sup> For you are all sons of God through faith in Christ Jesus.[^71][^72][^73][^74][^75][^76][^77][^78]

<sup>27</sup> For all of you who were baptized into Christ have clothed yourselves with Christ.[^79][^80][^81][^82][^83][^84][^85]

<sup>28</sup> There is neither Jew nor Greek, there is neither slave nor free man, there is neither male nor female; for you are all one in Christ Jesus.[^86][^87][^88][^89][^90][^91][^92][^93]

<sup>29</sup> And if you belong to Christ, then you are Abraham's descendants, heirs according to promise.[^94][^95][^96][^97][^98][^99]
