# Galatians 6

## Galatians 6:1-10

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> Brethren, even if anyone is caught in any trespass, you who are spiritual, restore such a one in a spirit of gentleness; [each one] looking to yourself, so that you too will not be tempted.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> Bear one another's burdens, and thereby fulfill the law of Christ.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> For if anyone thinks he is something when he is nothing, he deceives himself.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> But each one must examine his own work, and then he will have [reason for] boasting in regard to himself alone, and not in regard to another.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> For each one will bear his own load.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> The one who is taught the word is to share all good things with the one who teaches [him].

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> Do not be deceived, God is not mocked; for whatever a man sows, this he will also reap.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> For the one who sows to his own flesh will from the flesh reap corruption, but the one who sows to the Spirit will from the Spirit reap eternal life.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> Let us not lose heart in doing good, for in due time we will reap if we do not grow weary.

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> So then, while we have opportunity, let us do good to all people, and especially to those who are of the household of the faith.

## Galatians 6:11-18

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> See with what large letters I am writing to you with my own hand.

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> Those who desire to make a good showing in the flesh try to compel you to be circumcised, simply so that they will not be persecuted for the cross of Christ.

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> For those who are circumcised do not even keep the Law themselves, but they desire to have you circumcised so that they may boast in your flesh.

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> But may it never be that I would boast, except in the cross of our Lord Jesus Christ, through which the world has been crucified to me, and I to the world.

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> For neither is circumcision anything, nor uncircumcision, but a new creation.

###### v16
<sup>16</sup> And those who will walk by this rule, peace and mercy [be] upon them, and upon the Israel of God.

###### v17
<sup>17</sup> From now on let no one cause trouble for me, for I bear on my body the brand-marks of Jesus.

###### v18
<sup>18</sup> The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with your spirit, brethren. Amen.
