## Galatians 5:1-12

<sup>1</sup> It was for freedom that Christ set us free; therefore keep standing firm and do not be subject again to a yoke of slavery.[^1][^2][^3][^4][^5][^6][^7][^8]

<sup>2</sup> Behold I, Paul, say to you that if you receive circumcision, Christ will be of no benefit to you.

<sup>3</sup> And I testify again to every man who receives circumcision, that he is under obligation to keep the whole Law.[^9][^10]

<sup>4</sup> You have been severed from Christ, you who are seeking to be justified by law; you have fallen from grace.[^11][^12][^13][^14][^15][^16][^17][^18]

<sup>5</sup> For we through the Spirit, by faith, are waiting for the hope of righteousness.[^19][^20]

<sup>6</sup> For in Christ Jesus neither circumcision nor uncircumcision means anything, but faith working through love.[^21][^22][^23][^24]

<sup>7</sup> You were running well; who hindered you from obeying the truth?[^25][^26][^27][^28]

<sup>8</sup> This persuasion [did] not [come] from Him who calls you.

<sup>9</sup> A little leaven leavens the whole lump [of dough].[^29][^30][^31]

<sup>10</sup> I have confidence in you in the Lord that you will adopt no other view; but the one who is disturbing you will bear his judgment, whoever he is.

<sup>11</sup> But I, brethren, if I still preach circumcision, why am I still persecuted? Then the stumbling block of the cross has been abolished.

<sup>12</sup> I wish that those who are troubling you would even mutilate themselves.

## Galatians 5:13-26

<sup>13</sup> For you were called to freedom, brethren; only [do] not [turn] your freedom into an opportunity for the flesh, but through love serve one another.[^32][^33][^34][^35][^36][^37][^38][^39]

<sup>14</sup> For the whole Law is fulfilled in one word, in the [statement], 'YOU SHALL LOVE YOUR NEIGHBOR AS YOURSELF.'[^40][^41][^42][^43][^44][^45][^46][^47]

<sup>15</sup> But if you bite and devour one another, take care that you are not consumed by one another.[^48][^49][^50]

<sup>16</sup> But I say, walk by the Spirit, and you will not carry out the desire of the flesh.[^51][^52][^53][^54][^55][^56][^57][^58]

<sup>17</sup> For the flesh sets its desire against the Spirit, and the Spirit against the flesh; for these are in opposition to one another, so that you may not do the things that you please.[^59][^60][^61][^62][^63][^64][^65][^66]

<sup>18</sup> But if you are led by the Spirit, you are not under the Law.[^67][^68]

<sup>19</sup> Now the deeds of the flesh are evident, which are: immorality, impurity, sensuality,[^69][^70][^71][^72][^73][^74][^75][^76]

<sup>20</sup> idolatry, sorcery, enmities, strife, jealousy, outbursts of anger, disputes, dissensions, factions,

<sup>21</sup> envying, drunkenness, carousing, and things like these, of which I forewarn you, just as I have forewarned you, that those who practice such things will not inherit the kingdom of God.[^77][^78][^79][^80][^81][^82][^83][^84]

<sup>22</sup> But the fruit of the Spirit is love, joy, peace, patience, kindness, goodness, faithfulness,[^85][^86][^87][^88][^89][^90][^91][^92]

<sup>23</sup> gentleness, self-control; against such things there is no law.[^93][^94]

<sup>24</sup> Now those who belong to Christ Jesus have crucified the flesh with its passions and desires.[^95][^96][^97][^98][^99][^100][^101][^102]

<sup>25</sup> If we live by the Spirit, let us also walk by the Spirit.[^103][^104][^105][^106][^107][^108][^109][^110]

<sup>26</sup> Let us not become boastful, challenging one another, envying one another.[^111][^112][^113][^114]
