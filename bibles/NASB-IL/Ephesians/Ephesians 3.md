# Ephesians 3

## Ephesians 3:1-13

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> For this reason I, Paul, the prisoner of Christ Jesus for the sake of you Gentiles--

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> if indeed you have heard of the stewardship of God's grace which was given to me for you;

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> that by revelation there was made known to me the mystery, as I wrote before in brief.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> By referring to this, when you read you can understand my insight into the mystery of Christ,

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> which in other generations was not made known to the sons of men, as it has now been revealed to His holy apostles and prophets in the Spirit;

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> [to be specific], that the Gentiles are fellow heirs and fellow members of the body, and fellow partakers of the promise in Christ Jesus through the gospel,

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> of which I was made a minister, according to the gift of God's grace which was given to me according to the working of His power.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> To me, the very least of all saints, this grace was given, to preach to the Gentiles the unfathomable riches of Christ,

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> and to bring to light what is the administration of the mystery which for ages has been hidden in God who created all things;

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> so that the manifold wisdom of God might now be made known through the church to the rulers and the authorities in the heavenly [places].

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> [This was] in accordance with the eternal purpose which He carried out in Christ Jesus our Lord,

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> in whom we have boldness and confident access through faith in Him.

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> Therefore I ask you not to lose heart at my tribulations on your behalf, for they are your glory.

## Ephesians 3:14-21

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> For this reason I bow my knees before the Father,

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> from whom every family in heaven and on earth derives its name,

###### v16
<sup>16</sup> that He would grant you, according to the riches of His glory, to be strengthened with power through His Spirit in the inner man,

###### v17
<sup>17</sup> so that Christ may dwell in your hearts through faith; [and] that you, being rooted and grounded in love,

###### v18
<sup>18</sup> may be able to comprehend with all the saints what is the breadth and length and height and depth,

###### v19
<sup>19</sup> and to know the love of Christ which surpasses knowledge, that you may be filled up to all the fullness of God.

###### v20
<sup>20</sup> Now to Him who is able to do far more abundantly beyond all that we ask or think, according to the power that works within us,

###### v21
<sup>21</sup> to Him [be] the glory in the church and in Christ Jesus to all generations forever and ever. Amen.
