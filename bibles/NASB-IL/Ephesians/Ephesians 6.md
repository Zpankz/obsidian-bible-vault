## Ephesians 6:1-9

<sup>1</sup> Children, obey your parents in the Lord, for this is right.[^1][^2][^3][^4][^5][^6][^7][^8]

<sup>2</sup> HONOR YOUR FATHER AND MOTHER (which is the first commandment with a promise),[^9][^10][^11][^12]

<sup>3</sup> SO THAT IT MAY BE WELL WITH YOU, AND THAT YOU MAY LIVE LONG ON THE EARTH.[^13][^14]

<sup>4</sup> Fathers, do not provoke your children to anger, but bring them up in the discipline and instruction of the Lord.[^15][^16][^17][^18][^19][^20][^21][^22]

<sup>5</sup> Slaves, be obedient to those who are your masters according to the flesh, with fear and trembling, in the sincerity of your heart, as to Christ;[^23][^24]

<sup>6</sup> not by way of eyeservice, as men-pleasers, but as slaves of Christ, doing the will of God from the heart.[^25][^26][^27]

<sup>7</sup> With good will render service, as to the Lord, and not to men,[^28][^29][^30]

<sup>8</sup> knowing that whatever good thing each one does, this he will receive back from the Lord, whether slave or free.[^31]

<sup>9</sup> And masters, do the same things to them, and give up threatening, knowing that both their Master and yours is in heaven, and there is no partiality with Him.[^32][^33]

## Ephesians 6:10-18

<sup>10</sup> Finally, be strong in the Lord and in the strength of His might.[^34][^35][^36][^37][^38][^39][^40][^41]

<sup>11</sup> Put on the full armor of God, so that you will be able to stand firm against the schemes of the devil.[^42][^43][^44][^45][^46][^47][^48][^49]

<sup>12</sup> For our struggle is not against flesh and blood, but against the rulers, against the powers, against the world forces of this darkness, against the spiritual [forces] of wickedness in the heavenly [places].[^50][^51][^52][^53][^54][^55][^56][^57]

<sup>13</sup> Therefore, take up the full armor of God, so that you will be able to resist in the evil day, and having done everything, to stand firm.[^58][^59][^60][^61][^62][^63][^64][^65]

<sup>14</sup> Stand firm therefore, HAVING GIRDED YOUR LOINS WITH TRUTH, and HAVING PUT ON THE BREASTPLATE OF RIGHTEOUSNESS,[^66][^67][^68][^69][^70][^71][^72]

<sup>15</sup> and having shod YOUR FEET WITH THE PREPARATION OF THE GOSPEL OF PEACE;[^73][^74][^75]

<sup>16</sup> in addition to all, taking up the shield of faith with which you will be able to extinguish all the flaming arrows of the evil [one].[^76][^77][^78][^79][^80][^81][^82][^83]

<sup>17</sup> And take THE HELMET OF SALVATION, and the sword of the Spirit, which is the word of God.[^84][^85][^86][^87][^88][^89][^90][^91]

<sup>18</sup> With all prayer and petition pray at all times in the Spirit, and with this in view, be on the alert with all perseverance and petition for all the saints,[^92][^93][^94][^95][^96][^97][^98][^99]

<sup>23</sup> Peace be to the brethren, and love with faith, from God the Father and the Lord Jesus Christ.
