## Ephesians 2:4-10

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> But God, being rich in mercy, because of His great love with which He loved us,

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> even when we were dead in our transgressions, made us alive together with Christ (by grace you have been saved),

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> and raised us up with Him, and seated us with Him in the heavenly [places] in Christ Jesus,

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> so that in the ages to come He might show the surpassing riches of His grace in kindness toward us in Christ Jesus.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> For by grace you have been saved through faith; and that not of yourselves, [it is] the gift of God;

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> not as a result of works, so that no one may boast.

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> For we are His workmanship, created in Christ Jesus for good works, which God prepared beforehand so that we would walk in them.

## Ephesians 2:14-22

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> For He Himself is our peace, who made both [groups into] one and broke down the barrier of the dividing wall,

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> by abolishing in His flesh the enmity, [which is] the Law of commandments [contained] in ordinances, so that in Himself He might make the two into one new man, [thus] establishing peace,

###### v16
<sup>16</sup> and might reconcile them both in one body to God through the cross, by it having put to death the enmity.

###### v17
<sup>17</sup> AND HE CAME AND PREACHED PEACE TO YOU WHO WERE FAR AWAY, AND PEACE TO THOSE WHO WERE NEAR;

###### v18
<sup>18</sup> for through Him we both have our access in one Spirit to the Father.

###### v19
<sup>19</sup> So then you are no longer strangers and aliens, but you are fellow citizens with the saints, and are of God's household,

###### v20
<sup>20</sup> having been built on the foundation of the apostles and prophets, Christ Jesus Himself being the corner [stone],

###### v21
<sup>21</sup> in whom the whole building, being fitted together, is growing into a holy temple in the Lord,

###### v22
<sup>22</sup> in whom you also are being built together into a dwelling of God in the Spirit.
