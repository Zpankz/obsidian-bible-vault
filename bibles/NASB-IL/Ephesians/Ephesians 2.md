<sup>4</sup> But God, being rich in mercy, because of His great love with which He loved us,[^20][^21][^22][^23][^24][^25][^26][^27]

## Ephesians 2:14-22

<sup>14</sup> For He Himself is our peace, who made both [groups into] one and broke down the barrier of the dividing wall,[^89][^90][^91][^92][^93]

<sup>15</sup> by abolishing in His flesh the enmity, [which is] the Law of commandments [contained] in ordinances, so that in Himself He might make the two into one new man, [thus] establishing peace,[^94][^95]

<sup>16</sup> and might reconcile them both in one body to God through the cross, by it having put to death the enmity.[^96][^97][^98]

<sup>17</sup> AND HE CAME AND PREACHED PEACE TO YOU WHO WERE FAR AWAY, AND PEACE TO THOSE WHO WERE NEAR;[^99][^100]

<sup>18</sup> for through Him we both have our access in one Spirit to the Father.[^101][^102][^103][^104][^105][^106][^107]

<sup>19</sup> So then you are no longer strangers and aliens, but you are fellow citizens with the saints, and are of God's household,[^108][^109][^110][^111][^112][^113][^114]

<sup>20</sup> having been built on the foundation of the apostles and prophets, Christ Jesus Himself being the corner [stone],[^115][^116][^117][^118][^119][^120][^121][^122]

<sup>21</sup> in whom the whole building, being fitted together, is growing into a holy temple in the Lord,[^123][^124][^125]
