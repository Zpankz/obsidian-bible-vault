## Ephesians 4:2-16

<sup>2</sup> with all humility and gentleness, with patience, showing tolerance for one another in love,[^9][^10][^11][^12][^13][^14][^15][^16]

<sup>3</sup> being diligent to preserve the unity of the Spirit in the bond of peace.[^17][^18][^19][^20][^21][^22][^23][^24]

<sup>4</sup> [There is] one body and one Spirit, just as also you were called in one hope of your calling;[^25][^26][^27]

<sup>5</sup> one Lord, one faith, one baptism,[^28][^29][^30][^31]

<sup>6</sup> one God and Father of all who is over all and through all and in all.[^32][^33][^34][^35]

<sup>7</sup> But to each one of us grace was given according to the measure of Christ's gift.[^36][^37][^38][^39][^40]

<sup>8</sup> Therefore it says, 'WHEN HE ASCENDED ON HIGH, HE LED CAPTIVE A HOST OF CAPTIVES, AND HE GAVE GIFTS TO MEN.'[^41][^42]

<sup>9</sup> (Now this [expression], 'He ascended,' what does it mean except that He also had descended into the lower parts of the earth?[^43][^44]

<sup>10</sup> He who descended is Himself also He who ascended far above all the heavens, so that He might fill all things.)[^45]

<sup>11</sup> And He gave some [as] apostles, and some [as] prophets, and some [as] evangelists, and some [as] pastors and teachers,[^46][^47][^48][^49][^50][^51][^52][^53]

<sup>12</sup> for the equipping of the saints for the work of service, to the building up of the body of Christ;[^54][^55][^56]

<sup>13</sup> until we all attain to the unity of the faith, and of the knowledge of the Son of God, to a mature man, to the measure of the stature which belongs to the fullness of Christ.[^57][^58][^59][^60]

<sup>14</sup> As a result, we are no longer to be children, tossed here and there by waves and carried about by every wind of doctrine, by the trickery of men, by craftiness in deceitful scheming;[^61][^62][^63][^64][^65][^66][^67][^68]

<sup>15</sup> but speaking the truth in love, we are to grow up in all [aspects] into Him who is the head, [even] Christ,[^69][^70][^71][^72][^73][^74][^75][^76]

<sup>16</sup> from whom the whole body, being fitted and held together by what every joint supplies, according to the proper working of each individual part, causes the growth of the body for the building up of itself in love.[^77][^78][^79][^80][^81][^82]

## Ephesians 4:17-32

<sup>17</sup> So this I say, and affirm together with the Lord, that you walk no longer just as the Gentiles also walk, in the futility of their mind,[^83][^84][^85][^86]

<sup>18</sup> being darkened in their understanding, excluded from the life of God because of the ignorance that is in them, because of the hardness of their heart;[^87][^88][^89][^90]

<sup>19</sup> and they, having become callous, have given themselves over to sensuality for the practice of every kind of impurity with greediness.[^91][^92][^93]

<sup>20</sup> But you did not learn Christ in this way,[^94][^95][^96]

<sup>21</sup> if indeed you have heard Him and have been taught in Him, just as truth is in Jesus,[^97][^98]

<sup>22</sup> that, in reference to your former manner of life, you lay aside the old self, which is being corrupted in accordance with the lusts of deceit,[^99][^100][^101][^102][^103][^104][^105][^106]

<sup>23</sup> and that you be renewed in the spirit of your mind,[^107][^108][^109][^110][^111][^112][^113][^114]

<sup>24</sup> and put on the new self, which in [the likeness of] God has been created in righteousness and holiness of the truth.[^115][^116][^117][^118][^119][^120][^121][^122]

<sup>25</sup> Therefore, laying aside falsehood, SPEAK TRUTH EACH ONE [of you] WITH HIS NEIGHBOR, for we are members of one another.[^123][^124][^125][^126][^127][^128][^129]

<sup>26</sup> BE ANGRY, AND [yet] DO NOT SIN; do not let the sun go down on your anger,[^130][^131][^132][^133][^134][^135][^136][^137]

<sup>27</sup> and do not give the devil an opportunity.[^138][^139][^140][^141][^142][^143]

<sup>28</sup> He who steals must steal no longer; but rather he must labor, performing with his own hands what is good, so that he will have [something] to share with one who has need.[^144][^145][^146][^147]

<sup>29</sup> Let no unwholesome word proceed from your mouth, but only such [a word] as is good for edification according to the need [of the moment], so that it will give grace to those who hear.[^148][^149][^150][^151][^152][^153][^154][^155]

<sup>30</sup> Do not grieve the Holy Spirit of God, by whom you were sealed for the day of redemption.[^156][^157][^158][^159][^160][^161][^162][^163]

<sup>31</sup> Let all bitterness and wrath and anger and clamor and slander be put away from you, along with all malice.[^164][^165][^166][^167][^168][^169][^170]

<sup>32</sup> Be kind to one another, tender-hearted, forgiving each other, just as God in Christ also has forgiven you.[^171][^172][^173][^174][^175][^176][^177][^178]
