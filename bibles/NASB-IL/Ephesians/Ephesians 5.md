## Ephesians 5:3-20

<sup>3</sup> But immorality or any impurity or greed must not even be named among you, as is proper among saints;[^17][^18][^19][^20][^21][^22]

<sup>4</sup> and [there must be no] filthiness and silly talk, or coarse jesting, which are not fitting, but rather giving of thanks.[^23][^24][^25][^26][^27][^28]

<sup>5</sup> For this you know with certainty, that no immoral or impure person or covetous man, who is an idolater, has an inheritance in the kingdom of Christ and God.[^29][^30][^31][^32][^33]

<sup>6</sup> Let no one deceive you with empty words, for because of these things the wrath of God comes upon the sons of disobedience.[^34][^35][^36][^37][^38]

<sup>7</sup> Therefore do not be partakers with them;[^39][^40]

<sup>8</sup> for you were formerly darkness, but now you are Light in the Lord; walk as children of Light[^41][^42][^43][^44][^45][^46][^47][^48]

<sup>9</sup> (for the fruit of the Light [consists] in all goodness and righteousness and truth),[^49][^50][^51]

<sup>10</sup> trying to learn what is pleasing to the Lord.[^52][^53][^54][^55][^56][^57]

<sup>11</sup> Do not participate in the unfruitful deeds of darkness, but instead even expose them;[^58][^59][^60][^61][^62][^63][^64][^65]

<sup>12</sup> for it is disgraceful even to speak of the things which are done by them in secret.[^66][^67]

<sup>13</sup> But all things become visible when they are exposed by the light, for everything that becomes visible is light.[^68]

<sup>14</sup> For this reason it says, 'Awake, sleeper, And arise from the dead, And Christ will shine on you.'[^69][^70][^71][^72][^73][^74][^75][^76]

<sup>15</sup> Therefore be careful how you walk, not as unwise men but as wise,[^77][^78][^79][^80][^81][^82][^83][^84]

<sup>16</sup> making the most of your time, because the days are evil.[^85][^86][^87][^88][^89][^90][^91][^92]

<sup>17</sup> So then do not be foolish, but understand what the will of the Lord is.[^93][^94][^95][^96][^97][^98][^99][^100]

<sup>18</sup> And do not get drunk with wine, for that is dissipation, but be filled with the Spirit,[^101][^102][^103][^104][^105][^106][^107]

<sup>19</sup> speaking to one another in psalms and hymns and spiritual songs, singing and making melody with your heart to the Lord;[^108][^109][^110][^111][^112][^113][^114][^115]

<sup>20</sup> always giving thanks for all things in the name of our Lord Jesus Christ to God, even the Father;[^116][^117][^118][^119][^120][^121][^122][^123]

## Ephesians 5:21-33

<sup>21</sup> and be subject to one another in the fear of Christ.[^124][^125][^126][^127][^128][^129][^130]

<sup>22</sup> Wives, [be subject] to your own husbands, as to the Lord.[^131][^132][^133][^134][^135][^136][^137]

<sup>23</sup> For the husband is the head of the wife, as Christ also is the head of the church, He Himself [being] the Savior of the body.[^138][^139]

<sup>24</sup> But as the church is subject to Christ, so also the wives [ought to be] to their husbands in everything.

<sup>25</sup> Husbands, love your wives, just as Christ also loved the church and gave Himself up for her,[^140][^141][^142][^143][^144][^145]

<sup>26</sup> so that He might sanctify her, having cleansed her by the washing of water with the word,[^146][^147][^148][^149][^150][^151][^152][^153]

<sup>27</sup> that He might present to Himself the church in all her glory, having no spot or wrinkle or any such thing; but that she would be holy and blameless.[^154][^155][^156][^157][^158][^159][^160][^161]

<sup>28</sup> So husbands ought also to love their own wives as their own bodies. He who loves his own wife loves himself;[^162][^163][^164]

<sup>29</sup> for no one ever hated his own flesh, but nourishes and cherishes it, just as Christ also [does] the church,

<sup>30</sup> because we are members of His body.[^165][^166][^167][^168]

<sup>31</sup> FOR THIS REASON A MAN SHALL LEAVE HIS FATHER AND MOTHER AND SHALL BE JOINED TO HIS WIFE, AND THE TWO SHALL BECOME ONE FLESH.[^169][^170][^171]

<sup>32</sup> This mystery is great; but I am speaking with reference to Christ and the church.[^172][^173]

<sup>33</sup> Nevertheless, each individual among you also is to love his own wife even as himself, and the wife must [see to it] that she respects her husband.[^174][^175]
