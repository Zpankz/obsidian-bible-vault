# Amos 3

## Amos 3:1-8

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> Hear this word which the LORD has spoken against you, sons of Israel, against the entire family which He brought up from the land of Egypt:

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> 'You only have I chosen among all the families of the earth; Therefore I will punish you for all your iniquities.'

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> Do two men walk together unless they have made an appointment?

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> Does a lion roar in the forest when he has no prey? Does a young lion growl from his den unless he has captured [something]?

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> Does a bird fall into a trap on the ground when there is no bait in it? Does a trap spring up from the earth when it captures nothing at all?

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> If a trumpet is blown in a city will not the people tremble? If a calamity occurs in a city has not the LORD done it?

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> Surely the Lord GOD does nothing Unless He reveals His secret counsel To His servants the prophets.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> A lion has roared! Who will not fear? The Lord GOD has spoken! Who can but prophesy?

## Amos 3:9-15

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> Proclaim on the citadels in Ashdod and on the citadels in the land of Egypt and say, 'Assemble yourselves on the mountains of Samaria and see [the] great tumults within her and [the] oppressions in her midst.

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> 'But they do not know how to do what is right,' declares the LORD, 'these who hoard up violence and devastation in their citadels.'

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> Therefore, thus says the Lord GOD, 'An enemy, even one surrounding the land, Will pull down your strength from you And your citadels will be looted.'

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> Thus says the LORD, 'Just as the shepherd snatches from the lion's mouth a couple of legs or a piece of an ear, So will the sons of Israel dwelling in Samaria be snatched away-- With [the] corner of a bed and [the] cover of a couch!

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> 'Hear and testify against the house of Jacob,' Declares the Lord GOD, the God of hosts.

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> 'For on the day that I punish Israel's transgressions, I will also punish the altars of Bethel; The horns of the altar will be cut off And they will fall to the ground.

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> 'I will also smite the winter house together with the summer house; The houses of ivory will also perish And the great houses will come to an end,' Declares the LORD.
