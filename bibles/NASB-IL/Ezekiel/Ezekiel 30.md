# Ezekiel 30

## Ezekiel 30:1-19

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> The word of the LORD came again to me saying,

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> 'Son of man, prophesy and say, 'Thus says the Lord GOD, 'Wail, 'Alas for the day!'

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> 'For the day is near, Even the day of the LORD is near; It will be a day of clouds, A time [of doom] for the nations.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> 'A sword will come upon Egypt, And anguish will be in Ethiopia; When the slain fall in Egypt, They take away her wealth, And her foundations are torn down.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> 'Ethiopia, Put, Lud, all Arabia, Libya and the people of the land that is in league will fall with them by the sword.'

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> 'Thus says the LORD, 'Indeed, those who support Egypt will fall And the pride of her power will come down; From Migdol [to] Syene They will fall within her by the sword,' Declares the Lord GOD.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> 'They will be desolate In the midst of the desolated lands; And her cities will be In the midst of the devastated cities.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> 'And they will know that I am the LORD, When I set a fire in Egypt And all her helpers are broken.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> 'On that day messengers will go forth from Me in ships to frighten secure Ethiopia; and anguish will be on them as on the day of Egypt; for behold, it comes!'

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> 'Thus says the Lord GOD, 'I will also make the hordes of Egypt cease By the hand of Nebuchadnezzar king of Babylon.

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> 'He and his people with him, The most ruthless of the nations, Will be brought in to destroy the land; And they will draw their swords against Egypt And fill the land with the slain.

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> 'Moreover, I will make the Nile canals dry And sell the land into the hands of evil men. And I will make the land desolate And all that is in it, By the hand of strangers; I the LORD have spoken.'

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> 'Thus says the Lord GOD, 'I will also destroy the idols And make the images cease from Memphis. And there will no longer be a prince in the land of Egypt; And I will put fear in the land of Egypt.

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> 'I will make Pathros desolate, Set a fire in Zoan And execute judgments on Thebes.

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> 'I will pour out My wrath on Sin, The stronghold of Egypt; I will also cut off the hordes of Thebes.

###### v16
<sup>16</sup> 'I will set a fire in Egypt; Sin will writhe in anguish, Thebes will be breached And Memphis [will have] distresses daily.

###### v17
<sup>17</sup> 'The young men of On and of Pi-beseth Will fall by the sword, And the women will go into captivity.

###### v18
<sup>18</sup> 'In Tehaphnehes the day will be dark When I break there the yoke bars of Egypt. Then the pride of her power will cease in her; A cloud will cover her, And her daughters will go into captivity.

###### v19
<sup>19</sup> 'Thus I will execute judgments on Egypt, And they will know that I am the LORD.'''

## Ezekiel 30:20-26

###### v20
<sup>20</sup> In the eleventh year, in the first [month], on the seventh of the month, the word of the LORD came to me saying,

###### v21
<sup>21</sup> 'Son of man, I have broken the arm of Pharaoh king of Egypt; and, behold, it has not been bound up for healing or wrapped with a bandage, that it may be strong to hold the sword.

###### v22
<sup>22</sup> 'Therefore thus says the Lord GOD, 'Behold, I am against Pharaoh king of Egypt and will break his arms, both the strong and the broken; and I will make the sword fall from his hand.

###### v23
<sup>23</sup> 'I will scatter the Egyptians among the nations and disperse them among the lands.

###### v24
<sup>24</sup> 'For I will strengthen the arms of the king of Babylon and put My sword in his hand; and I will break the arms of Pharaoh, so that he will groan before him with the groanings of a wounded man.

###### v25
<sup>25</sup> 'Thus I will strengthen the arms of the king of Babylon, but the arms of Pharaoh will fall. Then they will know that I am the LORD, when I put My sword into the hand of the king of Babylon and he stretches it out against the land of Egypt.

###### v26
<sup>26</sup> 'When I scatter the Egyptians among the nations and disperse them among the lands, then they will know that I am the LORD.''
