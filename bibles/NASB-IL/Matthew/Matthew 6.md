## Matthew 6:1-4

<sup>1</sup> 'Beware of practicing your righteousness before men to be noticed by them; otherwise you have no reward with your Father who is in heaven.[^1][^2][^3][^4][^5][^6][^7][^8]

<sup>2</sup> 'So when you give to the poor, do not sound a trumpet before you, as the hypocrites do in the synagogues and in the streets, so that they may be honored by men. Truly I say to you, they have their reward in full.[^9][^10][^11]

<sup>3</sup> 'But when you give to the poor, do not let your left hand know what your right hand is doing,[^12][^13]

<sup>4</sup> so that your giving will be in secret; and your Father who sees [what is done] in secret will reward you.[^14][^15][^16][^17][^18][^19]

<sup>7</sup> 'And when you are praying, do not use meaningless repetition as the Gentiles do, for they suppose that they will be heard for their many words.[^36][^37][^38][^39]

<sup>8</sup> 'So do not be like them; for your Father knows what you need before you ask Him.[^40][^41][^42][^43][^44][^45]

## Matthew 6:25-34

<sup>25</sup> 'For this reason I say to you, do not be worried about your life, [as to] what you will eat or what you will drink; nor for your body, [as to] what you will put on. Is not life more than food, and the body more than clothing?[^146][^147][^148][^149][^150][^151][^152][^153]

<sup>26</sup> 'Look at the birds of the air, that they do not sow, nor reap nor gather into barns, and [yet] your heavenly Father feeds them. Are you not worth much more than they?[^154][^155][^156][^157][^158][^159][^160][^161]

<sup>27</sup> 'And who of you by being worried can add a [single] hour to his life?[^162][^163]

<sup>28</sup> 'And why are you worried about clothing? Observe how the lilies of the field grow; they do not toil nor do they spin,[^164][^165][^166]

<sup>29</sup> yet I say to you that not even Solomon in all his glory clothed himself like one of these.[^167]

<sup>30</sup> 'But if God so clothes the grass of the field, which is [alive] today and tomorrow is thrown into the furnace, [will He] not much more [clothe] you? You of little faith![^168][^169][^170]

<sup>31</sup> 'Do not worry then, saying, 'What will we eat?' or 'What will we drink?' or 'What will we wear for clothing?'[^171][^172][^173][^174][^175][^176][^177]

<sup>32</sup> 'For the Gentiles eagerly seek all these things; for your heavenly Father knows that you need all these things.[^178][^179][^180]

<sup>33</sup> 'But seek first His kingdom and His righteousness, and all these things will be added to you.[^181][^182][^183][^184][^185][^186][^187][^188]

<sup>34</sup> 'So do not worry about tomorrow; for tomorrow will care for itself. Each day has enough trouble of its own.[^189][^190][^191][^192][^193][^194][^195][^196]
