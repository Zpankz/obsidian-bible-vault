# Matthew 4

## Matthew 4:1-11

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> Then Jesus was led up by the Spirit into the wilderness to be tempted by the devil.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> And after He had fasted forty days and forty nights, He then became hungry.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> And the tempter came and said to Him, 'If You are the Son of God, command that these stones become bread.'

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> But He answered and said, 'It is written, 'MAN SHALL NOT LIVE ON BREAD ALONE, BUT ON EVERY WORD THAT PROCEEDS OUT OF THE MOUTH OF GOD.''

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> Then the devil took Him into the holy city and had Him stand on the pinnacle of the temple,

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> and said to Him, 'If You are the Son of God, throw Yourself down; for it is written, 'HE WILL COMMAND HIS ANGELS CONCERNING YOU'; and 'ON [their] HANDS THEY WILL BEAR YOU UP, SO THAT YOU WILL NOT STRIKE YOUR FOOT AGAINST A STONE.''

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> Jesus said to him, 'On the other hand, it is written, 'YOU SHALL NOT PUT THE LORD YOUR GOD TO THE TEST.''

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> Again, the devil took Him to a very high mountain and showed Him all the kingdoms of the world and their glory;

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> and he said to Him, 'All these things I will give You, if You fall down and worship me.'

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> Then Jesus said to him, 'Go, Satan! For it is written, 'YOU SHALL WORSHIP THE LORD YOUR GOD, AND SERVE HIM ONLY.''

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> Then the devil left Him; and behold, angels came and [began] to minister to Him.

###### v12

###### v13

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> 'THE LAND OF ZEBULUN AND THE LAND OF NAPHTALI, BY THE WAY OF THE SEA, BEYOND THE JORDAN, GALILEE OF THE GENTILES--

###### v16
<sup>16</sup> 'THE PEOPLE WHO WERE SITTING IN DARKNESS SAW A GREAT LIGHT, AND THOSE WHO WERE SITTING IN THE LAND AND SHADOW OF DEATH, UPON THEM A LIGHT DAWNED.'

<sup>17</sup> From that time Jesus began to preach and say, 'Repent, for the kingdom of heaven is at hand.'

###### v24
<sup>24</sup> The news about Him spread throughout all Syria; and they brought to Him all who were ill, those suffering with various diseases and pains, demoniacs, epileptics, paralytics; and He healed them.
