## Matthew 4:1-11

<sup>1</sup> Then Jesus was led up by the Spirit into the wilderness to be tempted by the devil.[^1][^2][^3][^4][^5]

<sup>2</sup> And after He had fasted forty days and forty nights, He then became hungry.[^6][^7][^8][^9]

<sup>3</sup> And the tempter came and said to Him, 'If You are the Son of God, command that these stones become bread.'

<sup>4</sup> But He answered and said, 'It is written, 'MAN SHALL NOT LIVE ON BREAD ALONE, BUT ON EVERY WORD THAT PROCEEDS OUT OF THE MOUTH OF GOD.''[^10][^11][^12][^13][^14][^15][^16][^17]

<sup>5</sup> Then the devil took Him into the holy city and had Him stand on the pinnacle of the temple,

<sup>6</sup> and said to Him, 'If You are the Son of God, throw Yourself down; for it is written, 'HE WILL COMMAND HIS ANGELS CONCERNING YOU'; and 'ON [their] HANDS THEY WILL BEAR YOU UP, SO THAT YOU WILL NOT STRIKE YOUR FOOT AGAINST A STONE.''[^18][^19][^20][^21][^22]

<sup>7</sup> Jesus said to him, 'On the other hand, it is written, 'YOU SHALL NOT PUT THE LORD YOUR GOD TO THE TEST.''[^23][^24][^25][^26][^27][^28][^29][^30]

<sup>8</sup> Again, the devil took Him to a very high mountain and showed Him all the kingdoms of the world and their glory;[^31]

<sup>9</sup> and he said to Him, 'All these things I will give You, if You fall down and worship me.'

<sup>10</sup> Then Jesus said to him, 'Go, Satan! For it is written, 'YOU SHALL WORSHIP THE LORD YOUR GOD, AND SERVE HIM ONLY.''[^32][^33][^34][^35][^36][^37][^38][^39]

<sup>11</sup> Then the devil left Him; and behold, angels came and [began] to minister to Him.[^40][^41][^42]

## Matthew 4:12-17

<sup>12</sup> Now when Jesus heard that John had been taken into custody, He withdrew into Galilee;[^43]

<sup>13</sup> and leaving Nazareth, He came and settled in Capernaum, which is by the sea, in the region of Zebulun and Naphtali.

<sup>14</sup> [This was] to fulfill what was spoken through Isaiah the prophet:[^44]

<sup>15</sup> 'THE LAND OF ZEBULUN AND THE LAND OF NAPHTALI, BY THE WAY OF THE SEA, BEYOND THE JORDAN, GALILEE OF THE GENTILES--[^45]

<sup>16</sup> 'THE PEOPLE WHO WERE SITTING IN DARKNESS SAW A GREAT LIGHT, AND THOSE WHO WERE SITTING IN THE LAND AND SHADOW OF DEATH, UPON THEM A LIGHT DAWNED.'[^46][^47][^48][^49][^50][^51][^52][^53]

<sup>24</sup> The news about Him spread throughout all Syria; and they brought to Him all who were ill, those suffering with various diseases and pains, demoniacs, epileptics, paralytics; and He healed them.

<sup>25</sup> Large crowds followed Him from Galilee and [the] Decapolis and Jerusalem and Judea and [from] beyond the Jordan.
