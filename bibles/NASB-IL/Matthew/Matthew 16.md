# Matthew 16

## Matthew 16:1-4

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> The Pharisees and Sadducees came up, and testing Jesus, they asked Him to show them a sign from heaven.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> But He replied to them, 'When it is evening, you say, '[It will be] fair weather, for the sky is red.'

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> 'And in the morning, '[There will be] a storm today, for the sky is red and threatening.' Do you know how to discern the appearance of the sky, but cannot [discern] the signs of the times?

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> 'An evil and adulterous generation seeks after a sign; and a sign will not be given it, except the sign of Jonah.' And He left them and went away.

## Matthew 16:5-12

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> And the disciples came to the other side [of the sea], but they had forgotten to bring [any] bread.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> And Jesus said to them, 'Watch out and beware of the leaven of the Pharisees and Sadducees.'

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> They began to discuss [this] among themselves, saying, '[He said that] because we did not bring [any] bread.'

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> But Jesus, aware of this, said, 'You men of little faith, why do you discuss among yourselves that you have no bread?

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> 'Do you not yet understand or remember the five loaves of the five thousand, and how many baskets [full] you picked up?

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> 'Or the seven loaves of the four thousand, and how many large baskets [full] you picked up?

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> 'How is it that you do not understand that I did not speak to you concerning bread? But beware of the leaven of the Pharisees and Sadducees.'

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> Then they understood that He did not say to beware of the leaven of bread, but of the teaching of the Pharisees and Sadducees.

## Matthew 16:13-20

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> Now when Jesus came into the district of Caesarea Philippi, He was asking His disciples, 'Who do people say that the Son of Man is?'

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> And they said, 'Some [say] John the Baptist; and others, Elijah; but still others, Jeremiah, or one of the prophets.'

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> He said to them, 'But who do you say that I am?'

###### v16
<sup>16</sup> Simon Peter answered, 'You are the Christ, the Son of the living God.'

###### v17
<sup>17</sup> And Jesus said to him, 'Blessed are you, Simon Barjona, because flesh and blood did not reveal [this] to you, but My Father who is in heaven.

###### v18
<sup>18</sup> 'I also say to you that you are Peter, and upon this rock I will build My church; and the gates of Hades will not overpower it.

###### v19
<sup>19</sup> 'I will give you the keys of the kingdom of heaven; and whatever you bind on earth shall have been bound in heaven, and whatever you loose on earth shall have been loosed in heaven.'

###### v20
<sup>20</sup> Then He warned the disciples that they should tell no one that He was the Christ.
