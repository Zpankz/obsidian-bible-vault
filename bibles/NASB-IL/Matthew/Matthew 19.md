## Matthew 19:3-12

<sup>3</sup> [Some] Pharisees came to Jesus, testing Him and asking, 'Is it lawful [for a man] to divorce his wife for any reason at all?'

<sup>4</sup> And He answered and said, 'Have you not read that He who created [them] from the beginning MADE THEM MALE AND FEMALE,[^1][^2][^3][^4][^5]

<sup>5</sup> and said, 'FOR THIS REASON A MAN SHALL LEAVE HIS FATHER AND MOTHER AND BE JOINED TO HIS WIFE, AND THE TWO SHALL BECOME ONE FLESH '?[^6][^7][^8][^9][^10]

<sup>6</sup> 'So they are no longer two, but one flesh. What therefore God has joined together, let no man separate.'[^11][^12][^13][^14][^15]

<sup>7</sup> They said to Him, 'Why then did Moses command to GIVE HER A CERTIFICATE OF DIVORCE AND SEND [her] AWAY?'[^16][^17]

<sup>8</sup> He said to them, 'Because of your hardness of heart Moses permitted you to divorce your wives; but from the beginning it has not been this way.

<sup>9</sup> 'And I say to you, whoever divorces his wife, except for immorality, and marries another woman commits adultery.'[^18][^19][^20][^21][^22][^23]

<sup>10</sup> The disciples said to Him, 'If the relationship of the man with his wife is like this, it is better not to marry.'

<sup>11</sup> But He said to them, 'Not all men [can] accept this statement, but [only] those to whom it has been given.

<sup>12</sup> 'For there are eunuchs who were born that way from their mother's womb; and there are eunuchs who were made eunuchs by men; and there are [also] eunuchs who made themselves eunuchs for the sake of the kingdom of heaven. He who is able to accept [this], let him accept [it].'[^24]

## Matthew 19:23-30

<sup>23</sup> And Jesus said to His disciples, 'Truly I say to you, it is hard for a rich man to enter the kingdom of heaven.[^39]

<sup>24</sup> 'Again I say to you, it is easier for a camel to go through the eye of a needle, than for a rich man to enter the kingdom of God.'[^40]

<sup>25</sup> When the disciples heard [this], they were very astonished and said, 'Then who can be saved?'

<sup>26</sup> And looking at [them] Jesus said to them, 'With people this is impossible, but with God all things are possible.'[^41][^42][^43][^44][^45][^46][^47][^48]

<sup>27</sup> Then Peter said to Him, 'Behold, we have left everything and followed You; what then will there be for us?'[^49][^50]

<sup>28</sup> And Jesus said to them, 'Truly I say to you, that you who have followed Me, in the regeneration when the Son of Man will sit on His glorious throne, you also shall sit upon twelve thrones, judging the twelve tribes of Israel.[^51]

<sup>29</sup> 'And everyone who has left houses or brothers or sisters or father or mother or children or farms for My name's sake, will receive many times as much, and will inherit eternal life.[^52][^53][^54][^55][^56][^57][^58][^59]

<sup>30</sup> 'But many [who are] first will be last; and [the] last, first.[^60][^61][^62]
