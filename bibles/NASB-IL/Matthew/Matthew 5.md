## Matthew 5:21-26

###### v21
<sup>21</sup> 'You have heard that the ancients were told, 'YOU SHALL NOT COMMIT MURDER ' and 'Whoever commits murder shall be liable to the court.'

###### v22
<sup>22</sup> 'But I say to you that everyone who is angry with his brother shall be guilty before the court; and whoever says to his brother, 'You good-for-nothing,' shall be guilty before the supreme court; and whoever says, 'You fool,' shall be guilty [enough to go] into the fiery hell.

###### v23
<sup>23</sup> 'Therefore if you are presenting your offering at the altar, and there remember that your brother has something against you,

###### v24

## Matthew 5:43-48

###### v43
<sup>43</sup> 'You have heard that it was said, 'YOU SHALL LOVE YOUR NEIGHBOR and hate your enemy.'

###### v44
<sup>44</sup> 'But I say to you, love your enemies and pray for those who persecute you,

###### v45
<sup>45</sup> so that you may be sons of your Father who is in heaven; for He causes His sun to rise on [the] evil and [the] good, and sends rain on [the] righteous and [the] unrighteous.

###### v46
<sup>46</sup> 'For if you love those who love you, what reward do you have? Do not even the tax collectors do the same?

###### v47
<sup>47</sup> 'If you greet only your brothers, what more are you doing [than others]? Do not even the Gentiles do the same?

###### v48
<sup>48</sup> 'Therefore you are to be perfect, as your heavenly Father is perfect.
