## Matthew 5:43-48

<sup>43</sup> 'You have heard that it was said, 'YOU SHALL LOVE YOUR NEIGHBOR and hate your enemy.'[^225][^226][^227][^228][^229][^230]

<sup>44</sup> 'But I say to you, love your enemies and pray for those who persecute you,[^231][^232][^233][^234][^235][^236][^237][^238]

<sup>46</sup> 'For if you love those who love you, what reward do you have? Do not even the tax collectors do the same?[^243][^244]

<sup>47</sup> 'If you greet only your brothers, what more are you doing [than others]? Do not even the Gentiles do the same?

<sup>48</sup> 'Therefore you are to be perfect, as your heavenly Father is perfect.[^245][^246][^247][^248][^249][^250][^251][^252]
