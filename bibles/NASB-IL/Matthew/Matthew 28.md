## Matthew 28:1-10

<sup>1</sup> Now after the Sabbath, as it began to dawn toward the first [day] of the week, Mary Magdalene and the other Mary came to look at the grave.[^1]

<sup>2</sup> And behold, a severe earthquake had occurred, for an angel of the Lord descended from heaven and came and rolled away the stone and sat upon it.

<sup>3</sup> And his appearance was like lightning, and his clothing as white as snow.

<sup>4</sup> The guards shook for fear of him and became like dead men.

<sup>5</sup> The angel said to the women, 'Do not be afraid; for I know that you are looking for Jesus who has been crucified.

<sup>6</sup> 'He is not here, for He has risen, just as He said. Come, see the place where He was lying.[^2][^3][^4][^5][^6][^7][^8][^9]

<sup>7</sup> 'Go quickly and tell His disciples that He has risen from the dead; and behold, He is going ahead of you into Galilee, there you will see Him; behold, I have told you.'

<sup>8</sup> And they left the tomb quickly with fear and great joy and ran to report it to His disciples.

<sup>9</sup> And behold, Jesus met them and greeted them. And they came up and took hold of His feet and worshiped Him.[^10]

<sup>10</sup> Then Jesus said to them, 'Do not be afraid; go and take word to My brethren to leave for Galilee, and there they will see Me.'
