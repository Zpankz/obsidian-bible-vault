# Matthew 8

## Matthew 8:1-4

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> When Jesus came down from the mountain, large crowds followed Him.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> And a leper came to Him and bowed down before Him, and said, 'Lord, if You are willing, You can make me clean.'

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> Jesus stretched out His hand and touched him, saying, 'I am willing; be cleansed.' And immediately his leprosy was cleansed.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> And Jesus said to him, 'See that you tell no one; but go, show yourself to the priest and present the offering that Moses commanded, as a testimony to them.'

## Matthew 8:5-13

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> And when Jesus entered Capernaum, a centurion came to Him, imploring Him,

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> and saying, 'Lord, my servant is lying paralyzed at home, fearfully tormented.'

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> Jesus said to him, 'I will come and heal him.'

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> But the centurion said, 'Lord, I am not worthy for You to come under my roof, but just say the word, and my servant will be healed.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> 'For I also am a man under authority, with soldiers under me; and I say to this one, 'Go!' and he goes, and to another, 'Come!' and he comes, and to my slave, 'Do this!' and he does [it].'

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> Now when Jesus heard [this], He marveled and said to those who were following, 'Truly I say to you, I have not found such great faith with anyone in Israel.

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> 'I say to you that many will come from east and west, and recline [at the table] with Abraham, Isaac and Jacob in the kingdom of heaven;

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> but the sons of the kingdom will be cast out into the outer darkness; in that place there will be weeping and gnashing of teeth.'

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> And Jesus said to the centurion, 'Go; it shall be done for you as you have believed.' And the servant was healed that [very] moment.
