## Matthew 7:1-6

<sup>5</sup> 'You hypocrite, first take the log out of your own eye, and then you will see clearly to take the speck out of your brother's eye.[^19][^20]

<sup>6</sup> 'Do not give what is holy to dogs, and do not throw your pearls before swine, or they will trample them under their feet, and turn and tear you to pieces.[^21][^22][^23][^24][^25][^26][^27][^28]

## Matthew 7:21-29

<sup>22</sup> 'Many will say to Me on that day, 'Lord, Lord, did we not prophesy in Your name, and in Your name cast out demons, and in Your name perform many miracles?'[^108][^109][^110][^111]

<sup>23</sup> 'And then I will declare to them, 'I never knew you; DEPART FROM ME, YOU WHO PRACTICE LAWLESSNESS.'[^112][^113][^114][^115][^116][^117][^118][^119]

<sup>24</sup> 'Therefore everyone who hears these words of Mine and acts on them, may be compared to a wise man who built his house on the rock.[^120][^121][^122][^123][^124][^125][^126][^127]
