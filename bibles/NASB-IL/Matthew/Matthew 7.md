# Matthew 7

## Matthew 7:1-6

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> 'Do not judge so that you will not be judged.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> 'For in the way you judge, you will be judged; and by your standard of measure, it will be measured to you.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> 'Why do you look at the speck that is in your brother's eye, but do not notice the log that is in your own eye?

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> 'Or how can you say to your brother, 'Let me take the speck out of your eye,' and behold, the log is in your own eye?

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> 'You hypocrite, first take the log out of your own eye, and then you will see clearly to take the speck out of your brother's eye.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> 'Do not give what is holy to dogs, and do not throw your pearls before swine, or they will trample them under their feet, and turn and tear you to pieces.

## Matthew 7:7-11

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> 'Ask, and it will be given to you; seek, and you will find; knock, and it will be opened to you.

<sup>8</sup> 'For everyone who asks receives, and he who seeks finds, and to him who knocks it will be opened.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> 'Or what man is there among you who, when his son asks for a loaf, will give him a stone?

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> 'Or if he asks for a fish, he will not give him a snake, will he?

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> 'If you then, being evil, know how to give good gifts to your children, how much more will your Father who is in heaven give what is good to those who ask Him!

## Matthew 7:21-29

###### v21
<sup>21</sup> 'Not everyone who says to Me, 'Lord, Lord,' will enter the kingdom of heaven, but he who does the will of My Father who is in heaven [will enter].

###### v22
<sup>22</sup> 'Many will say to Me on that day, 'Lord, Lord, did we not prophesy in Your name, and in Your name cast out demons, and in Your name perform many miracles?'

###### v23
<sup>23</sup> 'And then I will declare to them, 'I never knew you; DEPART FROM ME, YOU WHO PRACTICE LAWLESSNESS.'

###### v24
<sup>24</sup> 'Therefore everyone who hears these words of Mine and acts on them, may be compared to a wise man who built his house on the rock.

###### v25
<sup>25</sup> 'And the rain fell, and the floods came, and the winds blew and slammed against that house; and [yet] it did not fall, for it had been founded on the rock.

###### v26
<sup>26</sup> 'Everyone who hears these words of Mine and does not act on them, will be like a foolish man who built his house on the sand.

###### v27
<sup>27</sup> 'The rain fell, and the floods came, and the winds blew and slammed against that house; and it fell-- and great was its fall.'

###### v28
<sup>28</sup> When Jesus had finished these words, the crowds were amazed at His teaching;

###### v29
<sup>29</sup> for He was teaching them as [one] having authority, and not as their scribes.
