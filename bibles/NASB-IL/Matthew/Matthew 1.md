# Matthew 1

## Matthew 1:1-17

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> The record of the genealogy of Jesus the Messiah, the son of David, the son of Abraham:

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> Abraham was the father of Isaac, Isaac the father of Jacob, and Jacob the father of Judah and his brothers.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> Judah was the father of Perez and Zerah by Tamar, Perez was the father of Hezron, and Hezron the father of Ram.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> Ram was the father of Amminadab, Amminadab the father of Nahshon, and Nahshon the father of Salmon.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> Salmon was the father of Boaz by Rahab, Boaz was the father of Obed by Ruth, and Obed the father of Jesse.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> Jesse was the father of David the king. David was the father of Solomon by Bathsheba who had been the wife of Uriah.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> Solomon was the father of Rehoboam, Rehoboam the father of Abijah, and Abijah the father of Asa.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> Asa was the father of Jehoshaphat, Jehoshaphat the father of Joram, and Joram the father of Uzziah.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> Uzziah was the father of Jotham, Jotham the father of Ahaz, and Ahaz the father of Hezekiah.

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> Hezekiah was the father of Manasseh, Manasseh the father of Amon, and Amon the father of Josiah.

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> Josiah became the father of Jeconiah and his brothers, at the time of the deportation to Babylon.

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> After the deportation to Babylon: Jeconiah became the father of Shealtiel, and Shealtiel the father of Zerubbabel.

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> Zerubbabel was the father of Abihud, Abihud the father of Eliakim, and Eliakim the father of Azor.

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> Azor was the father of Zadok, Zadok the father of Achim, and Achim the father of Eliud.

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> Eliud was the father of Eleazar, Eleazar the father of Matthan, and Matthan the father of Jacob.

###### v16
<sup>16</sup> Jacob was the father of Joseph the husband of Mary, by whom Jesus was born, who is called the Messiah.

###### v17
<sup>17</sup> So all the generations from Abraham to David are fourteen generations; from David to the deportation to Babylon, fourteen generations; and from the deportation to Babylon to the Messiah, fourteen generations.

## Matthew 1:18-25

###### v18
<sup>18</sup> Now the birth of Jesus Christ was as follows: when His mother Mary had been betrothed to Joseph, before they came together she was found to be with child by the Holy Spirit.

###### v19
<sup>19</sup> And Joseph her husband, being a righteous man and not wanting to disgrace her, planned to send her away secretly.

###### v20
<sup>20</sup> But when he had considered this, behold, an angel of the Lord appeared to him in a dream, saying, 'Joseph, son of David, do not be afraid to take Mary as your wife; for the Child who has been conceived in her is of the Holy Spirit.

###### v21
<sup>21</sup> 'She will bear a Son; and you shall call His name Jesus, for He will save His people from their sins.'

###### v22
<sup>22</sup> Now all this took place to fulfill what was spoken by the Lord through the prophet:

###### v23
<sup>23</sup> 'BEHOLD, THE VIRGIN SHALL BE WITH CHILD AND SHALL BEAR A SON, AND THEY SHALL CALL HIS NAME IMMANUEL,' which translated means, 'GOD WITH US.'

###### v24
<sup>24</sup> And Joseph awoke from his sleep and did as the angel of the Lord commanded him, and took [Mary] as his wife,

###### v25
<sup>25</sup> but kept her a virgin until she gave birth to a Son; and he called His name Jesus.
