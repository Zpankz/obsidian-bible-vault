# Matthew 14

## Matthew 14:1-12

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> At that time Herod the tetrarch heard the news about Jesus,

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> and said to his servants, 'This is John the Baptist; he has risen from the dead, and that is why miraculous powers are at work in him.'

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> For when Herod had John arrested, he bound him and put him in prison because of Herodias, the wife of his brother Philip.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> For John had been saying to him, 'It is not lawful for you to have her.'

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> Although Herod wanted to put him to death, he feared the crowd, because they regarded John as a prophet.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> But when Herod's birthday came, the daughter of Herodias danced before [them] and pleased Herod,

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> so [much] that he promised with an oath to give her whatever she asked.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> Having been prompted by her mother, she said, 'Give me here on a platter the head of John the Baptist.'

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> Although he was grieved, the king commanded [it] to be given because of his oaths, and because of his dinner guests.

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> He sent and had John beheaded in the prison.

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> And his head was brought on a platter and given to the girl, and she brought it to her mother.

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> His disciples came and took away the body and buried it; and they went and reported to Jesus.

## Matthew 14:13-21

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> Now when Jesus heard [about John], He withdrew from there in a boat to a secluded place by Himself; and when the people heard [of this], they followed Him on foot from the cities.

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> When He went ashore, He saw a large crowd, and felt compassion for them and healed their sick.

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> When it was evening, the disciples came to Him and said, 'This place is desolate and the hour is already late; so send the crowds away, that they may go into the villages and buy food for themselves.'

###### v16
<sup>16</sup> But Jesus said to them, 'They do not need to go away; you give them [something] to eat!'

###### v17
<sup>17</sup> They said to Him, 'We have here only five loaves and two fish.'

###### v18
<sup>18</sup> And He said, 'Bring them here to Me.'

###### v19
<sup>19</sup> Ordering the people to sit down on the grass, He took the five loaves and the two fish, and looking up toward heaven, He blessed [the food], and breaking the loaves He gave them to the disciples, and the disciples [gave them] to the crowds,

###### v20
<sup>20</sup> and they all ate and were satisfied. They picked up what was left over of the broken pieces, twelve full baskets.

###### v21
<sup>21</sup> There were about five thousand men who ate, besides women and children.

## Matthew 14:22-33

###### v22
<sup>22</sup> Immediately He made the disciples get into the boat and go ahead of Him to the other side, while He sent the crowds away.

###### v23
<sup>23</sup> After He had sent the crowds away, He went up on the mountain by Himself to pray; and when it was evening, He was there alone.

###### v24
<sup>24</sup> But the boat was already a long distance from the land, battered by the waves; for the wind was contrary.

###### v25
<sup>25</sup> And in the fourth watch of the night He came to them, walking on the sea.

###### v26
<sup>26</sup> When the disciples saw Him walking on the sea, they were terrified, and said, 'It is a ghost!' And they cried out in fear.

###### v27
<sup>27</sup> But immediately Jesus spoke to them, saying, 'Take courage, it is I; do not be afraid.'

###### v28
<sup>28</sup> Peter said to Him, 'Lord, if it is You, command me to come to You on the water.'

###### v29
<sup>29</sup> And He said, 'Come!' And Peter got out of the boat, and walked on the water and came toward Jesus.

###### v30
<sup>30</sup> But seeing the wind, he became frightened, and beginning to sink, he cried out, 'Lord, save me!'

###### v31
<sup>31</sup> Immediately Jesus stretched out His hand and took hold of him, and said to him, 'You of little faith, why did you doubt?'

###### v32
<sup>32</sup> When they got into the boat, the wind stopped.

###### v33
<sup>33</sup> And those who were in the boat worshiped Him, saying, 'You are certainly God's Son!'
