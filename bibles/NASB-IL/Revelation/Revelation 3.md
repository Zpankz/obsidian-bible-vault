## Revelation 3:1-6

<sup>1</sup> 'To the angel of the church in Sardis write: He who has the seven Spirits of God and the seven stars, says this: 'I know your deeds, that you have a name that you are alive, but you are dead.[^1][^2][^3][^4][^5]

<sup>2</sup> 'Wake up, and strengthen the things that remain, which were about to die; for I have not found your deeds completed in the sight of My God.[^6][^7][^8][^9][^10][^11][^12]

<sup>3</sup> 'So remember what you have received and heard; and keep [it], and repent. Therefore if you do not wake up, I will come like a thief, and you will not know at what hour I will come to you.[^13][^14][^15][^16][^17][^18][^19][^20]

<sup>4</sup> 'But you have a few people in Sardis who have not soiled their garments; and they will walk with Me in white, for they are worthy.[^21][^22][^23][^24][^25]

<sup>5</sup> 'He who overcomes will thus be clothed in white garments; and I will not erase his name from the book of life, and I will confess his name before My Father and before His angels.[^26][^27][^28][^29][^30][^31][^32][^33]

<sup>6</sup> 'He who has an ear, let him hear what the Spirit says to the churches.'

## Revelation 3:7-13

<sup>7</sup> 'And to the angel of the church in Philadelphia write: He who is holy, who is true, who has the key of David, who opens and no one will shut, and who shuts and no one opens, says this:[^34][^35][^36][^37][^38]

<sup>8</sup> 'I know your deeds. Behold, I have put before you an open door which no one can shut, because you have a little power, and have kept My word, and have not denied My name.[^39][^40][^41][^42][^43][^44][^45][^46]

<sup>9</sup> 'Behold, I will cause [those] of the synagogue of Satan, who say that they are Jews and are not, but lie-- I will make them come and bow down at your feet, and [make them] know that I have loved you.[^47][^48][^49][^50]

<sup>10</sup> 'Because you have kept the word of My perseverance, I also will keep you from the hour of testing, that [hour] which is about to come upon the whole world, to test those who dwell on the earth.[^51][^52][^53][^54][^55][^56][^57][^58]

<sup>11</sup> 'I am coming quickly; hold fast what you have, so that no one will take your crown.[^59][^60][^61][^62][^63][^64][^65][^66]

<sup>12</sup> 'He who overcomes, I will make him a pillar in the temple of My God, and he will not go out from it anymore; and I will write on him the name of My God, and the name of the city of My God, the new Jerusalem, which comes down out of heaven from My God, and My new name.[^67][^68][^69][^70][^71][^72]

<sup>13</sup> 'He who has an ear, let him hear what the Spirit says to the churches.'
