## Titus 2:1-10

<sup>1</sup> But as for you, speak the things which are fitting for sound doctrine.[^1]

<sup>2</sup> Older men are to be temperate, dignified, sensible, sound in faith, in love, in perseverance.[^2][^3]

<sup>3</sup> Older women likewise are to be reverent in their behavior, not malicious gossips nor enslaved to much wine, teaching what is good,[^4][^5][^6][^7]

<sup>4</sup> so that they may encourage the young women to love their husbands, to love their children,[^8]

<sup>5</sup> [to be] sensible, pure, workers at home, kind, being subject to their own husbands, so that the word of God will not be dishonored.[^9][^10][^11][^12][^13][^14]

<sup>6</sup> Likewise urge the young men to be sensible;

<sup>7</sup> in all things show yourself to be an example of good deeds, [with] purity in doctrine, dignified,[^15][^16][^17]

<sup>8</sup> sound [in] speech which is beyond reproach, so that the opponent will be put to shame, having nothing bad to say about us.[^18][^19]

<sup>9</sup> [Urge] bondslaves to be subject to their own masters in everything, to be well-pleasing, not argumentative,

<sup>10</sup> not pilfering, but showing all good faith so that they will adorn the doctrine of God our Savior in every respect.

## Titus 2:11-14

<sup>11</sup> For the grace of God has appeared, bringing salvation to all men,[^20][^21][^22][^23][^24][^25][^26][^27]

<sup>12</sup> instructing us to deny ungodliness and worldly desires and to live sensibly, righteously and godly in the present age,[^28][^29][^30][^31][^32][^33][^34][^35]

<sup>13</sup> looking for the blessed hope and the appearing of the glory of our great God and Savior, Christ Jesus,[^36][^37][^38][^39][^40][^41][^42][^43]

<sup>14</sup> who gave Himself for us to redeem us from every lawless deed, and to purify for Himself a people for His own possession, zealous for good deeds.[^44][^45][^46][^47][^48][^49][^50][^51]
