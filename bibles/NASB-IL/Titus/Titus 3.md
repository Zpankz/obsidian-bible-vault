## Titus 3:1-8

<sup>1</sup> Remind them to be subject to rulers, to authorities, to be obedient, to be ready for every good deed,[^1][^2][^3][^4][^5]

<sup>2</sup> to malign no one, to be peaceable, gentle, showing every consideration for all men.[^6][^7][^8][^9]

<sup>3</sup> For we also once were foolish ourselves, disobedient, deceived, enslaved to various lusts and pleasures, spending our life in malice and envy, hateful, hating one another.[^10][^11][^12][^13][^14][^15][^16][^17]

<sup>4</sup> But when the kindness of God our Savior and [His] love for mankind appeared,[^18][^19][^20][^21]

<sup>5</sup> He saved us, not on the basis of deeds which we have done in righteousness, but according to His mercy, by the washing of regeneration and renewing by the Holy Spirit,[^22][^23][^24][^25][^26][^27][^28][^29]

<sup>6</sup> whom He poured out upon us richly through Jesus Christ our Savior,[^30]

<sup>7</sup> so that being justified by His grace we would be made heirs according to [the] hope of eternal life.[^31][^32]

<sup>8</sup> This is a trustworthy statement; and concerning these things I want you to speak confidently, so that those who have believed God will be careful to engage in good deeds. These things are good and profitable for men.[^33]

## Titus 3:9-15

<sup>9</sup> But avoid foolish controversies and genealogies and strife and disputes about the Law, for they are unprofitable and worthless.[^34][^35][^36][^37][^38][^39][^40]

<sup>10</sup> Reject a factious man after a first and second warning,[^41][^42][^43][^44][^45]

<sup>11</sup> knowing that such a man is perverted and is sinning, being self-condemned.

<sup>12</sup> When I send Artemas or Tychicus to you, make every effort to come to me at Nicopolis, for I have decided to spend the winter there.

<sup>13</sup> Diligently help Zenas the lawyer and Apollos on their way so that nothing is lacking for them.

<sup>14</sup> Our people must also learn to engage in good deeds to meet pressing needs, so that they will not be unfruitful.[^46]

<sup>15</sup> All who are with me greet you. Greet those who love us in [the] faith. Grace be with you all.
