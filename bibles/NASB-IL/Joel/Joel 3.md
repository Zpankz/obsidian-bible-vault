# Joel 3

## Joel 3:1-8

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> 'For behold, in those days and at that time, When I restore the fortunes of Judah and Jerusalem,

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> I will gather all the nations And bring them down to the valley of Jehoshaphat. Then I will enter into judgment with them there On behalf of My people and My inheritance, Israel, Whom they have scattered among the nations; And they have divided up My land.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> 'They have also cast lots for My people, Traded a boy for a harlot And sold a girl for wine that they may drink.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> 'Moreover, what are you to Me, O Tyre, Sidon and all the regions of Philistia? Are you rendering Me a recompense? But if you do recompense Me, swiftly and speedily I will return your recompense on your head.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> 'Since you have taken My silver and My gold, brought My precious treasures to your temples,

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> and sold the sons of Judah and Jerusalem to the Greeks in order to remove them far from their territory,

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> behold, I am going to arouse them from the place where you have sold them, and return your recompense on your head.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> 'Also I will sell your sons and your daughters into the hand of the sons of Judah, and they will sell them to the Sabeans, to a distant nation,' for the LORD has spoken.

## Joel 3:9-17

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> Proclaim this among the nations: Prepare a war; rouse the mighty men! Let all the soldiers draw near, let them come up!

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> Beat your plowshares into swords And your pruning hooks into spears; Let the weak say, 'I am a mighty man.'

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> Hasten and come, all you surrounding nations, And gather yourselves there. Bring down, O LORD, Your mighty ones.

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> Let the nations be aroused And come up to the valley of Jehoshaphat, For there I will sit to judge All the surrounding nations.

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> Put in the sickle, for the harvest is ripe. Come, tread, for the wine press is full; The vats overflow, for their wickedness is great.

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> Multitudes, multitudes in the valley of decision! For the day of the LORD is near in the valley of decision.

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> The sun and moon grow dark And the stars lose their brightness.

###### v16
<sup>16</sup> The LORD roars from Zion And utters His voice from Jerusalem, And the heavens and the earth tremble. But the LORD is a refuge for His people And a stronghold to the sons of Israel.

###### v17
<sup>17</sup> Then you will know that I am the LORD your God, Dwelling in Zion, My holy mountain. So Jerusalem will be holy, And strangers will pass through it no more.

## Joel 3:18-21

###### v18
<sup>18</sup> And in that day The mountains will drip with sweet wine, And the hills will flow with milk, And all the brooks of Judah will flow with water; And a spring will go out from the house of the LORD To water the valley of Shittim.

###### v19
<sup>19</sup> Egypt will become a waste, And Edom will become a desolate wilderness, Because of the violence done to the sons of Judah, In whose land they have shed innocent blood.

###### v20
<sup>20</sup> But Judah will be inhabited forever And Jerusalem for all generations.

###### v21
<sup>21</sup> And I will avenge their blood which I have not avenged, For the LORD dwells in Zion.
