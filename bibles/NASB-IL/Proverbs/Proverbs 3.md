## Proverbs 3:1-6

<sup>1</sup> My son, do not forget my teaching, But let your heart keep my commandments;[^1][^2][^3][^4][^5][^6][^7][^8]

<sup>2</sup> For length of days and years of life And peace they will add to you.[^9][^10][^11][^12][^13][^14][^15][^16]

<sup>3</sup> Do not let kindness and truth leave you; Bind them around your neck, Write them on the tablet of your heart.[^17][^18][^19][^20][^21][^22][^23][^24]

<sup>4</sup> So you will find favor and good repute In the sight of God and man.[^25][^26][^27][^28][^29][^30][^31][^32]

<sup>5</sup> Trust in the LORD with all your heart And do not lean on your own understanding.[^33][^34][^35][^36][^37][^38][^39][^40]

<sup>6</sup> In all your ways acknowledge Him, And He will make your paths straight.[^41][^42][^43][^44][^45][^46][^47][^48]

<sup>10</sup> So your barns will be filled with plenty And your vats will overflow with new wine.[^69][^70]

<sup>11</sup> My son, do not reject the discipline of the LORD Or loathe His reproof,[^71][^72][^73][^74][^75][^76]

## Proverbs 3:13-20

<sup>13</sup> How blessed is the man who finds wisdom And the man who gains understanding.[^82]

<sup>14</sup> For her profit is better than the profit of silver And her gain better than fine gold.[^83]

<sup>15</sup> She is more precious than jewels; And nothing you desire compares with her.

<sup>16</sup> Long life is in her right hand; In her left hand are riches and honor.[^84][^85]

<sup>17</sup> Her ways are pleasant ways And all her paths are peace.[^86]

<sup>18</sup> She is a tree of life to those who take hold of her, And happy are all who hold her fast.[^87][^88][^89]

<sup>19</sup> The LORD by wisdom founded the earth, By understanding He established the heavens.[^90][^91]

<sup>20</sup> By His knowledge the deeps were broken up And the skies drip with dew.

## Proverbs 3:27-35

<sup>27</sup> Do not withhold good from those to whom it is due, When it is in your power to do [it].[^108][^109][^110]

<sup>28</sup> Do not say to your neighbor, 'Go, and come back, And tomorrow I will give [it],' When you have it with you.[^111]

<sup>29</sup> Do not devise harm against your neighbor, While he lives securely beside you.

<sup>30</sup> Do not contend with a man without cause, If he has done you no harm.

<sup>31</sup> Do not envy a man of violence And do not choose any of his ways.[^112][^113]

<sup>32</sup> For the devious are an abomination to the LORD; But He is intimate with the upright.[^114]

<sup>33</sup> The curse of the LORD is on the house of the wicked, But He blesses the dwelling of the righteous.[^115][^116]

<sup>34</sup> Though He scoffs at the scoffers, Yet He gives grace to the afflicted.[^117][^118][^119][^120]

<sup>35</sup> The wise will inherit honor, But fools display dishonor.
