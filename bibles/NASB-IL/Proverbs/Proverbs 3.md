# Proverbs 3

## Proverbs 3:1-6

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> My son, do not forget my teaching, But let your heart keep my commandments;

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> For length of days and years of life And peace they will add to you.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> Do not let kindness and truth leave you; Bind them around your neck, Write them on the tablet of your heart.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> So you will find favor and good repute In the sight of God and man.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> Trust in the LORD with all your heart And do not lean on your own understanding.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> In all your ways acknowledge Him, And He will make your paths straight.

## Proverbs 3:7-12

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> Do not be wise in your own eyes; Fear the LORD and turn away from evil.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> It will be healing to your body And refreshment to your bones.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> Honor the LORD from your wealth And from the first of all your produce;

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> So your barns will be filled with plenty And your vats will overflow with new wine.

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> My son, do not reject the discipline of the LORD Or loathe His reproof,

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> For whom the LORD loves He reproves, Even as a father [corrects] the son in whom he delights.

## Proverbs 3:13-20

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> How blessed is the man who finds wisdom And the man who gains understanding.

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> For her profit is better than the profit of silver And her gain better than fine gold.

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> She is more precious than jewels; And nothing you desire compares with her.

###### v16
<sup>16</sup> Long life is in her right hand; In her left hand are riches and honor.

###### v17
<sup>17</sup> Her ways are pleasant ways And all her paths are peace.

###### v18
<sup>18</sup> She is a tree of life to those who take hold of her, And happy are all who hold her fast.

###### v19
<sup>19</sup> The LORD by wisdom founded the earth, By understanding He established the heavens.

###### v20
<sup>20</sup> By His knowledge the deeps were broken up And the skies drip with dew.

## Proverbs 3:27-35

###### v27
<sup>27</sup> Do not withhold good from those to whom it is due, When it is in your power to do [it].

###### v28
<sup>28</sup> Do not say to your neighbor, 'Go, and come back, And tomorrow I will give [it],' When you have it with you.

###### v29
<sup>29</sup> Do not devise harm against your neighbor, While he lives securely beside you.

###### v30
<sup>30</sup> Do not contend with a man without cause, If he has done you no harm.

###### v31
<sup>31</sup> Do not envy a man of violence And do not choose any of his ways.

###### v32
<sup>32</sup> For the devious are an abomination to the LORD; But He is intimate with the upright.

###### v33
<sup>33</sup> The curse of the LORD is on the house of the wicked, But He blesses the dwelling of the righteous.

###### v34
<sup>34</sup> Though He scoffs at the scoffers, Yet He gives grace to the afflicted.

###### v35
<sup>35</sup> The wise will inherit honor, But fools display dishonor.
