## Proverbs 23:19-28

###### v19
<sup>19</sup> Listen, my son, and be wise, And direct your heart in the way.

###### v20
<sup>20</sup> Do not be with heavy drinkers of wine, [Or] with gluttonous eaters of meat;

###### v21
<sup>21</sup> For the heavy drinker and the glutton will come to poverty, And drowsiness will clothe [one] with rags.

###### v22
<sup>22</sup> Listen to your father who begot you, And do not despise your mother when she is old.

###### v23
<sup>23</sup> Buy truth, and do not sell [it], [Get] wisdom and instruction and understanding.

###### v24
<sup>24</sup> The father of the righteous will greatly rejoice, And he who sires a wise son will be glad in him.

###### v25
<sup>25</sup> Let your father and your mother be glad, And let her rejoice who gave birth to you.

###### v26
<sup>26</sup> Give me your heart, my son, And let your eyes delight in my ways.

###### v27
<sup>27</sup> For a harlot is a deep pit And an adulterous woman is a narrow well.

###### v28
<sup>28</sup> Surely she lurks as a robber, And increases the faithless among men.

## Proverbs 23:29-35

###### v29
<sup>29</sup> Who has woe? Who has sorrow? Who has contentions? Who has complaining? Who has wounds without cause? Who has redness of eyes?

###### v30
<sup>30</sup> Those who linger long over wine, Those who go to taste mixed wine.

###### v31
<sup>31</sup> Do not look on the wine when it is red, When it sparkles in the cup, When it goes down smoothly;

###### v32
<sup>32</sup> At the last it bites like a serpent And stings like a viper.

###### v33
<sup>33</sup> Your eyes will see strange things And your mind will utter perverse things.

###### v34
<sup>34</sup> And you will be like one who lies down in the middle of the sea, Or like one who lies down on the top of a mast.

###### v35
<sup>35</sup> 'They struck me, [but] I did not become ill; They beat me, [but] I did not know [it]. When shall I awake? I will seek another drink.'
