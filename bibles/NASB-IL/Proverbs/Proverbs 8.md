## Proverbs 8:1-11

<sup>1</sup> Does not wisdom call, And understanding lift up her voice?

<sup>2</sup> On top of the heights beside the way, Where the paths meet, she takes her stand;

<sup>3</sup> Beside the gates, at the opening to the city, At the entrance of the doors, she cries out:

<sup>4</sup> 'To you, O men, I call, And my voice is to the sons of men.

<sup>5</sup> 'O naive ones, understand prudence; And, O fools, understand wisdom.

<sup>6</sup> 'Listen, for I will speak noble things; And the opening of my lips [will reveal] right things.

<sup>7</sup> 'For my mouth will utter truth; And wickedness is an abomination to my lips.

<sup>8</sup> 'All the utterances of my mouth are in righteousness; There is nothing crooked or perverted in them.

<sup>9</sup> 'They are all straightforward to him who understands, And right to those who find knowledge.

<sup>10</sup> 'Take my instruction and not silver, And knowledge rather than choicest gold.[^1][^2][^3]

<sup>11</sup> 'For wisdom is better than jewels; And all desirable things cannot compare with her.

## Proverbs 8:12-21

<sup>12</sup> 'I, wisdom, dwell with prudence, And I find knowledge [and] discretion.

<sup>13</sup> 'The fear of the LORD is to hate evil; Pride and arrogance and the evil way And the perverted mouth, I hate.[^4][^5][^6][^7][^8][^9][^10][^11]

<sup>14</sup> 'Counsel is mine and sound wisdom; I am understanding, power is mine.

<sup>15</sup> 'By me kings reign, And rulers decree justice.[^12]

<sup>16</sup> 'By me princes rule, and nobles, All who judge rightly.

<sup>17</sup> 'I love those who love me; And those who diligently seek me will find me.[^13][^14][^15][^16][^17][^18][^19][^20]

<sup>18</sup> 'Riches and honor are with me, Enduring wealth and righteousness.

<sup>19</sup> 'My fruit is better than gold, even pure gold, And my yield [better] than choicest silver.

<sup>20</sup> 'I walk in the way of righteousness, In the midst of the paths of justice,

<sup>21</sup> To endow those who love me with wealth, That I may fill their treasuries.

## Proverbs 8:22-31

<sup>22</sup> 'The LORD possessed me at the beginning of His way, Before His works of old.[^21][^22][^23]

<sup>23</sup> 'From everlasting I was established, From the beginning, from the earliest times of the earth.[^24]

<sup>24</sup> 'When there were no depths I was brought forth, When there were no springs abounding with water.

<sup>25</sup> 'Before the mountains were settled, Before the hills I was brought forth;

<sup>26</sup> While He had not yet made the earth and the fields, Nor the first dust of the world.

<sup>27</sup> 'When He established the heavens, I was there, When He inscribed a circle on the face of the deep,[^25][^26]

<sup>28</sup> When He made firm the skies above, When the springs of the deep became fixed,

<sup>29</sup> When He set for the sea its boundary So that the water would not transgress His command, When He marked out the foundations of the earth;[^27]

<sup>30</sup> Then I was beside Him, [as] a master workman; And I was daily [His] delight, Rejoicing always before Him,[^28]

<sup>31</sup> Rejoicing in the world, His earth, And [having] my delight in the sons of men.

## Proverbs 8:32-36

<sup>32</sup> 'Now therefore, [O] sons, listen to me, For blessed are they who keep my ways.[^29][^30]

<sup>33</sup> 'Heed instruction and be wise, And do not neglect [it].

<sup>34</sup> 'Blessed is the man who listens to me, Watching daily at my gates, Waiting at my doorposts.[^31][^32][^33][^34][^35]

<sup>35</sup> 'For he who finds me finds life And obtains favor from the LORD.[^36][^37][^38][^39]

<sup>36</sup> 'But he who sins against me injures himself; All those who hate me love death.'[^40][^41]
