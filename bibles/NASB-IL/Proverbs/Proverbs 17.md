## Proverbs 17:27-28

<sup>27</sup> He who restrains his words has knowledge, And he who has a cool spirit is a man of understanding.[^29][^30][^31][^32][^33][^34]

<sup>28</sup> Even a fool, when he keeps silent, is considered wise; When he closes his lips, he is [considered] prudent.[^35][^36][^37][^38]
