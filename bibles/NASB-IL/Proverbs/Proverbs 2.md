# Proverbs 2

## Proverbs 2:1-9

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> My son, if you will receive my words And treasure my commandments within you,

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> Make your ear attentive to wisdom, Incline your heart to understanding;

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> For if you cry for discernment, Lift your voice for understanding;

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> If you seek her as silver And search for her as for hidden treasures;

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> Then you will discern the fear of the LORD And discover the knowledge of God.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> For the LORD gives wisdom; From His mouth [come] knowledge and understanding.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> He stores up sound wisdom for the upright; [He is] a shield to those who walk in integrity,

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> Guarding the paths of justice, And He preserves the way of His godly ones.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> Then you will discern righteousness and justice And equity [and] every good course.

## Proverbs 2:10-22

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> For wisdom will enter your heart And knowledge will be pleasant to your soul;

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> Discretion will guard you, Understanding will watch over you,

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> To deliver you from the way of evil, From the man who speaks perverse things;

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> From those who leave the paths of uprightness To walk in the ways of darkness;

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> Who delight in doing evil And rejoice in the perversity of evil;

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> Whose paths are crooked, And who are devious in their ways;

###### v16
<sup>16</sup> To deliver you from the strange woman, From the adulteress who flatters with her words;

###### v17
<sup>17</sup> That leaves the companion of her youth And forgets the covenant of her God;

###### v18
<sup>18</sup> For her house sinks down to death And her tracks [lead] to the dead;

###### v19
<sup>19</sup> None who go to her return again, Nor do they reach the paths of life.

###### v20
<sup>20</sup> So you will walk in the way of good men And keep to the paths of the righteous.

###### v21
<sup>21</sup> For the upright will live in the land And the blameless will remain in it;

###### v22
<sup>22</sup> But the wicked will be cut off from the land And the treacherous will be uprooted from it.
