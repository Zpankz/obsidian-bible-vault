# Proverbs 4

## Proverbs 4:1-13

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> Hear, [O] sons, the instruction of a father, And give attention that you may gain understanding,

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> For I give you sound teaching; Do not abandon my instruction.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> When I was a son to my father, Tender and the only son in the sight of my mother,

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> Then he taught me and said to me, 'Let your heart hold fast my words; Keep my commandments and live;

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> Acquire wisdom! Acquire understanding! Do not forget nor turn away from the words of my mouth.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> 'Do not forsake her, and she will guard you; Love her, and she will watch over you.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> 'The beginning of wisdom [is]: Acquire wisdom; And with all your acquiring, get understanding.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> 'Prize her, and she will exalt you; She will honor you if you embrace her.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> 'She will place on your head a garland of grace; She will present you with a crown of beauty.'

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> Hear, my son, and accept my sayings And the years of your life will be many.

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> I have directed you in the way of wisdom; I have led you in upright paths.

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> When you walk, your steps will not be impeded; And if you run, you will not stumble.

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> Take hold of instruction; do not let go. Guard her, for she is your life.

## Proverbs 4:14-19

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> Do not enter the path of the wicked And do not proceed in the way of evil men.

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> Avoid it, do not pass by it; Turn away from it and pass on.

###### v16
<sup>16</sup> For they cannot sleep unless they do evil; And they are robbed of sleep unless they make [someone] stumble.

###### v17
<sup>17</sup> For they eat the bread of wickedness And drink the wine of violence.

###### v18
<sup>18</sup> But the path of the righteous is like the light of dawn, That shines brighter and brighter until the full day.

###### v19
<sup>19</sup> The way of the wicked is like darkness; They do not know over what they stumble.

## Proverbs 4:20-27

###### v20
<sup>20</sup> My son, give attention to my words; Incline your ear to my sayings.

###### v21
<sup>21</sup> Do not let them depart from your sight; Keep them in the midst of your heart.

###### v22
<sup>22</sup> For they are life to those who find them And health to all their body.

###### v23
<sup>23</sup> Watch over your heart with all diligence, For from it [flow] the springs of life.

###### v24
<sup>24</sup> Put away from you a deceitful mouth And put devious speech far from you.

###### v25
<sup>25</sup> Let your eyes look directly ahead And let your gaze be fixed straight in front of you.

###### v26
<sup>26</sup> Watch the path of your feet And all your ways will be established.

###### v27
<sup>27</sup> Do not turn to the right nor to the left; Turn your foot from evil.
