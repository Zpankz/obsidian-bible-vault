## Proverbs 4:1-13

<sup>1</sup> Hear, [O] sons, the instruction of a father, And give attention that you may gain understanding,[^1]

<sup>2</sup> For I give you sound teaching; Do not abandon my instruction.[^2]

<sup>3</sup> When I was a son to my father, Tender and the only son in the sight of my mother,

<sup>4</sup> Then he taught me and said to me, 'Let your heart hold fast my words; Keep my commandments and live;[^3][^4]

<sup>5</sup> Acquire wisdom! Acquire understanding! Do not forget nor turn away from the words of my mouth.[^5][^6][^7][^8]

<sup>6</sup> 'Do not forsake her, and she will guard you; Love her, and she will watch over you.[^9]

<sup>7</sup> 'The beginning of wisdom [is]: Acquire wisdom; And with all your acquiring, get understanding.[^10][^11][^12][^13]

<sup>8</sup> 'Prize her, and she will exalt you; She will honor you if you embrace her.

<sup>9</sup> 'She will place on your head a garland of grace; She will present you with a crown of beauty.'

<sup>10</sup> Hear, my son, and accept my sayings And the years of your life will be many.

<sup>11</sup> I have directed you in the way of wisdom; I have led you in upright paths.[^14]

<sup>12</sup> When you walk, your steps will not be impeded; And if you run, you will not stumble.[^15][^16]

<sup>13</sup> Take hold of instruction; do not let go. Guard her, for she is your life.[^17][^18][^19]

## Proverbs 4:14-19

<sup>14</sup> Do not enter the path of the wicked And do not proceed in the way of evil men.[^20][^21][^22][^23][^24][^25]

<sup>15</sup> Avoid it, do not pass by it; Turn away from it and pass on.

<sup>16</sup> For they cannot sleep unless they do evil; And they are robbed of sleep unless they make [someone] stumble.[^26][^27]

<sup>17</sup> For they eat the bread of wickedness And drink the wine of violence.

<sup>18</sup> But the path of the righteous is like the light of dawn, That shines brighter and brighter until the full day.[^28][^29][^30][^31][^32][^33][^34][^35]

<sup>19</sup> The way of the wicked is like darkness; They do not know over what they stumble.[^36]

## Proverbs 4:20-27

<sup>20</sup> My son, give attention to my words; Incline your ear to my sayings.[^37][^38][^39][^40][^41][^42][^43]

<sup>21</sup> Do not let them depart from your sight; Keep them in the midst of your heart.[^44]

<sup>22</sup> For they are life to those who find them And health to all their body.[^45][^46][^47][^48][^49]

<sup>23</sup> Watch over your heart with all diligence, For from it [flow] the springs of life.[^50][^51][^52][^53][^54][^55][^56][^57]

<sup>24</sup> Put away from you a deceitful mouth And put devious speech far from you.[^58][^59][^60]

<sup>25</sup> Let your eyes look directly ahead And let your gaze be fixed straight in front of you.[^61][^62][^63]

<sup>26</sup> Watch the path of your feet And all your ways will be established.[^64][^65][^66][^67][^68][^69][^70][^71]

<sup>27</sup> Do not turn to the right nor to the left; Turn your foot from evil.[^72][^73][^74][^75][^76]
