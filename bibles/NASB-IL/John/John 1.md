## John 1:5-14

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> The Light shines in the darkness, and the darkness did not comprehend it.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> There came a man sent from God, whose name was John.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> He came as a witness, to testify about the Light, so that all might believe through him.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> He was not the Light, but [he came] to testify about the Light.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> There was the true Light which, coming into the world, enlightens every man.

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> He was in the world, and the world was made through Him, and the world did not know Him.

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> He came to His own, and those who were His own did not receive Him.

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> But as many as received Him, to them He gave the right to become children of God, [even] to those who believe in His name,

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> who were born, not of blood nor of the will of the flesh nor of the will of man, but of God.

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> And the Word became flesh, and dwelt among us, and we saw His glory, glory as of the only begotten from the Father, full of grace and truth.

## John 1:19-28

###### v19
<sup>19</sup> This is the testimony of John, when the Jews sent to him priests and Levites from Jerusalem to ask him, 'Who are you?'

###### v20
<sup>20</sup> And he confessed and did not deny, but confessed, 'I am not the Christ.'

###### v21
<sup>21</sup> They asked him, 'What then? Are you Elijah?' And he said, 'I am not.' 'Are you the Prophet?' And he answered, 'No.'

###### v22
<sup>22</sup> Then they said to him, 'Who are you, so that we may give an answer to those who sent us? What do you say about yourself?'

###### v23
<sup>23</sup> He said, 'I am A VOICE OF ONE CRYING IN THE WILDERNESS, 'MAKE STRAIGHT THE WAY OF THE LORD,' as Isaiah the prophet said.'

###### v24
<sup>24</sup> Now they had been sent from the Pharisees.

###### v25
<sup>25</sup> They asked him, and said to him, 'Why then are you baptizing, if you are not the Christ, nor Elijah, nor the Prophet?'

###### v26
<sup>26</sup> John answered them saying, 'I baptize in water, [but] among you stands One whom you do not know.

###### v27
<sup>27</sup> '[It is] He who comes after me, the thong of whose sandal I am not worthy to untie.'

###### v28
<sup>28</sup> These things took place in Bethany beyond the Jordan, where John was baptizing.
