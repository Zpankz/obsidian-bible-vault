# John 15

## John 15:1-8

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> 'I am the true vine, and My Father is the vinedresser.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> 'Every branch in Me that does not bear fruit, He takes away; and every [branch] that bears fruit, He prunes it so that it may bear more fruit.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> 'You are already clean because of the word which I have spoken to you.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> 'Abide in Me, and I in you. As the branch cannot bear fruit of itself unless it abides in the vine, so neither [can] you unless you abide in Me.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> 'I am the vine, you are the branches; he who abides in Me and I in him, he bears much fruit, for apart from Me you can do nothing.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> 'If anyone does not abide in Me, he is thrown away as a branch and dries up; and they gather them, and cast them into the fire and they are burned.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> 'If you abide in Me, and My words abide in you, ask whatever you wish, and it will be done for you.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> 'My Father is glorified by this, that you bear much fruit, and [so] prove to be My disciples.

## John 15:9-17

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> 'Just as the Father has loved Me, I have also loved you; abide in My love.

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> 'If you keep My commandments, you will abide in My love; just as I have kept My Father's commandments and abide in His love.

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> 'These things I have spoken to you so that My joy may be in you, and [that] your joy may be made full.

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> 'This is My commandment, that you love one another, just as I have loved you.

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> 'Greater love has no one than this, that one lay down his life for his friends.

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> 'You are My friends if you do what I command you.

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> 'No longer do I call you slaves, for the slave does not know what his master is doing; but I have called you friends, for all things that I have heard from My Father I have made known to you.

###### v16
<sup>16</sup> 'You did not choose Me but I chose you, and appointed you that you would go and bear fruit, and [that] your fruit would remain, so that whatever you ask of the Father in My name He may give to you.

###### v17
<sup>17</sup> 'This I command you, that you love one another.

## John 15:18-25

###### v18
<sup>18</sup> 'If the world hates you, you know that it has hated Me before [it hated] you.

###### v19
<sup>19</sup> 'If you were of the world, the world would love its own; but because you are not of the world, but I chose you out of the world, because of this the world hates you.

###### v20
<sup>20</sup> 'Remember the word that I said to you, 'A slave is not greater than his master.' If they persecuted Me, they will also persecute you; if they kept My word, they will keep yours also.

###### v21
<sup>21</sup> 'But all these things they will do to you for My name's sake, because they do not know the One who sent Me.

###### v22
<sup>22</sup> 'If I had not come and spoken to them, they would not have sin, but now they have no excuse for their sin.

###### v23
<sup>23</sup> 'He who hates Me hates My Father also.

###### v24
<sup>24</sup> 'If I had not done among them the works which no one else did, they would not have sin; but now they have both seen and hated Me and My Father as well.

###### v25
<sup>25</sup> 'But [they have done this] to fulfill the word that is written in their Law, 'THEY HATED ME WITHOUT A CAUSE.'
