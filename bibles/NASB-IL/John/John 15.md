## John 15:1-8

<sup>1</sup> 'I am the true vine, and My Father is the vinedresser.[^1][^2][^3][^4][^5][^6][^7][^8]

<sup>2</sup> 'Every branch in Me that does not bear fruit, He takes away; and every [branch] that bears fruit, He prunes it so that it may bear more fruit.[^9][^10][^11][^12][^13][^14][^15][^16]

<sup>3</sup> 'You are already clean because of the word which I have spoken to you.[^17][^18][^19][^20]

<sup>4</sup> 'Abide in Me, and I in you. As the branch cannot bear fruit of itself unless it abides in the vine, so neither [can] you unless you abide in Me.[^21][^22][^23][^24][^25][^26][^27][^28]

<sup>5</sup> 'I am the vine, you are the branches; he who abides in Me and I in him, he bears much fruit, for apart from Me you can do nothing.[^29][^30][^31][^32][^33][^34][^35][^36]

<sup>6</sup> 'If anyone does not abide in Me, he is thrown away as a branch and dries up; and they gather them, and cast them into the fire and they are burned.[^37][^38][^39][^40][^41]

<sup>7</sup> 'If you abide in Me, and My words abide in you, ask whatever you wish, and it will be done for you.[^42][^43][^44][^45][^46][^47][^48][^49]

<sup>8</sup> 'My Father is glorified by this, that you bear much fruit, and [so] prove to be My disciples.[^50][^51][^52][^53][^54][^55][^56][^57]

## John 15:9-17

<sup>9</sup> 'Just as the Father has loved Me, I have also loved you; abide in My love.[^58][^59][^60][^61][^62]

<sup>10</sup> 'If you keep My commandments, you will abide in My love; just as I have kept My Father's commandments and abide in His love.[^63][^64][^65][^66][^67][^68][^69][^70]

<sup>11</sup> 'These things I have spoken to you so that My joy may be in you, and [that] your joy may be made full.[^71][^72][^73][^74][^75][^76][^77]

<sup>12</sup> 'This is My commandment, that you love one another, just as I have loved you.[^78][^79][^80][^81][^82][^83][^84][^85]

<sup>13</sup> 'Greater love has no one than this, that one lay down his life for his friends.[^86][^87][^88][^89][^90]

<sup>14</sup> 'You are My friends if you do what I command you.[^91][^92][^93][^94][^95][^96][^97]

<sup>15</sup> 'No longer do I call you slaves, for the slave does not know what his master is doing; but I have called you friends, for all things that I have heard from My Father I have made known to you.[^98][^99][^100][^101][^102][^103][^104][^105]

<sup>16</sup> 'You did not choose Me but I chose you, and appointed you that you would go and bear fruit, and [that] your fruit would remain, so that whatever you ask of the Father in My name He may give to you.[^106][^107][^108][^109][^110][^111][^112][^113]

<sup>17</sup> 'This I command you, that you love one another.[^114][^115][^116]

## John 15:18-25

<sup>18</sup> 'If the world hates you, you know that it has hated Me before [it hated] you.[^117][^118][^119][^120][^121][^122][^123][^124]

<sup>19</sup> 'If you were of the world, the world would love its own; but because you are not of the world, but I chose you out of the world, because of this the world hates you.[^125][^126][^127][^128]

<sup>20</sup> 'Remember the word that I said to you, 'A slave is not greater than his master.' If they persecuted Me, they will also persecute you; if they kept My word, they will keep yours also.[^129][^130]

<sup>21</sup> 'But all these things they will do to you for My name's sake, because they do not know the One who sent Me.

<sup>22</sup> 'If I had not come and spoken to them, they would not have sin, but now they have no excuse for their sin.[^131][^132][^133][^134]

<sup>23</sup> 'He who hates Me hates My Father also.

<sup>24</sup> 'If I had not done among them the works which no one else did, they would not have sin; but now they have both seen and hated Me and My Father as well.

<sup>25</sup> 'But [they have done this] to fulfill the word that is written in their Law, 'THEY HATED ME WITHOUT A CAUSE.'[^135][^136][^137]
