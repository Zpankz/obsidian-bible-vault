## John 17:1-5

<sup>1</sup> Jesus spoke these things; and lifting up His eyes to heaven, He said, 'Father, the hour has come; glorify Your Son, that the Son may glorify You,[^1][^2][^3][^4][^5][^6][^7]

<sup>2</sup> even as You gave Him authority over all flesh, that to all whom You have given Him, He may give eternal life.[^8][^9][^10][^11][^12][^13][^14][^15]

<sup>3</sup> 'This is eternal life, that they may know You, the only true God, and Jesus Christ whom You have sent.[^16][^17][^18][^19][^20][^21][^22][^23]

<sup>4</sup> 'I glorified You on the earth, having accomplished the work which You have given Me to do.[^24][^25][^26][^27]

<sup>5</sup> 'Now, Father, glorify Me together with Yourself, with the glory which I had with You before the world was.[^28][^29][^30][^31][^32][^33][^34]

<sup>9</sup> 'I ask on their behalf; I do not ask on behalf of the world, but of those whom You have given Me; for they are Yours;[^46][^47][^48]

<sup>10</sup> and all things that are Mine are Yours, and Yours are Mine; and I have been glorified in them.[^49][^50][^51]
