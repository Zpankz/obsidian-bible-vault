## John 10:1-18

<sup>1</sup> 'Truly, truly, I say to you, he who does not enter by the door into the fold of the sheep, but climbs up some other way, he is a thief and a robber.[^1][^2][^3][^4][^5][^6]

<sup>2</sup> 'But he who enters by the door is a shepherd of the sheep.

<sup>3</sup> 'To him the doorkeeper opens, and the sheep hear his voice, and he calls his own sheep by name and leads them out.[^7][^8]

<sup>4</sup> 'When he puts forth all his own, he goes ahead of them, and the sheep follow him because they know his voice.[^9][^10]

<sup>5</sup> 'A stranger they simply will not follow, but will flee from him, because they do not know the voice of strangers.'

<sup>6</sup> This figure of speech Jesus spoke to them, but they did not understand what those things were which He had been saying to them.

<sup>7</sup> So Jesus said to them again, 'Truly, truly, I say to you, I am the door of the sheep.[^11][^12][^13][^14][^15][^16]

<sup>8</sup> 'All who came before Me are thieves and robbers, but the sheep did not hear them.

<sup>9</sup> 'I am the door; if anyone enters through Me, he will be saved, and will go in and out and find pasture.[^17][^18][^19][^20][^21][^22][^23][^24]

<sup>10</sup> 'The thief comes only to steal and kill and destroy; I came that they may have life, and have [it] abundantly.[^25][^26][^27][^28][^29][^30][^31][^32]

<sup>11</sup> 'I am the good shepherd; the good shepherd lays down His life for the sheep.[^33][^34][^35][^36][^37][^38][^39][^40]

<sup>12</sup> 'He who is a hired hand, and not a shepherd, who is not the owner of the sheep, sees the wolf coming, and leaves the sheep and flees, and the wolf snatches them and scatters [them].

<sup>13</sup> '[He flees] because he is a hired hand and is not concerned about the sheep.

<sup>14</sup> 'I am the good shepherd, and I know My own and My own know Me,[^41][^42][^43][^44]

<sup>15</sup> even as the Father knows Me and I know the Father; and I lay down My life for the sheep.[^45][^46]

<sup>16</sup> 'I have other sheep, which are not of this fold; I must bring them also, and they will hear My voice; and they will become one flock [with] one shepherd.[^47][^48][^49][^50][^51][^52][^53][^54]

<sup>17</sup> 'For this reason the Father loves Me, because I lay down My life so that I may take it again.[^55]

<sup>18</sup> 'No one has taken it away from Me, but I lay it down on My own initiative. I have authority to lay it down, and I have authority to take it up again. This commandment I received from My Father.'[^56][^57][^58][^59][^60]

## John 10:22-38

<sup>22</sup> At that time the Feast of the Dedication took place at Jerusalem;

<sup>23</sup> it was winter, and Jesus was walking in the temple in the portico of Solomon.

<sup>24</sup> The Jews then gathered around Him, and were saying to Him, 'How long will You keep us in suspense? If You are the Christ, tell us plainly.'

<sup>25</sup> Jesus answered them, 'I told you, and you do not believe; the works that I do in My Father's name, these testify of Me.

<sup>26</sup> 'But you do not believe because you are not of My sheep.[^61]

<sup>27</sup> 'My sheep hear My voice, and I know them, and they follow Me;[^62][^63][^64][^65][^66][^67][^68][^69]

<sup>28</sup> and I give eternal life to them, and they will never perish; and no one will snatch them out of My hand.[^70][^71][^72][^73][^74][^75][^76][^77]

<sup>29</sup> 'My Father, who has given [them] to Me, is greater than all; and no one is able to snatch [them] out of the Father's hand.[^78]

<sup>30</sup> 'I and the Father are one.'[^79][^80][^81][^82][^83][^84][^85][^86]

<sup>31</sup> The Jews picked up stones again to stone Him.

<sup>32</sup> Jesus answered them, 'I showed you many good works from the Father; for which of them are you stoning Me?'

<sup>33</sup> The Jews answered Him, 'For a good work we do not stone You, but for blasphemy; and because You, being a man, make Yourself out [to be] God.'[^87]

<sup>34</sup> Jesus answered them, 'Has it not been written in your Law, 'I SAID, YOU ARE GODS '?[^88][^89][^90][^91][^92]

<sup>35</sup> 'If he called them gods, to whom the word of God came (and the Scripture cannot be broken),[^93]

<sup>36</sup> do you say of Him, whom the Father sanctified and sent into the world, 'You are blaspheming,' because I said, 'I am the Son of God '?

<sup>37</sup> 'If I do not do the works of My Father, do not believe Me;

<sup>38</sup> but if I do them, though you do not believe Me, believe the works, so that you may know and understand that the Father is in Me, and I in the Father.'[^94][^95][^96]
