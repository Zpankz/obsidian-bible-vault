# John 16

## John 16:1-6

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> 'These things I have spoken to you so that you may be kept from stumbling.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> 'They will make you outcasts from the synagogue, but an hour is coming for everyone who kills you to think that he is offering service to God.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> 'These things they will do because they have not known the Father or Me.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> 'But these things I have spoken to you, so that when their hour comes, you may remember that I told you of them. These things I did not say to you at the beginning, because I was with you.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> 'But now I am going to Him who sent Me; and none of you asks Me, 'Where are You going?'

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> 'But because I have said these things to you, sorrow has filled your heart.

## John 16:7-15

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> 'But I tell you the truth, it is to your advantage that I go away; for if I do not go away, the Helper will not come to you; but if I go, I will send Him to you.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> 'And He, when He comes, will convict the world concerning sin and righteousness and judgment;

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> concerning sin, because they do not believe in Me;

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> and concerning righteousness, because I go to the Father and you no longer see Me;

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> and concerning judgment, because the ruler of this world has been judged.

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> 'I have many more things to say to you, but you cannot bear [them] now.

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> 'But when He, the Spirit of truth, comes, He will guide you into all the truth; for He will not speak on His own initiative, but whatever He hears, He will speak; and He will disclose to you what is to come.

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> 'He will glorify Me, for He will take of Mine and will disclose [it] to you.

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> 'All things that the Father has are Mine; therefore I said that He takes of Mine and will disclose [it] to you.

## John 16:16-22

###### v16
<sup>16</sup> 'A little while, and you will no longer see Me; and again a little while, and you will see Me.'

###### v17
<sup>17</sup> [Some] of His disciples then said to one another, 'What is this thing He is telling us, 'A little while, and you will not see Me; and again a little while, and you will see Me'; and, 'because I go to the Father '?'

###### v18
<sup>18</sup> So they were saying, 'What is this that He says, 'A little while '? We do not know what He is talking about.'

###### v19
<sup>19</sup> Jesus knew that they wished to question Him, and He said to them, 'Are you deliberating together about this, that I said, 'A little while, and you will not see Me, and again a little while, and you will see Me'?

###### v20
<sup>20</sup> 'Truly, truly, I say to you, that you will weep and lament, but the world will rejoice; you will grieve, but your grief will be turned into joy.

###### v21
<sup>21</sup> 'Whenever a woman is in labor she has pain, because her hour has come; but when she gives birth to the child, she no longer remembers the anguish because of the joy that a child has been born into the world.

###### v22
<sup>22</sup> 'Therefore you too have grief now; but I will see you again, and your heart will rejoice, and no one [will] take your joy away from you.
