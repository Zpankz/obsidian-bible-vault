# Job 23

## Job 23:1-7

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> Then Job replied,

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> 'Even today my complaint is rebellion; His hand is heavy despite my groaning.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> 'Oh that I knew where I might find Him, That I might come to His seat!

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> 'I would present [my] case before Him And fill my mouth with arguments.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> 'I would learn the words [which] He would answer, And perceive what He would say to me.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> 'Would He contend with me by the greatness of [His] power? No, surely He would pay attention to me.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> 'There the upright would reason with Him; And I would be delivered forever from my Judge.

## Job 23:8-12

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> 'Behold, I go forward but He is not [there], And backward, but I cannot perceive Him;

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> When He acts on the left, I cannot behold [Him]; He turns on the right, I cannot see Him.

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> 'But He knows the way I take; [When] He has tried me, I shall come forth as gold.

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> 'My foot has held fast to His path; I have kept His way and not turned aside.

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> 'I have not departed from the command of His lips; I have treasured the words of His mouth more than my necessary food.

## Job 23:13-17

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> 'But He is unique and who can turn Him? And [what] His soul desires, that He does.

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> 'For He performs what is appointed for me, And many such [decrees] are with Him.

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> 'Therefore, I would be dismayed at His presence; [When] I consider, I am terrified of Him.

###### v16
<sup>16</sup> '[It is] God [who] has made my heart faint, And the Almighty [who] has dismayed me,

###### v17
<sup>17</sup> But I am not silenced by the darkness, Nor deep gloom [which] covers me.
