## Job 36:5-14

<sup>5</sup> 'Behold, God is mighty but does not despise [any]; [He is] mighty in strength of understanding.

<sup>6</sup> 'He does not keep the wicked alive, But gives justice to the afflicted.

<sup>7</sup> 'He does not withdraw His eyes from the righteous; But with kings on the throne He has seated them forever, and they are exalted.[^1][^2][^3][^4][^5][^6][^7][^8]

<sup>8</sup> 'And if they are bound in fetters, And are caught in the cords of affliction,

<sup>9</sup> Then He declares to them their work And their transgressions, that they have magnified themselves.

<sup>10</sup> 'He opens their ear to instruction, And commands that they return from evil.

<sup>11</sup> 'If they hear and serve [Him], They will end their days in prosperity And their years in pleasures.[^9][^10][^11][^12][^13]

<sup>12</sup> 'But if they do not hear, they shall perish by the sword And they will die without knowledge.

<sup>13</sup> 'But the godless in heart lay up anger; They do not cry for help when He binds them.

<sup>14</sup> 'They die in youth, And their life [perishes] among the cult prostitutes.

## Job 36:15-23

<sup>15</sup> 'He delivers the afflicted in their affliction, And opens their ear in [time of] oppression.

<sup>16</sup> 'Then indeed, He enticed you from the mouth of distress, Instead of it, a broad place with no constraint; And that which was set on your table was full of fatness.[^14]

<sup>17</sup> 'But you were full of judgment on the wicked; Judgment and justice take hold [of you].

<sup>18</sup> '[Beware] that wrath does not entice you to scoffing; And do not let the greatness of the ransom turn you aside.

<sup>19</sup> 'Will your riches keep you from distress, Or all the forces of [your] strength?

<sup>20</sup> 'Do not long for the night, When people vanish in their place.

<sup>21</sup> 'Be careful, do not turn to evil, For you have preferred this to affliction.

<sup>22</sup> 'Behold, God is exalted in His power; Who is a teacher like Him?[^15]

<sup>23</sup> 'Who has appointed Him His way, And who has said, 'You have done wrong '?

## Job 36:24-33

<sup>24</sup> 'Remember that you should exalt His work, Of which men have sung.

<sup>25</sup> 'All men have seen it; Man beholds from afar.

<sup>26</sup> 'Behold, God is exalted, and we do not know [Him]; The number of His years is unsearchable.[^16]

<sup>27</sup> 'For He draws up the drops of water, They distill rain from the mist,

<sup>28</sup> Which the clouds pour down, They drip upon man abundantly.

<sup>29</sup> 'Can anyone understand the spreading of the clouds, The thundering of His pavilion?

<sup>30</sup> 'Behold, He spreads His lightning about Him, And He covers the depths of the sea.

<sup>31</sup> 'For by these He judges peoples; He gives food in abundance.

<sup>32</sup> 'He covers [His] hands with the lightning, And commands it to strike the mark.

<sup>33</sup> 'Its noise declares His presence; The cattle also, concerning what is coming up.
