###### v1
<sup>1</sup> Then Job responded,

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> 'What a help you are to the weak! How you have saved the arm without strength!

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> 'What counsel you have given to [one] without wisdom! What helpful insight you have abundantly provided!

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> 'To whom have you uttered words? And whose spirit was expressed through you?

## Job 26:5-14

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> 'The departed spirits tremble Under the waters and their inhabitants.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> 'Naked is Sheol before Him, And Abaddon has no covering.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> 'He stretches out the north over empty space And hangs the earth on nothing.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> 'He wraps up the waters in His clouds, And the cloud does not burst under them.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> 'He obscures the face of the full moon And spreads His cloud over it.

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> 'He has inscribed a circle on the surface of the waters At the boundary of light and darkness.

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> 'The pillars of heaven tremble And are amazed at His rebuke.

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> 'He quieted the sea with His power, And by His understanding He shattered Rahab.

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> 'By His breath the heavens are cleared; His hand has pierced the fleeing serpent.

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> 'Behold, these are the fringes of His ways; And how faint a word we hear of Him! But His mighty thunder, who can understand?'
