# Job 13

## Job 13:1-12

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> 'Behold, my eye has seen all [this], My ear has heard and understood it.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> 'What you know I also know; I am not inferior to you.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> 'But I would speak to the Almighty, And I desire to argue with God.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> 'But you smear with lies; You are all worthless physicians.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> 'O that you would be completely silent, And that it would become your wisdom!

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> 'Please hear my argument And listen to the contentions of my lips.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> 'Will you speak what is unjust for God, And speak what is deceitful for Him?

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> 'Will you show partiality for Him? Will you contend for God?

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> 'Will it be well when He examines you? Or will you deceive Him as one deceives a man?

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> 'He will surely reprove you If you secretly show partiality.

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> 'Will not His majesty terrify you, And the dread of Him fall on you?

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> 'Your memorable sayings are proverbs of ashes, Your defenses are defenses of clay.

## Job 13:13-22

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> 'Be silent before me so that I may speak; Then let come on me what may.

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> 'Why should I take my flesh in my teeth And put my life in my hands?

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> 'Though He slay me, I will hope in Him. Nevertheless I will argue my ways before Him.

###### v16
<sup>16</sup> 'This also will be my salvation, For a godless man may not come before His presence.

###### v17
<sup>17</sup> 'Listen carefully to my speech, And let my declaration [fill] your ears.

###### v18
<sup>18</sup> 'Behold now, I have prepared my case; I know that I will be vindicated.

###### v19
<sup>19</sup> 'Who will contend with me? For then I would be silent and die.

###### v20
<sup>20</sup> 'Only two things do not do to me, Then I will not hide from Your face:

###### v21
<sup>21</sup> Remove Your hand from me, And let not the dread of You terrify me.

###### v22
<sup>22</sup> 'Then call, and I will answer; Or let me speak, then reply to me.

## Job 13:23-28

###### v23
<sup>23</sup> 'How many are my iniquities and sins? Make known to me my rebellion and my sin.

###### v24
<sup>24</sup> 'Why do You hide Your face And consider me Your enemy?

###### v25
<sup>25</sup> 'Will You cause a driven leaf to tremble? Or will You pursue the dry chaff?

###### v26
<sup>26</sup> 'For You write bitter things against me And make me to inherit the iniquities of my youth.

###### v27
<sup>27</sup> 'You put my feet in the stocks And watch all my paths; You set a limit for the soles of my feet,

###### v28
<sup>28</sup> While I am decaying like a rotten thing, Like a garment that is moth-eaten.
