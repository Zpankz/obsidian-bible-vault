# Job 27

## Job 27:1-6

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> Then Job continued his discourse and said,

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> 'As God lives, who has taken away my right, And the Almighty, who has embittered my soul,

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> For as long as life is in me, And the breath of God is in my nostrils,

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> My lips certainly will not speak unjustly, Nor will my tongue mutter deceit.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> 'Far be it from me that I should declare you right; Till I die I will not put away my integrity from me.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> 'I hold fast my righteousness and will not let it go. My heart does not reproach any of my days.

## Job 27:11-23

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> 'I will instruct you in the power of God; What is with the Almighty I will not conceal.

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> 'Behold, all of you have seen [it]; Why then do you act foolishly?

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> 'This is the portion of a wicked man from God, And the inheritance [which] tyrants receive from the Almighty.

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> 'Though his sons are many, they are destined for the sword; And his descendants will not be satisfied with bread.

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> 'His survivors will be buried because of the plague, And their widows will not be able to weep.

###### v16
<sup>16</sup> 'Though he piles up silver like dust And prepares garments as [plentiful as] the clay,

###### v17
<sup>17</sup> He may prepare [it], but the just will wear [it] And the innocent will divide the silver.

###### v18
<sup>18</sup> 'He has built his house like the spider's web, Or as a hut [which] the watchman has made.

###### v19
<sup>19</sup> 'He lies down rich, but never again; He opens his eyes, and it is no longer.

###### v20
<sup>20</sup> 'Terrors overtake him like a flood; A tempest steals him away in the night.

###### v21
<sup>21</sup> 'The east wind carries him away, and he is gone, For it whirls him away from his place.

###### v22
<sup>22</sup> 'For it will hurl at him without sparing; He will surely try to flee from its power.

###### v23
<sup>23</sup> '[Men] will clap their hands at him And will hiss him from his place.
