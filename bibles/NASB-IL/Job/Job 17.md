# Job 17

## Job 17:1-9

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> 'My spirit is broken, my days are extinguished, The grave is [ready] for me.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> 'Surely mockers are with me, And my eye gazes on their provocation.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> 'Lay down, now, a pledge for me with Yourself; Who is there that will be my guarantor?

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> 'For You have kept their heart from understanding, Therefore You will not exalt [them].

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> 'He who informs against friends for a share [of the spoil], The eyes of his children also will languish.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> 'But He has made me a byword of the people, And I am one at whom men spit.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> 'My eye has also grown dim because of grief, And all my members are as a shadow.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> 'The upright will be appalled at this, And the innocent will stir up himself against the godless.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> 'Nevertheless the righteous will hold to his way, And he who has clean hands will grow stronger and stronger.

## Job 17:10-16

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> 'But come again all of you now, For I do not find a wise man among you.

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> 'My days are past, my plans are torn apart, [Even] the wishes of my heart.

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> 'They make night into day, [saying], 'The light is near,' in the presence of darkness.

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> 'If I look for Sheol as my home, I make my bed in the darkness;

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> If I call to the pit, 'You are my father'; To the worm, 'my mother and my sister';

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> Where now is my hope? And who regards my hope?

###### v16
<sup>16</sup> 'Will it go down with me to Sheol? Shall we together go down into the dust?'
