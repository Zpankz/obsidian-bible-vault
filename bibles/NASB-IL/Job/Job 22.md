## Job 22:5-14

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> 'Is not your wickedness great, And your iniquities without end?

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> 'For you have taken pledges of your brothers without cause, And stripped men naked.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> 'To the weary you have given no water to drink, And from the hungry you have withheld bread.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> 'But the earth belongs to the mighty man, And the honorable man dwells in it.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> 'You have sent widows away empty, And the strength of the orphans has been crushed.

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> 'Therefore snares surround you, And sudden dread terrifies you,

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> Or darkness, so that you cannot see, And an abundance of water covers you.

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> 'Is not God [in] the height of heaven? Look also at the distant stars, how high they are!

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> 'You say, 'What does God know? Can He judge through the thick darkness?

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> 'Clouds are a hiding place for Him, so that He cannot see; And He walks on the vault of heaven.'

## Job 22:15-20

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> 'Will you keep to the ancient path Which wicked men have trod,

###### v16
<sup>16</sup> Who were snatched away before their time, Whose foundations were washed away by a river?

###### v17
<sup>17</sup> 'They said to God, 'Depart from us!' And 'What can the Almighty do to them?'

###### v18
<sup>18</sup> 'Yet He filled their houses with good [things]; But the counsel of the wicked is far from me.

###### v19
<sup>19</sup> 'The righteous see and are glad, And the innocent mock them,

###### v20
<sup>20</sup> [Saying], 'Truly our adversaries are cut off, And their abundance the fire has consumed.'

## Job 22:21-30

###### v21
<sup>21</sup> 'Yield now and be at peace with Him; Thereby good will come to you.

###### v22
<sup>22</sup> 'Please receive instruction from His mouth And establish His words in your heart.

###### v23
<sup>23</sup> 'If you return to the Almighty, you will be restored; If you remove unrighteousness far from your tent,

###### v24
<sup>24</sup> And place [your] gold in the dust, And [the gold of] Ophir among the stones of the brooks,

###### v25
<sup>25</sup> Then the Almighty will be your gold And choice silver to you.

###### v26
<sup>26</sup> 'For then you will delight in the Almighty And lift up your face to God.

###### v27
<sup>27</sup> 'You will pray to Him, and He will hear you; And you will pay your vows.

###### v28
<sup>28</sup> 'You will also decree a thing, and it will be established for you; And light will shine on your ways.

###### v29
<sup>29</sup> 'When you are cast down, you will speak with confidence, And the humble person He will save.

###### v30
<sup>30</sup> 'He will deliver one who is not innocent, And he will be delivered through the cleanness of your hands.'
