# Colossians 3

## Colossians 3:1-4

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> Therefore if you have been raised up with Christ, keep seeking the things above, where Christ is, seated at the right hand of God.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> Set your mind on the things above, not on the things that are on earth.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> For you have died and your life is hidden with Christ in God.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> When Christ, who is our life, is revealed, then you also will be revealed with Him in glory.

## Colossians 3:12-17

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> So, as those who have been chosen of God, holy and beloved, put on a heart of compassion, kindness, humility, gentleness and patience;

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> bearing with one another, and forgiving each other, whoever has a complaint against anyone; just as the Lord forgave you, so also should you.

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> Beyond all these things [put on] love, which is the perfect bond of unity.

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> Let the peace of Christ rule in your hearts, to which indeed you were called in one body; and be thankful.

###### v16
<sup>16</sup> Let the word of Christ richly dwell within you, with all wisdom teaching and admonishing one another with psalms [and] hymns [and] spiritual songs, singing with thankfulness in your hearts to God.

###### v17
<sup>17</sup> Whatever you do in word or deed, [do] all in the name of the Lord Jesus, giving thanks through Him to God the Father.

## Colossians 3:18-25

###### v18
<sup>18</sup> Wives, be subject to your husbands, as is fitting in the Lord.

###### v19
<sup>19</sup> Husbands, love your wives and do not be embittered against them.

###### v20
<sup>20</sup> Children, be obedient to your parents in all things, for this is well-pleasing to the Lord.

###### v21
<sup>21</sup> Fathers, do not exasperate your children, so that they will not lose heart.

###### v22
<sup>22</sup> Slaves, in all things obey those who are your masters on earth, not with external service, as those who [merely] please men, but with sincerity of heart, fearing the Lord.

###### v23
<sup>23</sup> Whatever you do, do your work heartily, as for the Lord rather than for men,

###### v24
<sup>24</sup> knowing that from the Lord you will receive the reward of the inheritance. It is the Lord Christ whom you serve.

###### v25
<sup>25</sup> For he who does wrong will receive the consequences of the wrong which he has done, and that without partiality.
