## Colossians 3:18-25

<sup>18</sup> Wives, be subject to your husbands, as is fitting in the Lord.[^118][^119][^120][^121]

<sup>19</sup> Husbands, love your wives and do not be embittered against them.[^122][^123][^124][^125][^126][^127]

<sup>20</sup> Children, be obedient to your parents in all things, for this is well-pleasing to the Lord.[^128][^129][^130][^131]

<sup>21</sup> Fathers, do not exasperate your children, so that they will not lose heart.[^132][^133]

<sup>22</sup> Slaves, in all things obey those who are your masters on earth, not with external service, as those who [merely] please men, but with sincerity of heart, fearing the Lord.[^134][^135][^136]

<sup>23</sup> Whatever you do, do your work heartily, as for the Lord rather than for men,[^137][^138][^139][^140][^141][^142][^143][^144]

<sup>24</sup> knowing that from the Lord you will receive the reward of the inheritance. It is the Lord Christ whom you serve.[^145][^146][^147][^148][^149][^150][^151][^152]

<sup>25</sup> For he who does wrong will receive the consequences of the wrong which he has done, and that without partiality.[^153][^154][^155]
