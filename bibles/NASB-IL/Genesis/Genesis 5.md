# Genesis 5

## Genesis 5:1-5

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> This is the book of the generations of Adam. In the day when God created man, He made him in the likeness of God.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> He created them male and female, and He blessed them and named them Man in the day when they were created.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> When Adam had lived one hundred and thirty years, he became the father of [a son] in his own likeness, according to his image, and named him Seth.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> Then the days of Adam after he became the father of Seth were eight hundred years, and he had [other] sons and daughters.

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> So all the days that Adam lived were nine hundred and thirty years, and he died.

## Genesis 5:6-20

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> Seth lived one hundred and five years, and became the father of Enosh.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> Then Seth lived eight hundred and seven years after he became the father of Enosh, and he had [other] sons and daughters.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> So all the days of Seth were nine hundred and twelve years, and he died.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> Enosh lived ninety years, and became the father of Kenan.

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> Then Enosh lived eight hundred and fifteen years after he became the father of Kenan, and he had [other] sons and daughters.

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> So all the days of Enosh were nine hundred and five years, and he died.

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> Kenan lived seventy years, and became the father of Mahalalel.

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> Then Kenan lived eight hundred and forty years after he became the father of Mahalalel, and he had [other] sons and daughters.

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> So all the days of Kenan were nine hundred and ten years, and he died.

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> Mahalalel lived sixty-five years, and became the father of Jared.

###### v16
<sup>16</sup> Then Mahalalel lived eight hundred and thirty years after he became the father of Jared, and he had [other] sons and daughters.

###### v17
<sup>17</sup> So all the days of Mahalalel were eight hundred and ninety-five years, and he died.

###### v18
<sup>18</sup> Jared lived one hundred and sixty-two years, and became the father of Enoch.

###### v19
<sup>19</sup> Then Jared lived eight hundred years after he became the father of Enoch, and he had [other] sons and daughters.

###### v20
<sup>20</sup> So all the days of Jared were nine hundred and sixty-two years, and he died.
