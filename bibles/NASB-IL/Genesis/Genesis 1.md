## Genesis 1:9-13

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> Then God said, 'Let the waters below the heavens be gathered into one place, and let the dry land appear'; and it was so.

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> God called the dry land earth, and the gathering of the waters He called seas; and God saw that it was good.

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> Then God said, 'Let the earth sprout vegetation: plants yielding seed, [and] fruit trees on the earth bearing fruit after their kind with seed in them'; and it was so.

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> The earth brought forth vegetation, plants yielding seed after their kind, and trees bearing fruit with seed in them, after their kind; and God saw that it was good.

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> There was evening and there was morning, a third day.

###### v16
<sup>16</sup> God made the two great lights, the greater light to govern the day, and the lesser light to govern the night; [He made] the stars also.

###### v17
<sup>17</sup> God placed them in the expanse of the heavens to give light on the earth,

###### v18
<sup>18</sup> and to govern the day and the night, and to separate the light from the darkness; and God saw that it was good.
