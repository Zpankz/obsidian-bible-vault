## Genesis 7:1-4

<sup>2</sup> 'You shall take with you of every clean animal by sevens, a male and his female; and of the animals that are not clean two, a male and his female;

<sup>3</sup> also of the birds of the sky, by sevens, male and female, to keep offspring alive on the face of all the earth.

<sup>4</sup> 'For after seven more days, I will send rain on the earth forty days and forty nights; and I will blot out from the face of the land every living thing that I have made.'

<sup>6</sup> Now Noah was six hundred years old when the flood of water came upon the earth.

<sup>8</sup> Of clean animals and animals that are not clean and birds and everything that creeps on the ground,

<sup>9</sup> there went into the ark to Noah by twos, male and female, as God had commanded Noah.

<sup>10</sup> It came about after the seven days, that the water of the flood came upon the earth.
