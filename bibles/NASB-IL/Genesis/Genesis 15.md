<sup>3</sup> And Abram said, 'Since You have given no offspring to me, one born in my house is my heir.'

<sup>4</sup> Then behold, the word of the LORD came to him, saying, 'This man will not be your heir; but one who will come forth from your own body, he shall be your heir.'

<sup>5</sup> And He took him outside and said, 'Now look toward the heavens, and count the stars, if you are able to count them.' And He said to him, 'So shall your descendants be.'[^9][^10][^11][^12]
