# Genesis 36

## Genesis 36:1-8

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> Now these are [the records of] the generations of Esau (that is, Edom).

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> Esau took his wives from the daughters of Canaan: Adah the daughter of Elon the Hittite, and Oholibamah the daughter of Anah and the granddaughter of Zibeon the Hivite;

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> also Basemath, Ishmael's daughter, the sister of Nebaioth.

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> Adah bore Eliphaz to Esau, and Basemath bore Reuel,

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> and Oholibamah bore Jeush and Jalam and Korah. These are the sons of Esau who were born to him in the land of Canaan.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> Then Esau took his wives and his sons and his daughters and all his household, and his livestock and all his cattle and all his goods which he had acquired in the land of Canaan, and went to [another] land away from his brother Jacob.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> For their property had become too great for them to live together, and the land where they sojourned could not sustain them because of their livestock.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> So Esau lived in the hill country of Seir; Esau is Edom.

## Genesis 36:9-19

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> These then are [the records of] the generations of Esau the father of the Edomites in the hill country of Seir.

###### v10
<sup>10</sup> These are the names of Esau's sons: Eliphaz the son of Esau's wife Adah, Reuel the son of Esau's wife Basemath.

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> The sons of Eliphaz were Teman, Omar, Zepho and Gatam and Kenaz.

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> Timna was a concubine of Esau's son Eliphaz and she bore Amalek to Eliphaz. These are the sons of Esau's wife Adah.

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> These are the sons of Reuel: Nahath and Zerah, Shammah and Mizzah. These were the sons of Esau's wife Basemath.

###### v14
<sup>14</sup> These were the sons of Esau's wife Oholibamah, the daughter of Anah and the granddaughter of Zibeon: she bore to Esau, Jeush and Jalam and Korah.

###### v15
<sup>15</sup> These are the chiefs of the sons of Esau. The sons of Eliphaz, the firstborn of Esau, are chief Teman, chief Omar, chief Zepho, chief Kenaz,

###### v16
<sup>16</sup> chief Korah, chief Gatam, chief Amalek. These are the chiefs descended from Eliphaz in the land of Edom; these are the sons of Adah.

###### v17
<sup>17</sup> These are the sons of Reuel, Esau's son: chief Nahath, chief Zerah, chief Shammah, chief Mizzah. These are the chiefs descended from Reuel in the land of Edom; these are the sons of Esau's wife Basemath.

###### v18
<sup>18</sup> These are the sons of Esau's wife Oholibamah: chief Jeush, chief Jalam, chief Korah. These are the chiefs descended from Esau's wife Oholibamah, the daughter of Anah.

###### v19
<sup>19</sup> These are the sons of Esau (that is, Edom), and these are their chiefs.
