# Genesis 13

## Genesis 13:1-4

###### v1
<sup>1</sup> So Abram went up from Egypt to the Negev, he and his wife and all that belonged to him, and Lot with him.

###### v2
<sup>2</sup> Now Abram was very rich in livestock, in silver and in gold.

###### v3
<sup>3</sup> He went on his journeys from the Negev as far as Bethel, to the place where his tent had been at the beginning, between Bethel and Ai,

###### v4
<sup>4</sup> to the place of the altar which he had made there formerly; and there Abram called on the name of the LORD.

## Genesis 13:5-9

###### v5
<sup>5</sup> Now Lot, who went with Abram, also had flocks and herds and tents.

###### v6
<sup>6</sup> And the land could not sustain them while dwelling together, for their possessions were so great that they were not able to remain together.

###### v7
<sup>7</sup> And there was strife between the herdsmen of Abram's livestock and the herdsmen of Lot's livestock. Now the Canaanite and the Perizzite were dwelling then in the land.

###### v8
<sup>8</sup> So Abram said to Lot, 'Please let there be no strife between you and me, nor between my herdsmen and your herdsmen, for we are brothers.

###### v9
<sup>9</sup> 'Is not the whole land before you? Please separate from me; if [to] the left, then I will go to the right; or if [to] the right, then I will go to the left.'

## Genesis 13:10-13

###### v10

###### v11
<sup>11</sup> So Lot chose for himself all the valley of the Jordan, and Lot journeyed eastward. Thus they separated from each other.

###### v12
<sup>12</sup> Abram settled in the land of Canaan, while Lot settled in the cities of the valley, and moved his tents as far as Sodom.

###### v13
<sup>13</sup> Now the men of Sodom were wicked exceedingly and sinners against the LORD.
